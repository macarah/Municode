328.3.1
A fee shall be payable to the Fire Department, upon the submittal of any final tract map for review by the Fire Department. The amount of the fee shall be as follows:
a.
$594.00 for each map consisting of 1-5 lots.
b.
$652.00 for each map consisting of 6-10 lots.
c.
$767.00 for each map consisting of 11-25 lots.
d.
$883.00 for each map consisting of 26-50 lots.
e.
$1,057.00 for each map consisting of 51 or more lots.
