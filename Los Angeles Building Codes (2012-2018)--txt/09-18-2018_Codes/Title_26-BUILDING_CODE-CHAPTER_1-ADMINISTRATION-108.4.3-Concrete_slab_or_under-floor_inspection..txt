108.4.3 - Concrete slab or under-floor inspection.
Inspection shall be made after all in-slab or under-floor reinforcing steel, building service equipment, conduit, piping accessories and other ancillary equipment items are installed but before any concrete is poured or floor sheathing installed, including the subfloor.
