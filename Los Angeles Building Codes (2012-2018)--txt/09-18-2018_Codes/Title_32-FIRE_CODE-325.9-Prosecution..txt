325.9 - Prosecution.
A person who violates Sections 325.2.1, 325.2.2, 325.3.2, 325.10, 503.2.1, 3104.21, 6107.2, or 6107.3 may be prosecuted and punished whether proceedings pursuant to Sections 325.3 - 325.8 inclusive, have been had or not. Proceedings pursuant to Sections 325.3 - 325.8 inclusive, are not a condition precedent to prosecution for violation of Sections 325.2.1, 325.2.2, 325.3.2, 325.10, 503.2.1, 3104.21, 6107.2, or 6107.3.
