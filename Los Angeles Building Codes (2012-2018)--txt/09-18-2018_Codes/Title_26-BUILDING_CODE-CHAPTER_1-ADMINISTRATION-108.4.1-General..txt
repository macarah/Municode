108.4.1 - General.
Reinforcing steel or structural framework of any part of any building or structure shall not be covered or concealed without first obtaining the approval of the Building Official.
Excavation and foundation reinforcement shall not be covered or concealed without first obtaining the approval of the Building Official.
Upon notification from the permit holder or the permit holder's agent, the Building Official shall make the inspections as set forth in Sections 108.4.2 through 108.4.8.
