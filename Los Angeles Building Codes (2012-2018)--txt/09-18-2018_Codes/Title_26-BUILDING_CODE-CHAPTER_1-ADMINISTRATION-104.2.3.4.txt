104.2.3.4
No person shall fail or refuse, after proper demand has been made upon such person as provided in this subsection, to promptly permit the Building Official or an authorized representative to make any inspection provided for by Section 104.2.3.2. Any person violating Section 104.2.3 shall be guilty of a misdemeanor.
