328.9.2.1
If during the oak tree inspection process, actual costs incurred reach 80 percent of the amount on deposit, the applicant shall be notified by the Fire Department and required to submit a minimum supplemental deposit in the amount of $5,000.00 directly to the Fire Department. There is no limit to the number of supplemental deposits that may be required to be submitted to the Fire Department prior to completion or withdrawal of the inspection report.
