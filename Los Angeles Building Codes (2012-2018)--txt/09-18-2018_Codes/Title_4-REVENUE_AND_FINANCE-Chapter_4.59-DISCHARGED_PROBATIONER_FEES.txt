Chapter 4.59 - DISCHARGED PROBATIONER FEES

4.59.010 - Discharged probationer fee.
The fee for filing a petition for a change of plea or setting aside of a verdict under Penal Code Section 1203.4 shall be $60.00 or the maximum amount permitted by law, whichever is greater.
