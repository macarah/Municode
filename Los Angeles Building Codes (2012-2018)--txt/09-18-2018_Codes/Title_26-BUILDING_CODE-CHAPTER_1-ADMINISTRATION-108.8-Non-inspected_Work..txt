108.8 - Non-inspected Work.
No person shall own, use, occupy or maintain any structure on which "Non-inspected Work" has been performed.
For the purposes of this Code, "Non-inspected Work" shall be defined as any erection, construction, enlargement, alteration, repair, movement, improvement, removal, connection, conversion, demolition or equipping, for which a permit was first obtained, pursuant to Section 106.1 supra, but which has progressed beyond the point indicated in successive inspections, including but not limited to inspections set forth in Section 108.4, 108.5 and Chapter 17, without first obtaining inspection and approval of the Building Official.
