106.5.7 - Combined swimming pool permit.
A combined swimming pool permit may be issued for a new swimming pool, spa or hot tub, which will include all building, electrical, plumbing, heating and excavation work. The combined swimming pool permit shall be subject to the requirements of this Code, the Residential Code, the Existing Building Code, the Green Building Standards Code, the Electrical Code, the Plumbing Code and the Mechanical Code, except that the fee for the combined swimming pool permit shall be as provided in Section 107.1 of this Code.
