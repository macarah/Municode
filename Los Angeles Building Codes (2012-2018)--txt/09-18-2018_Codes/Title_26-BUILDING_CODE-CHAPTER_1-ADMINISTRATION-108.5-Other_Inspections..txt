108.5 - Other Inspections.
In addition to the called inspections specified above, the Building Official may make or require any other inspections of any construction work to ascertain compliance with the provisions of this Code and other laws which are enforced by the building department.
For the purpose of determining compliance with Section 3403.8, the Building Official may cause any structure to be reinspected.
