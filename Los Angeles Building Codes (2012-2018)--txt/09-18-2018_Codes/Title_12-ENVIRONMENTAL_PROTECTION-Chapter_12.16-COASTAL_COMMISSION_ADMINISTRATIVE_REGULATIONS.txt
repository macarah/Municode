Chapter 12.16 - COASTAL COMMISSION ADMINISTRATIVE REGULATIONS

12.16.010 - Fee for processing approvals in concept.
For the purpose of defraying the expense involved in connection with the processing of approvals in concept (preliminary approvals) as required by Chapter 5, Subchapter 1, Article 1, Section 13052, Administrative Regulations, California Coastal Commission, a fee of $24.00 shall apply.
