108.7 - Inspection Requests.
It shall be the duty of the permit holder to notify the Building Official that work authorized by a permit is ready for inspection. The Building Official may require that every request for inspection be filed at least one working day before such inspection is desired. Such request may be in writing or by telephone at the option of the Building Official.
It shall be the duty of the person requesting any inspection required by this Code to provide access to and means for inspection of such work.
