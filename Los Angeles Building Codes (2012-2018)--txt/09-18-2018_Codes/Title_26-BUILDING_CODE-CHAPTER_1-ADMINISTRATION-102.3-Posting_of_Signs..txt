102.3 - Posting of Signs.
The Building Official shall cause to be posted on buildings required to be vacated or remain unoccupied a notice of "RESTRICTED USE" or "UNSAFE—Do Not Enter or Occupy" as described in Section 102.6.
(Ord. 2016-0053 § 2, 2016; Ord. 2007-0108 § 2 (part), 2007; Ord. 95-0065 § 3 (part), 1995.)
