906.1 - Where Required.
Portable fire extinguishers shall be installed in the following locations.
1.
In new and existing Group A, B, E, F, H, I, L, M, R-1, R-2, R-2.1, R-3.1, R 4, and S occupancies.
Exception: When approved by the fire code official for Group R-2 occupancies, portable fire extinguishers shall be required only in locations specified in Items 2 through 6 where each dwelling unit is provided with a portable fire extinguisher having a minimum rating of 1-A:10-B:C.
