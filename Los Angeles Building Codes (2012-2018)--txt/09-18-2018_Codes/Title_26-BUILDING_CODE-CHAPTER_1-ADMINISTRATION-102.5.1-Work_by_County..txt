102.5.1 - Work by County.
If the repairs, demolition or other work necessary to remove the unsafe condition as set forth in the Notice of Unsafe Building is not made within the designated period and a hearing has not been requested by any party concerned, the Building Official shall request that a hearing be held regarding the unsafe condition. If the finding by the Building Board of Appeals is not complied with within the period designated by the Board, the Building Official may then secure or demolish such portions of the structure, or may cause such work to be done, to the extent necessary to eliminate the hazard determined to exist by the Building Board of Appeals.
