103.4.5 - Appeal.
Any person having any right, title, lien or interest in the property so recorded or any part thereof may request a hearing before the Code Enforcement Appeals Board to appeal the violation notice. The request for hearing shall be submitted in writing within 30 days of the notice from the Building Official provided in accordance with Section 103.4.3. The time for appeal may be extended by the Building Official for good cause shown. Failure to submit a timely written request for appeal or to appear at a scheduled hearing shall be deemed a waiver of the right to a hearing before the Code Enforcement Appeals Board.
