110.2.3.9
Notwithstanding any other provisions of this Section, the Building Official may, at his or her discretion, deny a permit for any building, structure, or grading subject to hazard from landslide, settlement, or slippage, which cannot be mitigated and may endanger the health or safety of the occupants, adjoining property, or the public.
