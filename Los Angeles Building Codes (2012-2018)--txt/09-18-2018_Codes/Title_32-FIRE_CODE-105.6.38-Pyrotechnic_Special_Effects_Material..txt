105.6.38 - Pyrotechnic Special Effects Material.
An operational permit is required for use and handling of pyrotechnic special effects material. See Health and Safety Code Division 11, Part 2, Section 12500, et seq. for additional requirements. For pyrotechnic and special effects used for motion picture, television, and commercial productions, see Section 105.6.49(2).
