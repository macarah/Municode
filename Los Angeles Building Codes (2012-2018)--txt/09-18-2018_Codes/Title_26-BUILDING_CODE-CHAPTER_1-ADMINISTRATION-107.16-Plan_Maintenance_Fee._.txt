107.16 - Plan Maintenance Fee.*
Before issuing a building permit, the Building Official shall collect a plan maintenance fee for all building plans which are required to be retained by Section 19850 of the Health and Safety Code.
The amount of the plan maintenance fee shall be 2 percent of the building permit fee as set forth in Table 1-A provided, however, that the minimum fee shall be $10.70 and the maximum fee shall be $456.20. A plan maintenance fee shall be collected for each separate plan to be retained by the Building Official.
EXCEPTIONS:
1.
Single or multiple dwellings not more than two stories and basement in height.
2.
Garages and other structures appurtenant to buildings specified in Exception No. 1, above.
3.
Farm or ranch buildings appurtenant to buildings specified in Exception No. 1, above.
4.
Any one-story building where the span between bearing walls does not exceed 25 feet (7620 mm), except a steel frame or concrete building.
(Ord. 2013-0048 § 2, 2013; Ord. 2007-0108 § 2 (part), 2007; Ord. 95-0065 § 3 (part), 1995.)
*  Editor's note: Fee changes in this section were made by the Director of Public Works due to increases in the Consumer Price Index and are effective July 1, 2017.
