SECTION 112 - EARTHQUAKE FAULT MAPS
Earthquake fault zone maps within the County of Los Angeles prepared under Sections 2622 and 2623 of the California Resources Code which show traces of earthquake faults are hereby declared to be, on the date of official issue, a part of this Code, and may be referred to elsewhere in this Code. Earthquake fault zone maps revised under the above sections of the California Resources Code shall, on the date of their official issue, supersede previously issued maps which they replace.
Copies of each of the above maps shall be available for examination by the public at the Department of Public Works, Department of Regional Planning and the Executive Office of the Board of Supervisors.
