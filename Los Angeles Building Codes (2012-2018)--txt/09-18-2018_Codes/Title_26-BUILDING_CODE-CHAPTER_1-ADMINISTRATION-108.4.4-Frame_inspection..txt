108.4.4 - Frame inspection.
Inspection shall be made after the roof, roof deck or sheathing, all framing, braced walls, fire blocking and bracing are in place and all conduits, plumbing pipes, chimneys and vents to be concealed are complete and the rough electrical, plumbing, and heating wires, conduits, plumbing pipes, and ducts are approved.
