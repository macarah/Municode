110.2.3.11
When the proposed work involves a minor alteration or repair to an existing Group R-3 Occupancy building and/or its accessory structures. Minor alterations and repairs shall include the following:
1.
Roof mount photovoltaic solar systems that impose no more than 5 percent gravity load increase to the existing building.
2.
Ground mount photovoltaic solar systems.
3.
Recovering and reroofings.
4.
New and replacement mechanical and plumbing equipment.
5.
Window change-outs.
6.
Similar work as determined by the Building Official.
