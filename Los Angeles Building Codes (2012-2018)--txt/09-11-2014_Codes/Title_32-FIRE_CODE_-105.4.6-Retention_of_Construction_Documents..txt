105.4.6 - Retention of Construction Documents.
One set of construction documents shall be retained by the fire code official for a period of not less than 90 days from date of completion of the permitted work, or as required by section 19850 of the California Health and Safety Code. One set of approved construction documents shall be returned to the applicant, and said set shall be kept on the site of the building or work at all times during which the work authorized thereby is in progress.
