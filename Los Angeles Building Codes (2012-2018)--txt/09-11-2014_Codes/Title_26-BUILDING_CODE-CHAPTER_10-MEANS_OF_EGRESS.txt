CHAPTER 10 - MEANS OF EGRESS

SECTION 1029 - EMERGENCY ESCAPE AND RESCUE
1029.4 Operational constraints.
. . .
Where security bars (burglar bars) are installed on emergency egress and rescue windows or doors, such devices shall comply with California Building Standards Code, Part 12, Chapter 12-3 and other applicable provisions of Part 2.
