5706.3.9 - Permits.
For permits to drill, own, operate, or maintain an oil or natural gas well, see Section 105.6.16. No person shall drill, own, operate, or maintain any oil or natural gas well without first obtaining a permit. Each oil or natural gas well permit shall be valid only for the calendar year in which it is issued. The permit fee shall be $48.00. There shall be no charge for renewals.
