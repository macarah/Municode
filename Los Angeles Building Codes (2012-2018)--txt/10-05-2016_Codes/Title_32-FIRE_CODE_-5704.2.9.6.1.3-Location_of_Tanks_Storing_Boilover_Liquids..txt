5704.2.9.6.1.3 - Location of Tanks Storing Boilover Liquids.
Above-ground tanks for storage of liquids with boilover characteristics shall be located in accordance with Table 22.4.1.4 of NFPA 30. Shell-to-shell spacing between tanks shall not be less than the diameter of the largest tank.
