903.7 - Buildings Four or More Stories.
In multi-story buildings that are four or more stories, including any basement levels, the following devices shall be provided for the automatic fire sprinkler system at each floor level within an exit stairway enclosure:
1.
Indicating control valve.
2.
Water flow detector with an alarm bell.
3.
Drain valve.
4.
Inspector's test valve with sight gauge.
