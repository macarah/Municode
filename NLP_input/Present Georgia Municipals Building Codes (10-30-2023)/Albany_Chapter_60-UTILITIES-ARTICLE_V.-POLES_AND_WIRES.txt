Sec. 60-878. - Under control of superintendent of light and power department.
All poles, posts, cross-arms, wires and all electrical or other fixtures thereto appertaining, now placed, erected, owned or used, or which may hereafter be placed, erected, owned or used, under proper franchise or permission of the city, in or on any street, alley or public place in the city by any telegraph, telephone, electric light or power or street railway company, or any electrical construction or repair company, or any person, shall be under the control, direction and supervision of the superintendent of the light and power department.
(Code 1975, § 21-1; Code 1985, § 27-111)
Sec. 60-879. - Permit required for placing of poles, stringing of wires, etc.
Before exercising any right or privilege under any franchise heretofore granted or that may be hereafter granted to any of the persons named in section 60-878 relative to the placing of poles or stringing wires or changing the location of poles, cross-arms, wires or other fixtures thereto appertaining, or before any repair is done to the same, such persons shall first obtain written permission from the superintendent of the light and power department to do the same, which written permission shall specify exactly what is to be done and shall be signed by the superintendent of the light and power department and approved by the general manager of the water, gas and light commission.
(Code 1975, § 21-2; Code 1985, § 27-112)
Sec. 60-880. - Record of permits; report.
The superintendent of the light and power department shall keep a full and complete record of all permits issued under this article and when required, shall make a report thereof to the water, gas and light commission.
(Code 1975, § 21-3; Code 1985, § 27-113)
