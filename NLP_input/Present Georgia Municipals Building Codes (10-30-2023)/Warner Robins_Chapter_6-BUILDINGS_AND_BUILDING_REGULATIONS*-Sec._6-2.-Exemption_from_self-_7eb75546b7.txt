Sec. 6-2. - Exemption from self-inspection of certain water and sewer lines.
Pursuant to the authority contained in Section 3 of Act 1046 Georgia Laws, subsection (d) of Code Section 8-2-26 of the Official Code of Georgia Annotated, relating to self-inspection of water and sewer projects by master plumbers or utility contractors, shall not be applicable within the City of Warner Robins.
