Sec. 86-190. - NAICS 71391—Golf courses and country clubs.
modified
The following conditions are assigned in the R-2, R-4, R-C, RM, O&I and MX districts:
(1)
Must be located on a street having a classification of collector or greater.
(2)
Must be established on a lot having a minimum area of one acre and a minimum frontage of 150 feet.
(Ord. No. 2019-003 , § 1, 2-11-2019; Ord. No. 2019-011 , § 1, 6-3-2019; Ord. No. 2021-014 , § 1(Exh. A), 6-14-2021)
