ARTICLE VII. - DESIGN STANDARDS FOR BLOCKS AND LOTS

Sec. 74-201. - Block lengths and widths.
Block lengths and widths shall be as follows:
(1)
Blocks shall not be greater than 1,800 feet nor less than 600 feet in length, except in unusual severe circumstances must obtain approval of the planning commission.
(2)
Blocks shall be wide enough to allow two tiers of lots, except where fronting on major thoroughfares, limited access highways, or railroads, or prevented by topographical conditions or size of property. In these cases the planning commission may approve a single tier of lots of minimum depth to include a buffer zone.
(Res. No. 2001-08, § 1(701), 3-13-2001; Ord. No. 2021-26 , § 1, 12-14-2021)
Sec. 74-202. - Lot sizes and proportions.
(a)
Residential lots shall meet the lot width and lot area requirements of the zoning ordinance of the county at the time of the development in the zoning district where the property being developed is located.
(b)
Residential lots shall have a depth of not less than 150 feet and not less than 200 feet wide at building site.
(c)
Residential corner lots shall have adequate width to meet building setback requirements from both abutting streets.
(Res. No. 2001-08, § 1(702), 3-13-2001; Ord. No. 2021-26 , § 2, 12-14-2021)
Sec. 74-203. - Adequate building sites.
Each lot shall contain an adequate building site not subject to flooding and outside the limits of any existing easements or building setback lines required by this chapter and any existing zoning resolution.
(Res. No. 2001-08, § 1(703), 3-13-2001)
Sec. 74-204. - Panhandle or flag lots.
Panhandle or flag lots are prohibited.
(Res. No. 2001-08, § 1(704), 3-13-2001; Ord. No. 2021-26 , § 3, 12-14-2021)
Sec. 74-205. - Buidling setback lines.
(a)
Building setback lines shall be indicated on each plat.
(b)
The minimum building setback lines on each lot shall be no less than 30 feet from each side lot line and 30 feet from the rear lot line.
(c)
The minimum building set back line for the front lot line shall be 75 feet from the road right-of-way.
(Ord. No. 2003-14, § 1, 4-8-2003; Ord. No. 2021-26 , § 4, 12-14-2021)
Editor's note— Ord. No. 2003-14, § 1, adopted April 8, 2003, repealed § 74-205, in its entirety and enacted new provisions to read as herein set out. Prior to amendment, § 74-205 pertained to similar subject matter and derived from Res. No. 2001-08, § 1(705), adopted March 13, 2001.
Sec. 74-206. - Utilities.
All utilities in a class II and class III subdivisions shall be underground except in unusual circumstances in which case plans must be approved by the zoning officer and the chairman of the board of commissioners.
(Res. No. 2001-08, § 1(706), 3-13-2001; Ord. No. 2021-26 , § 5, 12-14-2021)
Secs. 74-207—74-230. - Reserved.
