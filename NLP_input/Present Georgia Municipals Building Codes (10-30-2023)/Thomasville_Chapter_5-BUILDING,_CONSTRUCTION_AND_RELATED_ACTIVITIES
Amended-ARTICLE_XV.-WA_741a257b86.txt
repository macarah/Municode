Sec. 5-520. - Exemptions.
Amended by Ordinance of 11-14-2022 (1)
The owner, or the owner's agent, of a building undergoing new construction or repair or renovation who is entitled to an exemption from the standards specified in O.C.G.A. § 8-2-3(c) shall obtain the exemption by applying at the office of the chief building official for the city. A fee of $25.00shall be charged for the inspection and issuance of such exemption.
(Code 1991, § 5-455; Ord. of 3-23-1992, § XII)
