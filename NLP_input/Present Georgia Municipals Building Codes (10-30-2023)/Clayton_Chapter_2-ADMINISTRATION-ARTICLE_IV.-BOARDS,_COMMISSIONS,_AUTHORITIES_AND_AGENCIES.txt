ARTICLE IV. - BOARDS, COMMISSIONS, AUTHORITIES AND AGENCIES

Sec. 2-204. - Establishment by council.
The city council shall establish such boards, commissions, and authorities as it deemed necessary or desirable from time to time to fulfill any investigative, quasi-judicial or quasi-legislative function the mayor and council, upon such terms as are provided by the council by ordinance in accordance with the city's Charter.
Sec. 2-205. - Rules, regulations and bylaws.
Unless otherwise provided by the city council, each board, commission, and authority established by the city council may establish such bylaws, rules and regulations, not inconsistent with the city's Charter, the Code, or general law, as it deems appropriate and necessary for the fulfillment of its duties or the conduct of its affairs; provided, however, that copies of such bylaws, rules and regulations shall be filed with the city clerk.
