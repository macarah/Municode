ARTICLE I. - IN GENERAL

Sec. 2-1. - Consolidated fee schedule.
The county shall maintain a consolidated fee schedule for permits, licenses and other county fees, charges and costs. Unless otherwise indicated, the amount of any fees, charges and costs imposed by the county, including, but not limited to, those set forth in the county's consolidated fee schedule, shall be established from time to time by action of the board of commissioners, as it deems necessary. A copy of the consolidated fee schedule shall be kept in the office of the county clerk and be made available for public examination during normal working hours.
Sec. 2-2. - County commissioners' regular meetings.
Board of commissioners' regular meetings shall be held on the second Wednesday at 9:00 a.m. and the fourth Tuesday at 6:00 p.m. each month.
(Code 1991, § 2-1; Mo. of 1-12-1983(1); Ord. No. 95-01, § 1, 2-13-1995; Ord. No. 2011-02, 4-13-2011; Ord. No. 2014-01 , 3-25-2014)
Sec. 2-3. - Appearance before board.
Anyone wishing to be put on the agenda shall notify the board of commissioners' office by 12:00 noon of the Friday before the regularly scheduled meeting. He should also state his name, what it is in reference to and any other pertinent information. Anyone addressing the board during a meeting should state his name and the physical address at which they reside so the minutes can accurately reflect all necessary information.
(Code 1991, § 2-2; Mo. of 5-9-1984)
Sec. 2-4. - Rules of order.
In the event of a question as to order or procedure in the course of business by the board of commissioners, the most current edition of Robert's Rules of Order shall be the authority consulted and observed.
(Code 1991, § 2-3; Res. of 2-11-1987)
Sec. 2-5. - Issuance of violation citations.
The board of commissioners hereby authorizes the following persons to issue citations for violations of county ordinances:
(1)
County sheriff and certified deputy sheriffs;
(2)
County health department;
(3)
County director of community development or his designee;
(4)
Public works director or his designee;
(5)
Animal control officers; and
(6)
Other such officials as may be designated from time to time by the board of commissioners.
(Code 1991, § 2-5; Res. of 4-10-1985; Res. of 2-10-1992)
Sec. 2-6. - Emergency medical service rates.
Rates for emergency medical services, ambulance service, advanced emergency calls and basic EMS transfers shall be as determined from time to time by the board of commissioners and set forth in the consolidated fee schedule.
(Code 1991, § 2-6; Mo. of 1-12-1983(3); Mo. of 5-18-1988)
Sec. 2-7. - Smoking in county buildings.
Smoking shall be prohibited in all county buildings, on county property and in all county vehicles, and no smoking signs shall be placed throughout the courthouse and other county buildings.
(Code 1991, § 2-7; Mo. of 3-11-1981; Ord. No. 93-02, § 1, 11-8-1993)
State Law reference— Smoking in public places prohibited, O.C.G.A. § 16-12-2.
Sec. 2-8. - Prohibiting use of county resources to promote candidates.
(a)
It shall be unlawful for any person to use or, if such person is directly responsible for the supervision of a person or property, to allow the use of, any county resource, personnel or property, for the promotion of any political candidate or cause.
(b)
Nothing in this section shall be implied or considered an attempt to curtail or restrict a person or employee from exercising his free will in voting for or supporting candidates or causes of his choice while off duty and without use of county resources.
(Code 1991, § 2-8; Ord. No. 92-0608, § 3A, 6-8-1992)
