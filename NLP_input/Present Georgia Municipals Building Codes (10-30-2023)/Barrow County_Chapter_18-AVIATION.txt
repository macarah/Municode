Sec. 18-31. - Definitions.
The following words, terms and phrases, when used in this article, shall have the meanings ascribed to them in this section, except where the context clearly indicates a different meaning:
Runway: An area designated by the county airport authority on which aircraft shall be permitted to land and from which aircraft shall be permitted to take off.
Taxiway: An area designated by the county airport authority on which aircraft shall be permitted to move along the surface of the ground before takeoff or after landing.
Unauthorized vehicle: Any car, truck, van, motorcycle, recreational vehicle or other motorized vehicle which has not received specific permission from the county airport authority to operate on runways and taxiways at the Winder-Barrow Airport.
(Code 1993, § 3-26)
Cross reference— Definitions generally, § 1-2.
Sec. 18-32. - Penalty.
Persons found to be in violation of this article shall be guilty of a misdemeanor and shall be punishable by a fine not to exceed $500.00 or imprisonment for not more than 30 days.
(Code 1993, § 3-29)
Sec. 18-33. - Purpose of article.
The purpose of this article is to provide a means of enforcing runway and taxiway safety precautions at the Winder-Barrow Airport.
(Code 1993, § 3-27)
Sec. 18-34. - Unauthorized vehicles prohibited in certain areas.
All persons are prohibited from operating unauthorized vehicles upon runways and taxiways or in any other areas prohibited by the airport authority at the Winder-Barrow Airport.
(Code 1993, § 3-28)
