ARTICLE 98-20. - FEE SCHEDULE

Sec. 98-20.1. - Fee schedule.
A.
Fees. The city clerk shall maintain an official fee schedule for permits and approval processes referenced in this chapter and adopted by resolution of city council from time to time. The fee schedule shall be available to the public in the office of the city clerk. Fees associated with such permits and approval processes shall include, but not be limited to, application fees, legal fees, and public notice costs, among others, payable promptly upon written notification of the fee amount to the applicant by the city. All fees shall be paid prior to any required public hearing. In the event such fees have not been paid prior to any public hearing, such hearing shall be postponed until payment of the fees has been made in full and the applicant shall be obligated for the original cost of the public notice, among other fees, and all re-advertising costs.
B.
Refunds upon withdrawal of an application. All fees associated with a public notice and the actual cost of application processing shall be non-refundable upon the city incurring those costs. The timeframe in which such processing occurs shall be the date the public notice has been ordered.
C.
Fees to be paid in advance. Final action on any permit application or petition shall not be taken until all applicable fees, charges, and expenses have been paid in full.
