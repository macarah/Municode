Sec. 82-51. - Purpose.
The purpose of this division is to adopt the state department of human resources on-site management systems rules and regulations (chapter 290-5-26) as a county ordinance in order to facilitate the enforcement of those rules in the county.
(Ord. No. 91-4, § I, 8-6-1991)
Sec. 82-52. - Penalty.
Any person, corporation, partnership, or other entity violating any provision of this division shall be tried before the magistrate court of the county. Upon conviction, a violation of this division may be punished as provided in section 1-9.
(Ord. No. 91-4, § III, 8-6-1991)
Sec. 82-53. - Compliance with rules and regulations.
All persons in the county who dispose of the sewage from their residence or any residence owned by them shall follow all rules and regulations outlined in the state department of human resources manual for on-site sewage management systems (chapter 290-5-26) or be in violation of the ordinance adopted in section 82-51.
(Ord. No. 91-4, § II, 8-6-1991)
