ARTICLE II. - SUPERIOR COURT

Sec. 16-19. - Distribution of drug forfeiture pool.
Pursuant to authority of O.C.G.A. § 16-13-49, the office of the district attorney of the county (Alcovy judicial circuit) shall receive ten percent of all amounts hereafter distributed from the drug forfeiture pool created pursuant to said section. Said payments shall be used to provide for payment of any and all necessary expenses for the operation of said office, and shall be in addition to and not in reduction of sums otherwise appropriated to the use of said office by the county board of commissioners.
(Code 2001, § 5-101; Ord. of 3-2-1992)
Sec. 16-20. - Superior court judge, retirement supplement.
As authorized by the laws of this state, any retirement benefit being paid by the Georgia Judicial Retirement System on account of a former superior court judge of this circuit shall be supplemented by this county as follows: the supplement shall bear to such benefit the same percentage which the respective salary supplements paid by the county to active superior court judges bears to the state salary of active superior court judges, but in no event shall such percentage be less than the percentage which the supplement paid by this county to such retired judge at the time of his/her/their retirement bears to the state salary paid to him/her/them at that time; and such supplement shall be paid in equal monthly installments.
(Code 2001, § 5-102; Ord. of 3-3-1987)
Secs. 16-21—16-43. - Reserved.
ARTICLE III. - JAIL CONSTRUCTION AND STAFFING

Sec. 16-44. - Jail construction and staffing.
The Jail Construction and Staffing Act (O.C.G.A. § 15-21-90 et seq.) is hereby put into effect in the county and such additional penalty assessments shall be imposed and collected and all funds collected therefrom shall be used exclusively for the construction, operation, or staffing of the county jail, that county shall enter into such intergovernmental contracts as may be convenient and proper with the municipalities within the county so as to provide for the imposition and collection of such additional penalty assessments in all courts operated by the municipalities within the county, and to comply with all provisions of the Jail Construction and Staffing Act in every respect.
(Code 2001, § 16-101; Ord. of 5-1-1990)
