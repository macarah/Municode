Sec. 2-126. - Declaration of need.
It is hereby determined and declared to be a pressing, existing and future need for a hospital authority, to function in the City of Louisville and Jefferson County and their environs for the purpose of providing adequate and proper public health facilities in the area, thereby promoting the general welfare of the citizenry.
(Code 1996, § 2-61; Res. of 8-26-1977)
State Law reference— Hospital authority for each county created by statute, declaration of need required, O.C.G.A. § 31-7-72.
Sec. 2-127. - Activation.
There is hereby activated the public body corporate and politic known as the Hospital Authority of Jefferson County and the City of Louisville which was created upon the adoption and approval of the Hospital Authorities Law as set forth in O.C.G.A. § 31-7-72 et seq.
(Code 1996, § 2-62; Res. of 8-26-1977)
State Law reference— Declaration of need operates to activate authority, O.C.G.A. § 31-7-72.
Sec. 2-128. - Members.
The hospital authority shall consist of seven members as jointly approved by the hospital authority and the governing bodies. Two board members shall be designees of the City of Louisville and five board members shall be designees of the county. Board members shall be appointed for staggered terms as specified by resolution of the appointing governing body. If at the end of any term of office of any trustee, a successor thereto shall not have been elected, then the trustee whose term of office shall have expired shall continue to hold office until his successor shall be so elected. Each successor trustee shall be elected by the respective municipality or county which elected his predecessor, and shall serve for the same term.
(Code 1996, § 2-63; Res. of 8-26-1977; Res. of 1-9-1990)
State Law reference— Members of hospital authority, O.C.G.A. § 31-7-72.
