ARTICLE I. - IN GENERAL

Sec. 40-1. - Depository financial institutions tax.
(a)
Levied. Pursuant to O.C.G.A. § 48-6-93, there is hereby levied for each year an annual business license tax upon depository financial institutions having an office located within the city at the rate of 0.25 percent of the state's gross receipts, as defined and allocated in this section, of such depository financial institutions. The minimum annual amount of such levy upon any depository financial institution shall be $1,000.00.
(b)
Due date of taxes. The due date of taxes levied by this section shall be April 1 of each year.
(Code 1982, § 2-5-30.1; Code 2000, § 78-2)

Sec. 40-22. - [Purpose.]
The city council shall assess, levy, and collect an ad valorem tax on all real and personal property within the corporate limits of the city that is subject to such taxation by the state and county.
(Ord. No. 21-02 , § 1, 9-13-2021)
Sec. 40-23. - [Use.]
The city council shall use this revenue to defray the costs of operating the city government, of providing governmental services, for the repayment of principal and interest on general obligations, and for any other public purpose as determined by the city council in its discretion.
(Ord. No. 21-02 , § 2, 9-13-2021)
Sec. 40-24. - [Mileage rate.]
The millage rate for the city property tax shall be 3.271. The city property tax must be paid by December 20, 2021. Pursuant to O.C.G.A. § 48-2-40 et seq., the city shall assess interest and penalties as prescribed by law.
(Ord. No. 21-02 , § 3, 9-13-2021)
Sec. 40-25. - [Payment.]
The city property tax shall be paid in one lump sum by the due date specified above; however, the city council authorizes the voluntary payment of city property taxes prior to the time when due.
(Ord. No. 21-02 , § 4, 9-13-2021)
