ARTICLE XII. - INSURANCE BUSINESSES

Sec. 10-499. - Company and agency reports.
(a)
Every insurance company doing business within the city shall file with the community affairs department, on forms prescribed by the city, a report showing the names and addresses of its agents representing such company in the city; the location and person in charge of each business location within the city operated and maintained by such company; the classes of insurance written; and such other reasonable information as may be required, and, in addition, shall furnish complete information regarding the premium received, by class, from policies written on risks residing or located within the city. Such reports shall be made over affidavit of an officer of such company. It is hereby declared to be a violation of this Code for any person, including any agents, to knowingly give false or incomplete information on any such report. This report shall be filed at the time of paying the premium tax.
(b)
The person responsible for each insurance business location shall file with the community affairs department a report, on forms prescribed by the city, showing the address of the business location; the class or classes of insurance written; the names of the persons writing insurance at the location; the names of the companies represented; and such other reasonable information as may be required. This report shall be made over affidavit of the person in charge of that business location. It is hereby declared to be a violation of this Code for any person, including agents, to knowingly give false or incomplete information on any such report. This report shall be filed at the time of paying the premium tax.
(Code 1976, § 7-1009; Code 1985, § 6-30)
Sec. 10-500. - Insurer license fees.
The city has levied for each year, an annual license fee of $50.00 upon each insurer doing business within the city. For each separate business location in excess of one not covered by section 10-501, which is operating on behalf of insurers within the city, the city has levied a license fee in the amount as provided in the fee schedule of the issuing department. For the purposes of this article, the term "insurer" means company which is authorized to transact business in any of the classes of insurance designated in O.C.G.A. § 33-3-5.
(Code 1976, § 3-2031; Code 1985, § 6-31; Ord. of 12-12-1983; Ord. of 8-6-1984, § 1; Ord. of 4-6-1987; Ord. of 2-1-1999 § 1(7))
Sec. 10-501. - License fee for insurance certain risks at additional business locations.
For each separate business location, not otherwise subject to a license fee hereunder, operated and maintained by a business organization which is engaged in the business of lending money or transacting sales involving term financing and in connection with such loans or sales, offers, solicits or takes application for insurance through a licensed agent of an insurer for insurance, the insurer shall pay an additional license fee per location in the amount provided in the fee schedule of the issuing department for the year 1987.
(Code 1976, § 3-2032; Code 1985, § 6-32; Ord. of 12-12-1983; Ord. of 8-6-1984, § 2; Ord. of 4-6-1987)
Sec. 10-502. - Insurance agency license fees; independent insurance agencies, brokers, etc., not otherwise licensed.
The city has levied for the year 1987 and for each year thereafter on independent agencies and brokers an annual license fee per location in the amount provided in the fee schedule of the issuing department from which an insurance business is conducted and which is not subject to the company license fee imposed by section 10-500.
(Code 1976, § 3-2033; Code 1985, § 6-33; Ord. of 12-12-1983; Ord. of 8-6-1984, § 3; Ord. of 4-6-1987)
Sec. 10-503. - Gross premiums tax imposed on life insurance.
The city has levied for each year an annual tax based solely upon gross direct premiums upon each insurer writing life, accident and sickness insurance within the city in an amount equal to one percent of the gross direct premiums received during the calendar year in accordance with O.C.G.A. § 33-8-8.1. The term "gross direct premiums," as used in this section, means gross direct premiums as used in O.C.G.A. § 33-8-4. The premium tax levied by this section is in addition to the license fees imposed by section 10-500.
(Code 1976, § 3-2034; Code 1985, § 6-34; Ord. of 12-12-1983)
Sec. 10-504. - Gross premiums tax on all other insurers.
The city has levied for each year an annual tax upon each insurer, other than an insurer transacting business in the class of insurance designated in O.C.G.A. § 33-3-5(1), doing business within the city in an amount equal to two and one-half percent of the gross direct premiums received during the calendar year, in accordance with O.C.G.A. § 33-8-8.2. The term "gross direct premiums," as used in this section, means gross direct premiums as used in O.C.G.A. § 33-8-8.2(a).
(Code 1976, § 3-2035; Code 1985, § 6-35; Ord. of 12-12-1983)
Sec. 10-505. - Due date for license fees.
License fees imposed in sections 10-500 through 10-502 shall be due and payable on February 15 of each year.
(Code 1976, § 3-2036; Code 1985, § 6-36; Ord. of 12-12-1983)
Sec. 10-506. - Additional insurance license fee provisions.
The standard industrial classification business license schedule adopted from time to time by the city council shall amend the fees set forth in the previous sections dealing with insurance licenses.
(Code 1985, § 6-38; Ord. of 4-6-1987)
