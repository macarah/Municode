Chapter 20 - MANUFACTURED HOMES[1]
ARTICLE I. - IN GENERAL

Secs. 20-1—20-18. - Reserved.
ARTICLE II. - PERMITS AND DECALS

Sec. 20-19. - Location permit required.
The owner of any mobile home now located in the county or any owner of a mobile home desiring to locate such mobile home in the county shall obtain from the county tax commissioner a permit authorizing the location of such mobile home within the respective confines of the county when the owner thereof is using or intends to use such mobile home as a residence for himself or others for any other purpose.
(Code 2003, § 30-31; Res. of 3-7-1972)
Sec. 20-20. - Permit for relocation.
The owner of a mobile home used as a residence may not relocate such mobile home within the confines of the county or any other county without first obtaining a permit from the county tax commissioner.
(Code 2003, § 30-32; Res. of 3-7-1972)
Sec. 20-21. - Payment of local taxes.
The tax commissioner shall ascertain that all applicable local taxes have been paid before any permit provided in this article shall be issued. The owner shall submit proof that all applicable municipal taxes have been paid before such permit will be issued.
(Code 2003, § 30-33; Res. of 3-7-1972)
Sec. 20-22. - Issuance of permit and decals.
No mobile home shall be moved into the county or from one location to another in the county without having a current year's decal. To receive an appropriate decal, the owner of any mobile home shall comply with O.C.G.A. §§ 48-5-492—48-5-495 and all requirements of these four sections must be met before the county will consider the mobile home as being in compliance with the decal regulations.
(Code 2003, § 30-34; Res. of 3-7-1972)
Sec. 20-23. - Display of decals.
All decals issued under this article shall be affixed to all mobile homes in such manner as to be easily visible for inspection.
(Code 2003, § 30-35; Res. of 3-7-1972)
Sec. 20-24. - Fees imposed.
A fee, as set forth in the schedule of fees and charges on file in the office of the county clerk, shall be charged for the issuance of a permit under this article and for the annual renewal of such permits authorizing the location of a mobile home within the confines of the county.
(Code 2003, § 30-36; Res. of 3-7-1972)
Sec. 20-25. - Penalty for violation.
Any person failing to comply with the provisions or violating the provisions of this article shall be punished as provided in section 1-19. The county tax commissioner shall be duly authorized to assess and collect any penalty that may accrue under this article.
(Code 2003, § 30-37; Res. of 3-7-1972)
Sec. 20-26. - When fees due.
All fees pursuant to this article shall be due on or before April 1 of each year.
(Code 2003, § 30-38; Res. of 3-7-1972)
