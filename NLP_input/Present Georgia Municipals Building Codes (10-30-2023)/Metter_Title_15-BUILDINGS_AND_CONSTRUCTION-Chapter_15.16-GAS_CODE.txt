Chapter 15.16 - GAS CODE
Sections:

15.16.010 - Adopted.
There is adopted by and for the city the Standard Gas Code, 1991 Edition including Chapter 1, recommended by the Southern Building Code Congress with revisions as adopted by the Georgia Department of Community Affairs, which is incorporated in this section, and made a part hereof for all purposes, a copy of which is filed in the office of the city clerk.
