ARTICLE VI. - NATURAL GAS UTILITY

Sec. 94-301. - Service regulations.
All residential, commercial, and special customers who are furnished natural gas by the city as licensee of a state public service commission certified natural gas marketer shall pay such rates, charges, and deposits and be governed by the same service rules as pertain to customers who receive water, electric or sewer services from the city as stated in article II of this chapter, except as regulated by the state public service commission or under contract with a certified natural gas marketer, in which event the commission's regulations or the contract provisions shall govern.
(Ord. No. 672, § 1, 6-14-1999)
