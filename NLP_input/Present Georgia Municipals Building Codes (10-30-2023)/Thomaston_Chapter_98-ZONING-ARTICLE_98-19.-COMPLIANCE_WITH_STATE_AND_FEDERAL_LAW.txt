ARTICLE 98-19. - COMPLIANCE WITH STATE AND FEDERAL LAW

Sec. 98-19.1. - Compliance with state and federal law.
No provision set forth in this chapter is intended to preempt any state or federal laws or regulations. Should any provision set forth herein be in conflict with any state or federal laws or regulations, the state or federal law and regulations shall prevail and be controlling.
