Chapter 5.24 - CHARITABLE ORGANIZATIONS
Sections:

5.24.010 - Authority to solicit contributions.
Charitable organizations registered in accordance with Section 43-17-5 of the Official Code of Georgia Annotated and such organizations exempt from registration in accordance with O.C.G.A., Section 43-17-9 are authorized to solicit contributions on the streets and highways within the city, subject to compliance with the provisions of this chapter.
(Ord. 97-11 § 1)
5.24.020 - Permit required.
Charitable organizations seeking a permit pursuant to this chapter shall file an application, in the form provided by the city, with the city clerk. Such application shall include the name, address and telephone number of the organization, the offices of the organization, the date(s), time(s), and place(s) of the proposed solicitation and must include an agreement by the organization to hold harmless and indemnity the city from liability for damage or injury arising out of the permitted solicitation. Such application must be executed by a duly authorized office of said organization. Such organization must provide a copy of the declaration page of a liability insurance policy which provides liability insurance coverage, with limits of at least one million dollars ($1,000,000.00) per occurrence for the permitted solicitation to the city. Such policy shall include an endorsement listing the city as a named insured.
Solicitation for contributions on the streets and highways within the city without a permit shall be punished as provided in this code.
(Ord. 97-11 §§ 2, 3)
