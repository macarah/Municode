ARTICLE I. - IN GENERAL

Sec. 110-1. - One principle structure per lot.
No more than one single-family or two-family dwelling unit shall be constructed on any lot, tract, or parcel of land. Each distinct business use shall occupy a separate lot, excepting strip centers, malls, or buildings containing more than one business.
(Ord. of 2-3-2015, § 8.1)
Sec. 110-2. - Plat required.
After the effective date of this subpart, no person shall subdivide land within Long County and the City of Ludowici before the filing of, and approval by the governing authority of, preliminary and final plats, as required by this subpart.
(Ord. of 2-3-2015, § 8.2)
