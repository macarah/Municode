Sec. 2-2-301. - Regular meetings—Day and time.
The regular monthly meeting of the board of commissioners will be held on the third Tuesday of each month beginning at 7:00 p.m. and continuing until all business of the board is concluded.
The board may find it necessary to change the day or time of said meeting for good reason at which time the meeting date and time will be announced as specified according to the open meetings laws of the state.
(Res. of 8-2-88, § 1; Res. of 7-18-90; Res. of 1-5-99; Mo. of 1-2-01; Res. of 1-7-03; Ord. No. 2009-01, § 2, 3-24-09; Ord. No. 2010-14, § 2, 9-14-10)
Cross reference— Regular meetings of board of commissioners, § 1-2-30.
Sec. 2-2-302. - Same—Persons addressing board; notification of board.
All persons wishing to address the board on matters pertinent to the conduct of government of the county shall notify the clerk/administrator or his/her office before 12:00 noon on the Thursday next preceding the regularly scheduled meeting date to request that time be reserved for them at such meeting on the agenda of the board. The board reserves the right to deny time to any person or group that has not followed the procedure set forth above.
(Res. of 8-2-88, § 2; Res. of 1-7-03)
Sec. 2-2-303. - Parliamentary procedure.
The board of commissioners hereby adopts Robert's Rules of Order as their parliamentary procedure.
(Mo. of 1-2-91)
Sec. 2-2-304. - Reports distributed for meetings.
Administration for the efficiency of the board that the discussions on all the reports be done in writing on a monthly basis and that these reports be placed in the members mailboxes no later than the Friday before the Tuesday meeting at 12:00 p.m. so that each member will have an opportunity to read and prepare for the meeting and can call the county clerk to request that a particular person or department head attend the board meeting to answer any questions as each one of these has other responsibilities and it is proper and fitting for the board to allow them time to do their jobs.
(Mo. of 1-2-91; Mo. of 1-5-93)
Sec. 2-2-305. - Reserved.
Sec. 2-2-306. - Rules for agenda and meetings.
(a)
Agenda.
(1)
The agenda will be closed on the Thursday prior to the Tuesday meeting at noon. This will allow the commissioner's office to have the agenda to the newspaper on Friday for publication.
(2)
Commissioners, constitutional officers and the senior appointed official in the commissioner's office are authorized to place items on the agenda.
(3)
Items may be added to the agenda after the closeout time (see subsection (1) above) with the approval of three (3) of the five (5) commissioners.
(b)
Meetings.
(1)
Robert's Rules of Order will apply as amended by this commission.
(2)
The members of the board of commissioners are all honorable men and women whose only concern is the welfare of our great county. The board will endeavor to maintain civility at all times with each other and with the public.
(3)
Only items on the agenda are allowed to come to a vote by the commission. This rule can be suspended on an individual item by an aye vote of a majority of the commissioners.
(4)
Requests for an unfinanced requirement (UFR) (an item not in a department's budget), will be handled in the following manner:
a.
The request will be submitted to the commission in writing to include the justification of need and suggested department line item reductions to pay for the UFR.
b.
The commission will discuss the request and if the commission finds merit in the request the chair will entertain a motion and a second to pass a first reading of the UFR.
c.
The UFR, upon passage of a first reading, will automatically become an agenda item for the next commission meeting. Upon approval of a second reading, the item will be placed for bid and upon purchase the county and department budget adjusted accordingly.
d.
This rule can be suspended for approval to bid after the first reading in cases of emergencies and the motion and aye vote of all commissioners present.
(Res. of 5-15-01, §§ I, II; Res. No. 04-05, § 2, 8-10-04)
