Sec. 66-1. - Adoption of subdivision regulations.
The ordinance entitled, Land Subdivision Regulations of Commerce, Georgia, adopted in 1983, is hereby incorporated by reference into this Code. Such ordinance shall be on file for public inspection in the office of the city clerk.
(Code 1987, § 21-201)
