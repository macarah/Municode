ARTICLE I. - IN GENERAL

Sec. 8-1. - Authority.
The regulation and regulatory fees provided for in this chapter are imposed pursuant to the general police powers granted to the county and allowed by O.C.G.A. title 48, ch. 13, art. 5 (O.C.G.A. § 48-13-2 et seq.).
(Code 1992, § 6-1; Ord. No. 2010-01, § 1, 5-13-2010; Ord. No. 2013-16, § 1(6-1), 10-24-2013)
