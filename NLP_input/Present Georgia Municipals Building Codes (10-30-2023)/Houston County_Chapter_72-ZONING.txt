Sec. 72-1. - Provisions saved from repeal.
The comprehensive land development regulations for the unincorporated area of the county adopted as an ordinance on March 22, 1971, and referred to in section 8-3021 of the 1976 Code, in addition to sections 8-3022 and 8-3023 of the 1976 Code relating to the notice and charges for rezoning hearings, are not repealed and shall continue in full force and effect pending a revision of the subject matter by the county.
Sec. 72-2. - Board of zoning appeals.
The county board of zoning appeals is hereby created. This board shall have five members, who shall serve for overlapping four-year terms.
(Code 1976, § 8-3001; Min. of 7-20-93(1), § 12)
