ARTICLE II. - BUILDING CODE

Sec. 5-32. - Adopted.
(a)
There is hereby adopted by the city for the purpose of establishing rules and regulations for the construction, alteration, removal, demolition, equipment, use and occupancy, location and maintenance of buildings and structures, including permits and penalties, the International Building Code as adopted by the State of Georgia with Georgia State Amendments and including any chapters pertaining to administration and appendices included in the International Building Code but not adopted by the state, as revised and the whole thereof, and such code is hereby adopted and incorporated as fully as if set out at length in this article, and the provisions thereof shall be controlling in the construction of all buildings and other structures therein contained within the corporate limits of the city. One copy is filed in the office of the building department, and is available for review by the general public upon request.
(b)
In addition to any other requirements established by this section for the issuance of a building permit by virtue of the adoption of the International Building Code, each owner of property for which such building permit is sought will execute a hold harmless and indemnity agreement as specified in section 5-423(c).
(c)
There is hereby also adopted, for the purpose of establishing rules and regulations pertaining to the accessibility of buildings and structures to the disabled, that certain building code known as the Georgia Accessibility Code, as adopted by the state and the whole thereof, and such code is hereby adopted and incorporated as fully as if set out at length in this article, and the provisions thereof shall be controlling in the construction of all buildings and other structures therein contained within the corporate limits of the city. One copy is filed in the office of the building department, and is available for review by the general public upon request.
(Code 1958, § 7-1; Code 1991, § 5-31; Ord. of 3-23-1992, § II(a); Ord. of 10-23-1995, § I; Ord. No. 01302002-1 , § II, 1-30-2002; Ord. No. 07232008-1 , § III, 7-23-2008; Ord. No. 02242020-1 , § 4, 2-24-2020)
State Law reference— International Building Code as mandatory state standard, O.C.G.A. §§ 8-2-20(9)(B)(i)(I), 8-2-25(a), 8-2-28.

Sec. 5-34. - Certificate of occupancy required for mobile home; fee.
Amended by Ordinance of 11-14-2022 (1)
(a)
No person shall locate a mobile home within the city without first obtaining a separate building permit for such mobile home from the chief building official. No fee shall be charged for such building permit.
(b)
No mobile home shall be occupied until the necessary inspections have been completed, and the chief building official shall have issued a certificate of occupancy therefor. The fee for issuing a certificate of occupancy shall be $25.00.
(Code 1958, § 7-5; Code 1991, § 5-33)
