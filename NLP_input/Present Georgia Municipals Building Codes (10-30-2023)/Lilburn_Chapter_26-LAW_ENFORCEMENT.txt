Sec. 26-19. - Visitors.
Any visitor will leave the police station upon the request of the officer in charge, when the officer in charge deems such leave to be necessary. No unauthorized person will be allowed behind any desk in the police station. No person will be allowed in the station house to sell goods or to offer them for sale, or to canvass or solicit for any purpose without written permission from the chief of police. Such permission shall be given upon a showing that there will be compliance with all applicable laws and a further showing that the operation of the police station will not be impaired or disrupted.
(Code 1972, § 2-22(1); Code 2001, § 4-1-2)
