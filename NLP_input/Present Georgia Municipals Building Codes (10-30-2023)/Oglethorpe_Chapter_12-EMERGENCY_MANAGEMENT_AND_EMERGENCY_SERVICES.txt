Sec. 12-1. - Emergency curfew.
The mayor of the city is hereby empowered and authorized to declare a state of emergency in the city when he determines that conditions resulting from acts of god or man warrant emergency actions and procedures.
(1)
The mayor is empowered to call an emergency curfew between the hours he deems necessary to ensure safety and law and order for a period of time not exceeding 72 hours.
(2)
The mayor shall then call a special city council meeting to determine whether the emergency curfew shall be terminated or extended for a longer period of time. Said meeting shall be held no later than 72 hours after the emergency curfew is established.
(Code 1986, § 14-47)
