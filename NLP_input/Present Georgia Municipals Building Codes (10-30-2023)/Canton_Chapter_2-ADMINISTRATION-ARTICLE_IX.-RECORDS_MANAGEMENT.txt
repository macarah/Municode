Sec. 2-438. - Open records.
All municipal records, except those that by order of a state court or by law are prohibited from being open to public inspection, shall be open for personal inspection by any state during the hours of operation of the administrative service herein below prescribed.
Sec. 2-439. - Fees for records and other administrative services.
Fees for photocopies and other media provided to and facsimile transmissions sent or received by city personnel on behalf of and at the request of city residents, as well as fees for other city administrative services, shall be as provided in the city fee schedule.
Sec. 2-440. - Records management policy.
The city council adopts all applicable state law regarding municipal records management plans and records retention. The city clerk shall manage the city's records and is authorized to act for and on behalf of the mayor and city council in directing and coordinating all records management matters under the Georgia Records Act, O.C.G.A. § 50-18-90 et seq., and records retention schedules approved by the state. No record shall be destroyed except as authorized by the state records retention schedule.
