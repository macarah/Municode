ARTICLE IV. - DEPARTMENTS

Sec. 2-207. - Established.
The following city departments are hereby established:
(1)
Police;
(2)
Fire;
(3)
Water and sewer (maintenance and operations);
(4)
Sanitation;
(5)
Streets;
(6)
Transportation;
(7)
Building and development;
(8)
Technology;
(9)
Finance; and
(10)
Planning and zoning.
(Code 1985, § 2-4-4; Code 2002, § 2-211)
