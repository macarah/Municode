ARTICLE VI. - MECHANICAL CODE

Sec. 6-121. - Standard Mechanical Code—Adopted.
The Standard Mechanical Code, to adopt and enforce the latest edition, as adopted and amended by the Georgia Department of Community Affairs.
(Code 1976, § 8-1071; Ord. No. 77-88, 12-29-88; Ord. No. 35-91, § 11, 9-19-91; Ord. No. 41-95, § 8, 9-28-95; Ord. No. 37-01, § 14, 9-6-01)
Sec. 6-122. - Same—Insertions, deletions and changes.
In the Standard Mechanical Code as adopted, the phrase "Construction Board of Adjustments and Appeals" and all provisions related thereto shall be construed to mean the construction board of adjustment and appeals as defined in Division 3 of Article II of Chapter 6 and all provisions therein shall supercede the requirements of the Standard Mechanical Code.
(Code 1976, § 8-1072; Ord. No. 35-91, § 12, 9-19-91; Ord. No. 41-95, § 9, 9-28-95; Ord. No. 37-01, § 15, 9-6-01)
Sec. 6-123. - Permit fees.
The permit fees are as referenced on the fee schedule at City of Cartersville Code, section 17-80.
(Ord. No. 61-99, § 1, 1-7-00; Ord. No. 42-05, § 4, 7-7-05)
