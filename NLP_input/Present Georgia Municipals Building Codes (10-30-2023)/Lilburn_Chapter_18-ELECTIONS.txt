Sec. 18-1. - Election districts.
The area comprising the corporate limits of the city, as the same is now or shall hereafter exist, shall constitute the sole election district of the city.
(Code 2001, § 2-2-31; Ord. of 10-13-1980)
Sec. 18-2. - Polling place.
The polling place within the city shall be the city hall located at 340 Main Street.
(Code 2001, § 2-2-32; Ord. of 10-13-1980; Ord. No. 2017-513, 10-9-2017)
State Law reference— Polling places, O.C.G.A. § 21-2-265.
Sec. 18-3. - Three-day qualifying period for the positions of mayor and city council members.
(a)
The qualifying period for candidates offering for the positions of mayor or city council member in the City of Lilburn shall be held and conducted in accordance with O.C.G.A. § 21-2-132(c)(3)(A), beginning on Monday and concluding on Wednesday of said qualifying period.
(b)
The qualifying fees for the positions of Mayor and City Council Member of the City of Lilburn are hereby established to be three percent of the salary of the position sought.
(Ord. No. 2016-498, §§ 1, 2, 2-8-2016; Ord. No. 2021-567 , 6-14-2021)
