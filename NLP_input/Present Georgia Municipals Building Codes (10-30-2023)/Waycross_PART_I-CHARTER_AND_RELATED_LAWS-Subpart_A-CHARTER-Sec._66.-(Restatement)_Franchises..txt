Sec. 66. - (Restatement) Franchises.
Be it further enacted, that said city commission shall not grant any public franchises to any person, firm or corporation without reserving in said grant the right to tax said franchise, and the right to pass and adopt from time to time such ordinances or regulations, regulating and governing the exercise of such franchises as may be deemed expedient or necessary; and no franchise shall be granted for a period longer than 50 years.
