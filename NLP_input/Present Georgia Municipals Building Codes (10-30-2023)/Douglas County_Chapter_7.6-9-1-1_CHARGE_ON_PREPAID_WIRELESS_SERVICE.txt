Chapter 7.6 - 9-1-1 CHARGE ON PREPAID WIRELESS SERVICE

Sec. 7.6-1. - 9-1-1 charge on prepaid wireless transactions.
In accordance with O.C.G.A. § 46-5-134.2, there is hereby imposed a prepaid wireless 9-1-1 charge as defined by O.C.G.A. § 46-5-134.2(a)(4) upon every prepaid wireless retail transaction occurring within the jurisdiction of the public service answering point in the amount of seventy-five cents ($0.75).
(Ord. of 10-18-11)
Sec. 7.6-2. - Collection of 9-1-1 charge on prepaid wireless transactions.
Prepaid wireless 9-1-1 charges collected by sellers shall be remitted to the commissioner of the department of revenue at the times and in the manner provided by O.C.G.A. Title 48, Chapter 8, with respect to the sales and use tax imposed on prepaid wireless calling service.
(Ord. of 10-18-11)
Sec. 7.6-3. - Administrative provisions.
The clerk of the county is hereby directed to file with the state revenue commissioner a certified copy of this chapter and amendments thereto, in accordance with O.C.G.A. § 465-134.2(j)(1), within ten (10) days of enactment of this chapter.
(Ord. of 10-18-11)
Sec. 7.6-4. - Depositing of funds; use of funds.
In accordance with O.C.G.A. § 46-5-134.2(j)(5), funds received by this county from charges imposed by this chapter shall be deposited in the emergency telephone system fund maintained by this county pursuant to O.C.G.A. § 46-5-134 and kept separate from general revenue of the jurisdiction; all such funds shall be used exclusively for the purposes authorized by O.C.G.A. § 46-5-134(f).
(Ord. of 10-18-11)
Sec. 7.6-5. - Repealer.
All ordinances and parts of ordinances in conflict with this chapter are hereby prospectively repealed. The county reserves all rights under prior ordinances and laws existing prior to the enactment of this chapter.
(Ord. of 10-18-11)
