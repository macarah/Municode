ARTICLE I. - GENERAL

Sec. 14-1. - Amusement devices; code adopted; penalty for violation.
(a)
The Standard Amusement Device Code, 1985 edition, one copy of which is on file with the office of the city clerk where it shall be available for public inspection, is hereby adopted as the Amusement Device Code of the City of Kennesaw, and all provisions of said code are effective in the city; except such provisions as are in conflict with this Code, or as are modified, amended or deleted by this Code.
(b)
Anyone violating the provisions of the Amusement Device Code shall, upon conviction, be punished as provided in section 1-11 of this Code.
(Ord. No. 1996-4, § 1, 7-15-96; Ord. No. 2007-06, 2-5-07)

Sec. 14-81. - Location restrictions.
(a)
No arcade or amusement center of any type, including but not limited to a pool hall, shall be issued a business license in the city if located within 600 feet of a residence, church, public property, school, or library; provided however, this shall not apply to city, church, or civic club sponsored recreational activities.
(b)
In reaching a decision regarding the location of an establishment referred to in subsection (a) of this section, the mayor and council may take into consideration in its discretion the same factors that are considered in determining the location of alcoholic beverage businesses.
(Code 1986, § 9-6-1; Ord. No. 2007-06, 2-5-07)
