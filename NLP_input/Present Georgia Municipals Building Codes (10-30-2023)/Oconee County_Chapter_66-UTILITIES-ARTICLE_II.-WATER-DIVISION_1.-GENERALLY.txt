DIVISION 1. - GENERALLY

Sec. 66-31. - Water system specifications.
To establish policies, procedures, requirements and standards to implement the Georgia Safe Drinking Water Act (O.C.G.A. § 12-5-170 et seq.), the water system specifications adopted by the board of commissioners dated November 7, 2000, are adopted by reference and shall be controlling within the county as if fully set forth in this Code. Such specifications are on file in the office of the county clerk.
