ARTICLE IX. - OVERLAY DISTRICTS

Sec. 9-1. - Airport overlay district.
A.
Purpose. This overlay district is intended to establish height restrictions within the flight approach zones to the Barrow County Airport.
B.
The maximum height for all buildings and structures within the Airport Overlay District shall be 35 feet. No variances shall be granted from this restriction.
C.
The following uses shall be prohibited:
1.
Macro telecommunications facilities
2.
Telecommunications tower or antenna
