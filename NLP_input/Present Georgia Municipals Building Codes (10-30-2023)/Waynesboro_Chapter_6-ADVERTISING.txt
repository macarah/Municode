ARTICLE II. - HANDBILLS

Sec. 6-26. - Scattering.
No person shall loosely scatter or throw any handbills or other advertising matter upon the surface of the public streets, ways or grounds of the city or within the yards of private residences.
(Code 1974, § 10-12; Code 1995, § 6-1)
State Law reference— Litter Control Law, O.C.G.A. § 16-7-40 et seq.; littering highways, O.C.G.A. § 40-6-249.
Sec. 6-27. - Posting.
No person shall cause advertisements, handbills or political posters to be posted on any public property or utility poles. No person shall allow any such posting on any wall, post or other private premises in such manner that they may be loosened or blown down and scattered by the wind; nor shall such posting be done without the consent of the property owner.
(Code 1974, § 10-13; Code 1995, § 6-2)
