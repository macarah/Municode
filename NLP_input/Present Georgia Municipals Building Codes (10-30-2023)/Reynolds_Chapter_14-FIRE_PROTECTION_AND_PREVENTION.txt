ARTICLE I. - IN GENERAL

Sec. 14-1. - Fire department to answer calls in city and area served with city water.
The fire department of the city is hereby authorized and directed to answer all calls within the city and all fire calls within the area served by city water.
(Code 1973, § 7-1)
Sec. 14-2. - Charge for calls outside city but within one mile radius.
There shall be a charge for each fire call answered by the city fire truck outside the city. The fee shall be in an amount as established by the mayor and council from time to time.
(Code 1973, § 7-2)
Sec. 14-3. - Damaging fire plugs.
It shall be a misdemeanor for any person or persons to hit, knock down or otherwise injure a fire plug, either from carelessness or otherwise, and the violator shall be subject to a fine of at least an amount that it will cost to replace same as a minimum fine.
(Code 1973, § 7-3)
Sec. 14-4. - Compensation of volunteer department.
The city shall pay the volunteer fire department for all drills and for all fires. The compensation for drills and fires shall be in amounts as established by the mayor and council from time to time.
(Code 1973, § 7-4)
Secs. 14-5—14-15. - Reserved.
ARTICLE II. - FIRE PREVENTION

Sec. 14-16. - Fire prevention code adopted.
There is hereby adopted for the purpose of establishing rules and regulations for the prevention of fire, that certain code known as the "Rules and Regulations for the State Minimum Fire Safety Standards" (Ga. Comp. Rules & Regs. Chapter 120-3-3, effective January 1, 2015, as amended) as promulgated by the state safety fire commissioner to establish the state's minimum fire safety standards pursuant to O.C.G.A. § 25-2-4, and the whole thereof save and except such portions as are hereafter debted, modified or amended, a copy of which code is attached hereto and made a part hereof, and said code is hereby adopted and incorporated as fully as if set out at length herein.
(Code 1973, § 7-11)
