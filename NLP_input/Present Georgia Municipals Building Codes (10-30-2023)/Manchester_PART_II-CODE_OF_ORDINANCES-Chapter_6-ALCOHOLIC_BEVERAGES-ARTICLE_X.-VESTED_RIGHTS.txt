Editor's note— An ordinance of December 13, 2021 , § 1, renumbered the former article IX, Vested Rights, as article X.

Sec. 6-201. - Vested right status for existing establishments.
No establishment, by virtue of being in operation prior to June 15, 2004, shall be allowed to sell distilled spirits by the drink without meeting all requirements of this chapter.
(Ord. of 11-16-2010)
