Sec. 5-44. - Supplementary to other laws.
In interpreting and applying the provisions of the plumbing code adopted in this article, they shall be held to be the minimum requirements for the promotion of public health, safety and general welfare, and as supplementary to and cooperative with all other laws, codes or regulations of the state, county or city, formulated in the interest of public health, safety or general welfare; and where the plumbing code imposes a greater restriction than is imposed by other laws, codes or regulations, the plumbing code shall govern, but where any other laws, ordinances, rules or regulations impose greater restrictions than are required by the plumbing code, such other provision shall govern.
(Code 1968, § 19-3)

Sec. 5-46. - Interpretation.
The building inspector, acting as plumbing inspector, is hereby given authority to interpret all of the rules in this article and the plumbing code adopted herein. The construction made by the plumbing inspector shall have the legal effect of, and shall be enforceable as, an administrative determination of the legislative intent.
(Code 1968, § 19-5)
Sec. 5-47. - Attestation, filing of copies.
One (1) copy of the plumbing code adopted in this article shall be on file in the building inspection office.
(Code 1968, § 19-2; Ord. ( W-1-20 ), § VII, of 4-7-20)
Sec. 5-48. - Amendments.
The plumbing code adopted in this article is hereby amended in the particular provided in this article.
(Code 1968, § 19-6)
Sec. 5-49. - Penalties.
Section 106.3(a) of the plumbing code adopted in this article is hereby amended to read as follows:
"A person who shall violate a provision of this code or fails to comply therewith or with any of the requirements thereof, upon conviction thereof in the recorder's court, shall be punished as provided in section 1-8 of the Code of Ordinances of the city. Where any offense continues from day to day, each day's continuance thereof shall be deemed a separate offense."
(Code 1968, § 19-6)
Sec. 5-50. - Low-flow plumbing regulations—Definitions.
[Unless context indicates otherwise, the following words and phrases as used in sections 5-50—5-55 shall have the following meanings respectively ascribed to them:]
Commercial means any type of building other than residential.
Construction means the erection of a new building or the alteration of an existing building in connection with its repair or renovation or in connection with making an addition to an existing building and shall include the replacement of a malfunctioning, unserviceable, or obsolete faucet, showerhead, toilet, or urinal in an existing building.
Residential means any building or unit of a building intended for occupancy as a dwelling but shall not include a hotel or motel.
(Ord. (W-3-91) of 3-5-91)
