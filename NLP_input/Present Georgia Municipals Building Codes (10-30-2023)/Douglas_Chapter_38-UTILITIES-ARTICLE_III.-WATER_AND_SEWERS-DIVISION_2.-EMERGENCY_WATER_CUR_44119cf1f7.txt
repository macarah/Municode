DIVISION 2. - EMERGENCY WATER CURTAILMENT PROCEDURE

Sec. 38-87. - Intent.
The city and its service area are subject to a limited supply of water utilized for residential, commercial and industrial purposes; and in order to protect the health, safety and welfare of the citizens of the city and those persons utilizing the water system, it becomes necessary at times of drought and/or other emergencies to curtail use of water by various classes of customers of the city water department in order to provide sufficient water for residential purposes. Therefore, the commission adopts an emergency water curtailment plan as put forth in this division.
(Code 1978, § 23-70; Code 1993, pt. II, § 30-76)
Sec. 38-88. - Authority.
The city manager and/or the director of the water department are hereby authorized to institute curtailment and/or discontinuance of service as required to ensure the delivery of water to residential users of water for home usage and food preparation purposes.
(Code 1978, § 23-71; Code 1993, pt. II, § 30-77)
Sec. 38-89. - Sequence.
The sequence of curtailment or discontinuance of use of the water system is hereby established as follows:
(1)
Request to users for voluntary curtailment.
(2)
Discontinuance of use for recreational, yards, gardens and vehicles.
(3)
Discontinuance of use by large industrial users of 100,000 gallons per day and above.
(4)
Discontinuance of use by small industrial users of 99,000 gallons per day and below.
(5)
Discontinuance of use by large commercial users of 50,000 gallons per day and above.
(6)
Discontinuance of use by small commercial users of 49,000 gallons per day and below.
(Code 1978, § 23-72; Code 1993, pt. II, § 30-78)
Sec. 38-90. - Nondiscontinuance of water use for certain users.
The following class of users of the water system may, under extreme circumstances, be required to curtail the use of water, but shall not be required to discontinue such use for food preparation purposes and emergency use:
(1)
Hospitals.
(2)
Nursing/convalescent homes.
(3)
Pharmacies.
(4)
Doctors' offices.
(5)
Public and private emergency delivery services.
(6)
Public utility services.
(7)
Restaurants.
(Code 1978, § 23-73; Code 1993, pt. II, § 30-79)
Sec. 38-91. - Notification.
The city manager and/or the director of the water system and/or their designated representatives are hereby authorized to notify those users of water requiring curtailment and/or their discontinuance of the use of water by telephone, in person or in writing of the requirement for curtailment or discontinuance of such use of water.
(Code 1978, § 23-74; Code 1993, pt. II, § 30-80)
Sec. 38-92. - Timely compliance required.
The user of water, upon receipt of notification from the city manager and/or the director of the water department or their authorized representative, shall immediately take appropriate steps to curtail or discontinue the use of water as directed by the notification so given. In no event shall the user delay completion of the required action by the notification beyond 30 minutes from the time the notification is received by the user.
(Code 1978, § 23-75; Code 1993, pt. II, § 30-81)
Sec. 38-93. - Resumption of service.
When, in the judgment of the city manager and/or the director of the water department, the resumption of water service to those users of water can be accomplished, those users of the system whose supply of water has been curtailed and/or discontinued shall be notified of such resumption of service.
(Code 1978, § 23-76; Code 1993, pt. II, § 30-82)
Sec. 38-94. - Responsibility of water user upon receipt of resumption notification.
It shall be the responsibility of the user of water upon receipt of resumption notification to take any and all steps necessary to ensure the safety and security of all persons and property and equipment utilizing and supervised by the user of water, within the 30-minute time allotted in section 38-92.
(Code 1978, § 23-77; Code 1993, pt. II, § 30-83)
Sec. 38-95. - When additional time required after resumption notification.
If the user of water service shall require additional preparation time for resumption of water service to the installation served, a responsible party shall immediately notify the director of the water department of such condition and request an extension of time for the resumption of service.
(Code 1978, § 23-78; Code 1993, pt. II, § 30-84)
Sec. 38-96. - Enforcement.
The city manager and/or the director of the water department or their authorized representatives are hereby charged with the enforcement of this division and are hereby authorized to take all steps necessary to ensure compliance with the terms and conditions hereof, including, but not limited to, curtailment or termination of service to the violator and/or issuance of citations for violation hereof.
(Code 1978, § 23-79; Code 1993, pt. II, § 30-85)
Sec. 38-97. - Penalty.
Violation of the provisions of this division shall be punished as provided in section 1-11.
(Code 1978, § 23-80; Code 1993, pt. II, § 30-86)
Secs. 38-98—38-122. - Reserved.
