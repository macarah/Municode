Sec. 2-21. - Procedure for adopting ordinances.
All ordinances adopted by the city council shall be adopted in the following manner:
(1)
The title of a proposed ordinance or the proposed ordinance in full at the council's discretion shall be read by the city administrator or mayor at a meeting of the city council.
(2)
The proposed ordinance, at the council's discretion, may be adopted or rejected at such meeting, or it may be carried over to the next meeting.
(Comp. 1976, § 3-101; Code 1989, § 2-1; Ord. of 4-7-1975(2), §§ 1, 2)
