Chapter 32 - HEALTH REGULATIONS[1]

State Law reference— Local governing authorities and boards of health authorized to adopt rules and regulations governing "tattoo studios," O.C.G.A. § 31-40-1 et seq.

Sec. 32-1. - Adoption of board of health regulations.
The board of commissioners hereby adopts and incorporates by reference the rules and regulations for "Body Tattoo/Body Piercing Establishments and Operators" as adopted by the Morgan County Board of Health on May 9, 2011 (effective June 8, 2011).
(Ord. of 8-2-2011(1), § 4(exh. A))
Sec. 32-2. - Violations.
Any violation of the regulations adopted herein, or of those rules and regulations set forth by the department of community health may be punishable as any other violation of the provisions of this Code.
(Ord. of 8-2-2011(1), § 4(exh. A))
