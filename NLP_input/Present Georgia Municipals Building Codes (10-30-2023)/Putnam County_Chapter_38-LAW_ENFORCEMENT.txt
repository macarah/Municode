Sec. 38-1. - Sheriff's concurrent jurisdiction.
The county sheriff shall be authorized to enforce this Code and all ordinances of the county, concurrently with any other person who is already, or who shall be in the future, authorized by the board of commissioners to enforce this Code and ordinances of the county.
(Res. of 3-6-1998)
State Law reference— Sheriffs, O.C.G.A. § 15-16-1 set seq.
Sec. 38-2. - Solicitor-general's concurrent jurisdiction.
The solicitor-general shall be authorized to prosecute all violations of this Code and all ordinances of the county, concurrently with any other person who is already, or who shall be in the future, authorized by the board of commissioners to prosecute all violations of this Code and ordinances of the county.
(Amend. of 8-16-2011)
Secs. 38-3—38-9. - Reserved.
Sec. 38-10. - Code enforcement officers.
The Putnam County Board of Commissioners shall from time to time, when necessary, hire and appoint code enforcement officer(s), whether part or full time to enforce the provisions of this Code. Said officers shall have the authority to issue and serve citations and to give testimony in any court having jurisdiction of the offense cited.
(Amend. of 4-19-2005)
