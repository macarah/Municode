ARTICLE I. - IN GENERAL

Sec. 82-1. - Uniform rules of the road adopted.
(a)
Pursuant to O.C.G.A. §§ 40-6-372—40-6-376, O.C.G.A. §§ 40-6-2—40-6-395, known as the uniform rules of the road, and the definitions contained in the O.C.G.A. § 40-1-1 are adopted as and for the traffic regulations of the county with like effect as if recited in this section.
(b)
Unless another penalty is expressly provided by state law, every person convicted of a violation of any provision of this section shall be punished in accordance with section 1-12.
Sec. 82-2. - Speed detection devices.
The board of commissioners approve and desire the procurement of and use of appropriate vehicle speed detection devices by the county sheriff's department.
(Res. of 2-12-1985)
