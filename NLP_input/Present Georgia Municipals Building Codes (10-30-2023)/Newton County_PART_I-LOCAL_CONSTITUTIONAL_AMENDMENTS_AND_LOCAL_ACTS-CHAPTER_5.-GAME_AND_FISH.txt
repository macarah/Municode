CHAPTER 5. - GAME AND FISH
ARTICLE I. - IN GENERAL

Sec. 5-101. - Wire fish-baskets.
Be it enacted by the General Assembly of Georgia that it shall be lawful for the citizens of Rockdale and Newton Counties to use wire fish-baskets in the waters and streams of said counties, during the months of July and August of each year, provided that such baskets are constructed of wire of not less than 1 1/2-inch mesh.
(1933 Ga. Laws, p. 654)
Sec. 5-102. - Seining.
Be it further enacted that it shall be lawful and permissible for citizens of said counties to seine in the waters and streams of Rockdale and Newton Counties, during the months of July and August of each year.
(1933 Ga. Laws, p. 654)
