ARTICLE I. - IN GENERAL

Secs. 20-1—20-18. - Reserved.
ARTICLE II. - HISTORICAL SOCIETY

Sec. 20-19. - Objectives.
The Laurens County Historical Society, Inc., is hereby designated as the official agency of the county to act on its behalf in the discovery, procurement, and preservation of historical sites, structures and buildings relating to the history of the United States, in general, and the county, in particular.
(Code 1994, § 50-26; Res. of 7-2-1968, § (1))
Sec. 20-20. - Funding.
The Laurens County Historical Society, Inc., is authorized to receive on behalf of the county such grants of federal and state funds as are available, needful and proper to accomplish the objectives set forth in section 20-19, on such terms and conditions as are prescribed by the particular authorized federal or state agency.
(Code 1994, § 50-27; Res. of 7-2-1968, § (2))
Sec. 20-21. - Liability.
The Laurens County Historical Society, Inc., acting as the official agency of the county for the purposes and objectives set forth in this article, shall not be authorized to incur any indebtedness, liability or obligation against the county.
(Code 1994, § 50-28; Res. of 7-2-1968, § (3))
