ARTICLE I. - IN GENERAL

Sec. 11-1. - Plat review fees.
(a)
Prior to the certification of zoning by the zoning administrator, a site plan must be submitted and approved by the city engineer for any new nonresidential use, or change of use, and for all nonresidential construction including expansions and additions.
(b)
A review and compliance fee of one hundred fifty dollars ($150.00) will be collected and a receipt provided for upon the submission of each site plan to the city engineer and/or the resubmission of each site plan that was not approved.
(Ord. No. 87-21, 5-19-87)
