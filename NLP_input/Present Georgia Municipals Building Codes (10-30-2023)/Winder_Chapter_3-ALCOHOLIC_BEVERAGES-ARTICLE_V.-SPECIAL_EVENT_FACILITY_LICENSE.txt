ARTICLE V. - SPECIAL EVENT FACILITY LICENSE

Sec. 3-130. - Special events license required.
Any owner of a special events facility wishing to sell or offer for sale malt beverages or wines for consumption on the premises shall obtain a license required by this article which shall supplemental to and in addition to any other alcoholic beverage license required by this chapter.
(Ord. (W-10-12) of 9-11-12)
Sec. 3-131. - State law.
No license shall be issued under this article if the granting of such license would constitute a violation of state law or regulations.
(Ord. (W-10-12) of 9-11-12)
Sec. 3-132. - Number of licenses.
No business shall be licensed under this article to any person or entity already holding a maximum of two (2) licenses under this chapter.
(Ord. (W-10-12) of 9-11-12)
Sec. 3-133. - Application requirements.
Alcoholic beverage licenses issued under this article shall only allow the sale of ale, beer, malt beverages and wine for sale for consumption only on the premises of the special events facility for which the license is issued.
(Ord. (W-10-12) of 9-11-12)
Sec. 3-134. - Limitations.
(a)
At any special event at which alcoholic beverages are sold or offered for sale, the license holder shall provide a City of Winder uniformed police officer for safety and security purposes beginning at the time the first drink is poured or served and until fifteen (15) minutes following the close of the event. At least one (1) officer shall be provided for the first two hundred (200) persons in attendance and at least one (1) additional officer for every additional one hundred (100) persons in attendance.
(b)
The City of Winder Fire Chief or his designee, the City of Winder Building Inspector or his designee and the City of Winder Police Chief or his designee shall recommend to the city administrator any additional limitations to the license which they deem necessary to protect public health, safety and welfare for any special events at which alcoholic beverages will be sold or offered for sale. The city administrator shall present any special conditions or limitations to the city council. The city council may incorporate such limitations on the license when granted.
(Ord. (W-10-12) of 9-11-12)
