Sec. 291. - (Restatement) Intent.
The intention of this Act is to authorize and empower the City of Waycross, Georgia, through its governing authority to provide a pension system for employees of such city, and dependents of employees, and to prescribe by ordinance or ordinances the conditions and methods by which the pension system shall be handled, and to prescribe all of the regulations with respect to same not in conflict with the provisions of this Act.
