Editor's note— An ordinance adopted April 9, 2019 , Pt. I, repealed Art. IV in its entirety and enacted new provisions to read as herein set out. Former Art. IV, §§ 13-71 and 13-72 pertained to tourist courts, and derived from Ord. No. 91-01, § 1, adopted Jan. 8, 1991.

Sec. 13-71. - Rules and regulations for tourist accommodations adopted.
All tourist accommodations service providers shall be in compliance with the Rules of the Department of Public Health Chapter 511-6-2, Tourist Accommodations as introduced January 1, 2014, now and as may be amended hereafter, and the same are hereby adopted by reference as if fully set forth herein.
( Ord. of 4-9-19(2) , Pt. 1)
