Sec. 6-1. - County animal control ordinance adopted.
The county animal control ordinance as now or hereafter amended shall apply in the city and such ordinance is hereby adopted by reference. No person shall violate such ordinance.
(Ord. No. 2012-14, 9-10-2012)
