ARTICLE I. - IN GENERAL

Sec. 2-1. - Records management plan.
The city council authorizes and adopts the following records management plan set out in this section. The city administrator is hereby designated as the coordinator of the records management plan and authorized to act for and on behalf of the mayor and council in directing and coordinating all records management matters under the Georgia Records Act and records retention schedules approved by the state. The same records shall be maintained in accordance with approved retention schedules. No record shall be destroyed except as authorized by the state records retention schedule.
(Code 1990, § 2-1; Ord. No. 206, 5-10-1988)
State Law reference— Inspection and copying of public records, O.C.G.A. § 50-18-70 et seq.; records management programs for local governments, O.C.G.A. § 50-18-99.
