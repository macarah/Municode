Sec. 26-1. - Emergency management.
The city shall perform civil defense and emergency management functions in accordance with the Clayton County Emergency Operations Plan adopted March 20, 2001, and the provisions of the mutual aid agreement entered into between the city and the county on February 13, 2003, as said plan and agreement may be amended or modified from time to time.
(Ord. No. 04-12, § 2, 5-17-2004)
Sec. 26-2. - Annual leave plus hazard compensation.
new
(a)
Whenever a national emergency is declared by the President of the United States, a state emergency is declared by the Governor of the State of Georgia, and/or a civil emergency pursuant to section 1.13 of the City Charter is declared by the City of Jonesboro, those workers deemed essential by the city to continue doing their duties during said declared emergency shall receive their regular pay and shall accrue additional annual leave in an amount equal to half of the hours worked during the declared emergency (i.e., an employee deemed essential and working 40 hours during the time of a declared emergency would earn 20 additional hours of annual leave).
(b)
During said declared emergency, each department director shall keep track of the hours worked by the essential employees on a document provided by the city manager and shall report said hours to the city manager at the conclusion of each business week.
(Ord. No. 2020-011 , § 1, 4-13-2020)
