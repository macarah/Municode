ARTICLE V. - MECHANICAL SYSTEMS

Sec. 6-191. - Adoption of mechanical code.
A certain document, one (1) copy of which is on file in the office of the city clerk, being marked and designated as the 2012 International Mechanical Code, is hereby adopted as the mechanical code of the city; all of the regulations, provisions, conditions and terms of this International Mechanical Code are hereby referred to, adopted and made a part of this article as if set forth verbatim herein, with the additions, insertions, deletions and changes as are prescribed in the following section.
(Code 1977, § 9-1350; Ord. No. 43-89, § 4, 8-21-89; Ord. No. 45-91, § 3, 8-19-91; Ord. No. 52-93, § 1, 10-4-93; Ord. No. 62-04, 12-20-04; Memo. of 7-12-11; Ord. No. 07-14, § 1, 3-17-14)
Editor's note— The user's attention is directed to the editor's note at the title of this chapter wherein the city's intention of enforcing the latest edition of the International Mechanical Code is set forth.
Charter reference— Adoption of technical codes by reference, § 2.26.
State Law reference— Buildings and housing, § 8-2-20 et seq.
Sec. 6-192. - Additions, insertions, deletions and changes.
(a)
Wherever the words "mechanical official" are used in the code they shall mean the "building official."
(b)
The words "administrative authority" and the words "local governing body" as used in this code shall mean the "mayor and council of the City of Warner Robins."
(c)
The words "department of law" as used in the code shall mean the "city attorney."
(d)
The words "mechanical inspection department" shall mean the "building inspection department."
(Code 1977, § 9-1351)
