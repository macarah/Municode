ARTICLE VII. - ART SHOP ESTABLISHMENTS

Sec. 6-166. - Art shop businesses.
For the purposes of this article, an art shop is defined in section 6-1 of this chapter. Art shops that allow consumption of alcohol on the premises are subject to the provisions of this article. Such establishments may not hold a license to sell and may not sell or serve any alcoholic beverages.
(Ord. No. 692 , pt. III, 11-17-15; Ord. No. 754 , pt. XXIII, 10-16-18)
Sec. 6-167. - License required; compliance with applicable regulations.
It shall be unlawful to operate an establishment governed under the provisions of this article without first obtaining a license from the finance director and paying such fees and taxes as may be required by the ordinances, rules and regulations of the city.
(Ord. No. 692 , pt. III, 11-17-15; Ord. No. 801 , pt. XXVII, 9-21-21)
Sec. 6-168. - Licensed art shop hours of alcohol consumption; required closing time.
(a)
Alcohol may be consumed on the premises of a licensed art shop only during the following hours:
(1)
Monday through Saturday hours are from 9:00 a.m. until 11:59 p.m.
(2)
Sunday hours are from 12:30 p.m. until 11:59 p.m.
(b)
Licensed art shops must close their premises to the public and clear their premises of patrons by 12:30 a.m.
(Ord. No. 692 , pt. III, 11-17-15; Ord. No. 754 , pt. XXIV, 10-16-18)
Sec. 6-169. - Art shop activities.
(a)
An art shop licensed under this section may allow customers to bring in a bottle or bottles of wine and beer to be consumed on the premises. The customer must remove any unconsumed wine or beer from the premises.
(b)
An art shop shall not store, keep, or handle any wine or beer belonging to the customer, and no owner, employee or agent of the art shop shall pour or have any contact with the wine or beer.
(c)
No customer of an art shop shall consume more than two, six ounce servings of wine or two, 12 ounce servings of beer during a three-hour period or four, six ounce servings of wine or four, 12 ounce servings of beer within a single business day.
(d)
The brown-bagging of wine and/or beer in an art shop shall be subject to this Code and to state law, and no one who is under 21 years of age or intoxicated may consume wine or beer in an art shop.
(e)
Nothing in this section shall prohibit an art shop from serving food provided it meets all of the requirements provided in this City Code and is properly permitted by the city.
(Ord. No. 692 , pt. III, 11-17-15)
