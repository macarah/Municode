DIVISION 2. - CANDIDATES

Sec. 10-19. - Qualification fees.
The qualification fee for candidates filing a notice of candidacy for the city council in any general or special election shall be three percent of the candidate's annual salary for the office of city councilmember, which fee shall be paid to the municipal superintendent at the time of filing such notice of candidacy. The qualification fee for candidates filing a notice of candidacy for the office of mayor in any general or special election shall be three percent of the candidate's annual salary for the office of mayor, which fee shall be paid to the municipal superintendent at the time of filing such notice of candidacy.
(Code 1985, § 2-302)
State Law reference— Candidate qualification fee, O.C.G.A. § 21-2-131.

DIVISION 3. - VOTING

Sec. 10-42. - Election districts.
The area comprising the corporate limits of the city, as the same is now or shall hereafter exist, shall constitute the sole election district of the city.
(Code 1985, § 2-402)
Sec. 10-43. - Polling places.
The polling place within the city shall be the city hall.
(Code 1985, § 2-403)
