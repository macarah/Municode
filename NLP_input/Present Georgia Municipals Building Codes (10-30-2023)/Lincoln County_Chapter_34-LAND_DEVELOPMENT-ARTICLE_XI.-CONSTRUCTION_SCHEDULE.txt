Sec. 34-341. - Prior approval.
No construction activity of any kind including grading, installation of improvements, and building shall begin on any land subject to these regulations without prior approval of the preliminary plat by the planning commission.
(Ord. No. 48, § 12-1, 1-9-2003)
Sec. 34-342. - Grading.
Grading operations may begin following approval of the preliminary plat.
(Ord. No. 48, § 12-2, 1-9-2003)
Sec. 34-343. - Utilities.
Utility installation shall not occur until the director has approved the rough grade of the street and shoulder preparation.
(Ord. No. 48, § 12-3, 1-9-2003)
Cross reference— Utilities, ch. 70.
Sec. 34-344. - Inspections.
Periodic inspection during the installation of the physical improvements in a subdivision shall be made by the director to ensure conformity with the approved plans and specifications. The subdivider shall notify the director when each phase of the installation is completed and ready for inspection.
(Ord. No. 48, § 12-4, 1-9-2003)
Sec. 34-345. - Sale and transfer.
No lot or parcel of land shall be sold or transferred or a building permit issued until the final plat, of which such lot or parcel is a part, shall have been approved and recorded as provided for in this chapter.
(Ord. No. 48, § 12-5, 1-9-2003)
Sec. 34-346. - Building permit.
The director shall not issue any permit for the erection of any building or structure to be located in any subdivision, a plat whereof is required to be recorded pursuant to the provisions of this chapter, until such plat shall have been admitted to record as provided for in this chapter.
(Ord. No. 48, § 12-6, 1-9-2003)
Cross reference— Buildings and building regulations, ch. 18.
Sec. 34-347. - Occupancy.
No dwelling within the county may be occupied for dwelling purposes until all required utility installations, including water supply and sanitary sewer systems, have been completed to the satisfaction of the county health department and the director.
(Ord. No. 48, § 12-7, 1-9-2003)
Secs. 34-348—34-380. - Reserved.
