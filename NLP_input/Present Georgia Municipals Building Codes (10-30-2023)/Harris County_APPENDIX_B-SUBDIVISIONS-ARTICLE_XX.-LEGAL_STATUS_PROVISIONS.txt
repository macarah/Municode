ARTICLE XX. - LEGAL STATUS PROVISIONS

Sec. 2001. - Separability.
Should any section or provision of this resolution be declared invalid or unconstitutional by any court of competent jurisdiction, such declaration shall not affect the validity of the resolution as a whole or part thereof which is not specifically declared to be invalid or unconstitutional.
Sec. 2002. - Conflict with other regulations.
Whenever the provisions of this resolution and those of some other ordinance, resolution, or statute apply to the same subject matter, that ordinance or resolution requiring the highest, or more strict, standard shall govern.
Sec. 2003. - Legal form and sufficiency.
This document should be approved as to its legal form and sufficiency by the legal counsel of the local government prior to its adoption.
Sec. 2004. - Effective date.
This resolution shall take effect and be in force from and after the 1st day of its adoption [January 2, 1990], the public welfare demanding it.
