ARTICLE I. - IN GENERAL

Sec. 2-1. - Records management.
(a)
The city clerk or the clerk's designee shall be responsible for the administration of records management activities for the city.
(b)
No record shall be destroyed except in accordance with retention schedules as approved by the state records committee on March 7, 1983, and which are approved by the city.
(c)
A copy of the retention schedules shall remain on file in city hall. Whenever any records are destroyed, a certificate of destruction shall be prepared and filed at city hall indicating the type of records destroyed, the dates covered, and the volumes destroyed.
(Code 1988, § 2-6)
State Law reference— Georgia Records Act, O.C.G.A. § 50-18-90 et seq.; open records, O.C.G.A. § 50-18-70 et seq.
Sec. 2-2. - Fees for use of city auditorium.
The governing body shall designate a committee, which shall fix and determine the charges for use of the city auditorium, such charges to be imposed for each such use.
(Code 1988, § 8-4)
