Sec. 58. - (Revision) Collection of delinquent taxes and fees.
The commission of the City of Waycross, by ordinance, may provide generally for the collection of delinquent taxes or any other revenue due the city under sections 51 through 57 of this Act [Charter] by whatever reasonable means as are not precluded by law. This shall include providing for the dates when the taxes or fees are due; late penalties or interest; issuance and execution of fi. fas.; creation and priority of liens; making delinquent taxes and fees personal debts of the persons required to pay the taxes or fees imposed; revoking city licenses for failure to pay any city taxes for [or] fees; and providing for the assignment or transfer of tax executions.
