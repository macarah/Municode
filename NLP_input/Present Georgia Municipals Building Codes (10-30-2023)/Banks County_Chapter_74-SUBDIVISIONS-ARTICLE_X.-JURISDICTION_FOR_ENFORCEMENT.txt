ARTICLE X. - JURISDICTION FOR ENFORCEMENT

Sec. 74-301. - Boundaries of jurisdictional area.
The boundaries of the area of jurisdiction of this chapter shall be the boundaries of the county, excluding that land which lies within the boundaries of any municipality within the county. The land within these municipalities may be included under this chapter only after legal action is taken by the individual governing bodies of these municipalities to lawfully adopt this chapter.
(Res. No. 2001-08, § 1, 3-13-2001)
