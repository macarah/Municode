ARTICLE I. - IN GENERAL

Sec. 20-1. - Local sales and use tax.
A joint city/county local sales and use tax at the rate of one percent is hereby levied by the governing authority of the city.
(Code 1974, § 2-9; Code 1991, § 2-7)
State Law reference— Joint county and municipal sales and use tax, O.C.G.A. § 48-8-80 et seq.; rate of tax, O.C.G.A. § 48-8-82.
