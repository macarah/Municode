CHAPTER 16.28 - LEGAL STATUS

Sections:
16.28.010 - Conflict with other laws.
When the provisions of this chapter are in conflict with any other ordinance, the most restrictive standards shall apply.
16.28.020 - Severability.
Should any section or provision of this title be declared by a court of law to be unconstitutional or invalid, such declaration shall not affect the validity of the remaining provisions of this title other than the part that is declared unconstitutional or invalid.
16.28.030 - Appeal provisions.
A.
Any appeal of a decision of the community development director, city administrator, public works director, or planning commission shall be made to the City Council by presenting the appeal in writing to the City Clerk within thirty (30) calendar days of the decision. The City Clerk shall immediately forward a copy of the appeal to the City Administrator or other official in charge of scheduling matters for the City Council. An appeal shall be scheduled at the next regular meeting of the City Council if practical, but no later than twenty-one (21) calendar days from the date of the appeal.
B.
Any appeal of a City Council decision related to a preliminary or final plat or a decision regarding the appeal of subsection A. above shall be made by filing a petition for writ of certiorari to the Superior Court of Henry County.
