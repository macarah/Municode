ARTICLE I. - IN GENERAL

Sec. 1-2-1. - Water and sewer authority.
(1)
The county board of commissioners hereby authorizes that the chairman of the water and sewer authority shall be compensated in an amount as determined from time to time by the authority.
(2)
The board further authorizes that the remaining members of the authority shall be compensated in an amount as determined from time to time by the authority.
(3)
The compensation payable to the chairman and members of the authority shall be paid from the funds of the authority.
(Res. of 8-26-03)
