Sec. 70-1. - Uniform rules of the road.
(a)
Adoption by reference. Pursuant to chapter 6, title 40 of O.C.G.A. (O.C.G.A. §§ 40-6-372 through 40-6-376, O.C.G.A. §§ 40-6-1—40-6-395 (except for O.C.G.A. §§ 40-6-393 and 40-6-394), chapter 2, title 40 of O.C.G.A. (O.C.G.A. § 40-2-20) and chapter 5, title 40 of O.C.G.A. (O.C.G.A. § 40-5-20), the Uniform Rules of the Road, and the definitions contained in O.C.G.A. § 40-1-1 are hereby adopted as and for the traffic regulations of this county with like effect as if recited in this chapter.
(b)
Penalties. Unless otherwise provided herein, any person convicted of a violation of any provision of this chapter shall be punished according to the general penalty described in section 1-9.
(Code 2000, § 11-101)

Sec. 70-4. - Public road signs.
(1)
No person shall remove, disfigure, destroy or in any manner interfere with the public's use of public road signs.
(2)
Any person violating this section shall be guilty of a misdemeanor and, upon conviction, shall be subject to a fine in accordance with the schedule set by the board of commissioners or judge.
(Code 2000, § 11-104)
