ARTICLE VI. - MANUFACTURERS

Sec. 4-381. - Permits.
It shall be unlawful to operate an establishment governed under the provisions of this article without first obtaining a license from the finance department and paying such fees and taxes as may be required by the ordinances, rules and regulations of the city.
(Ord. No. O2018-05-17, ยง 4.253, 5-29-2018)
Sec. 4-382. - Types of sales permitted.
(a)
Notwithstanding any other provision of this article, tastings and direct-to-consumer sales, including both on-premises and off-premises sales, from the licensed premises shall be permitted under the manufacturers' license in accordance with state law, as amended from time to time.
(b)
Notwithstanding any other provision of this article, and in addition to on-premises sales and package sales, a licensed brewer or brewpub may furnish or sell growlers directly to consumers in accordance with state law, as amended from time to time.
(Ord. No. O2018-05-17, ยง 4.254, 5-29-2018)
Sec. 4-383. - Hours of sale.
Notwithstanding any other provision of this article, licensed manufacturers may sell or furnish alcohol directly to consumers on Sundays between the hours of 12:30 p.m. and 1:59 a.m., without having to satisfy any food sales requirement.
(Ord. No. O2018-05-17, ยง 4.255, 5-29-2018)
Sec. 4-384. - Farm wineries.
Farm wineries shall be licensed as a manufacturer and shall be permitted to manufacture, operate, and sell alcohol to the full extent permitted under state law, as amended from time to time.
(Ord. No. O2018-05-17, ยง 4.256, 5-29-2018)
