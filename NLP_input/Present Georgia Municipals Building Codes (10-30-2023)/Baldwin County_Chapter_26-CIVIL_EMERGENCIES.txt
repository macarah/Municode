ARTICLE III. - EMERGENCY 911 SERVICE

Sec. 26-61. - Provisions saved from repeal.
Any ordinance or resolution providing for or implementing an emergency 911 system and imposing a fee for such service is not repealed and shall continue in full force and effect pending a revision of the subject matter by the county.
