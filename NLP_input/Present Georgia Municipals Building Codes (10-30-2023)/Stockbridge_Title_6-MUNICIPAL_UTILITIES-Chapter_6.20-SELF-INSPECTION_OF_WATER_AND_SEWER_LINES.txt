Chapter 6.20 - SELF-INSPECTION OF WATER AND SEWER LINES

6.20.010 - Self-inspection of water and sewer lines.
Pursuant to the authority contained in Section 3 of Act 1046 Georgia Laws, subsection (d) of Code Section 8-2-26 of the Official Code of Georgia Annotated, relating to self-inspection of water and sewer projects by master plumbers or utility contractors, shall not be applicable within the city of Stockbridge.
