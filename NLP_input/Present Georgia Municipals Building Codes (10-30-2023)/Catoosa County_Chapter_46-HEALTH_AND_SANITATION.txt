Chapter 46 - HEALTH AND SANITATION[1]

Sec. 46-1. - Health regulations adopted.
(a)
There is adopted by reference the rules and regulations promulgated by the state pertaining to:
(1)
On-site sewage management; and
(2)
Food service rules and regulations.
(b)
Copies of such rules and regulations adopted by this section shall be maintained on file in the office of the county clerk.
