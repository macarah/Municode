ARTICLE II. - FIRE DISTRICT NO. 3[2]

Footnotes:
--- (2) ---
Related laws reference— Fire district no. 1 and fire district no. 2 created, art. II, div. 2.

Sec. 46-26. - Created; boundaries.
There is created within the county, pursuant to the authority of article IX, section II, paragraph VI of the Constitution of the State of Georgia of 1983, a special service district to be known as fire district no. 3 of the county, and more particularly described as follows:
Containing all those lands within the boundaries of the county exclusive of the incorporated area of any municipality, and exclusive of those areas lying within fire district no. 1 of the county as created by Act No. 258, 1965 Ga. Laws, page 2680, and exclusive of those areas within fire district no. 2 of the county, as created by Act No. 258, 1965 Ga. Laws, page 2680 and as subsequently amended from time to time by resolution of the board of commissioners.
(Res. of 12-7-86)
Sec. 46-27. - Ad valorem tax; use of funds.
The board of commissioners shall annually at the time of the setting of all other ad valorem tax millages, or as near thereto as practicable, establish a millage to be levied in fire district no. 3 of the county against all property subject to ad valorem taxes under the then-current laws of the state. The tax commissioner of the county shall collect the monies and pay them over to the county, and all funds levied from the tax shall be accounted for separately by the county and expended exclusively for fire protection purposes in the unincorporated areas of the county including, but not limited to, fire equipment purchases including vehicles, salaries of personnel, training expenses of personnel, and expenses of administration.
(Res. of 12-7-86)
