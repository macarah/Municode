Chapter 26 - CEMETERIES[1]

Sec. 26-1. - Lot prices.
The cost of cemetery lots sold in the three city-owned cemeteries shall be as set by the mayor and city council.
(Code 1982, § 6-1; Ord. of 11-19-1998)
Sec. 26-2. - Lot sale regulations.
(a)
All cemetery lot sales shall be by cash at the time of purchase or by lot sales contract.
(b)
Cemetery lot sales contracts shall be distributed to all funeral directors in the city.
(c)
Funeral directors shall be responsible for having such sales contracts properly executed by the prospective purchasers.
(d)
Upon the execution of a sales contract by a prospective purchaser, such contract shall be returned to the city clerk within seven days from the date of execution of such contract.
(e)
The city clerk is authorized to accept such sales contracts on behalf of the mayor and city council.
(f)
Upon the acceptance of a sales contract by the city clerk, a copy of the accepted contract shall be mailed to the purchaser.
(g)
All payments due under such contracts shall be made to the city by the purchasers.
(Code 1982, § 6-1.1)
Sec. 26-3. - Limit on number of plots to be sold in new sections of Oaklawn Cemetery and Willow Lake Cemetery.
In the new sections of Oaklawn Cemetery and Willow Lake Cemetery, only four grave site plots, measuring 12 feet by 18 feet, will be sold.
(Code 1982, § 6-2)
Sec. 26-4. - Slab and chips inside coping.
On any grave site where coping is installed around the site, the purchaser of the plot shall install a cement slab and chips inside the coping.
(Code 1982, § 6-3)
Sec. 26-5. - Entering cemetery; premises; hours restricted; exceptions.
(a)
It shall be unlawful for any person to enter upon the premises of any cemetery owned by the city between 30 minutes after sunset and 30 minutes before sunrise, violation of a notice posted or exhibited at the main entrance to such cemetery or at any point of entry thereto.
(b)
Provisions of this section shall not apply to:
(1)
Law enforcement officials;
(2)
Government employees on official business;
(3)
Employees of any contractor maintaining the cemeteries under contract with the city; or
(4)
That portion of Murray Road which lies within the boundaries of Oaklawn Cemetery.
(Code 1982, § 6-4)
Sec. 26-6. - Mounds.
No mounds shall be raised upon any grave above the general level of the lot. Unsightly mounds may be removed and the grave resodded at the general level of the lot.
(Code 1982, § 6-5)
Sec. 26-7. - Restrictions in certain sections.
In all sections opened after 1982, within the existing cemeteries, the following restrictions shall apply: No trees, shrubs, hedges, fences or enclosures of any kind will be permitted on grave site plots.
(Code 1982, § 6-6; Res. No. 17-0316-12, 3-16-2017)
