ARTICLE II. - SMOKEFREE ENVIRONMENT[2]

Footnotes:
--- (2) ---
State Law reference— Smoking in public places, O.C.G.A. § 16-12-2.

Sec. 42-31. - Designation of county buildings.
(a)
The public health and interest of the employees of the county and its citizens utilizing the buildings owned by the county shall best be served by designating county buildings as smokefree environments.
(b)
The public buildings owned by the county are designated as smokefree environments, and appropriate notice of such designations shall be placed in or upon county buildings.
(Res. of 1-2-1997)
