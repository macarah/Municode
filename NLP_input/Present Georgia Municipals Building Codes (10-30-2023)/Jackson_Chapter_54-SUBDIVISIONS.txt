Sec. 54-1. - Provisions saved from repeal.
The City of Jackson's subdivision regulations were completely revised on March 4, 2008. A copy of the city's subdivision regulations may be obtained from city hall or at the office of planning and zoning for Butts County.
(Amend. of 3-4-2008)
