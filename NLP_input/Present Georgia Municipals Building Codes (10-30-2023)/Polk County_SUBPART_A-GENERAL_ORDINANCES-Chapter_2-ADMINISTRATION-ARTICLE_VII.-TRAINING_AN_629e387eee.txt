ARTICLE VII. - TRAINING AND TRAVEL

Sec. 2-500. - Purpose.
This policy is designed to provide county employees with guidelines for requesting and documenting the use of county funds for training and/or travel purposes.
(Ord. of 10-14-08)
Sec. 2-501. - Definitions.
[The following words, terms and phrases, when used in this article shall have the meanings ascribed to them in this section, except where the context clearly indicates a different meaning:]
Advance payment. Approved payment made by the county to an employee for planned expenses prior to a training and/or travel event. Payments must be documented with original receipts for all expenditures excluding meals.
Commercial transportation. Any entity providing transportation of people or goods for pay.
Direct payment. Approved payment made by the county directly to vendors for training and/or travel expenses incurred by county employees. Payments must be documented with original receipts for all expenditures excluding meals.
Expenses. Approved expenditures incurred by county employees for training and/or travel relating to an employees job duties or responsibilities. Allowable expenses are limited to registration, tuition, actual lodging cost for standard rooms, meals and tips, mileage, parking fees, commercial transportation, and other expenses as approved by the county manager and/or the board of commissioners.
Lodging. A hotel, motel, inn, apartment, or similar entity that furnishes lodging to the public for pay.
Reimbursement. Approved payment made by the county to an employee for allowable expenses following the completion of training and/or travel that was paid for with personal funds. Payments must be documented with original receipts for all expenditures excluding meals.
Training. A conference, meeting, workshop, seminar, session, convention, or school directly related to an employees job duties or responsibilities.
Travel. A single or multiple-day trip completed as a necessary part of job duties and/or is necessary to attend approved training.
(Ord. of 10-14-08)
Sec. 2-502. - Training and travel approval.
The following qualifications shall be met prior to approval of all employee training and/or travel requests:
(1)
Funds for training must be allocated in current budget.
(2)
Travel is directly related to an employees job duties or responsibilities.
(3)
Travel is a necessary part of job duties and/or is necessary to attend approved training.
(4)
Must have approval from department head.
(5)
Must have approval from county manager.
(6)
Training and/or travel is within the State of Georgia.
(Ord. of 10-14-08)
Sec. 2-503. - Special approval.
Special approval for training and/or travel shall be required from the board of commissioners in the following cases:
(1)
Training and travel requiring an amendment to the current budget.
Approval for training and/or travel shall be required from the county manager or his designee in the following cases:
(2)
Training and travel outside the State of Georgia.
(3)
Other training and travel not authorized by this policy.
(Ord. of 10-14-08; Res. No. 2020-21, § 1, 9-1-2020)
Sec. 2-504. - Allowable expenses.
The following are training and travel expenses that are typically eligible for payment under this policy based upon prior approval:
(1)
Registration, tuition, or related fees.
(2)
Actual lodging cost for standard room.
(3)
Meal costs and tips not to exceed the following per day:
$50.00, Including tip amounts.
*  Breakfast shall not be paid for single day trips and will only be paid for the second and subsequent days of travel.
**  Dinner shall only be paid in the event that travel extends past 8:00 p.m.
(4)
Mileage for the use of an employee's personal vehicle associated with approved training and/or travel at the standard mileage rate issued by the IRS.
(5)
Parking and toll fees.
(6)
Commercial transportation (airplane, train, bus, taxi etc.).
(7)
Fuel for county vehicles.
(8)
Other related or necessary travel expenses with prior approval from the county manager or the board of commissioners.
(Ord. of 10-14-08; Ord. No. 2022-57, § 1, 4-5-2022; Ord. No. 2022-64, § 1, 6-7-2022)
Sec. 2-505. - Non-allowable expenses.
The following expenses shall not be allowed for payment by the county:
(1)
Personal leisure or entertainment activities.
(2)
Alcoholic beverages.
(3)
Between meal snacks or drinks.
(4)
Maintenance or repairs of personal vehicles.
(5)
Fuel cost for personal vehicles.
(6)
Gifts or personal purchases.
(7)
Expenditures for non-authorized persons and/or employees.
(8)
Any other expenses determined by the county manager or the board of commissioners to be non-allowable.
(Ord. of 10-14-08)
Sec. 2-506. - Requests for training and/or travel.
(a)
Requests for training and/or travel shall typically be made at least two weeks prior to the date of training and/or travel within the State of Georgia and at least four weeks prior to the date of training and/or travel outside of the State of Georgia. Training and/or travel request made less than two weeks prior to the date of training and/or travel will be considered on a case-by-case basis.
(b)
Completed travel request forms, available from the finance department, shall be submitted to department heads for departmental review and approval. Following departmental approval, travel request shall be forwarded to the county manager for approval. Travel request forms shall include a detailed training/travel itinerary (i.e. travel schedules and training brochures) and a detailed itemization of expenses associated with the travel request.
(c)
Approved expenses associated with training and/or travel shall be paid by one or more of the following methods with approval by the county manager.
(1)
Reimbursement payment.
(2)
Advance payment. If actual authorized expenses are greater than the amount of an advance payment, then the difference shall be reimbursed to the employee. If such expenses are less than the amount of an advance payment, then the difference shall be immediately paid to the commissioners office.
(3)
Direct payment. If a direct payment is made, and is nonrefundable, the employee may be held liable for reimbursement to the county if the employee fails to attend without valid cause. The board of commissioners or personnel committee shall make a determination in such cases.
(d)
Within five days following training and/or travel, a settlement of expenses must be completed and submitted to the finance department. Original receipts for all qualifying expenses, excluding meals, shall be submitted as part of this settlement.
(Ord. of 10-14-08)
Sec. 2-507. - Travel pay.
(a)
Hourly rate employees.
(1)
Required training. When training is required by the county, an employee shall be paid for actual travel time and actual time spent in training. Sleep and leisure time shall not qualify for pay.
(2)
Optional training. When training is optional (not required by the county, but is approved as job related), an employee shall be paid for the lessor of actual travel and training time or eight hours per day.
(3)
Required travel. When travel is required as part of normal job duties, an employee shall be paid for actual time.
(4)
Overtime.
a.
When training and/or travel are required as outlined above, then such actual hours shall apply toward the accrual of overtime.
b.
A department head shall obtain the approval of the county manager prior to requiring employees to attend training.
(5)
Miscellaneous training. An employee requesting training and/or travel which is neither required or job related may, at the discretion of the department head, be granted leave without pay to attend without the county's participation in associated expenses.
(b)
Salaried employees.
(1)
Required/optional training. When a salaried employee attends required or optional training, the employee shall be paid his or her regular salary. If training is required during an employee's normal off day, the employee may be eligible for comp time off with the approval of both the department head and the county manager.
(2)
Miscellaneous training. A salaried employee requesting to attend training and/or travel that is neither required or job related may, at the discretion of the department head, be granted leave without pay to attend without the county's participation in associated expenses.
(Ord. of 10-14-08)
Sec. 2-508. - Training and travel policy amendment.
This policy may be amended as necessary by a three-fifths majority vote of the board of commissioners.
(Ord. of 10-14-08)
