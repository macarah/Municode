ARTICLE IV. - LAND DEVELOPMENT

Sec. 86-171. - Applicability.
Except where general provisions contained within this article may otherwise conflict with specific provisions contained within the County Code, this article shall provide regulations consistent with nationally recognized practices for erosion control, grading, stormwater management, driveways, and road construction for commercial and residential construction projects.
(Ord. No. 98-138, ยง 1, 11-17-98)
Sec. 86-172. - Adoption; compliance.
Except where such general provisions may otherwise conflict with specific provisions contained within the County Code, the Clayton County Land Development Guidelines, Department of Transportation and Development (1998 Edition), and all amendments thereto, are hereby adopted by reference thereto and incorporated herein as part of the County Code. Accordingly, except where such general construction requirements may otherwise conflict with specific construction requirements contained within the County Code, erosion control, grading, stormwater management, driveways, and road construction for commercial and residential construction projects shall conform in all respects to the minimum requirements set forth in the Clayton County Land Development Guidelines, Department of Transportation and Development (1998 Edition), as may hereafter be amended.
