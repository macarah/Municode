Sec. 86-185. - NAICS 81341—Civic and social organizations, without private bar or restaurant.
modified
The following conditions are assigned in the H-1, H-2, O&I, MX, C-1, C-2 and M-1 districts:
(1)
Must be located on a street having a classification of collector or greater.
(2)
Must be established on a lot having a minimum area of one acre and a minimum frontage of 150 feet.
(Ord. No. 2019-003 , § 1, 2-11-2019; Ord. No. 2019-011 , § 1, 6-3-2019; Ord. No. 2020-006 , § 7, 3-9-2020)
