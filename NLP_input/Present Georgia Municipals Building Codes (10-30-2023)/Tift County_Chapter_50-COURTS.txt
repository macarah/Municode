Editor's note— Pursuant to an order entered March 1, 1977, by Judge W. J. Forehand, Judge of Superior Courts, Tifton Judicial Circuit, rules of practice and procedure were promulgated for the superior court, state court and magistrate court of the county. Such rules relate to criminal matters, extension of time on appeal and the county law library. A copy of the subject order is on file in the office of the clerk of the board of commissioners.
Cross reference— Offenses and miscellaneous provisions, ch. 78.
Related laws references—State court, art. VII; probate court judge, art. IX.
State Law reference— Magistrate courts established, Ga. Const. art. VI, § I, ¶ I; O.C.G.A. § 15-10-1; magistrate courts generally, O.C.G.A. § 15-10-1 et seq.; jurisdiction of municipal courts and magistrate courts to abate nuisances, O.C.G.A. § 41-2-5.
