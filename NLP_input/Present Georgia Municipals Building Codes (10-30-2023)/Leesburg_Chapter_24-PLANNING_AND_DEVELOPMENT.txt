ARTICLE II. - JOINT COUNTY PLANNING COMMISSION

Sec. 24-19. - Created.
There is created a joint planning commission to be known as the Leesburg-Smithville-Lee County Planning Commission. The planning commission shall exercise all powers delegated to it under respective zoning, subdivision and land development ordinances of the City of Leesburg, the City of Smithville, and the county now in effect and which may be adopted in the future, as well as any and all powers delegated to the planning commission under any other ordinance or resolution of the City of Leesburg, the City of Smithville, or the county. Without limitation on the delegation of power established in this section, the Leesburg-Smithville-Lee County Planning Commission shall also have the authority to conduct public hearings, to make recommendations to the governing bodies of the City of Leesburg, the City of Smithville, and the county regarding all matters of zoning and subdivision regulations and land development under the respective ordinances and/or resolutions of each of such governments.
(Code 2003, § 38-31; Res. of 10-1-1996, § 1)
Sec. 24-20. - Composition.
The Leesburg-Smithville-Lee County Planning Commission shall be composed of seven members. The county board of commissioners shall appoint one member from each district established by the act of the general assembly providing for the election of the members of the county board of commissioners for a total of five members from such districts. One member shall be appointed by the Leesburg City Council. One member shall be appointed by the governing body of the City of Smithville.
(Code 2003, § 38-32; Res. of 10-1-1996, § 2)
Sec. 24-21. - Promulgation of rules and regulations.
The members of the planning commission are authorized to promulgate certain rules and regulations governing the operation of the commission, including, but not limited to, the adoption of bylaws, the election of officers, and the conduct of public hearings and procedures as provided in O.C.G.A. § 36-66-1 et seq.
(Code 2003, § 38-33; Res. of 10-1-1996, § 3)
