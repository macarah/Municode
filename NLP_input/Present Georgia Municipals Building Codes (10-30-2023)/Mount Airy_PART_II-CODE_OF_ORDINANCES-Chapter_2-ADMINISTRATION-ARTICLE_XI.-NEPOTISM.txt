ARTICLE XI. - NEPOTISM

Sec. 2-686. - Definitions.
For the purpose of this article, immediate family is defined as any person who is related to an official or employee as husband, wife, father, mother, stepfather, stepmother, grandfather, grandmother, guardian, ward, sister, brother, half-brother, half-sister, son, daughter, grandchild, father-in-law, mother-in-law, son-in-law, daughter-in-law, brother-in-law, sister-in-law, uncle, aunt, nephew or niece.
Sec. 2-687. - Prohibited.
(a)
No official or employee shall employ or advocate for employment of any member of his immediate family in any agency or department over which he exercises jurisdiction or control. No official or employee shall take any action, or refrain from any action, or give any remuneration, tangible or intangible, in exchange for or in consideration of employment of any member of his immediate family by any other town official or employee.
(b)
No two members of the same immediate family may serve simultaneously on the town council. For purposes of this section, town council specifically includes the mayor.
(c)
No person shall be qualified for election to the town council if any member of his immediate family is an officer or department head within the town government.
Sec. 2-688. - Hiring, promotion and transfer.
No person shall be employed, promoted, or transferred to any town department when, as a result, he should or would be supervising or receiving supervision from a member of his immediate family.
