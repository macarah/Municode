ARTICLE V. - ADMINISTRATION

Sec. 22-141. - Zoning administrator appointed; duties.
Amended by Ordinance of 06-12-2023 (2)
The chief building official is hereby appointed as the zoning administrator, and it shall be the zoning administrator's duty to administer and enforce this article.
(Code 1958, § 24-17; Code 1991, § 22-131)
Sec. 22-142. - Sign permits.
Amended by Ordinance of 06-12-2023 (2)
The zoning administrator shall receive applications for the construction of signs, as required by this article. Such applications shall follow the same forms as required for building permits. The zoning administrator shall process such sign applications and shall issue sign permits and sign permit numbers for proposed signs which comply with the requirements of this chapter.
(Code 1958, § 24-17.03; Code 1991, § 22-134)
