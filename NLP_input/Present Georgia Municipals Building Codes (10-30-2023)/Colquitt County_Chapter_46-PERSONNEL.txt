Sec. 46-1. - Provisions not affected by Code.
Nothing in this Code or the ordinance adopting this Code shall affect any ordinance or resolution establishing or amending the personnel policy of the county and all such ordinances or resolutions are hereby recognized as continuing in full force and effect to the same extent as if set out at length in this Code.
