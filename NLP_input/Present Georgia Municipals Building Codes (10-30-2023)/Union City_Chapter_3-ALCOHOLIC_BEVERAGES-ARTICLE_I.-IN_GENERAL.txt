ARTICLE I. - IN GENERAL

Sec. 3-1. - Public drinking prohibited.
It shall be unlawful for any person to consume any malt, vinous or spirituous beverage or any other form of alcoholic beverage, mixed or plain, in or on any of the following places within the city:
(1)
Any street, alley, municipal building or facility, school or auditorium;
(2)
Any public place where games or contests are played, e.g., a baseball park, football field, tennis court and other places where people assemble to engage in or view games or athletic events; and
(3)
Any public parking lot and any parking area incidental to a commercial or industrial enterprise.
(Ord. No. 80-9, 11-4-80)
Cross reference— Streets, sidewalks and other public places, Ch. 12.
