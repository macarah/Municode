Section 1. - Authorization to require telephone companies to divide maintenance fees among subscribers.
Pursuant to the authority of O.C.G.A. § 46-5-133(e)(1), the governing authority of Carroll County is authorized to implement and to exercise the powers conferred by O.C.G.A. § 46-5-133 relating to the authority to require telephone companies to divide maintenance fees for the operation of enhanced emergency telephone number "911" systems among subscribers.
