ARTICLE XVIII. - LEGAL STATUS PROVISIONS

Sec. 181. - Conflict with Other Ordinances.
Portions of other ordinances that conflict with portions of this Ordinance are repealed. Non-conflicting parts of those ordinances remain in effect.
Sec. 182. - Validity.
Should any section or provision of this Ordinance be declared by the courts to be unconstitutional or invalid, that declaration will not affect the validity of the Ordinance as a whole nor any part of it other than the part that was declared to be unconstitutional or invalid.
Sec. 183. - Effective Date.
This Ordinance takes effect on the date of its adoption.
