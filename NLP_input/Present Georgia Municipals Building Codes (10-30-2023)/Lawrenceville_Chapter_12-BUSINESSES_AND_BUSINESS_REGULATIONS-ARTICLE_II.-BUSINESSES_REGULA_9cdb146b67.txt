DIVISION 2. - INSURANCE BUSINESSES

Sec. 12-73. - License and fee.
(a)
License required. Each person, agency, firm or company doing an insurance business within the municipal corporate limits shall be required to obtain a license from the City Clerk in the manner specified in this chapter.
(b)
Fee established. In order to obtain a license, each person, agency, firm or company doing an insurance business shall pay to the City the annual license fee as provided under section 34-2.
(Code 2005, § 32-201; Ord. of 7-11-2011)
