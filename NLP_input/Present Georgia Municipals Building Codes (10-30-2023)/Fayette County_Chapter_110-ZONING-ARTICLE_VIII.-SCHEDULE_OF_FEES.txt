ARTICLE VIII. - SCHEDULE OF FEES

Sec. 110-263. - Fees.
A schedule of fees shall be established from time to time by the board of commissioners. The schedule shall be maintained in the office of the zoning administrator and shall be available to the public. All fees required by this article shall be in addition to any other fees and charges required under other ordinances as enacted by the board of commissioners.
(Code 1992, § 20-10-1; Ord. No. 2010-11, § 1, 12-9-2010)
