Sec. 14-1. - Henry County Animal Control Ordinance.
(a)
The Animal Control Ordinance of Henry County, Georgia, codified as sections 3-4-1 through 3-4-59 (with the exception of section 3-4-52), of the Henry County Code of Ordinances, adopted July 16, 2012, as amended from time to time, is hereby incorporated herein by reference as if fully rewritten herein.
(b)
A copy of the current Animal Control Ordinance of Henry County is attached to Ordinance No. 375 as Exhibit "A" and shall be kept on file with the office of the city clerk and the City of Hampton Police Department.
(Ord. No. 375, § 2, 10-2-12)
Sec. 14-2. - Animal control board (County Code section 3-4-52).
(a)
The mayor and city council may act as the animal control board for the City of Hampton and, if so, shall conduct the hearings provided for in this chapter.
(b)
The mayor and city council may create or establish an animal control board. If created or established the board shall consist of five members. All members shall be appointed by the mayor and city council. The initial terms of members shall be as follows: One member to serve for one year; two members to serve for two years; and two members to serve for three years. Thereafter, all members appointed to the board by the mayor and city council shall be appointed for terms of three years and until their successors are appointed.
(c)
No member of the board shall participate in a hearing on any matter in which such member previously participated in the classification of the dog at issue. In the event of a vacancy during the term of any member by reason of death, resignation, or otherwise, the appointment of a successor by the mayor and city council shall be for the remainder of the unexpired term of such member.
(Ord. No. 375, § 3, 10-2-12)
