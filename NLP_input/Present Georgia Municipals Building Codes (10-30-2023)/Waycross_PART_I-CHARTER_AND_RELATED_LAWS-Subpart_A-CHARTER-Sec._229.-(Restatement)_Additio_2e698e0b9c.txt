Sec. 229. - (Restatement) Additional powers.
The mayor and commission of said city shall have full power and authority to prescribe all such other rules and regulations as may, in their discretion, be necessary to grade, pave, drain and otherwise improve the streets and alleys of said city, and to assess the cost thereof against abutting property, and to enforce by execution, the payment of such assessments and to carry out all the provisions of this Act and all other laws on the subject of street and alley improvements.
