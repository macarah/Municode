Sec. 54-1. - Use of tobacco products prohibited in certain areas of Callaway Stadium.
(a)
The following areas of Callaway Stadium shall be and are hereby declared to be tobacco free areas, and the use of tobacco products in all forms shall be prohibited in the following areas inside Callaway Stadium:
(1)
On the coaching and playing fields.
(2)
Inside all dressing rooms, restrooms, concession stands, and press boxes.
(3)
In all spectator seating areas, whether permanent or temporary.
(b)
The county parks and recreation commission is authorized and directed to place appropriate signs at the entrance and inside Callaway Stadium designating the areas described in subsection (a) of this section to be nontobacco use areas.
(c)
The county parks and recreation commission is further authorized and directed to have announcements made at the beginning of and at other appropriate times during all events conducted at Callaway Stadium informing the participants and spectators of the designation of the areas described in subsection (a) of this section as nontobacco use areas.
(Code 1987, § 10.5-2)
