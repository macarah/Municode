ARTICLE X. - DONATION BOXES

Sec. 18-260. - Definition.
As used herein, the term "donation box" shall be defined as any unattended container, receptacle, or similar device used for soliciting and collecting donations of clothing and/or other salvageable personal property and shall not include any unattended donation box within a building or drop boxes for books and audio visual items of the city library.
(Ord. No. 2014-001, 3-17-14)
Sec. 18-261. - Donation boxes prohibited.
Donation boxes are prohibited in the city. Any donation box existing within the city on the effective date of the ordinance from which this section derived, shall be removed within 30 days of the effective date of this section. Any property owner allowing a donation box to remain on his property in violation of this section shall be punishable by a fine of $250.00 per offense and each day of violation shall be considered a separate offense.
(Ord. No. 2016-005, § II, 7-18-16)
Editor's note— Ord. No. 2016-005, § I, adopted July 18, 2016, repealed §§ 18-261—18-267 and enacted a new § 18-261 as set out herein. Former §§ 18-261—18-267 pertained to donation boxes and derived from Ord. No. 2014-001, adopted March 17, 2014.
