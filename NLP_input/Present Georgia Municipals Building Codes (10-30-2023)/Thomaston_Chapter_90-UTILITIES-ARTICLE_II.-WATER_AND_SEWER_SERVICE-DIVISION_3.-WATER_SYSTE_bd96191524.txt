DIVISION 3. - WATER SYSTEM BACKFLOW PREVENTION

Sec. 90-130. - Adoption of Appendix D of the Standard Plumbing Code.
Appendix D of the Standard Plumbing Code (SBCCI), which is referred to in O.C.G.A. § 8-2-20(9)(B)(i)(V) be, and hereby is, adopted as an ordinance of the city, subject, however, to the following modifications thereof:
(1)
The reference in Appendix D to the "plumbing official" shall be construed to mean the building official and his subordinates as provided for in subsection 18-28(1) of this Code.
(2)
The board of adjustments and appeals created by section 18-56 of this Code shall exercise all of the functions and shall be vested with all of the rights and powers of the control board referred to in section D105 of said appendix.
(Ord. No. 889, § 1, 8-4-98; Ord. No. 1026, § 3, 3-3-09)
Note— Former § 90-60. See editor's note, art. II, div. 2.
Sec. 90-131. - Adoption of water system backflow emergency response plan.
(a)
The document entitled "City of Thomaston, Georgia Water System Back-Flow Prevention by Containment Policy and Procedures," which is dated August 4, 1998, and bears the certification thereon, signed by the city clerk that it is the document referred to in this paragraph of this section be, and hereby is, incorporated herein by this reference, and adopted as an ordinance of the city, as though it was set out herein in full, the original of which shall remain on file in the office of said city clerk and shall be available for inspection by the public.
(b)
The document entitled "City of Thomaston, Georgia Water System Back-Flow Emergency Response Plan," which is dated August 4, 1998, and bears a certification thereon, signed by the city clerk that it is the document referred to in this paragraph of this section be, and hereby is, incorporated herein by this reference, and adopted as a policy of the city, as though it was set out herein in full, the original of which shall remain on file in the office of said city clerk and shall be available for inspection by the public.
(Ord. No. 889, § 1, 8-4-98; Ord. No. 1026, § 3, 3-3-09)
Note— Former § 90-61. See editor's note, art. II, div. 2.
