Sec. 801. - Registration, licensing of vehicles.
Effective January 1, 1987, vehicles shall be registered and licensed to operate for the ensuing calendar year and thereafter in DeKalb County during designated registration periods as provided in Code section 40-2-20.1 of the O.C.G.A.
