ARTICLE IV. - HOUSING CODE

Sec. 10-97. - Adopted.
(a)
The International Residential Code for One- and Two-Family Dwellings, 2006 edition, as amended by the state department of community affairs, is hereby adopted by reference as though it was fully copied herein.
(b)
Any matters in such code which are contrary to existing ordinances of the city shall prevail and ordinances in conflict therewith are repealed.
(Code 1962, § 12A-1; Code 1986, § 5-61; Ord. No. 309, § 1, 11-4-1985; Ord. No. 381, §§ I—IV, 2-6-1990)
Secs. 10-98—10-122. - Reserved.
