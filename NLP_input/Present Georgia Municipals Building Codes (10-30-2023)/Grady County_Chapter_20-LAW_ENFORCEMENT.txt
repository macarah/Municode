Sec. 20-19. - Establishment; supervision, rules.
The board of commissioners, pursuant to O.C.G.A. § 36-8-1 et seq., hereby establishes the county police department and further hereby grants to the county police department all powers and authorities as may be given such department by the laws of the state, as may be directed by the board of commissioners or its designated agent and hereby establishes same as a department within the county and designates the title of the department head as superintendent of public safety, and further hereby directs the county administrator to establish such regulations and rules regarding such department as may be reasonable and necessary.
(Code 1994, § 32-26; Res. of 2-18-1992)
