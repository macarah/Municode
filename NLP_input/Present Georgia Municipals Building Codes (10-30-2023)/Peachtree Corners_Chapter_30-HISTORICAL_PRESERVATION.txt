ARTICLE II. - HISTORICAL RESTORATION AND PRESERVATION BOARD

Sec. 30-19. - Creation; membership; term; duties; city historian.
(a)
There is hereby created the Peachtree Corners Historical Restoration and Preservation Board.
(b)
The board shall consist of five members.
(c)
One member shall be appointed by the mayor and city council for each commissioner district from among the residents of that district and the term of office for each such member shall be two years.
(d)
A city historian shall be appointed by the mayor and city council to serve at the commissioners' pleasure, and such historian shall be an ex officio member of the historical restoration and preservation board.
(e)
The board is hereby charged with the duty of advising the chairperson and mayor and city council on the restoration and preservation of city historical matters and such other duties as the mayor and city council may require.
(Ord. of 7-1-2012, § 58-26)
