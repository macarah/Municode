ARTICLE V. - EMPLOYEE BENEFITS

Sec. 2-214. - Worker's compensation coverage.
While performing their elected duties the board of commissioners, sheriff, probate judge, clerk of court and tax commissioner shall be covered under the workers' compensation laws of the state as stated in O.C.G.A. § 34-9-1.
(Res. of 2-8-2000, § I)
Sec. 2-215. - Volunteers and elected officials covered by the ACCG-Group Self-Insured Workers' Compensation Fund.
The following volunteers and elected officials shall be covered under the workers' compensation laws of the state:
(1)
Volunteer firefighters;
(2)
Volunteer law enforcement personnel that are post certified;
(3)
Volunteer members of emergency management or civil defense organization, emergency medical service or rescue organization;
(4)
Volunteer first responder; and
(5)
Elected county officers and elected members of the county board of commissioners.
(Res. of 10-18-2005, § I)
Sec. 2-216. - Other benefits.
Nothing contained herein shall prevent the county from providing other benefits, such as health insurance and retirement benefits, to employees.
Secs. 2-217—2-238. - Reserved.
