ARTICLE IV. - PEDDLERS, SOLICITORS AND CANVASSERS[3]

Sec. 10-101. - Registration required.
Any person peddling, soliciting or canvassing within the city shall be required to register and obtain an identification card as provided by this article.
(Code 1985, § 8-3-1)
Sec. 10-102. - Temporary business license required.
Any person desiring to peddle, solicit or canvass within the city shall first obtain a temporary business license as required by this chapter.
(Code 1985, § 8-3-2)
Sec. 10-103. - Investigation of applicants.
The chief of police shall have authority to conduct investigations on such applicants including requiring fingerprints and photographs.
(Code 1985, § 8-3-3)
Sec. 10-104. - Exhibition of license.
Each registrant shall at all times while in the city have upon his person the city business license and shall exhibit the same when requested to do so by any law enforcement officer or by any municipal authority and by any person being solicited. Possession of this business license shall not in any way represent an endorsement or approval of any products or project by the city.
(Code 1985, § 8-3-4)
