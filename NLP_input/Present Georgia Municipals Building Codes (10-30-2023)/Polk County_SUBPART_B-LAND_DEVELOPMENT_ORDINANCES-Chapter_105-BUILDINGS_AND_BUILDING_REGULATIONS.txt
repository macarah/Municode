ARTICLE III. - MOVING BUILDINGS

Sec. 105-41. - Restriction on movement, relocation and transportation of structures on public roads.
No person, firm, corporation, partnership, association or other entity shall move, transport or relocate or cause to be moved, transported or relocated any house, dwelling, building or other structure on any public road, street or highway within the unincorporated areas of the county without first obtaining a moving permit from the superintendent of public works.
(Ord. of 10-4-1987, § 1)
Sec. 105-42. - Applicability.
This chapter shall apply to the movement, transportation or relocation of any house, dwelling, building or other structure which has been extracted from its foundation or other permanent location by means of dollies, jacks, or other devices, so as to be made portable, movable or otherwise subject to being transported or hauled, when said structure was not constructed or designed for that purpose; provided, however, that structures as defined herein shall not include manufactured homes as defined by the zoning ordinance and/or O.C.G.A. § 8-2-131(3).
(Ord. of 10-4-1987, § 2)
Sec. 105-43. - Application for permit.
An application for a moving permit, as provided herein, shall be filed with the superintendent of public works at least 48 hours prior to the proposed moving date and shall include:
(1)
The type of load to be moved;
(2)
The exact route and roads to be used;
(3)
The time of day or night when the proposed moving will take place;
(4)
A statement that all public utilities whose facilities may be affected have been notified;
(5)
Any application fee as may be required by a resolution of the board of commissioners of the county;
(6)
Such other information as the superintendent of public works may deem proper.
(Ord. of 10-4-1987, § 3)
Sec. 105-44. - Issuance of permit; adherence to terms thereof.
The superintendent of public works is authorized to issue a moving permit to the applicant and may attach thereto such restrictions and conditions as he may deem necessary to protect the health, safety and welfare of the citizens of the county. The terms, conditions and restrictions of the moving permit shall be strictly adhered to by the mover and no deviation therefrom shall be permitted without the express written permission of the superintendent of public works.
(Ord. of 10-4-1987, § 4)
Sec. 105-45. - Limitation on issuance of moving permit on state highways.
When any part of the movement, transportation or relocation is on a state highway, no moving permit shall be issued by the superintendent of public works unless the applicant has first obtained a state permit for such movement. The provisions of this chapter are in addition to those required by state regulation for its permit.
(Ord. of 10-4-1987, § 5)
Sec. 105-46. - Penalties.
The punishment imposed for any violation of this article section shall not exceed a fine of $1,000.00 or six months' imprisonment or both. See fines as referenced in Subpart A; Chapter 1, Section 1-18, Fine Schedule.
(Ord. No. 2019-01, § 1, 2-5-2019)
