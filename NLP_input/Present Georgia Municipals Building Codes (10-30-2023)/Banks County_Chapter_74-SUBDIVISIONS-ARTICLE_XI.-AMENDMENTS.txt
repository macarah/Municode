ARTICLE XI. - AMENDMENTS

Sec. 74-331. - Hearings; notice.
This chapter may be amended. Before enacting an amendment to this chapter, the planning commission shall hold a public hearing thereon, notice of which shall be published at least 15 days but not more than 45 days prior to such hearing in a newspaper of general circulation in the county.
(Res. No. 2001-08, § 1, 3-13-2001)
