ARTICLE V. - PACKAGE SALE ESTABLISHMENTS

Sec. 4-298. - Hours of operation.
(a)
No holder of a license under this chapter for the package sales of malt beverages or wine for consumption off the premises where sold shall sell, give away or otherwise dispense malt beverages or wine for off-premises consumption between the hours of 12:00 midnight, Saturday, and 6:00 a.m., Monday, and between the hours of 2:00 a.m. and 6:00 a.m. on other days, and shall not permit the place of business to be open for the sale of malt beverages and wine on Sundays, and any other days prohibited by state law.
(b)
No license holder shall sell malt beverages or wine on Christmas Day.
(c)
The sale of malt beverages and wine is allowed on Election Day.
(Code 2011, § 4-298; Ord. No. 80-2, § 80-014, 2-19-1980; Ord. of 8-13-2002(2); Ord. of 11-9-2004)
Sec. 4-299. - Sign with license number and prohibition of consumption on premises to be displayed.
Each person licensed to sell malt beverages and wine for off-premises consumption shall have printed on the front window adjacent to the main entrance of the licensed premises, the inscription "City License No. _____ — Consumption of Beer, Malt Beverages, and Wine on Premises Strictly Prohibited," in uniform letters, not less than three inches and not more than four inches in height.
(Code 2011, § 4-299; Ord. No. 80-2, § 80-016, 2-19-1980)
Sec. 4-300. - Condition of premises.
(a)
All licensed premises shall be kept in clean and in proper sanitary condition and in full compliance with the provisions and regulations governing the conditions of the premises. It shall be unlawful to permit any disturbance of the peace or obscenity, or any lewd, immoral or improper entertainment, conduct, or practices on the premises.
(b)
The fire department shall, upon request of the city clerk, city manager, or city council, inspect each premises and report its findings to the same. All premises licensed hereunder shall conform at all times with all fire regulations of the city.
(Code 2011, § 4-300; Ord. No. 80-2, § 80-017, 2-19-1980)
