ARTICLE II. - POLICE DEPARTMENT

Sec. 58-26. - Provisions incorporated by reference.
The police department is authorized to adopt the police department general orders manual and implement policy changes upon the approval of the chief of police. The chief of police shall apprise the public safety committee whenever such policy changes are initiated. Any policy which the chief of police determines to be a high liability policy and is being significantly modified should be reviewed by the city attorney prior to adoption.
(Ord. No. 2006-18, § 1, 8-7-06)
