Sec. 12-1. - Adoption of state election law.
The city council recognizes the applicability of the state municipal election code as codified in O.C.G.A. § 21-2-1 et seq, to the conduct of all elections held in said city and does hereby incorporate by reference herein all pertinent parts of the state municipal election code as the same now exists and as the same may hereafter be amended.
(Ord. of 8-23-1999)
Sec. 12-2. - Declarations of candidacy.
The filing of declarations of candidacy for all persons seeking election to the city council is hereby supplemented and amended as follows:
(1)
The undersigned, a resident and qualified elector of the _______ district of the city and State of Georgia, does hereby announce that he is a candidate for council member of the _______ district in the forthcoming election to be held and conducted in the city and I further declare that if elected to such office that I will qualify and serve by taking the required oath or affirmance of office.
(2)
I further declare that I am not indebted to the city for any overdue indebtedness of any nature whatsoever.
(3)
I further declare that if elected to the office which I am seeking that I will not during the term of my office own any indebtedness to the city which would be classified as past due or delinquent.
(4)
At the end of each supplemental notice of candidacy, each candidate must sign the following statement: "I understand that any false statement knowingly made by me in this supplemental notice of candidacy and affidavit will subject me to criminal penalties as provided by law."
(Ord. of 12-28-2009)
