ARTICLE I. - IN GENERAL

Sec. 2-1. - Responsibility for records management.
The clerk is responsible for records management activities on behalf of the city, and records shall be maintained in accordance with the approved retention schedule.
(Code 1986, § 2-52; Ord. of 11-21-1983)

ARTICLE II. - MAYOR AND COUNCIL

Sec. 2-21. - Time and place of special council meetings.
Any meeting other than a regular meeting shall be called at such time and place as the mayor shall deem proper.
(Code 1986, § 2-1)
