Chapter 2 - ADMINISTRATION

Sec. 2-1. - Special meetings of council.
Called or special meetings shall be held in accordance with section 2.02 of the Charter.
(Ord. of unknown date, § 1)
State Law reference— Open meeting requirements, O.C.G.A. § 50-14-1 et seq.
Sec. 2-2. - Fixing of fees, charges or rates.
Whenever, in this Code, language providing that fees, charges or rates are as established by the city or other language of similar import appears, such language shall mean that such fees, charges or rates are as established by resolution; provided, however, that:
(1)
If the Charter or state law provides that such fees, charges, or rates must be established by ordinance or some other formality, such language shall mean that such fees, charges or rates must be established by ordinance or such other formality as is required by law or the Charter.
(2)
Nothing in this section shall preclude the establishment of fees, charges, or rates by ordinance if establishment of fees, charges, or rates by ordinance is not prohibited by the Charter or state law.
