Editor's note— Ord. No. 11-12-066, § 1, adopted Dec. 5, 2011, repealed the former Chapter 5.24. §§ 5.24.010—5.24.060, and enacted a new Chapter 5.24 as set out herein. The former Chapter 5.24 pertained to similar subject matter and derived from Ord. 01-11-05C, §§ 1—5; Ord. 95-1212C(part); and Ord. 11-06-026, § 1.

5.24.010 - Insurer's license fee.
There is hereby levied for the year 2012 and for each year thereafter an annual license fee upon each insurer doing business within the City of Locust Grove, Georgia in the amount of fifty dollars. For each separate business location in excess of one not covered by section 5.24.020, which is operating on behalf of such insurers within the City of Locust Grove, Georgia, there is hereby levied a license fee in the amount of fifty dollars. For the purposes of this ordinance, the term "insurer" means a company which is authorized to transact business in any of the classes of insurance designated in O.C.G.A. Section 33-3-5.
(Ord. No. 11-12-066, § 1, 12-5-11)
5.24.020 - License fees for insurers insuring certain risks at additional business locations.
For each separate business location, not otherwise subject to a license fee hereunder, operated and maintained by a business organization which is engaged in the business of lending money or transacting sales involving term financing and in connection with such loans or sales offers, solicits or takes application for insurance through a licensed agent of an insurer for insurance said insurer shall pay an additional license fee of seventeen and 50/100 dollars per location for the year 2012 and for each year thereafter.
(Ord. No. 11-12-066, § 1, 12-5-11)
5.24.030 - Gross premiums tax imposed on life insurers.
There is hereby levied for the year 2012 and for each year thereafter an annual tax based solely upon gross direct premiums upon each insurer writing life, accident and sickness insurance within the corporate limits of the City of Locust Grove, Georgia in an amount equal to one percent of the gross direct premiums received during the preceding calendar year in accordance with O.C.G.A. Section 33-8-8.1. Gross direct premiums as used in this section shall mean gross direct premiums as used in O.C.G.A. Section 33-8-4. The premium tax levied by this section is in addition to the license fees imposed by Section 5.24.010 of this chapter.
(Ord. No. 11-12-066, § 1, 12-5-11)
5.24.040 - Gross premiums tax, all other insurers.
There is hereby levied for the year 2012 and for each year thereafter an annual tax based solely upon gross direct premiums upon each insurer, other than an insurer transacting business in the class of insurance designated in Sections 5.24.010 and 5.24.020, doing business within the State of Georgia in an amount equal to two and one-half percent of the gross direct premiums received during the preceding calendar year in accordance with O.C.G.A. Section 33-8-8.2. Gross direct premiums as used in this section shall mean gross direct premiums as used in O.C.G.A. Section 33-8-4. The premium tax levied by this section is in addition to the license fees imposed by Section 5.24.010 of this chapter.
(Ord. No. 11-12-066, § 1, 12-5-11)
5.24.050 - Due date for license fees.
License fees imposed in Sections 5.24.010, 5.24.020 and 5.24.040 of this ordinance shall be due and payable on the first day of January 2012 and on the first day of January each subsequent year.
(Ord. No. 11-12-066, § 1, 12-5-11)
5.24.060 - Administrative provisions.
The city clerk is hereby directed to forward a duly certified copy of this ordinance to the Insurance Commissioner of the State of Georgia within forty-five days of its enactment.
(Ord. No. 11-12-066, § 1, 12-5-11)
