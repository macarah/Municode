Chapter 10 - COURTS[1]

Footnotes:

State Law reference— State courts of counties, O.C.G.A. § 15-7-1 et seq.; probate courts, O.C.G.A. § 15-9-1 et seq.

Sec. 10-1. - Bailiff pay.
The compensation for bailiffs will be in amounts as established by the Board of Commissioners from time to time.
(Code 2003, ch. 5, art. I; Mot. of 11-10-1992)
Sec. 10-2. - Criminal warrant fees.
The criminal warrant fee shall be in an amount as established by the Board of Commissioners from time to time.
(Code 2003, ch. 5, art. I; Mot. of 8-6-1991)
Sec. 10-3. - Good behavior warrant fees.
The good behavior warrant fee shall be in an amount as established by the Board of Commissioners from time to time.
(Code 2003, ch. 5, art. I; Mot. of 1-6-1998)
