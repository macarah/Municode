ARTICLE VI. - USED CAR DEALERS[2]
Footnotes:
--- (2) ---
State Law reference— Used Motor Vehicle Dealers' and Used Motor Vehicle Parts Dealers' Registration Act, O.C.G.A. § 43-47-1 et seq.

DIVISION 1. - GENERALLY

Sec. 10-226. - Exceptions.
Nothing in this article shall be deemed to apply to any individual making an isolated sale of his own vehicle.
(Code 2003, § 32-211(7))
Sec. 10-227. - Inspections.
The police chief may make inspections from time to time for the purpose of seeing that the records required herein are being kept.
(Code 2003, § 32-211(6))
Sec. 10-228. - Records.
It shall be the further duty of every used car or used motor vehicle dealer licensed hereunder to immediately report to the police chief the presence in his place of business of any motor vehicle on which the serial or engine number has been defaced or altered.
(Code 2003, § 32-211(5))
Secs. 10-229—10-249. - Reserved.
DIVISION 2. - LICENSE[3]

Footnotes:
--- (3) ---
State Law reference— Local licensing used motor vehicle dealers, O.C.G.A. § 43-47-13.

Sec. 10-250. - License required.
Any used car or used motor vehicle dealer, as such terms are defined in O.C.G.A. title 43, ch. 47, who does business within this municipality shall be required to obtain a license from the city clerk/treasurer in the manner specified in this chapter.
(Code 2003, § 32-211(1))
Sec. 10-251. - Review of application.
No action on any application for a license under this division shall be taken by the city council until the police chief has reviewed such application and forwarded his recommendation thereon to the city clerk/treasurer in the manner specified in this chapter.
(Code 2003, § 32-211(3))
Sec. 10-252. - Restriction on issuance.
No license under this division shall be issued to any applicant who has not been licensed by the state board of registration of used car dealers.
(Code 2003, § 32-211(4))
Sec. 10-253. - Fee established.
The annual business license fee for each used car or used motor vehicle dealer doing business in the city shall be set by resolution of the council.
(Code 2003, § 32-211(2))
