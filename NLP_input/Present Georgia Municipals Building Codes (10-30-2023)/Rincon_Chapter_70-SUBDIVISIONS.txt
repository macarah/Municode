Chapter 70 - SUBDIVISIONS[1]

Sec. 70-1. - Adoption of regulations.
The subdivision regulations of the city, adopted as an ordinance by the mayor and council on August 22, 1983, as now or hereafter amended, are hereby incorporated into this chapter and made a part of this chapter. A copy of the subdivision regulations shall be maintained on file in the office of the city clerk.
(Code 1984, § 16-101)
State Law reference— Approval by planning commission or governing authority on plat of subdivision required for filing or recording in superior court clerk's office, O.C.G.A. § 15-6-67(d).
