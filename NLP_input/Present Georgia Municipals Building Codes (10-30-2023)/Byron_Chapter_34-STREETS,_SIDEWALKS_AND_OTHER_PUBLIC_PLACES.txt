Sec. 34-5. - Encroachments.
modified
It shall be unlawful for any property owner or other person to build and fence or erect any building on the streets or sidewalks of the city or to plant any trees, without first obtaining permission of the city council.
(Code 1935, § 104; Code 1987, § 18-5; Ord. No. 2022-5 , 2-14-2022)
Sec. 34-6. - Obstructions; alterations; damaging.
modified
It shall be unlawful for any person to alter any public street or the sidewalk thereof or cut any ditch across the street or sidewalk or make any new bridge necessary or otherwise obstruct any street or sidewalk, without first obtaining authority from the city council. It shall be unlawful for any person to dig, remove or haul away any dirt, sand or rock from any street or sidewalk or to remove or damage any bridge, pipe, drain or sewer on the city streets without first obtaining permission from the mayor and council.
(Code 1935, § 10; Code 1987, § 18-6; Ord. No. 2022-5 , 2-14-2022)
State Law reference— Obstructing streets, O.C.G.A. § 16-11-43.
Sec. 34-7. - Maintenance of sidewalks by property owners.
modified
It shall be the duty of all property owners in the city to maintain and keep in passable condition the sidewalks abutting the property owned by them.
(Code 1935, § 102; Code 1987, § 18-7; Ord. No. 2022-5 , 2-14-2022)

ARTICLE II. - STREET, ALLEY CLOSURE AND STREET NAME CHANGES

Sec. 34-31. - Consideration by city council.
modified
The city council shall consider the closing or changing of the name of any public street or alley within the corporate limits only upon receipt of petition signed by 60 percent of property owners and/or residents abutting the public street for which the name change or closing is requested.
(Ord. No. 2017-3 , 7-10-2017; Ord. No. 2022-5 , 2-14-2022)
Editor's note— Ord. No. 2022-5 , adopted Feb. 14, 2022, amended the title of § 34-31 to read as herein set out. The former § 34-31 title pertained to consideration by mayor and council.
Sec. 34-32. - Procedure for consideration.
modified
Upon receipt of petition requesting a name change or closing of any public street or alley, the city council shall verify that the petition contains the requisite signatures of 60 percent of property owners and/or residents of the public street or alley. Thereafter, a notice of meeting in which the petition and request is to be considered shall be published in the local newspaper a minimum of two times commencing at least 20 days prior to the meeting. This notice shall contain the date and place of meeting, the current name of the street or alley to be changed or closed, or if a name change is requested, the requested name change.
(Ord. No. 2017-3 , 7-10-2017; Ord. No. 2022-5 , 2-14-2022)
Sec. 34-33. - Effective date of change.
modified
Upon the change of any public street within the city, the effective date of the change shall not be effective for a period of at least 90 days after passage of the ordinance.
(Ord. No. 2017-3 , 7-10-2017; Ord. No. 2022-5 , 2-14-2022)
