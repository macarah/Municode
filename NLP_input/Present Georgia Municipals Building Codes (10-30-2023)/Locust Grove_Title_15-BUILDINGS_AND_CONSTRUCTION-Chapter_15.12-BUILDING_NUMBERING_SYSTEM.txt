Chapter 15.12 - BUILDING NUMBERING SYSTEM
Sections:

15.12.010 - Numbers to be assigned by building department.
Street numbers for dwelling units and places of business on all public streets, and street numbers and/or building numbers for dwelling units and places of business within apartment projects and nonresidential developments located on private streets, shall be assigned by the building department in accordance with administrative procedures.
(Prior code § 6-6-2 (part))
15.12.020 - Posting of numbers.
Each dwelling unit and place of business shall have light-reflective numbers posted and maintained in a prominent place on the property, visible from the street providing public access, the address of the dwelling unit or place of business.
A.
For residences using mailboxes: Two-inch-high light-reflective numbers with contrasting background;
B.
For residences which do not use mailboxes and within one hundred feet of roadway: Three-inch-high light-reflective numbers with contrasting background;
C.
For residences which do not use mailboxes and are more than one hundred feet from roadway: Three-inch-high light-reflective numbers visible from the roadway with contrasting background; and
D.
For all commercial establishments: Three-inch-high light-reflective numbers with contrasting backgrounds.
(Prior code § 6-6-2 (a))
15.12.030 - Violation—Penalty.
Any person violating the terms of this chapter or rules and regulations promulgated pursuant thereto shall be punished in recorders court for the city by a fine not to exceed five hundred dollars and/or sixty days in jail for each separate violation. Each day the violation continues shall be considered a separate offense.
(Prior code § 6-6-2 (part))
