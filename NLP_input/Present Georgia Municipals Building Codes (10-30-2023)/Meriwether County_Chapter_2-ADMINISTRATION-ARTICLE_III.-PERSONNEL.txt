ARTICLE III. - PERSONNEL

Sec. 2-58. - County personnel policy.
Unless otherwise addressed in this Code, all county personnel policy shall be as set out in the county's personnel policy manual, which may be amended from time to time by action of the board of commissioners, and which shall be available for examination and copying in the office of the county clerk during normal business hours.
