CHAPTER 17.56 - O-I OFFICE INSTITUTIONAL DISTRICT

Sections:
17.56.010 - Purpose and permitted uses.
A.
The O-I office institutional district is intended to encourage and provide suitable areas for business and professional offices, hospitals, medical and dental clinics and certain related activities, with a minimum of interference from traffic and conflicting uses.
B.
Within any O-I Office Institutional District, the following uses shall be permitted unless one or more of these uses are otherwise prohibited, not authorized, limited, or restricted by the City Council when acting upon a zoning application and/or rezoning of a parcel(s) of properly:
1.
Business colleges, barber and beauty colleges, art schools, music and dance studios, and trade schools, provided that all activities shall be enclosed.
2.
Business and profession offices; including medical, dental, legal, financial, architectural, engineering, real estate, insurance and manufacturing representatives, provided no goods are offered for sale at retail.
3.
Clubs and lodges catering exclusively to members and their guests.
4.
Florist shops.
5.
Government offices.
6.
Hospitals.
7.
Medical and dental clinics, but not veterinary clinics.
8.
Nursing and rest homes.
9.
Off-street automobile parking lot provided that a landscape strip of at least five (5) feet is provided along all property lines in addition to any other buffers required by this Title.
10.
Pharmacies, excluding drive through window, provided that merchandise is limited to drugs and medical supplies and devices.
11.
Libraries, museums, art galleries, and similar facilities.
(Ord. No. 98-12-14-05, (part); Ord. No. 04-12-06-B, § XXV, 12-6-2004; Ord. No. 08-01-22(C), § XXIX, 1-22-2008)
17.56.020 - Special Uses.
Within any O-I Office Institutional District, the following uses may be allowed upon obtaining a special use permit from the City Council:
A.
Churches and associated buildings.
B.
Day care facilities when constructed as part of a planned office park on a lot of at least three acres.
C.
Rooming/boarding house.
D.
Halfway house.
(Ord. No. 04-12-06-B, § XXVI, 12-6-2004; Ord. No. 08-01-22(C), § XXX, 1-22-2008; Ord. No. 08-12-29, § IX, 12-29-2008)
