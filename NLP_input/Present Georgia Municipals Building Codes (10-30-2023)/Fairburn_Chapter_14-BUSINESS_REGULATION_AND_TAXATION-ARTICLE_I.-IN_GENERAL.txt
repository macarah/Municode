ARTICLE I. - IN GENERAL

Sec. 14-1. - Permit for signs or advertising of business.
It shall be unlawful for any person to erect any signboard, display advertisement or bill poster, or other structure for advertising purposes, without first having obtained from the mayor and council full permission therefor. Application for permission to erect and maintain such signboard, display advertisement, bill poster or other structure for advertising shall be made to the mayor and council in writing. Said application shall set out fully the kind, character, size and probable duration of said advertising device, its location and the goods, wares, service or thing advertised.
(Code 1941, § 1201; Code 1966, § 16-22)
