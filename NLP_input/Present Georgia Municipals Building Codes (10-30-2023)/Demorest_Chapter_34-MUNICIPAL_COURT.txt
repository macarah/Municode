Sec. 34-1. - Contempt of court.
Any person guilty of any disrespectful conduct during any session of the municipal court, or during any council meeting, may be fined for contempt in a sum not exceeding $10.00, or imprisonment in the city jail not exceeding 48 hours for each offense.
(Code 1955, § 97)
