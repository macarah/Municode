ARTICLE I. - FREEPORT EXEMPTION

Sec. 74-1. - Freeport exemption.
Sixty percent of the value of that tangible personal property defined in Ga. Const. art. VII, § II, ¶ III(1), (2), (3) shall be exempt from ad valorem taxation, including all taxes levied for educational and state purposes, which exemption shall commence January 1, 1998.
(Res. of 12-8-1997)
State Law reference— Authority for exemption for certain tangible personal property from ad valorem taxation, O.C.G.A. § 48-5-48.2.
Secs. 74-2—74-9. - Reserved.
ARTICLE II. - HOTEL/MOTEL TAX

Sec. 74-10. - Excise tax.
There is levied and imposed an excise tax at the rate of five percent of the charge of the public for and upon the furnishing for value to the public of any room or rooms, lodging, or accommodations furnished by any person or legal entity within the county operating a hotel, motel, inn, lodge, tourist camp, tourist cabin or other place in which rooms, lodging, or accommodations are regularly furnished for value. Said tax shall not include the sale or charges for any rooms, lodging, or accommodations furnished for a period of more than ten consecutive days, or for the use of meetings rooms.
(Res. of 10-11-2021, § 3)
Sec. 74-11. - When due.
Said excise tax shall be paid monthly beginning January 1, 2022, within 20 days after the end of each month. Vendors collecting said tax shall be allowed a percentage of the tax due and accounted for in the amount of three percent in the form of a deduction in submitting, reporting and paying the amount due, if said amount is not delinquent at the time of payment. Upon the failure to make a timely report and remittance by the 20th day of the month next succeeding the month in which such sales were made, said collection fee shall be forfeited. In addition, a penalty of 25 percent per month for failure to pay the excise tax within ten days of the due date shall be imposed. Vendors shall pay said tax with such remittance form as prescribed by the city. All books and records of each vendor shall be subject to inspection and audit by the mayor and city council to verify compliance with this article.
(Res. of 10-11-2021, § 4)
Sec. 74-12. - Tax in addition to existing fees, taxes, etc.
The excise tax provided for hereinabove shall be in addition to any license fee or occupation tax or charge which, may now or in the future be imposed upon vendors within the city.
(Res. of 10-11-2021, § 5)
Sec. 74-13. - Proceeds.
Proceeds from such tax shall be expended pursuant to O.C.G.A. § 48-13-51(a)(l)(D)(3).
(Res. of 10-11-2021, § 6)
Sec. 74-14. - Applicability.
This article shall continue in effect as amended from time to time and from the original adoption date of the ordinance from which it is derived (January 1, 2022).
(Res. of 10-11-2021, § 7)
Sec. 74-15. - Authorized.
Said article shall be authorized under O.C.G.A. § 48-13-51(a)(l)(D)(3) allowing the city to collect said hotel/motel tax at rate of five percent.
(Res. of 10-11-2021, § 8)
