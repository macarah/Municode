Sec. 287. - (Restatement) Contract not created.
This Act, or any ordinance passed in pursuance thereof, shall not create a contractual relation between the City of Waycross, Georgia, and any employee thereof. In case there shall at any time be less funds on hand than are needed to carry out the terms of this Act, the actual funds on hand shall be prorated among the beneficiaries.
