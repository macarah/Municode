Sec. 6-70. - Residential buildings containing less than seven hundred square feet.
Residential buildings containing less than seven hundred (700) square feet are permitted within the City of Cairo upon the following conditions:
(1)
All residences shall be located within zoning area R-1.
(2)
Subject to the requirement of section 22-15, substandard lots, all residences shall be located on a lot of the minimum size of seven thousand five hundred (7,500) square feet.
(3)
The person or entity desiring to build the residence shall present with the application to construct the residence a floor plan sited on a plat of survey dated within six (6) months of the application.
(4)
The residence shall be connected to the electric system, water system and sanitary sewer system of the City of Cairo.
(5)
The floor plan and subsequent construction shall provide a location for a refrigerator and stove/oven.
(6)
All residences shall meet current building code requirements of the City of Cairo including required doorways and windows.
(7)
Each person who is a permanent resident shall have a minimum of one hundred fifty (150) square feet of floor space.
(Ord. No. 779, 2-8-16)
