Sec. 6-1. - Animal control within the corporate limits located in Haralson County.
Per the provisions of the Service Delivery Strategy Agreement with Haralson County, said county is responsible for animal control within that portion of the city lying within Haralson County. To establish a uniform code of ordinance for enforcement of animal control with Haralson County, the city hereby adopts by reference, and incorporates herein, Chapter 8, Animal Control of the Haralson County Code of Ordinances, as revised or amended, as its animal control ordinance is effective within the confines of Haralson County within the city.
Sec. 6-2. - Animal control within the corporate limits located in Carroll County.
Per the provisions of the Service Delivery Strategy Agreement with Carroll County, said county is responsible for animal control within that portion of the city lying within Carroll County. To establish a uniform code of ordinance for enforcement of animal control with Carroll County, the city hereby adopts by reference, and incorporates herein, Chapter 14, Animals of the Carroll County Code of Ordinances, as its animal control ordinance is effective within the confines of Carroll County within the city.
