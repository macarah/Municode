ARTICLE I. - IN GENERAL

Sec. 22-1. - Bankrupt sales.
It shall be unlawful for any person to add new merchandise to a strictly bankrupt sale conducted as such within the city. Any person who advertises by placard, or otherwise, over his store or place of business, or in any newspaper or by handbills, or otherwise, that a bankrupt sale is being carried on in such store or place of business, or that the stock being sold is a bankrupt stock, shall be deemed for the purpose of this section to be carrying on a strictly bankrupt sale.
(Code 1976, § 11-5001)
