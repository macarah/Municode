Sec. 46-1. - Chief of police department.
Subject to the general supervision of the city administrator, the police department shall be under the direction and control of the police chief.
(Code 1980, § 3-1-1)
Sec. 46-2. - Composition of department.
The department shall consist of those officers and employees of those ranks and positions as approved by the mayor and council.
(Code 1980, § 3-1-2)
Sec. 46-3. - Responsibilities.
The department shall be responsible for patrol, traffic control, investigation of accident, investigation of crimes, apprehension of offenders, court appearances, security of business establishments, and for any other matters of public safety and law enforcement as directed by the mayor and council.
(Code 1980, § 3-1-3)
