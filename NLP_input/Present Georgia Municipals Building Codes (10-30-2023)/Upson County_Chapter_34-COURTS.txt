Sec. 34-26. - Prosecution upon citation or accusation.
Violations of this Code or county ordinances shall be prosecuted in the magistrate court of the county upon citation or upon accusation by the county attorney.
(Ord. No. 71, § 1, 9-17-1993)
Sec. 34-27. - Issuance of citations for violations.
All law enforcement officers and county employees or officers who are provided with citations are hereby authorized to issue citations for the violation of this Code or county ordinances.
(Ord. No. 71, § 2, 9-17-1993)
