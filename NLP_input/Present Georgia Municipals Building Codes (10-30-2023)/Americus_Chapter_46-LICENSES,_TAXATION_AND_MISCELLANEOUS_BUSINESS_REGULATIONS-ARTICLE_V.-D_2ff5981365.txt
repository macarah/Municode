ARTICLE V. - DEPOSITORY FINANCIAL INSTITUTIONS LICENSE TAX

Sec. 46-155. - Business license tax levied.
In accordance with O.C.G.A. § 48-6-93, there is hereby levied for the year 1984, and for each year thereafter, an annual business license tax upon the depository financial institutions located within the city at a rate of 0.25 percent of the gross receipts of such depository financial institution. The term "gross receipts" shall mean gross receipts as defined in O.C.G.A. § 48-6-93. Depository financial institutions shall mean state and national banks, state building and loan associations and federal savings and loan associations.
(Code 1986, § 14-91; Ord. of 12-28-1983, § 1)
Sec. 46-156. - Minimum due.
The minimum annual amount of business license tax due from any depository financial institution pursuant to section 46-155 shall be established by ordinance.
(Code 1986, § 14-92; Ord. of 12-28-1983, § 2)
Sec. 46-157. - Form for filing of return.
Pursuant to O.C.G.A. § 48-6-93(c), each depository financial institution subject to this tax shall file a return of its gross receipts with the finance director of the city on March 1 of the year following the year in which such gross receipts are measured. Such return shall be in the manner and in the form prescribed by the commissioner of the department of revenue based on the allocation method set forth in of O.C.G.A. § 48-6-93(d). The city council shall assess and collect the tax levy pursuant to this article based upon the information provided in such return.
(Code 1986, § 14-93; Ord. of 12-28-1983, § 3; Ord. No. O-04-12-33, 12-16-2004)
Sec. 46-158. - Due date.
Taxes levied pursuant to this article shall be due no later than 30 days after filing of the return prescribed by section 46-157 unless extended by the city council.
(Code 1986, § 14-94; Ord. of 12-28-1983, § 4; Ord. No. O-04-12-33, 12-16-2004)
Sec. 46-159. - Administrative provisions.
The finance director of the city is hereby directed to forward a copy of this article to each depository financial institution in the city and to the home office of each such depository financial institution if located outside the city.
(Code 1986, § 14-95; Ord. of 12-28-1983, § 5; Ord. No. O-04-12-33, 12-16-2004)
Secs. 46-160—46-186. - Reserved.
