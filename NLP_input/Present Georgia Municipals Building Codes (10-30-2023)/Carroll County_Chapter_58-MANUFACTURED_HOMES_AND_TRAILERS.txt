Sec. 58-1. - Provisions saved from repeal.
The ordinance entitled "Reporting Requirements for Manufactured Home Park Owners," dated April 12, 1990, and the ordinance entitled "Resolution in Reference to Mobile Homes, Carroll County, Georgia," dated March 26, 1982, are not repealed and shall continue in full force and effect pending a revision of the subject matter by the county.
