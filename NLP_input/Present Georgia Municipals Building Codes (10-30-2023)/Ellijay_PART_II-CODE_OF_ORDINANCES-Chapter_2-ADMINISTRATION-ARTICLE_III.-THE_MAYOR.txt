ARTICLE III. - THE MAYOR

Sec. 2-141. - Compensation.
The mayor shall receive $22.000.00 per year plus meeting pay and full city benefits as compensation.
(Code 1979, § 3-301; Mo. of 12-19-2011)
Sec. 2-142. - Mayor pro tempore.
The councilmember receiving the most votes in the general election shall act as mayor pro tem, who during a temporary absence or disability of the mayor shall possess the powers of mayor.
(Code 1979, § 3-302)
