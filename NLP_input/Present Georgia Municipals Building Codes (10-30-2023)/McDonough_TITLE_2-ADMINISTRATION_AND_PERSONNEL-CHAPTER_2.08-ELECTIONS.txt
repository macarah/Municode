Sections:
2.08.010 - Voting Precincts—Polling places.
A.
Pursuant to O.C.G.A. § 21-2-260 et seq., the City of McDonough, Georgia is hereby divided into two Voting Precincts as follows:
1.
Precinct One shall be comprised of Ward One and Ward Four of the City; and
2.
Precinct Two shall be comprised of Ward Two and Ward Three of the City.
B.
Pursuant to O.C.G.A. § 21-2-265, the polling place for all voting precincts within the City of McDonough, Georgia, is designated as the Henry County Board of Elections and Registration Office, located at 345 Phillips Drive, McDonough, Georgia. All registered voters residing in Precinct One and Precinct Two shall vote at the Henry County Board of Elections and Registration Office.
(Ord. No. 10-06-07(D), § I, 6-7-2010; Ord. No. 10-10-4(D), § I, 10-4-2010)
2.08.020 - Voting; General.
A.
Voting recorders and tabulating machines shall be used for tabulating and recording the vote at all elections held in the City.
B.
Henry County shall perform all duties as superintendent of elections as specified under Chapter 2 of Title 21 of the Official Code of Georgia Annotated, with the exception of the qualification of candidates.
(Ord. No. 10-06-07(D), § I, 6-7-2010; Ord. No. 11-08-15(C), § 1, 8-15-2011)
