ARTICLE I. - IN GENERAL

Sec. 10-1. - Definitions.
The following words, terms and phrases, when used in this chapter, shall have the meanings ascribed to them in this section, except where the context clearly indicates a different meaning:
DWV means drain, waste, and vent piping and materials associated with building drain and venting systems, as further defined and delineated in the plumbing code adopted by the county.
SEER means seasonal energy efficiency ratio as defined by the Air Conditioning, Heating, and Refrigeration Institute (AHRI), Arlington, Virginia.
Sec. 10-2. - Violations and penalties.
Any person or agent, who shall violate any provision of the technical codes, or any portion of this chapter, shall be guilty of a misdemeanor which is punishable as set forth in section 1-11. Each person shall be considered guilty of a separate offense for each and every day or portion thereof during which any violation of any of the provisions of this chapter is committed or continue.
(Ord. of 9-21-2006(3), § 7; Ord. of 2-21-2008, § 5)
