DIVISION 14. - MISCELLANEOUS PROVISIONS

Sec. 12-331. - Pretreatment charges and fees.
The control authority may adopt reasonable fees for reimbursement of the costs of development and administration of the control authority's pretreatment program, which may include:
(1)
Fees for wastewater discharge permit applications including the cost of processing such applications;
(2)
Fees for monitoring, inspection, and surveillance procedures including the cost of collection and analyzing a user's discharge, and reviewing monitoring reports submitted by users;
(3)
Fees for reviewing and responding to accidental discharge procedures and construction;
(4)
Fees for filing appeals; and
(5)
Other fees as the control authority may deem necessary to carry out the requirements contained herein. These fees relate solely to the matters covered by this article and are separate from all other fees, fines, and penalties chargeable by the control authority.
Sec. 12-332. - Severability.
If any provision of this article is invalidated by any court of competent jurisdiction, the remaining provisions shall not be affected and shall continued in full force and effect.
Sec. 12-333. - Conflicts.
All other ordinances and parts of other ordinances inconsistent or conflicting with any part of this article, are hereby repealed to the extent of the inconsistency or conflict.
Sec. 12-334. - Effective date.
This article shall be in full force and effect immediately following its passage, approval and publication, as provided by law.
