Chapter 14 - CIVIL EMERGENCIES

Sec. 14-1. - National Incident Management System (NIMS).
The city hereby adopts the National Incident Management System (NIMS) as established under HSPD 5 and the Unified Command System as established under O.C.G.A. ยง 38-3-57 as its system of preparing for and responding to disaster incidents, and directs all incident managers and response organizations in the city to train and exercise and use these systems in their response operations.
