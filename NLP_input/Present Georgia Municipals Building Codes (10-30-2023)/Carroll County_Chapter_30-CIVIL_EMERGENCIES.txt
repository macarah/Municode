Editor's note— The county ordinance relative to emergency management adopted July 28, 1992, promulgates regulations pertaining to civil emergencies and emergency management and is published separately by the Carroll County emergency management agency. The purpose of the plan is to ensure preparedness, appropriate response and timely recovery from natural or manmade hazards that may affect the county. A copy of the plan is on file in the office of the clerk of the board of commissioners.
