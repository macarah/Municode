ARTICLE I. - IN GENERAL

Sec. 2-1. - Polling place location.
The location of the polling place for all elections held in the city shall be the Unadilla City Hall, 1616 Borum Street, Unadilla, Georgia, 31092
Sec. 2-2. - Election superintendent.
The city clerk shall act for the mayor and council as election superintendent and shall provide necessary ballots, tally sheets, and all other necessary materials for use by the poll officials in holding any and all elections, held from time to time in the city.
(Prior Code, § 7-4)
Sec. 2-3. - Worker's compensation.
The city clerk is authorized to obtain worker's compensation insurance for all municipal employees including volunteer firemen.
(Prior Code, § 10-21)
Sec. 2-4. - [Records retention schedules.]
(a)
Those local government records retention schedules as promulgated by the Office of the Secretary of State of the State of Georgia, as amended from time to time, and found on the following website:
http:/sos.georgia.gov.archives/pdf/state_spec_reports/LocalGovernmentSchedule2009.pdf
are hereby approved and adopted as records retention schedules for the City of Unadilla.
(b)
The city clerk is responsible for records management activities and is charged with the responsibility for directing and coordinating all records management matters.
(c)
No record shall be destroyed except in accordance with approved retention schedules.
(d)
Whenever any records are destroyed, a certificate of destruction shall be prepared and forwarded to the city clerk which shall show the type of records destroyed, the approved retention schedule number, the dates covered, and the volume destroyed.
(Ord. No. 11-2, §§ 1—4, 3-8-2011)
Editor's note—
Sections 1—4 of Ord. No. 11-2, adopted March 8, 2011, did not specify manner of inclusion; hence, codification as § 2-4 was at the discretion of the editor.
