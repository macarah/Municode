ARTICLE XIV. - FEE SCHEDULE

Sec. 40-286. - Rates, fees and charges.
A schedule of rates, fees and charges for permits, inspections, and other services provided by this chapter is hereby adopted. Such rates, fees, and charges shall be determined from time to time by the mayor and council and set forth [in] a written schedule on file and available for inspection in the office of the city clerk.
(Ord. of 7-1-2002, Art. XIV)
