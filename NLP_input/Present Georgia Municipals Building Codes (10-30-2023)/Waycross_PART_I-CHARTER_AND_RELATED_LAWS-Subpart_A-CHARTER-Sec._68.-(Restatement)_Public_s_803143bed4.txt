Sec. 68. - (Restatement) Public service corporations.
Be it further enacted, that it shall be within the power of the city board or department especially affected, or of the city commission to compel any and all public service corporations operating or doing business in said city to remove and replace and readjust at their own expense all tracks, poles, conduits, wires and other appurtenances and structures used by them, in the prosecution of their business functions, whenever the same may in the discretion of such departments, or said city commission, be necessary to carry out any ordinance or plan of the municipal government of the City of Waycross, or to the best interest of the city and its inhabitants.
