Sec. 12-148. - Levied generally; multiple locations.
There is hereby levied an annual license fee upon each insurer doing business within the city and for each separate business location in excess of one, not covered by section 12-149, that is operating on behalf of such insurers within the city, in the amount provided in the city fee schedule.
(Code 1999, § 62-71; Ord. of 11-8-1983, § 1; Ord. of 10-9-2001, § 1)
State Law reference— Local tax on life insurance companies, O.C.G.A. § 33-8-8.1.
Sec. 12-149. - Insurers insuring certain risks at additional business locations.
For each separate business location, not otherwise subject to a license fee under this article, operated and maintained by a business organization which is engaged in the business of lending money or transacting sales involving term financing and in connection with such loans or sales offers, solicits or takes application for insurance through a licensed agent of an insurer for insurance, the insurer shall pay an additional annual license fee per location per year in the amount provided in the city fee schedule.
(Code 1999, § 62-72; Ord. of 11-8-1983, § 2; Ord. of 10-9-2001, § 2)
State Law reference— Local tax on other than life insurance companies, O.C.G.A. § 33-8-8.2.
Sec. 12-150. - Additional locations of independent agencies and brokers.
There is hereby levied an annual license fee upon independent agencies and brokers for each separate business location from which a life insurance business is conducted and which is not subject to the company license fee imposed by either section 12-148 or 12-149, for each such location within the city, in the amount provided in the city fee schedule.
(Code 1999, § 62-73; Ord. of 11-8-1983, § 3)
Sec. 12-151. - Gross premiums tax imposed on life insurers.
There is hereby levied an annual tax based solely upon gross direct premiums upon each insurer writing life, accident and sickness insurance within the state in an amount equal to one percent of the gross direct premiums received during the preceding calendar year in accordance with O.C.G.A. § 33-8-8.1. The premium tax levied by this section is in addition to the license fees imposed under this article.
(Code 1999, § 62-74; Ord. of 11-8-1983, § 4; Ord. of 10-9-2001, § 3)
State Law reference— Municipal authority to impose license fee on life insurance companies, O.C.G.A. § 38-8-8.
Sec. 12-152. - Gross premiums tax on all other insurers.
There is hereby levied an annual tax based solely upon gross direct premiums upon each insurer, other than an insurer transacting business in the class of insurance designated in O.C.G.A. § 33-3-5(1), doing business within this state in an amount equal to 2½ percent of the gross direct premiums received during the preceding calendar year in accordance with O.C.G.A. § 33-8-8.2. The premium tax levied by this section is in addition to the license fees imposed under this article.
(Code 1999, § 62-75; Ord. of 11-8-1983, § 5; Ord. of 10-9-2001, § 4)
State Law reference— Similar provision, O.C.G.A. § 33-8-8.2.
Sec. 12-153. - Due date.
License fees imposed in this article shall be due and payable on the first day of each year.
(Code 1999, § 62-76; Ord. of 11-8-1983, § 6; Ord. of 10-9-2001, § 5)
