Sec. 4½-1. - Licenses, insurance required.
No ambulance service shall operate within the City of Dublin, Georgia without a valid state license, a valid city license and insurance coverage issued by an insurance company licensed to do business in Georgia providing at least the minimum coverage required for ambulances under the laws of the state of Georgia. A copy of said licenses and a copy of a current and valid certificate of insurance shall be maintained on file with the city clerk.
(Ord. No. 80-10, § 1, 5-1-80)
Cross reference— Licenses generally, § 12-1 et seq.
Sec. 4½-2. - Requirements for attendants.
When answering calls, ambulance attendants shall have on their person a current CPR card and a copy of a current EMT certificate. A copy of said card and certificate shall be on file with the city clerk.
(Ord. No. 80-10, § 2, 5-1-80)
Sec. 4½-3. - Display of emergency light permit.
An emergency light permit shall be displayed on the lower right corner of the front windshield of all in-service ambulances.
(Ord. No. 80-10, § 3, 5-1-80)
