Sec. 16-1. - Personnel management system.
The city personnel management system document is on file in the office of the city clerk.
Sec. 16-2. - Retirement plan.
(a)
The document entitled "A Retirement Plan for the Employees of the City of Avondale Estates, Georgia," and any amendments thereto attached to the ordinance from which this section is derived as exhibit A and comprising 62 pages, is hereby adopted as the retirement plan of the city.
(b)
The document attached to the ordinance from which this section is derived as Appendix A to the retirement plan of the city and consisting of pages A-1 through A-16, is hereby adopted as the joint trust agreement for the administration of such plan by the city and the state municipal employees' benefit system.
(c)
The documents referred to in subsections (a) and (b) are hereby made public records and shall be maintained by the city clerk as such and be accessible to the members of the general public at city hall during all normal business hours.
(d)
The effective date of this section was fifteen (15) days after final passage in accordance with section 1-401 of the city Charter, or not earlier than January 1, 1989, whichever was later, and the effective dates of the attachments hereto shall so correspond.

Sec. 16-3. - Personnel board established; term; procedures; duties.
(a)
There is hereby established the personnel board of the city to consist of five (5) residents who hold no other office with and shall remain residents of the city during the term for which appointed to the personnel board.
(b)
Members of the personnel board shall be appointed by resolution, with terms to be four (4) years except for the first personnel board appointed, two (2) members of which shall be appointed for one-year terms, one (1) member for a two-year term, one (1) member for a three-year term and one (1) member for a four-year term. Thereafter, all terms shall be four (4) years.
(c)
The operating procedures of the personnel board shall be those set out in the personnel management system manual as the same now exists or is hereinafter amended, it being the intent hereof that the personnel board shall perform all functions previously assigned to the board of mayor and commissioners in the said personnel management system manual.
(Ord. No. 9903, §§ 1—3, 5-24-99)
