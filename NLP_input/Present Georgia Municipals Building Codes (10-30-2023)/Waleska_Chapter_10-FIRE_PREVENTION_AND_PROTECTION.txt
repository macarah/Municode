Chapter 10 - FIRE PREVENTION AND PROTECTION[1]

Footnotes:
--- (1) ---
Editor's note— Fire protection services are provided by the county.

Sec. 10-1. - County standards adopted.
The provisions of chapter 28 of the Code of Ordinances, Cherokee County, Georgia as now or hereafter amended are hereby adopted by reference.
Sec. 10-2. - Tampering with alarm system.
It shall be unlawful for any person to willfully, maliciously, or mischievously interfere or tamper with any fire alarm box or any of the appliances or apparatus connected therewith, located within the corporate limits.
(Code 1986, § 3-2-4)
Sec. 10-3. - Protection of fire hydrants.
No person shall plant any vegetation, shrubbery, trees, flowers, or plantings of any kind within a radius of six feet of any fire hydrant within the city limits.
(Code 1986, § 3-2-5; Ord. of 9-14-1987)
