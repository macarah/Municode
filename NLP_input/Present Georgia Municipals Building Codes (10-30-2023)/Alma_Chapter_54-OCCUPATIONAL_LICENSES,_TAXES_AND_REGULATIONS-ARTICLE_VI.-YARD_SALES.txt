ARTICLE VI. - YARD SALES[4]

Footnotes:
--- (4) ---
Editor's note— An ordinance adopted July 9, 2018 amended Art. VI in its entirety to read as herein set out. Former Art. VI, §§ 54-206—54-216, pertained to similar subject matter, and derived from the Code of 1981, §§ 14-90—14-100; and Ord. No. 2009-03, adopted May 4, 2009.

Sec. 54-206. - Definition.
Yard sale means a garage sale, rummage sale or any other sale held by individuals for purpose of disposing of personal items owned by the person and no longer needed or wanted.
(Ord. of 7-9-2018)
Sec. 54-207. - Locations.
Yard sales may only be held at one of the two following locations:
(1)
Property owned by the person or persons conducting the yard sale; or
(2)
The City of Alma Farmers Market.
(Ord. of 7-9-2018)
Sec. 54-208. - Permit required.
Persons wishing to conduct a sale at the City of Alma Farmers Market must first obtain a permit and permission from the office of the city clerk to conduct a yard sale at the City of Alma Farmers Market.
(Ord. of 7-9-2018; Mins. of 7-9-2018, § 1)
Sec. 54-209. - Number.
No person shall conduct more than one yard sale per quarter for a total of four yard sales during the calendar year.
(Ord. of 7-9-2018)
Sec. 54-210. - Penalty.
Any person found guilty of violation of this article shall be guilty of a misdemeanor and subject to a fine of not more than $250.00 and court costs.
(Ord. of 7-9-2018)
Secs. 54-211—54-240. - Reserved.
