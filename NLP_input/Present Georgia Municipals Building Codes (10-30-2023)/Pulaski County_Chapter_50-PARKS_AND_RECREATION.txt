ARTICLE II. - COUNTY PARK RULES AND REGULATIONS

Sec. 50-31. - Specific rules and regulations.
(a)
The following rules and regulations are adopted and ordered enforced by the appropriate enforcement authorities as to the county property known as the County Park, formerly the Hawkinsville State Park:
(1)
No alcoholic beverages are permitted, and no firearms are allowed.
(2)
All litter and debris must be removed or placed in appropriate receptacles designed for such purpose.
(3)
Overnight camping is restricted to bona fide campers and others using such property for strictly recreation purposes.
(4)
No loitering by persons not using the premises for bona fide recreation purposes is permitted.
(5)
Loud, abusive or profane language is prohibited.
(6)
Camping for more than one night is prohibited without approval of the commissioner or the sheriff's department.
(b)
Violators of subsection (a) of this section shall, upon conviction, be punished as provided in section 1-11.
(Res. of 6-1-1976)
