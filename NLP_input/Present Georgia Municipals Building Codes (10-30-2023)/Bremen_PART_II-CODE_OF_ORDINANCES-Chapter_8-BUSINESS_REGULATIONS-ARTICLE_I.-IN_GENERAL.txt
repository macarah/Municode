ARTICLE I. - IN GENERAL

Sec. 8-1. - Registration, tax, license require.
Unless otherwise required, every person conducting business within the city limits shall obtain a business registration and shall pay occupation tax as in article III of this chapter; also, certain businesses shall follow additional licensing requirements set forth in article II of this chapter.
Sec. 8-2. - Compliance with local, state and federal laws required.
Each business operating in the city shall maintain full compliance with all applicable local, state and federal laws; failure to comply with any regulation or statute shall be grounds for revocation of business registration.
Sec. 8-3. - Businesses receiving certain exemptions.
The following businesses shall receive the exemptions to any requirement imposed under this chapter pursuant to the provisions of other general laws of the state:
(1)
Those businesses regulated by the state public service commission;
(2)
Those electrical service businesses organized under O.C.G.A. title 46, ch. 3;
(3)
Any farm operation for the production from or on the land of agricultural products, but not including agribusiness;
(4)
Cooperative marketing associations as provided by O.C.G.A. § 2-10-105;
(5)
Insurance companies as provided by O.C.G.A. § 33-8-8 et seq.;
(6)
Those businesses as provided by O.C.G.A. § 48-5-355;
(7)
Agricultural products and livestock raised in the state as provided by O.C.G.A. § 48-5-356;
(8)
Depository financial institutions as provided by O.C.G.A. § 48-6-93; and
(9)
Facilities operated by a charitable trust, furnishing for value rooms, lodging or accommodations as provided by O.C.G.A. § 48-13-55.
