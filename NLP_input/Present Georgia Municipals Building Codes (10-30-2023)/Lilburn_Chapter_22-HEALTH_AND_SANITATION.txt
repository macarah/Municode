Chapter 22 - HEALTH AND SANITATION[1]

Sec. 22-1. - Food service; compliance with county board of health regulations.
All persons owning, operating or constructing food service establishments or engaged in food service shall comply with the rules of the county board of health pertaining thereto.
(Code 2001, § 8-12-1; Ord. of 9-8-1997)
Cross reference— Food service rules, app. C, art. I.
Sec. 22-2. - On-site sewage management system contractors; compliance with county board of health regulations.
All persons owning, operating or constructing on-site sewage management systems or doing business as an on-site sewage management system contractor shall comply with the rules of the county board of health pertaining thereto.
(Code 2001, § 8-10-1; Ord. of 9-8-1997)
Cross reference— Onsite sewage management rules, app. C, art. II.
Sec. 22-3. - Public swimming pools; compliance with county board of health regulations.
All persons owning, operating or constructing swimming pools shall comply with regulation number 5 of the county board of health.
(Code 2001, § 8-9-1; Ord. of 9-8-1997)
Cross reference— Swimming pool rules, app. C, art. III.
Sec. 22-4. - Tourist courts; compliance with county board of health regulations.
All persons owning, operating or constructing tourist courts shall comply with the rules of the county board of health pertaining thereto.
(Code 2001, § 8-11-1; Ord. of 9-8-1997)
Cross reference— Tourist court rules, app. C, art. IV.
Sec. 22-5. - Vector control; compliance with county board of health regulations.
All persons shall comply with the rules of the county board of health pertaining to vector control.
(Code 2001, § 8-13-1; Ord. of 9-8-1997)
Cross reference— Vector control rules, app. C, art. V.
