Editor's note— An ordinance adopted Sept. 17, 1991, did not specifically amend the Code; hence, its inclusion herein as Art. XV, § 6-270 was at the discretion of the editor.

Sec. 6-270. - Code adopted.
There is hereby adopted for the purpose of establishing standards and procedures for structural strength, stability, sanitation, adequate light and indoor air quality, and safety to life and property from fire and other hazards incident to the alteration, repair, removal, demolition, use and occupancy of existing buildings and structures, the Standard Existing Code, latest edition, published by the Southern Building Code Congress International, Inc., 900 Montclair Road, Birmingham, Alabama 35212-1206, which code is hereby adopted by reference thereto. At least one (1) copy of the code is on file in the office of the building official.
(Ord. of 9-17-91; Ord. of 8-19-97)
