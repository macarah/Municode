ARTICLE IX. - EXCAVATION AND GRADING

Sec. 6-180. - Excavation and grading code adopted by reference.
There is hereby adopted by reference as fully and to the same extent as if set out at length herein, subject to all amendments and conflicting provisions contained in this Code of Ordinances, that certain published code known as the Standard Excavation and Grading Code, latest edition as adopted and amended by the Georgia Department of Community Affairs (DCA) one (1) copy of which is on file with the office of clerk of the city, which shall be the official copy of such code and so designated by the city clerk.
(Ord. No. 501, § 10, 1-12-98)
