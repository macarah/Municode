State Law reference— Pedestrians, O.C.G.A. § 40-6-90 et seq.

Sec. 17-171. - Use of crosswalks.
Pedestrians shall not cross any street or roadway in a business district or any designated streets or highways except in a crosswalk.
(Code 1976, § 8-5003.1)
Sec. 17-172. - Crossing at right angles.
No pedestrian shall cross a roadway at any place other than by a route at right angles to the curb or by the shortest route to the opposite curb, except in a crosswalk.
(Code 1976, § 8-5003.2)
