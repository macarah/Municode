Sec. 3-1. - Load limits.
Except where otherwise posted, the load limit on streets and highways in the county shall be as provided by state law or regulation.
State Law reference— Dimensions and weight of vehicles and loads, O.C.G.A. §§ 32-6-20 et seq., 40-6-371.
Sec. 3-2. - Speed limits.
Except where otherwise posted, the speed limit on streets and highways in the county shall be as provided by state law or regulation.
Editor's note— The laws and regulations referenced in § 3-2 are not set out at length herein, but are on file and available for inspection in the office of the county clerk.
State Law reference— Speed restrictions, O.C.G.A. § 40-6-180 et seq.; state and local speed zones, O.C.G.A. §§ 40-6-182 et seq., 40-6-371.
