ARTICLE I. - PREAMBLE AND ENACTMENT CLAUSE

Sec. 101. - Enactment.
For the purposes of promoting the health, safety, morals, convenience, order, prosperity and general welfare of the county; to encourage economically sound and stable land developments; to assure the provision of required streets, utilities, and other facilities and services to land developments; to assure the adequate provision of safe and convenient traffic access and circulation, both vehicular and pedestrian, in land development; to assure the provision of needed public open spaces and building sites in land developments through the dedication or reservation of land for recreational, educational, and other public purposes; and to assure that land is developed in conformity with the Comprehensive Plan of the Harris County Board of Commissioners [the Board of Commissioners] of Harris County, Georgia, do ordain and enact into law the following articles and sections.
(Ord. No. 4-92, 12-15-92)
Sec. 102. - Reserved.
Editor's note— Ord. No. 1-93D, adopted Oct. 5, 1993, provided for the deletion of § 102, Exemptions, in its entirety. Such section was derived from provisions adopted at a public hearing held Jan. 2, 1990; and Ord. No. 4-92, adopted Dec. 15, 1992.
