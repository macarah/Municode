Editor's note— The City of Albany sponsors a contributory defined benefit pension plan covering substantially all employees. Copies of applicable ordinances are on file in the offices of the city clerk and city human resources department.
State Law reference— Municipal authority to establish, maintain and modify merit systems, retirement systems and insurance plans for its employees, Ga. Const. 1983, art. IX, § II, ¶ III(a)(14), O.C.G.A. § 36-34-2(4); municipal authority to fix compensation and benefits for its employees and members of its governing authority, O.C.G.A. § 36-35-4; the state Municipal Employees Benefit System, O.C.G.A. § 47-5-1 et seq.

Sec. 2-589. - Records.
The city shall keep such records and make such reports as may be required by applicable state or federal laws or regulations.
(Code 1975, § 19-55; Code 1985, § 2-230)
Sec. 2-590. - Certain agreements with employees not covered by article prohibited.
There is hereby excluded from this article any authority to make any agreement with respect to any position or any employee or official not authorized to be covered by applicable state or federal laws or regulations.
(Code 1975, § 19-56; Code 1985, § 2-231)
