ARTICLE X - LEGAL PROVISIONS

Section 10.1 - Provisions of charter severable.
The provisions of this charter are severable, and if any part thereof shall be held contrary to the constitution or the laws of the state the same shall not affect the remaining parts thereof.
(Ga. L. 1977, p. 3541, Sec. 10.1)
Section 10.2 - Penalties.
Violations of any provisions of this charter, for which a penalty is not specifically provided for herein, is hereby declared to be a misdemeanor and shall be punishable by a fine of not more than $500 or by imprisonment not to exceed 60 days or both such fine and imprisonment.
(Ga. L. 1977, p. 3541, Sec. 10.2 as amended by Ord. No. 4196, 8/14/85)
Section 10.3 - Taking effect; repeal of conflicting law.
The provisions of this act shall become effective January 1, 1978.
All laws and parts of laws in conflict with this act be and the same are hereby repealed.
(Ga. L. 1977, p. 3541, Sec. 10.3)
