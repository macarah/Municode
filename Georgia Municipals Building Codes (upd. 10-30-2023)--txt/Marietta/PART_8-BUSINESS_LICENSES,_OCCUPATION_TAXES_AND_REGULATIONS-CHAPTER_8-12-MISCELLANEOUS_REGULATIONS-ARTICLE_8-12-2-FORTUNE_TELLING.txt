ARTICLE 8-12-2 - FORTUNE TELLING

8-12-2-010 - Unlawful to practice without occupation tax certificate.
It is unlawful for any person to practice astrology, palm reading, fortune telling, clairvoyant, or character reading in the city unless the person holds a valid occupation tax certificate to engage in said practice of astrology, palm reading, fortune telling, clairvoyant, or character reading.
(Code 1978, § 8-3001; Ord. No. 5390, § 3, 12/20/94; Ord. No. 5575, 6/12/96)
8-12-2-020 - Application form.
Applications for occupation tax certificates to practice astrology, palm reading, fortune telling, clairvoyant, or character reading in the city shall be made by the applicant upon a form prescribed by the city. The application shall be presented to the city manager for verification of information and compliance with this Code section. The form shall include, but shall not be limited to, the name and residential address of the applicant; the address where the business is to be conducted; the amount of bond or insurance posted, if required; a statement by the applicant as to arrests and dispositions for law violations by the applicant. The form shall also have attached thereto the statement of the chief of police as to the results of his or her investigation; a statement from the tax collector that there are no outstanding taxes, special assessments, fines, or other moneys owing to the city, either by the applicant or upon the property where the proposed business is to be conducted; a certification from city zoning personnel that the property whereon the business is to be conducted is properly zoned. No application shall be accepted by the city unless all of the information and statements provided for by this section accompany the application.
(Code 1978, § 8-3002; Ord. No. 5390, § 3, 12/20/94; Ord. No. 5575, 6/12/96; Ord. No. 5762, 10/8/97, § 8)
8-12-2-030 - Investigation of character.
The chief of police shall cause an investigation to be conducted into the character of each and every applicant for an occupation tax certificate to practice astrology, palm reading, fortune telling, clairvoyant, or character reading. Each applicant shall be fingerprinted and the fingerprints checked locally to determine any undisclosed criminal record.
(Code 1978, § 8-3003; Ord. No. 5390, § 3, 12/20/94; Ord. No. 5575, 6/12/96)
8-12-2-040 - Payment of taxes.
The city manager shall cause an inquiry to be made into the city tax records to determine if the applicant has any outstanding taxes or special assessments that are delinquent against the subject property or any other monies owing to the city.
(Code 1978, § 8-3004; Ord. No. 5390, § 3, 12/20/94; Ord. No. 5575, 6/12/96)
8-12-2-050 - Grant of privilege, standards for granting.
A.
All occupation tax certificates herein shall be a mere grant or privilege to carry on those businesses during the term of the occupation tax certificate subject to all the terms and conditions imposed by the charter and related laws, applicable provisions of this Code and other ordinances of the city relating to those businesses.
B.
Occupation tax certificates may be granted hereunder only if the investigation reveals that the applicant, if an individual, or the officers or directors, if the applicant is a corporation, or partners, including limited partners, if the applicant is a partnership, has not been convicted or pled guilty to any crime related to the practice of fortune telling or any other crime involving moral turpitude within a period of five years immediately prior to the date of application for the occupation tax certificate.
C.
Consideration of a license application by the city manager or his designee shall be in accordance with Section 8-4-080(E) of this Code.
(Code 1978, § 8-3005; Ord. No. 5390, § 3, 12/20/94; Ord. No. 5575, 6/12/96; Ord. No. 5762, 10/8/97, § 9)
