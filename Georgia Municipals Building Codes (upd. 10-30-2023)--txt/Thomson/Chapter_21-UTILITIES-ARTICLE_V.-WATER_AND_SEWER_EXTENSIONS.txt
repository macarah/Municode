ARTICLE V. - WATER AND SEWER EXTENSIONS

Sec. 21-201. - Notice of violations.
No person shall be convicted of violation of this article until he has been notified in writing by the city administrator of such violation at least five days prior to making a case for such violation.
(Ord. No. 455, § 5, 3-8-90)
Sec. 21-202. - Penalty for violations.
Any person convicted of violation of this article shall be punished in accordance with section 1-14.
(Ord. No. 455, § 7, 3-8-90)
Sec. 21-203. - Separate offenses.
After five days notice has been given to any person by the city administrator that each day thereafter that extensions to the water and wastewater system are not installed in accordance with this article, shall constitute a separate and distinct violation of this article and punishment may be imposed accordingly.
(Ord. No. 455, § 6, 3-8-90)
Sec. 21-204. - Design, construction standards.
All water and sanitary sewer extensions shall be designed and constructed to meet or exceed the minimum requirements of the Standard Technical Specifications and Detailed Drawings for Sanitary Sewer Extension and Water System Extensions dated January 1990 prepared by M. R. Chasman & Associates, P.C., Athens, Georgia.
(Ord. No. 455, § 1, 3-8-90)
Sec. 21-205. - Specifications for materials.
The following type materials shall be included in the standard specifications for the city water and wastewater systems:
(1)
Water system:
a.
Pipes three inches through 48 inches may be ductile iron, class 50 or greater, cement lined, and shall be in accordance with ANSI/AWWA C104/A21.4, latest revision.
b.
Pipes four inches through 12 inches may be PVC, working pressure of 100 psi or greater, cast iron OD sized, and shall be in accordance with AWWA C900, latest revision.
c.
Service lines may be copper as per ASDTM B88, type K or L; or galvanized steel pipe conforming to ASTM B36-10.
(2)
Wastewater system:
a.
Pipes six inches through 48 inches may be ductile iron class 50 or greater, cement lined, and shall be in accordance with ANSI/AWWA C104/A21.4, latest revision.
b.
Pipes six inches through 15 inches may be ABS or PVC truss pipe as per ASTM D2680.
c.
Pipes four inches through 12 inches may be single wall PVC as per ASTM 3034, S DR 35 or greater.
(Ord. No. 455, § 2, 3-8-90)
Sec. 21-206. - Inspections.
The water and sewerage superintendent for the city, or his authorized representatives, shall inspect and approve the installation of water and sewer extensions before backfill, observe the hydrostatic pressure and leakage tests to ensure that the findings are in accordance with the requirements of the specifications aforementioned and to perform additional testing as needed to ensure that the water and sewer extensions are constructed in accordance with good practices.
(Ord. No. 455, § 3, 3-8-90)
Sec. 21-207. - Nonconforming system.
There is no requirement that present lines not meeting the specifications prescribed by this article be replaced immediately, but it is intended that any further extensions of the water and wastewater systems shall conform to this article and the requirements hereof.
(Ord. No. 455, § 4, 3-8-90)
Secs. 21-208—21-230. - Reserved.
