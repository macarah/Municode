Sec. 8-1. - State law governs elections.
City elections shall be conducted pursuant to the Georgia Municipal Election Code [O.C.G.A. § 21-3-1 et seq.].
(Code 1977, § 8-4)
Sec. 8-2. - Voter eligibility.
Any person who is a resident of the city and who is registered with the Fulton or Coweta County board of registrars shall be eligible to vote in a municipal primary election.
(Code 1977, § 8-2)
State Law reference— Authority to so provide, O.C.G.A. § 21-3-123.
Sec. 8-3. - Qualification by candidates; time.
(a)
Candidates for any office must qualify at least twenty-two (22) days prior to and no more than fifty-two (52) days prior to any election. The actual dates of qualification shall be determined by the mayor and council.
(b)
The entrance fee for qualifying for mayorshall be determined pursuant to O.C.G.A. § 21-3-90. The entrance fee for qualifying for a councilperson shall be determinedf by O.C.G.A. § 21-3-90.
(Code 1977, § 8-7; Ofrd. No. 89-50, 10-2-89)
State Law reference— Filing notice of candidacy, O.C.G.A. § 21-3-91.
Sec. 8-4. - Polling place.
The city hall is designated as the polling place.
(Code 1977, § 8-5)
State Law reference— Fixing of polling places, O.C.G.A. § 21-3-163 et seq.
