Sec. 54-1. - Shortterm work program.
Pursuant to the Georgia Planning Act of 1989 (O.C.G.A. § 36-70-1 et seq.), the 1999-2002 five-year work program is adopted.
(Res. No. 99-03, 11-10-1998)
Sec. 54-2. - Provisions saved from repeal.
Any comprehensive plan adopted by the county is not repealed and shall continue in full force and effect.
