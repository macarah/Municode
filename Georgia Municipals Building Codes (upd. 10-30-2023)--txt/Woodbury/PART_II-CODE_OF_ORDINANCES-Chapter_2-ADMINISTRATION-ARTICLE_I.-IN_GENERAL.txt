ARTICLE I. - IN GENERAL

Sec. 2-1. - Adoption of seal.
The seal of the city shall be disc-shaped and shall have the words "City of Woodbury, Georgia" inscribed about the outer circumference and the words "Corporate Seal" inscribed across the center.
(Code 1980, § 1-110; Code 2001, § 2-1)
Sec. 2-2. - Records.
All municipal records, except those which by order of a state court or by law are prohibited from being open to public inspection, shall be open for personal inspection by any citizen of the state during the hours of operation of the administrative service.
(Code 1980, § 3-103(4); Code 2001, § 2-2)
State Law reference— Inspection of public records, O.C.G.A. § 45-6-6; offenses involving public records, documents and other items, O.C.G.A. § 45-11-1; records management plans for local governments, O.C.G.A. § 50-18-90; records management plan required, O.C.G.A. § 50-18-99(e); records as public property, O.C.G.A. § 50-18-102.
