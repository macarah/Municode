SECTION 13. - DUTIES OF LAND USE ADMINISTRATOR (COUNTY MANAGER), COUNTY COMMISSION AND COURTS ON MATTERS OF APPEAL

13.1 - Land Use Administrator (County Manager).
It is the intent of these regulations that the Land Use Administrator (County Manager) be chiefly:
A)
Serve as administrative secretary to the Planning Commission.
B)
Maintain public records concerning the administration of the Land Use Ordinance, including all maps, amendments, Certificates of Land Use Compliance, Special exception Uses, Variances, and records of public hearings.
C)
To the extent practical, collect data and keep informed as to the best Land Use practices in order to be qualified to make recommendations to the Planning Commission, all of which must be approved by the County Commission.
D)
Undertake other relevant duties as may be delegated by the County Commission.
13-2 - County Commission.
It is further the intent of these regulations that the duties of the County Commission shall be to adopt or reject proposed amendments to these regulations as detailed in Section 12. Matters of nonenforcement of this ordinance may be brought to the County Commission's attention by aggrieved parties only after the aggrieved party has followed the complaint procedure. Corrections to problems of non-enforcement shall be handled by the Board of Commissioners. Recourse from all decisions of the County Commission shall be to the courts as provided by law.
(Ord. of 9-8-2016; Ord. of 6-15-2017, Exh. A)
