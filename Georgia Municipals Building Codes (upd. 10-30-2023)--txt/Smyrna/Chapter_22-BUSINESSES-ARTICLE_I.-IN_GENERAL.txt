ARTICLE I. - IN GENERAL

Sec. 22-1. - Boardinghouses and rooming- houses; access of officers.
(a)
Every person owning or operating a boardinghouse or roominghouse in the city is required to permit any officer of the city to inspect the premises of such boardinghouse or roominghouse at all times during the day and night.
(b)
It shall be unlawful for any person owning, operating or controlling a boardinghouse or roominghouse to refuse access to any such officer desiring access for the purpose of inspection.
(Code 1977, § 15-32)
