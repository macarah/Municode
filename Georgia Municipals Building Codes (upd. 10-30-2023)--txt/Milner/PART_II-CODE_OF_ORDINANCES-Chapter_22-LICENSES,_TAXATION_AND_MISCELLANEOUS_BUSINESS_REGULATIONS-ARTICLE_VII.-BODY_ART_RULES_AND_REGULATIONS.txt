ARTICLE VII. - BODY ART RULES AND REGULATIONS

Sec. 22-201. - Body art rules and regulations.
The rules and regulations for body art studios and body artists adopted by the Lamar County Board of Health, as amended, are incorporated herein by reference and made a part of this article as if fully rewritten herein. Said rules and regulations shall be enforced by the City of Milner, and any violations thereof may be tried in the municipal court of the city or any other court of competent jurisdiction.
(Ord. No. 13-2, § 1, 11-14-2013)
