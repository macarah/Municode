Chapter 101 - GENERAL AND ADMINISTRATIVE PROVISIONS

Sec. 101-1. - Title.
The title of this subpart shall be known as the "Land Development Code of the County of Charlton, Georgia." This subpart may also be cited as the "Land Development Code" or the "LDC."
Sec. 101-2. - Applicability of subpart A, chapter 1.
Unless otherwise stated or where the context of the provision clearly shows a different intent, the provisions of chapter 1 under subpart A, General Code of Ordinances, shall apply to the titles contained in this subpart.
Sec. 101-3. - Fee requirements.
Reasonable fees to offset the costs of administration of this subpart may be set by resolution of the board of county commissioners, from time to time, unless state law requires them to be established by ordinance.
