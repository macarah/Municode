Chapter 46 - ELECTIONS

Sec. 46-1. - Election superintendent.
(a)
Creation of position; appointment. The position of election superintendent is created.
(b)
Compensation. The election superintendent shall receive such compensation as prescribed by council.
(c)
Duties and powers. The election superintendent shall have all of the duties and powers prescribed in O.C.G.A. § 21-2-70, and such other powers and duties as are fixed for him from time to time by the council or by state law. It shall be his duty to prepare and publish, in the manner provided by the Georgia Election Code (O.C.G.A. § 21-2-1 et seq.), all notices and advertisements in connection with the conduct of elections; he shall furnish the proper election officials forms and blanks, including tally and return sheets, paper ballots and other necessary forms, as required by law for the conduct of any such election.
(d)
Election contract with county. The Burke County Board of Elections and Registrars shall perform all duties as superintendent of elections for the city as specified in O.C.G.A. title 21 (Elections), ch. 2 (Elections and Primaries Generally).
(Code 1974, § 7-2; Code 1995, § 46-3)
State Law reference— Duty of municipal superintendent to conduct all municipal elections within his municipality, O.C.G.A. § 21-2-70; appointment of superintendent, O.C.G.A. § 21-2-70.1.
