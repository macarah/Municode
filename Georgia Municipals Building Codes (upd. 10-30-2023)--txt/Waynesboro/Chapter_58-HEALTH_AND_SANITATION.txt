ARTICLE II. - HOSPITAL AUTHORITY

Sec. 58-26. - Created; powers.
There is hereby created a hospital authority to function in the city and county, which shall be known as Waynesboro-Burke County Hospital Authority, all as provided for and contemplated under O.C.G.A. § 31-7-70 et seq., which authority shall have all the powers, rights, privileges and authority as provided in such statutes, as well as the duties defined therein.
(Code 1974, § 11-40; Code 1995, § 58-26)
State Law reference— Creation of hospital authority, O.C.G.A. § 31-7-72.
Sec. 58-27. - Area of operation.
The area of operation of the hospital authority shall be the city and county.
(Code 1974, § 11-41; Code 1995, § 58-27)
Sec. 58-28. - Membership; term of office; certificate of appointment.
The hospital authority shall consist of nine members, three to be appointed by the city council and six to be appointed by the board of commissioners of the county. Should a vacancy occur by the death, resignation, expiration of term or otherwise, of any of the city-appointed members, the council shall forthwith nominate and appoint to the hospital authority a successor to fill the expired term. The terms of office shall be four years. Each city-appointed member shall be a resident of the city. A certificate of appointment or reappointment of any member shall be filed with the city manager/clerk and such certificate shall be conclusive evidence of the date and proper appointment of such member.
(Code 1974, § 11-42; Code 1995, § 58-28)
