Secs. 13-1—13-25. - Reserved.
ARTICLE II. - TRUCK TRAFFIC

Sec. 13-26. - Through routes—Designation.
The county shall determine on which roads in the county through truck traffic is prohibited and shall post a legible notice showing such prohibition, each notice to conform to the regulations of the department of transportation promulgated under authority of O.C.G.A. § 32-6-50.
(Res. of 11-1-88, § 1)
Sec. 13-27. - Same—Prohibited use.
It shall be unlawful for any person to haul, drive, or bring on any road in the county road system any truck with the exception of light trucks or pickups, on any such road which may be posted "through trucks prohibited," except for such trucks as may be driven on such roads for local traffic. Any violation shall be punished in accordance with the provisions of section 1-12.
(Res. of 11-1-88, § 2)
Sec. 13-28. - Same—Trial of violators.
Violations of this article shall be tried by the magistrate court of the county pursuant to O.C.G.A. § 15-10-60 et seq.
(Res. of 11-1-88, § 3)
Secs. 13-29—13-50. - Reserved.
ARTICLE III. - SPEED LIMITS

Sec. 13-51. - 45 mph on Rumble Road.
It shall be unlawful for any person to operate any vehicle on Rumble Road (County Road #34) from a point one thousand (1,000) feet east of the east end of the I-75 bridge to a point one thousand (1,000) feet west of the west end of I-75 bridge, a distance of 0.41 miles, in excess of forty-five (45) miles per hour.
(Res. of 4-5-88)
Sec. 13-52. - Signs.
Signs giving notice of the speed limits set forth in this article shall be erected by the county.
(Res. of 4-5-88)
