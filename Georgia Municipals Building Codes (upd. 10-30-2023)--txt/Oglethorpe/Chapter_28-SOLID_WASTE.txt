Sec. 28-1. - Garbage collection fee.
(a)
There shall be a garbage collection fee for all residences, businesses, and schools within the corporate limits of the city in the amount established by the city.
(b)
For all purposes of this chapter, the term "garbage" means that normal and ordinary waste and refuse incident to the operation and maintenance of a household, business, or school, and includes only such waste and refuse as can be placed in garbage containers. The term does not include the waste scraps from a manufacturing businesses.
(c)
The garbage collection fee shall be included on the monthly water and sewerage bill where applicable; and in the event a consumer does not receive a water and sewerage bill, he shall be billed separately for the garbage collection fee. Nonpayment of the garbage collection fee shall be collected by cutoff of all services, same to include garbage collection, water and sewerage services, and such other sanctions as are authorized by law.
(Code 1986, § 12-3)
