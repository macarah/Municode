Sec. 14-1. - Voting places.
The voting places or election locations shall be designated by the Coffee County Board of Election and Registration for all municipal elections held within the city.
(Code 1978, § 2-2; Code 1993, pt. II, § 2-437; Ord. No. 05-28.1-91, 5-28-1991)
State Law reference— Duty of governing authority to fix location of polling places, O.C.G.A. § 21-2-265.
