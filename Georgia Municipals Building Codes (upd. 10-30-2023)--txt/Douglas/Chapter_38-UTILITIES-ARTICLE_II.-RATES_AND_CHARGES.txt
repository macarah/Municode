ARTICLE II. - RATES AND CHARGES

Sec. 38-27. - Tap fees for water, gas and sewer.
(a)
Water. For making taps and setting meters inside and outside the city limits, the prices set forth in the schedule of fees and charges apply. For any meter larger than two inches, the charge shall be the actual cost of meter plus backflow valve plus gate valves and customer builds the meter box. For the fire tap only, the charge shall be the actual cost of tap plus an amount per year/per riser shall apply.
(b)
Sewer. Sewer tap fees shall be as set forth in the schedule of fees and charges. Water meters installed for the purpose of watering lawns, filling pools, etc., shall not be charged sewer usage.
(c)
Natural gas. For making taps and setting meters inside and outside the city limits, the prices set forth in the schedule of fees and charges shall apply.
(Code 1978, § 23-20; Code 1993, pt. II, § 90-36; Ord. No. 08-27.1-90, § 23-20, 8-27-1990; Ord. No. 12.09.1-91, § 23-20, 12-9-1991)
Sec. 38-28. - Water rate schedule.
The water rates shall apply in the furnishing of water services by the city through its municipally owned water system:
(1)
Inside city. The monthly water rates set forth in the schedule of fees and charges shall apply to commercial, industrial and residential users within the city.
(2)
Outside city. The charge for the provision of water outside the corporate limits of the city shall be twice the current rate charged for the provision of water to all classes of users of water inside the corporate limits of the city.
(Code 1978, § 23-21; Code 1993, pt. II, § 90-37; Ord. No. 12.09.1-91, § 23-21, 12-9-1991)
Sec. 38-29. - Sewer rate schedule.
(a)
Inside city. The rates set forth in the schedule of fees and charges shall apply in the furnishing of sewer services by the city through its municipally owned sewer system.
(b)
Outside city. The charge for the use of sewer facilities outside the corporate limits of the city shall be twice the current rate charged for all classes of users of sewer facilities inside the corporate limits of the city.
(Code 1978, § 23-22; Code 1993, pt. II, § 90-38; Ord. No. 12.09.1-91, § 23-22, 12-9-1991)
Sec. 38-30. - Gas rates.
The rates and other charges for metered gas service to customers in the city shall be according to the schedule of fees and charges.
(Code 1978, § 23-23; Code 1993, pt. II, § 90-39)
Sec. 38-31. - Gas metering, billing.
(a)
In the operation of the gas department of the city, all services shall be fully metered and no free services shall be rendered to any person.
(b)
There shall be a monthly rendition of bills.
(Code 1978, § 23-24; Code 1993, pt. II, § 90-40)
Sec. 38-32. - Electric rate schedule.
The schedule of rates and charges applicable to the service furnished by the electric department shall be according to the schedule of fees and charges.
(Code 1978, § 23-25; Code 1993, pt. II, § 90-41)
Sec. 38-33. - Change of rate schedules without notice.
The rate schedules for water, electricity and sewage disposal services of the city hereinafter specified are subject to review and change by the board of commissioners without notice to the user.
(Code 1978, § 23-26; Code 1993, pt. II, § 90-42)
Sec. 38-34. - Due date; discontinuance of service for nonpayment.
The charges for utility services shall be payable within 15 days from the billing date. A ten percent penalty shall be assessed as of the 16th day. Should any of the charges not be paid within an 18-day period from the billing date, any or all of the service may be immediately discontinued.
(Code 1978, § 23-27; Code 1993, pt. II, § 90-43)
Sec. 38-35. - Execution in case of delinquency.
The city shall have the authority and be vested with the power to issue an execution and the same placed on record in the office of the clerk of the superior court of the county, to enforce the collection of any sum owed to the city by any person for the use of water, wastewater, electrical current, gas or other public utility of the city, and such executions shall be issued, handled and collected under the same rules and regulations as set forth in the Charter of the city, section 6.12, for the issuance, handling and collection of executions for taxes due the city.
(Code 1978, § 23-28; Code 1993, pt. II, § 90-44)
Secs. 38-36—38-58. - Reserved.
