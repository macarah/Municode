Sec. 2-724. - Depositories.
The depositories for regular funds of the city, excluding funds that are held in connection with sinking funds that have to be deposited in designated banks, shall be such banks as shall be designated by the board of commissioners. The city clerk shall deposit all funds of the city, not otherwise designated, in the banks designated as the depository for that year, on such conditions, as to bond, etc., as may be fixed and directed by the city manager and the board of commissioners.
(Code 1978, § 2-99; Code 1993, pt. II, § 2-1)
