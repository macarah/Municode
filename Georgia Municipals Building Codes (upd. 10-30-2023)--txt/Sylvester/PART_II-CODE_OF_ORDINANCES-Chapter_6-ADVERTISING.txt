Sec. 6-1. - Billposting.
It shall be unlawful for any person to post or cause to be posted any bills on any buildings in the city, except with the consent of the owner or occupant of the building and that of the mayor and council.
(Code 1965, § 14-51)
Sec. 6-2. - Drumming business in streets and other places.
It shall be unlawful for any person engaged in business in the city to solicit, by drumming in the streets and sidewalks, or anywhere within the city outside his own premises, the patronage or trade of any person.
(Code 1965, § 14-52)
