ARTICLE VII. - POULTRY HOUSES AND SWINE OPERATIONS[7]

Footnotes:

Cross reference— Animals, ch. 14.

Sec. 38-581. - Permit authority.
All permits will be obtained from the land development officer appointed and designated by the commissioner of the county.
Sec. 38-582. - Distance requirements.
No poultry house or any type of swine operation shall be less than 550 feet from the residence of another person, recreation park or center, hospital or convalescent home.
(Ord. of 8-8-1997, ch. X, art. II; Res. of 12-10-99, § 1)
Sec. 38-583. - Exemptions.
Any currently existing poultry or swine building shall be exempt from the 550-foot distance requirement in section 38-582; new structures must comply with the 550-foot requirement.
(Ord. of 8-8-1997, ch. X, art. III; Res. of 12-10-99, § 2)
Sec. 38-584. - Name on building permit.
(a)
The permit will be in the individual company's name who will be owning such operation.
(b)
Each individual obtaining such a permit shall furnish proof to the county building permit officer that such provisions of this article are complied with.
(Ord. of 8-8-1997, ch. X, art. IV)
