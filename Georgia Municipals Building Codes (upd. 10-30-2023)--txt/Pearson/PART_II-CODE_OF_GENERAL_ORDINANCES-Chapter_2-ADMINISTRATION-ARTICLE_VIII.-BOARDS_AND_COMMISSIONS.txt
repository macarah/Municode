ARTICLE VIII. - BOARDS AND COMMISSIONS

Sec. 2-428. - Recreation committee.
The recreation facilities and activities of the city shall be governed, regulated, and supervised by the recreation committee of the city. Any and all complaints regarding the recreation programs and use of facilities shall be referred to the recreation committee for appropriate action. Any problems which cannot be satisfactorily resolved by the committee may be appealed to the city council for resolution.
(Ord. of 5-19-1981)
