Note— The provisions of § 5-151 above were enacted by Ord. No. 90-1, adopted Jan. 11, 1990. These new provisions have been treated as replacing §§ 5-151—5-159, which pertained to similar subject matter and were derived from Ord. No. 80-2, §§ 1—9, adopted Aug. 28, 1980.

Sec. 5-151. - Code adopted; penalty.
(a)
For the purpose of regulating the installation, maintenance and repair of swimming pools, equipment and appliances, there is hereby adopted by the City of Port Wentworth the Standard Swimming Pool Code, latest edition, promulgated by the Southern Building Code Congress International, Inc., at least one (1) copy of which is on file in the office of the city administrator, and the same is hereby adopted by reference and made a part hereof as fully as if set out at length herein; subject, however, to all amendments, substitutions, deletions and modifications herein contained, and to all provisions of this Code of Ordinances in conflict therewith.
(b)
Any person violating any provision of this code shall be guilty upon conviction an shall be punished as provided by law for violation of a city ordinance.
(Ord. No. 90-1, 1-11-90; Ord. No. 91-8, 9-26-91; Ord. No. 96-05, 3-28-96; Ord. No. 97-3, 2-13-97)
