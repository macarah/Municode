ARTICLE XI. - CODE ENFORCEMENT DEPARTMENT

Sec. 2-450. - Created; composition.
There is hereby created the code enforcement department of the city. This department shall consist of the code enforcement officer and such other officers and employees as may be provided for by the city council.
(Code 1981, § 2-200; Ord. of 11-11-1974, § 1)
Sec. 2-451. - Office of code enforcement officer; city manager ex officio officer until appointment; supervision of employees.
The city council shall appoint the code enforcement officer on an annual basis. The city manager shall serve as ex officio code enforcement officer unless or until the office is filled by appointment. The code enforcement officer shall have control and supervision over all employees assigned to the code enforcement department, subject to the control of the city manager. Reference in this Code or any code adopted herein to building inspector or building official shall be deemed to refer to the code enforcement officer.
(Code 1969, § 5-1; Code 1981, § 2-201; Ord. of 11-11-1974, § 2)
Sec. 2-452. - Enforcing laws; making inspections; issuing permits.
The code enforcement officer shall enforce all laws and ordinances in the city relating to the construction, repair, alteration or reinspection of buildings or structures, and the use of streets or parkways in connection therewith, and other matters assigned to such officer, and shall make or cause to be made all inspections needed for that purpose. The code enforcement officer shall issue or cause to be issued all proper permits for such work after payment of the fees required therefor to the city clerk. If the positions of plumbing inspector, electrical inspector, mechanical inspector, or other such inspectors, are provided for, they shall be assigned to the code enforcement department.
(Code 1969, § 5-3; Code 1981, § 2-202; Ord. of 11-11-1974, § 3)
Sec. 2-453. - Enforcement of zoning and subdivision ordinances.
The code enforcement officer shall be the zoning enforcement officer of the city and shall see to the enforcement of the zoning ordinances and subdivision regulations (chapters 78 and 94).
(Code 1981, § 2-203; Ord. of 11-11-1974, § 4)
