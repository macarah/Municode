ARTICLE I. - IN GENERAL

Sec. 10-1. - Family entertainment.
(a)
Defined. For the purposes of this section, the term "family recreation center" shall mean a place of business open to the public which has as its primary form of entertainment billiards, video or pinball machines and other similar games.
(b)
Hours. No family recreation center shall be kept open, conducted or operated by any person within the city except during the hours of 8:00 a.m. to 11:00 p.m. on Monday through Thursday, between the hours of 8:00 a.m. to 12:00 midnight Friday and Saturday and between the hours of 1:00 p.m. to 6:00 p.m. on Sunday.
(c)
Unlawful occurrences. No family recreation center under this section shall permit or allow any drinking of intoxicants.
(Code 1985, §§ 8-2-1—8-2-3)
