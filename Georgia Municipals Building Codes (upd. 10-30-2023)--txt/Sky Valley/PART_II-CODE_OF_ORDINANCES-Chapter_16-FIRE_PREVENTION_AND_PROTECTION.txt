Sec. 16-1. - Fireworks, torches and incendiary devices.
(a)
It shall be unlawful for any person or agency to set off any fireworks, torch, or incendiary devices during extremely dry weather or conditions. The city council will determine the said time of the effectiveness of this chapter.
(b)
Any corporation or agency must apply for a permit to conduct an organized fireworks display within the city.
(c)
Any person violating this section will receive a warning on the first offense and be subject to section 1-8 on all subsequent offenses.
(Ord. No. 90-10, 7-2-1990)
State Law reference— Fireworks, O.C.G.A. § 25-10-1 et seq.
