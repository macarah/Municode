Sec. 282. - (Restatement) Death, disability benefits.
The City of Waycross, Georgia, shall have the power to grant pensions or other compensation to employees and/or to the dependents of any employee in any, each, and all departments thereof, where and when such employee loses his life or health or becomes totally and permanently disabled as the result of injuries incurred while actually engaged in the performance of his duties in the employ of said City of Waycross, Georgia.
