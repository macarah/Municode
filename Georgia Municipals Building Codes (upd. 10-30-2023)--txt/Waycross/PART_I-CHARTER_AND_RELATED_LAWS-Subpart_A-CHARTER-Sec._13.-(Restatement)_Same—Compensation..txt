Sec. 13. - (Restatement) Same—Compensation.
The city commission shall fix, and hereby establishes, the salary and compensation of the mayor in an amount not to exceed $12,600.00 per annum. The city commission shall fix, and hereby establishes, the salary and compensation of each of the other members of the commission in an amount not to exceed $9,600.00 per annum. The salary and compensation of the mayor and the other members of the commission as fixed and established by the commission shall be paid in equal monthly installments from the funds of the City of Waycross.
(1970 Ga. Laws (Act No. 819), p. 2222, § 1; Ord. No. 81-11, § 1, 3-3-1981; Ord. No. 21-06 , § 1, 3-16-2021)
Editor's note— Ord. No. 21-06 , § 2, adopted March 16, 2021, states that "the increase in salary shall not be effective and paid until "after the taking of office of those elected at the next regular municipal election which is held immediately following the date on which the action to increase the compensation was take" or, in other words, said increase in salary shall not be effective and paid until January 5, 2022. See O.C.G.A. § 36-35-4(a)(2)."
