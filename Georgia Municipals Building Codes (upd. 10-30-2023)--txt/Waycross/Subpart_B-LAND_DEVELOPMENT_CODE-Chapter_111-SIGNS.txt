Chapter 111 - SIGNS
ARTICLE I. - IN GENERAL

Sec. 111-1. - Signs on public property, etc.
No person shall place, post or locate, in whole or in part, any sign on any public property, public building, dedicated right-of-way as established by O.C.G.A. § 32-6-51 or public right-of-way if said right-of-way is maintained by the City of Waycross and shall not attach any sign or poster to any public sign or utility pole. Any sign or poster in violation of this section is declared to be a public nuisance, and city officials having jurisdiction of the public building, public property, dedicated public right-of-way or public right-of-way maintained by the city, including without limitation law enforcement officers, may immediately remove or direct the removal of the same.
( Ord. No. O21-09 , § 1, 4-6-2021)
