Sec. 290. - (Restatement) Definition.
The term "employee" as used in this Act shall be deemed to embrace and include all persons performing regular service in the employ of the City of Waycross, Georgia, in any, each, and all departments thereof, but the term "employee" as used in this Act shall not apply to any member of the city commission, nor to any officer elected by the people, nor to the city physician, city attorney, city recorder, nor to any casual or part-time employees.
