Sec. 10. - (Restatement) Same—Date of election.
The general election held in the City of Waycross for the election of members of the city commission and members of the board of education of the City of Waycross shall be held on the fourth Tuesday in November next preceding the expiration of their several terms.
(1966 Ga. Laws (Act No. 376), p. 3151, § 1)
