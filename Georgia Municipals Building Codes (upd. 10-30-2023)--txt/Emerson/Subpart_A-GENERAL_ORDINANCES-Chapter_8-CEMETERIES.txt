Sec. 8-1. - Loitering in cemeteries and disorderly conduct.
It shall be unlawful for any person to loiter or remain in any cemetery located in the city except during services therein, or to be engaged in disorderly conduct in or near the entrances to said cemetery. Each cemetery shall have posted therein at least one sign which states "No Loitering."
(Ord. of 7-27-1992)
