Sec. 18-151. - Police inspections.
All persons operating junk shops, yards and all such dealers who are issued a license for same shall accept the license on condition that their places of business shall be subject to visitation and inspection by the police of the city at any time, and on refusal of the dealer or person in charge of the place of business, it shall be closed at once on order of the licensing authorities.
(Code 1987, § 18-210)
