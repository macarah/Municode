Sec. 46-1. - Personnel policies, rules and regulations not affected.
Nothing in this article shall affect any ordinance or resolution of the governing authority of the city relating to the personnel policies, rules and regulations governing the officers and employees of the city. All such ordinances and resolutions are recognized as continuing in full force and effect to the same extent as if set out in this Code.
(Ord. No. 1508, § I, 11-5-2019)
Sec. 46-2. - Retirement plan.
(a)
Adopted by reference. The retirement plan for the employees of the city, as amended and restated from time to time, which is on file in the office of the city clerk, has been adopted by the governing authority of the city, and such plan is incorporated herein by reference, as if fully set forth. Such plan encompasses the adoption agreement, any addendum to the adoption agreement, the Georgia Municipal Employees Benefit System (GMEBS) Master Plan Document, and the GMEBS Trust Agreement, which constitute a contract between the city and GMEBS, all as authorized and provided by O.C.G.A. § 47-5-1 et seq.
(b)
Effective date. The effective date of the retirement plan, as amended and restated from time to time, shall be as of the date of adoption of the ordinance or resolution concerning such plan by the governing authority of the city.
(c)
Copy on file. A copy of the retirement plan, as amended and restated from time to time, is on file in the office of city clerk at city hall.
(Ord. No. 1508, § I, 11-5-2019)
