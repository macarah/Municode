CHAPTER 15.16 - FACTORY-BUILT, MODULAR AND CLOSED-WALL CONSTRUCTION BUILDINGS

Sections:
15.16.010 - Inspection.
All factory-built buildings, modular buildings or closed-wall construction buildings other than those fabricated on the location site shall be inspected by the city inspection department during construction at the fabrication or manufacturing location.
(Prior Code § 5-10-1; Ord. of 8-12-1985, § 10-1)
15.16.020 - Permit required.
It is unlawful to construct or install any factory-built building, modular building or closed-wall construction building on any land in the corporate limits of the city without first obtaining a permit therefor as provided in this chapter.
(Prior Code § 5-10-2; Ord. of 8-17-1987, § 10-2)
15.16.030 - Application—Fee.
Any person, firm or corporation desiring a permit for the construction or installation of any factory-built building, modular building or closed-wall construction building shall make application to the community development department director prior to the fabrication or manufacturing of such building. The application shall set forth the name of the manufacturer, the location of the manufacturer, date or dates of fabrication or manufacture, along with a copy of the plans and specifications for the building. The community development department director may require such other additional information as may be necessary to process the application. The applicant shall pay a permit fee of $3,500.00. The fee shall include all inspection fees for the inspection of the building at the point of manufacture or fabrication as well as inspection at the installation site, including footing and foundation inspection.
(Prior Code § 5-10-3; Ord. of 10-3-1995, § 2(part); Ord. of 8-12-1985, § 10-3; Ord. No. 05-02-07A, § 22, 2-7-2005; Ord. No. 06-05-01(A), § 23, 5-1-2006)
15.16.040 - Violations—Penalties.
Any person, firm or corporation who may violate the provisions of this chapter, upon conviction thereof, shall be punished as provided by section 1.08.010 of this code.
(Prior Code § 5-10-4; Ord. of 8-12-1985, § 10-4; Ord. of 10-3-1995, § 2(part))
