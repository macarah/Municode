Sec. 44-19. - Uniform Rules of the Road adopted.
(a)
Adoption by reference. Pursuant to O.C.G.A. §§ 40-6-372 through 40-6-376, the provisions of O.C.G.A. §§ 40-6-1 to 40-6-395 (except for §§ 40-6-393 and 40-6-394), known as the Uniform Rules of the Road, and the definitions contained in O.C.G.A. §§ 40-1-1 and 40-2-20 and O.C.G.A. § 40-5-20 are hereby adopted as and for the traffic regulations of the city with like effect as if recited herein.
(b)
Penalties. Unless another penalty is expressly provided by law, any person convicted of a violation of any provision of this section shall be punished by a fine according to the penalties described in the general provisions chapter.
(c)
Suspended or revoked licenses. Pursuant to O.C.G.A. § 40-5-121, the municipal court shall be authorized to impose punishment in accordance with state law for convictions of driving while a license is suspended or revoked.
(d)
Speed limits. The city shall, from time to time, adopt by ordinance a list of roads and speed limits to be maintained by the city administrator.
(Code 1992, § 11-101)
