Editor's note— The city compiles and publishes separate, additional administrative regulations and related matters in its manual of personnel policies and procedures. The manual incorporates the retirement plan for city employees and includes regulations pertaining to operation of the police and fire departments. Official copies of all documentation are on file in the office of the city clerk.

Sec. 2-71. - Mayor's recommendations.
The mayor shall recommend the appointment of the city officers, boards and committee members.
Sec. 2-72. - Departmental organization.
The administrative service of the city may be divided into the following departments and department heads:
Department of public works, director of public works
Police department, police chief
Fire department, fire chief
(Code 1976, § 3-601)
Sec. 2-73. - Provisions saved from repeal.
The ordinance adopted January 8, 1997, which created the office of city manager is not repealed and shall continue in full force and effect.
