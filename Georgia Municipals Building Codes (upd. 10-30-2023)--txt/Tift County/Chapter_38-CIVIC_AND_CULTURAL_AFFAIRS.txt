ARTICLE II. - ARTS EXPERIMENT STATION

Sec. 38-36. - Designation as agency concerned with arts and humanities.
The board of commissioners of the county designates the Arts Experiment Station as the county's public agency specifically concerned with the arts and humanities of the county.
(Res. of 5-27-92, § 1)
Sec. 38-37. - Responsibilities.
The responsibilities of the Arts Experiment Station are as follows:
(1)
Coordinate county-wide efforts to develop the arts and humanities and to formulate and implement goals for the cultural development of the county.
(2)
Serve as coordinator among county, state and federal agencies, other organizations and institutions which provide and sustain cultural services in our county.
(3)
Serve in accordance with policies and procedures developed and promulgated by the Arts Experiment Station, as the official arts agency for the county. In that capacity, the Arts Experiment Station will make recommendations to the board of commissioners of the county for funding and support to local cultural agencies under federal, state and county guidelines.
(Res. of 5-27-92, § 2)
