DIVISION 2. - ADMINISTRATION OF HUMAN RESOURCES SYSTEM

Sec. 2-323. - Responsibility for administration, enforcement of rules and regulations.
The responsibility for administration and enforcement of human resources rules and regulations is vested in the human resources director per guidelines established by council. The human resources director (sometimes "human resources") shall have responsibility for day-to-day operation of human resources. The manager and city clerk share this role, unless otherwise decided by vote of the council.
