Editor's note— Ord. No. 05-03, §§ 1-3, adopted May 5, 2005, did not specifically amend this Code. Hence, inclusion of said ordinance provisions was at the editor's discretion.

Sec. 2-276. - [Department created.]
The Department of Environmental Compliance and Animal Control is created pursuant to authority granted in the City Charter of the City of Ashburn section 1.13(p).
(Ord. No. 05-03, § 1, 5-5-2005)
Sec. 2-277. - [Department head to be recommended.]
Pursuant to Section 2.33(1) of the City Charter of the City of Ashburn, the city manager is instructed to recommend for approval by the city council a department head for the Department of Environmental Compliance and Animal Control.
(Ord. No. 05-03, § 2, 5-5-2005)
Sec. 2-278. - [Duties.]
The department head shall enforce and carry out the provisions of the City of Ashburn Property Maintenance Ordinance, Animal Control Ordinance, and Business and Occupation Tax Ordinance, all as amended from time to time, and shall enforce and carry out the provisions of such other ordinances and resolutions adopted by the mayor and city council when enforcement of such ordinance or resolution is vested in the Department of Environmental Compliance and Animal Control. The department head and employees of the department shall carry out any additional duties as assigned by the city manager.
(Ord. No. 05-03, § 3, 5-5-2005)
