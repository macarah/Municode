Chapter 15.04 - GENERAL PROVISIONS
Sections:

15.04.010 - Administration and enforcement of technical codes.
All permitting, enforcement and compliance activities with regard to the various technical codes adopted in this title shall be performed by the Henry County planning and zoning department.

15.04.020 - Mayor and council to hear appeals.
Any person aggrieved by any decision or action of the county planning and zoning department with regard to the technical codes adopted in this title may file an appeal of such decision or action with the mayor and council, which body shall exercise the powers and perform the duties of the boards of adjustments and appeals established by such codes.
