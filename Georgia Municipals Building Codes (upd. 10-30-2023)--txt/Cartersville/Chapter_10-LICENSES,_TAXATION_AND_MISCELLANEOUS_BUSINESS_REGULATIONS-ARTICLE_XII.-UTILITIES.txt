ARTICLE XII. - UTILITIES

Sec. 10-401. - Electric companies.
Any person, firm, company, or corporation furnishing electrical energy within the city limits of the City of Cartersville shall pay a license fee equal to four (4) percent of the gross sales of electric energy to customers, other than to the City of Cartersville, Georgia, service under rate schedules prescribed by the Georgia Public Service Commission within the corporate limits of the city, payable quarterly for the preceding quarterly period.
(Ord. No. 26-75, 12-30-75)
Editor's note— Ord. No. 26-75, adopted Dec. 30, 1975 was never included in the original codification of this Code, and at the request of the city is hereby codified as §§ 10-401 and 10-402 of this Code as of May 5, 2000.
Sec. 10-402. - Natural gas companies.
Any person, firm, company, or corporation furnishing natural gas within the city limits of the City of Cartersville, Georgia shall pay a license fee equal to four (4) percent of the gross sales of natural gas to customers within the City of Cartersville, under rates prescribed by the Georgia Public Service Commission, payable quarterly for the preceding quarterly period.
(Ord. No. 26-75, 12-30-75)
Note— See the editor's note following § 10-401.
