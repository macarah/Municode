Sec. 2-196. - Election district; polling place.
The area within the corporate limits shall constitute an election district in the city. Those persons living within the designated election district shall vote at the Community House on Varnedoe Avenue.
(Code 1976, § 2-2001)
Sec. 2-197. - Required votes for election; primaries.
A candidate for municipal office shall be elected upon receiving a plurality of the votes cast for such office at the regular municipal election, and no primary elections for municipal offices shall be held.
(Code 1976, § 2-2004)
State Law reference— Vote required for nomination or election, O.C.G.A. § 21-3-407.
Sec. 2-198. - Appointment of a municipal superintendent of elections.
The Board of Elections of Chatham County, Georgia, is authorized to perform all duties of the municipal superintendent of elections in Garden City, Georgia, with the exception of the qualification of candidates which shall be the responsibility of a qualified individual appointed by council.
(Ord. No. 2011-1, § 1, 1-18-11)
Editor's note— Ord. No. 2011-1, § 1, adopted Jan. 18, 2011, repealed the former § 2-198, and enacted a new § 2-198 as set out herein. Former § 2-198 pertained to county board of elections to be municipal superintendent of elections and derived from Code 1976, § 2-2007 and Ord. of Sept. 21, 1987(2), § 1.
Sec. 2-199. - Qualification fee for municipal office.
Qualification fees for the offices of mayor and councilmember shall be governed by O.C.G.A. § 21-2-131, and any amendments thereto.
(Ord. No. 2011-1, § 2, 1-18-11)
Editor's note— Ord. No. 2011-1, § 2, adopted Jan. 18, 2011, repealed the former § 2-199, and enacted a new § 2-199 as set out herein. Former § 2-199 pertained to qualification fee and derived from Ord. of Aug. 21, 1995, § 1.
