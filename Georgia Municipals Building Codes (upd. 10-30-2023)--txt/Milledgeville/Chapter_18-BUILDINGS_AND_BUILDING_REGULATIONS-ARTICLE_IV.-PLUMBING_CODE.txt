ARTICLE IV. - PLUMBING CODE[5]

Footnotes:

Charter reference— Adoption of technical codes, § 13.
Cross reference— Utilities, ch. 90.

Sec. 18-181. - Adopted.
The latest edition of the Standard/International Plumbing Code, including the Appendix, as adopted and amended by the Georgia Department of Community Affairs, is hereby adopted as the official plumbing code of the city.
(Code 1986, § 5-36; Ord. No. O-02-03, 1-22-2002)
Sec. 18-182. - Enforcement.
The building official shall also be known as the plumbing official for the enforcement of this article.
(Code 1986, § 5-37)
Secs. 18-183—18-220. - Reserved.
Editor's note— Inasmuch as Ord. No. O-02-03 adopts the latest edition of the Standard/International Plumbing Code, the provisions of the former section 18-183, which pertained to amendments to the 1997 edition of the Standard Plumbing Code and derived from the Code of 1986, § 5-138, have been removed from this Code at the discretion of the editor.
