Sec. 18-301. - Adopted.
The latest edition of the Standard/International Mechanical Code, including the Appendix, as adopted and amended by the Georgia Department of Community Affairs, is hereby adopted as the official mechanical code of the city.
(Code 1986, § 5-66; Ord. No. O-02-03, 1-22-2002)
Sec. 18-302. - Enforcement.
The building official shall also be known as the mechanical official for the enforcement of this article.
(Code 1986, § 5-67)
