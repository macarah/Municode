Sec. 18-221. - Adopted.
The latest edition of the Standard/International Gas Code, including the Appendix, as adopted and amended by the Georgia Department of Community Affairs, is hereby adopted as the official gas code of the city.
(Code 1986, § 5-51; Ord. of 7-26-1988, § 1; Ord. of 3-12-1991, § 1; Ord. of 11-24-1992, § 1; Ord. No. O-02-03, 1-22-2002)
Sec. 18-222. - Enforcement.
The building official shall also be known as the gas inspector for the enforcement of this article.
(Code 1986, § 5-52)
Sec. 18-223. - Bond; license.
No person shall engage in or work at the installation, extension or alteration of consumer's gas piping or certain gas appliances, until such person shall have secured a license and shall have executed and delivered to the clerk and treasurer a good and sufficient bond in the penal sum of $205.00, with corporate surety, conditioned for the faithful performance of all such work, entered upon or contracted for, in strict accordance and compliance with the provisions of this article. The bond required in this section shall expire on January 1 next following its approval by the clerk and treasurer, and thereafter on January 1 of each year a new bond, in form and substance as required in this section, shall be given by such person to cover all such work as shall be done during such year.
(Code 1986, § 5-53)
