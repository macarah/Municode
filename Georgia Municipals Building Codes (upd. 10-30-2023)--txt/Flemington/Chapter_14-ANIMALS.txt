ARTICLE II. - DOGS

Sec. 14-19. - Dangerous dogs.
(a)
The purpose of this section is to implement the Dangerous Dog Control Law (O.C.G.A. § 4-8-20 et seq.).
(b)
Rabies control officers for the county board of health are designated as dog control officers for the city and are hereby given authority to carry out the duties of dog control officers as provided in the Dangerous Dog Control Law (O.C.G.A. § 4-8-20 et seq.).
(c)
The county board of health is hereby designated to hold, for the city, hearings provided for in O.C.G.A. § 4-8-24.
(Ord. of 12-28-1988, §§ I, II)
