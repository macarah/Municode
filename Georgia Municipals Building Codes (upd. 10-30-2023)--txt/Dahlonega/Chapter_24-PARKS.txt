ARTICLE II. - PUBLIC CONDUCT IN PARKS

Sec. 24-19. - Hancock Park, City Park, Anthony Park, and Wimpy Mill Park curfew.
The city council has determined that there will be a curfew set for Hancock Park, City Park, Anthony Park and Wimpy Mill Park to prevent violence and crime by persons utilizing the park within the evening hours.
( Ord. No. 2004-05, Amend. 1, § 1, 2-2-2015 ; Ord. No. 2022-04 , 2-7-2022)
Sec. 24-20. - Conduct prohibited.
It shall be unlawful for any person to loiter, wander, stroll, or otherwise be on or within the grounds of Hancock Park, City Park, Anthony Park and Wimpy Mill Park between the hours of 9:00 p.m. and 7:00 a.m. from April 1, through October 31, and between the hours of 6:00 p.m. and 8:00 a.m. from November 1, through March 31, except as permitted by formal written permission of the city.
( Ord. No. 2004-05, Amend. 1, § 2, 2-2-2015 ; Ord. No. 2022-04 , 2-7-2022)
Sec. 24-21. - Penalty for violation.
Each offense under this article shall be punishable as a misdemeanor via citation to the municipal court of the city, and each offense shall incur a fine of $100.00.
( Ord. No. 2004-05, Amend. 1, § 3, 2-2-2015 )
Sec. 24-22. - Other park policies and fees.
Other park policies and fees shall be as established by the city and on file with the city clerk.
( Ord. No. 2004-05, Amend. 1, § 4, 2-2-2015 )
