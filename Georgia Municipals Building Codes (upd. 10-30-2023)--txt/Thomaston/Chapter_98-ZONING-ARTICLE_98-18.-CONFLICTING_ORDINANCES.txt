ARTICLE 98-18. - CONFLICTING ORDINANCES

Sec. 98-18.1. - Conflicting ordinances.
A.
When the provisions of any other applicable statute require more restrictive standards than are required by this chapter, the provisions of that statute shall govern. When the provisions of this chapter impose more restrictive standards than are required in or under any other applicable statute, the provisions of this chapter shall govern.
