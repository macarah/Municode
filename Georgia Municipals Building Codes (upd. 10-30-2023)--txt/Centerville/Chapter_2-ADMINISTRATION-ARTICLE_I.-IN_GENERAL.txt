ARTICLE I. - IN GENERAL

Sec. 2-1. - City Hall business hours.
The hours City Hall will be open for business shall be as provided from time to time by the city council.
(Code 1992, § 2-1; Ord. of 12-5-1972(2))
Sec. 2-2. - Designation of official newspaper of city.
The Houston Home Journal is designated as the official newspaper of the city.
(Code 1992, § 2-2; Res. of 2-5-1963)
Sec. 2-3. - Records management.
The city council adopts all applicable state law regarding municipal records management plans and records retention. The city clerk shall be the coordinator of the city's records management plan and authorized to act for and on behalf of the city council in directing and coordinating all records management matters under the Georgia Records Act and records retention schedules approved by the state. The same records shall be maintained in accordance with approved retention schedules. No record shall be destroyed except as authorized by the state records retention schedule.
State Law reference— Inspection and copying of public records, O.C.G.A. § 50-18-70 et seq.; records management programs for local governments, O.C.G.A. § 50-18-99.
Sec. 2-4. - Identity theft prevention program.
In compliance with 16 CFR 681.2 and for purposes of detecting, preventing and mitigating identity theft by identifying and detecting identity theft red flags and by responding to such red flags in a manner that will prevent identity theft, the city has adopted an identity theft prevention program, a copy of which is available in the office of the city clerk.
(Ord. No. 2016-5 , 6-21-2016)
