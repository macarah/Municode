Sec. 2-267. - Use of county registration list; voter eligibility.
The city elects to use the voter registration list for the county. Any person who is a resident of the city and who is registered as an elector with the board of registrars of the county shall be eligible to vote in an election in the city. The registrar of the city shall notify the board of registrars of the county of this election and shall further request a list of the registered voters residing within the city.
(Code 1992, § 2-71; Ord. No. 91-8, §§ 1, 2, 5-7-1991)
State Law reference— Duty of county registrars to provide list of electors upon requirement of governmental subdivision within the county, O.C.G.A. § 21-2-227.
Sec. 2-268. - Type of voting equipment.
The type of voting equipment used for voting at each polling location shall be direct recording electronic (DRE) units.
(Code 1992, § 2-72; Ord. No. 2007-4, 6-19-2007)
State Law reference— Voting machines generally, O.C.G.A. § 21-2-320 et seq.; direct electronic recording voting systems, O.C.G.A. § 21-2-379.1 et seq.
Sec. 2-269. - Counting of ballots.
Absentee, advance, and in-person ballots will be counted using optical scan (AccuVote-OS) equipment.
(Code 1992, § 2-73; Ord. No. 2007-4, 6-19-2007)
State Law reference— Tabulation of ballots in primaries and elections in which DRE equipment is used, O.C.G.A. § 21-2-379.11.
