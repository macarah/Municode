ARTICLE VII. - BAIL BONDSMEN

Sec. 8-201. - Approval required.
Each bail bondsman wishing to operate in the city shall first be approved by the mayor and council before conducting any business within the city.
(Ord. No. 12-05-CCO, § 6-281, 4-3-2012)
Sec. 8-202. - Suspension or revocation of business.
(a)
Approval to operate as a bail bondsman in the city may be suspended by the mayor and council for violations of this article or of the laws of the state by the bail bondsman or by anyone acting in his employ.
(b)
Approval to operate as a bail bondsman in the city may be revoked for just cause in accordance with the provisions of this article and all other city ordinances governing the operations of businesses within the city.
(Ord. No. 12-05-CCO, § 6-282, 4-3-2012)
Sec. 8-203. - Applicable state law.
The provisions of O.C.G.A. §§ 17-6-30, 17-6-50 and all other laws of the state regulating bail bondsmen are hereby adopted in total and made a part of this article.
(Ord. No. 12-05-CCO, § 6-283, 4-3-2012)
Secs. 8-204—8-229. - Reserved.
