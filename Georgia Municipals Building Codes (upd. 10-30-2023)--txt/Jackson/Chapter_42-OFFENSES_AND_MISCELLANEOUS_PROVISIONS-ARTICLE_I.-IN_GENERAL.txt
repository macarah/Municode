ARTICLE I. - IN GENERAL

Sec. 42-1. - Outside burning; permit required; penalty for violation of section.
(a)
No outside burning of any nature shall be allowed in the city limits without first obtaining a permit for such purpose from the city's fire department. No rubber material which includes tires shall be burned at any time.
(b)
Violation of this section shall be punishable, upon conviction, as provided in section 1-11.
(Ord. of 7-21-1986)
Sec. 42-2. - False identification.
(a)
A person who gives a false name, address, or date of birth to a city police officer in the lawful discharge of his official duties with the intent of misleading the officer as to his identity or birthdate is guilty of a violation of this section.
(b)
Violations of this section shall be punished according to section 1-11 of this Code.
(Ord. No. 99-4, §§ 1, 2, 5-4-1999)
