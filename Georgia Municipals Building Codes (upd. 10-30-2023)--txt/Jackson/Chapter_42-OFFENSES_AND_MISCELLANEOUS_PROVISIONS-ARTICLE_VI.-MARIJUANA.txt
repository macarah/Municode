ARTICLE VI. - MARIJUANA

Sec. 42-141. - Definitions.
The following words, terms and phrases, when used in this article, shall have the meanings ascribed to them in this section, except where the context clearly indicates a different meaning:
Marijuana shall have the definition stated in O.C.G.A. § 16-13-21(16) as now codified or hereafter amended.
All other words and phrases contained in this article shall have the meanings ascribed to them in the definitions listed in O.C.G.A. § 16-13-21, which are hereby specifically incorporated herein by reference to same.
(Ord. No. 96-22, art. I(A), 5-21-1996)
Cross reference— Definitions generally, § 1-2.
Sec. 42-142. - Purpose.
The purpose of this article is to establish the offense of possession of marijuana to be tried in the municipal court of the city.
(Ord. No. 96-22, 5-21-1996)
Sec. 42-143. - Possession.
It shall be unlawful for any person to purchase, possess or have under his control one ounce or less of marijuana in the city.
(Ord. No. 96-22, art. I(B), 5-21-1996)
Sec. 42-144. - Penalty.
The municipal court of the city shall have authority to try those cited with possession of marijuana. Those convicted shall be punished as provided in section 1-11.
(Ord. No. 96-22, art. I(C), 5-21-1996)
