Article 10.7 - Insurance Companies

Section 10.7.1 - Insurers License Fees.
There is hereby levied for the year 2002 and for each year thereafter an annual license fee upon each insurer doing business within the City of Roswell, Georgia in the amount of $150.00. For each separate business location in excess of one not covered by section 10.7.2, which is operated and maintained by such company within the City of Roswell, Georgia, there is hereby levied a license fee in the amount of $150.00. For the purposes of this ordinance, the term "insurer" means a company which is authorized to transact business in any of the classes of insurance designated in O.C.G.A. § 33-3-5.
(2001-12-01, Amended, 12/03/2001)
Section 10.7.2 - License Fees for Insurers Insuring Certain Risks at Additional Business Locations.
For each separate business location, not otherwise subject to a license fee hereunder, operated and maintained by a business organization which is engaged in the business of lending money or transacting sales involving term financing and in connection with such loans or sales offers, solicits or takes application for insurance through a licensed agent of an insurer for insurance said insurer shall pay an additional license fee of $52.50 per location for the year 2002 and for each year thereafter.
(2001-12-01, Amended, 12/03/2001)
Section 10.7.3 - Gross Premiums Tax Imposed on Life Insurers.
There is hereby levied for the year 2002 and for each year thereafter an annual tax based solely upon gross direct premiums upon each insurer writing life, accident and sickness insurance within the State of Georgia in an amount equal to one (1) percent of the gross direct premiums received during the preceding calendar year in accordance with O.C.G.A. § 33-8-8.1. Gross direct premiums as used in this section shall mean gross direct premiums as used in O.C.G.A. § 33-8-4. The premium tax levied by this section is in addition to the license fees imposed by section 10.7.1 of this ordinance.
(2001-12-01, Amended, 12/03/2001)
Section 10.7.4 - Gross Premiums Tax, All Other Insurers.
There is hereby levied for the year 2002 and for each year thereafter an annual tax based solely upon gross direct premiums upon each insurer, other than an insurer transacting business in the class of insurance designated in O.C.G.A. § 33-3-5(1), doing business within the State of Georgia in an amount equal to two and one-half (2½) percent of the gross direct premiums received during the preceding calendar year in accordance with O.C.G.A. § 33-8-8.2. Gross direct premiums as used in this section shall mean gross direct premiums as used in O.C.G.A. § 33-8-4. The premium tax levied by this section is in addition to the license fees imposed by section 10.7.1.
(2001-12-01, Amended, 12/03/2001)
Section 10.7.5 - Due Date for License Fees; Delinquency.
License fees imposed in sections 10.7.1 and 10.7.2 shall be due and payable on the first day of each year. Any license fee not paid by February 15 of the year for which it is due shall be delinquent.
Section 10.7.6 - Reports Required.
(a)
Every insurance company subject to the license fees set forth in section 10.7.1 of this article shall file with the finance department on forms prescribed by the department a statement showing the names and addresses of its agents representing such company in the city; the location and person in charge of every business location within the city operated and maintained by such company; and such other reasonable information as may be required and, in addition, shall furnish complete information regarding the premiums received, from policies written on risks residing or located within the city. Such statements shall be verified by affidavit of an officer of such company and it shall be an offense for any person, firm, corporation or agent thereof to knowingly or willfully make or furnish a false or incomplete statement or report hereunder. Said statement shall be filed at the time of paying the license fee.
(b)
The person responsible for each insurance business location subject to license fees under section 10.7.2 shall file with the finance department a statement, on forms prescribed by the department, showing the address of the business location, the name of the persons writing insurance at such location, and the names of the companies represented at such location, and such other reasonable information as may be required. Such statement shall be verified by affidavit of the person in charge of such business location, and it shall be an offense under this Code for any person, firm, corporation or agent thereof to knowingly or willfully make or furnish a false or incomplete statement of the facts required by this article. Said statements shall be filed at the time of paying the license fee.
(c)
All reports and statements required to be filed under this article shall be confidential, but the information contained therein shall be for the use of the officers of the city.
(2001-12-01, Amended, 12/03/2001)
Section 10.7.7 - Enforcement.
(a)
The fees and taxes levied by this article may be enforced by execution in the same manner as other taxes of the city.
(b)
For a violation of this article the guilty person or company shall be punished as provided in section 1.1.3, and in addition to such punishment, such violation shall be grounds for refusing or revoking a license.
