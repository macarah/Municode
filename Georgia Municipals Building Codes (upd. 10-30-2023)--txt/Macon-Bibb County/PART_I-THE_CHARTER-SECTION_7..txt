SECTION 7.
On the effective date of this charter, the operations and employees of the Municipal Court of the City of Macon shall continue its operations without interruption resulting from the adoption of this charter. The employees of such court shall become employees of the restructured government of Macon-Bibb County. The court shall be known as the Municipal Court of Macon-Bibb County. The judge of the Municipal Court of the City of Macon shall be authorized to serve as the judge of said court. Vacancies in the office of judge of the Municipal Court of Macon-Bibb County shall be filled by appointment of the mayor. Such court shall have jurisdiction as provided by law for municipal courts for all covered offenses and all code and ordinance violations occurring in Macon-Bibb County.
