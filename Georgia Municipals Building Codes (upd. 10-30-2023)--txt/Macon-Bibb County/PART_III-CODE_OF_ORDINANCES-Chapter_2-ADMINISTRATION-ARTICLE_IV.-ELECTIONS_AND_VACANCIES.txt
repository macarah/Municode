ARTICLE IV. - ELECTIONS AND VACANCIES

Sec. 2-66. - Election; qualifications; terms; term limits.
(a)
The mayor and commission shall be elected in nonpartisan elections for four-year terms as provided in the Charter.
(b)
Qualifications for election of mayor and commissioner shall be as provided in the Charter.
(c)
The mayor shall be limited to serving two four-year terms of office and each commission member shall be limited to serving three four-year terms of office as provided in the Charter.
Sec. 2-67. - Filling of vacancies.
In the event of a vacancy in the office of mayor or commissioner, the vacancy shall be filled for the remainder of the term by special election or appointment as provided in the Charter.
