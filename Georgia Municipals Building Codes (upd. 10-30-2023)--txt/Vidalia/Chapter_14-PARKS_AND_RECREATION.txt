ARTICLE II. - RECREATION BOARD

Sec. 14-20. - Creation; purpose.
A recreation board is created.
(Code 1968, § 2-115)
Sec. 14-21. - Membership.
The recreation board shall consist of seven (7) members who reside within the city.
(Code 1968, § 2-116)
Sec. 14-22. - Appointment, terms of members.
The term of office of the members of the recreation board shall be for five (5) years and until their successors are appointed and qualified, except that for and at the pleasure of the council any member of the board may be dismissed from office and a new member appointed to fill the unexpired term, and except that the members of such board first appointed shall be for such terms that the term of one member shall expire annually thereafter. The members of the recreation board shall be appointed by the council, and vacancies shall be filled by the council by appointment for the unexpired term. No person shall serve more than two (2) consecutive terms on the board.
(Code 1968, § 2-117)
Sec. 14-23. - Organization.
Immediately after their appointment, the members of the recreation board shall meet and organize by electing one of their members president and such other officers as may be necessary.
(Code 1968, § 2-118)
Sec. 14-24. - Adoption of bylaws, rules and regulations.
The recreation board shall have power to adopt bylaws, rules and regulations for the conduct of its affairs and for the proper conduct of recreation in the city.
(Code 1968, § 2-119)
Sec. 14-25. - Advisory function.
The recreation board shall advise the city on public playgrounds, playfields, indoor recreation centers and other recreation facilities.
(Code 1968, § 2-120)
