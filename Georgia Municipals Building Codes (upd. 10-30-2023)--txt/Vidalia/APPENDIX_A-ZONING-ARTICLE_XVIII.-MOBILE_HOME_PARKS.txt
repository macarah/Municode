ARTICLE XVIII. - MOBILE HOME PARKS[2]
There shall be no future development and no authorization for the location of mobile home parks within the city limits of Vidalia from the effective date of this ordinance.
(Ord. of 12-14-98)

Editor's note— An ordinance adopted Dec. 14, 1998, deleted Art. XVIII in its entirety and enacted new provisions in lieu thereof. Formerly, such provisions derived from the 1994 Code and an ordinance adopted Nov. 21, 1994.
