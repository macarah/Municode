CHAPTER 1. - GENERAL PROVISIONS
ARTICLE I. - IN GENERAL
ARTICLE II. - NORTHEAST GEORGIA REGIONAL DEVELOPMENT CENTER

Sec. 1-201. - Northeast Georgia Regional Development Center.
(a)
Pursuant to 1996 Ga. Laws, p. 1493, Newton County has transferred its membership from the McIntosh Trail Regional Development Center to the Northeast Georgia Regional Development Center.
(b)
The text of this Act is incorporated as if fully set out herein.
