Chapter 3.08 - PROPERTY TAXES

3.08.010 - Tax imposed—Rate.
An annual ad valorem tax upon all real and personal property within the city is hereby levied. The millage rate therefor shall be established each year by resolution of the mayor and council.
(Added during 1991 codification)
3.08.020 - Tax due and payable—Refund conditions.
A.
All ad valorem taxes due the city shall be billed and paid in one (1) payment.
B.
Ad valorem tax bills, as adjusted to conform to the tax digest of the county shall be payable on or before November 15th, but in the event of adjustment by the State Revenue Commissioner, the city shall, within a reasonable time thereafter as may be determined by resolution of the mayor and council, after receipt from the county of the adjustment information, refund any overpayments or bill for any underpayments.
(Added during 1991 codification)
Editor's Note: Henry County provides all tax collection services to the city.
