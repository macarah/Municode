2.24.010 - Employee drug testing generally.
From time to time, the city may enact policies and procedures relating to drug testing for its employees in its employee policy manual. Employees will be subject to any such policies contained in said manual.
(Ord. No. OR 13-293, § 1, 2-11-2013)
