ARTICLE II. - TAXICABS[2]

Footnotes:
--- (2) ---
Cross reference— Businesses, ch. 18.

Sec. 74-31. - Compliance with provisions.
All persons or firms operating taxicabs in the city shall, in addition to obtaining the occupation tax certificate required by chapter 18 of this Code, comply with the regulations of this article.
(Code 1982, § 9-3-1)
Sec. 74-32. - Liability insurance.
Each vehicle shall be covered by public liability insurance for the protection of the passengers and public, in the amount of $100,000.00.
(Code 1982, § 9-3-2)
Sec. 74-33. - Qualifications of driver.
No taxicab shall be operated by a driver under the age of 18 years. Each driver shall be licensed as a chauffeur under state law.
(Code 1982, § 9-3-3)
Sec. 74-34. - Application for license.
Each operator shall file his application for license in both his individual, corporate or partnership name, as well as any trade name he may use.
(Code 1982, § 9-3-4)
