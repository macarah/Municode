ARTICLE I. - IN GENERAL

Sec. 20-1. - Fire hydrants.
(a)
Protection. All water fire hydrants in the city system are hereby protected from any type of entry, tampering, damage or adjustment of any kind except as authorized by the city fire department, and the mayor and council.
(b)
Permission to utilize required. Any other individual or entity seeking access to water at a hydrant must first apply for and receive written permission from the city fire department or the mayor and council and shall only be permitted entry or use of the hydrant for approved purposes when accompanied by a member of the city fire department or water superintendent of the city in order to ensure proper use of the hydrant.
(Code 1999, § 34-1; Ord. of 12-13-1983)
