Sec. 42-31. - Adopted.
Pursuant to the Georgia Planning Act of 1989, as set forth in O.C.G.A. § 36-70-1 et seq., the Eatonton/Putnam County Comprehensive Plan 1992-2012 is adopted. The master copy of such plan is on file in the office of the county clerk.
(Res. of 8-18-1992)
Editor's note— By resolution adopted November 18, 1997, the county adopted a five-year work program update in compliance with the Georgia Planning Act of 1989. An official copy of the work program update is on file in the office of the county clerk.
By a resolution adopted March 2, 2001, the county amended its five-year short term work program.
By resolution adopted August 21, 2007, the county adopted the community agenda portion of the 20-year Comprehensive Plan update. An official copy is on file in the office of the county clerk.
