ARTICLE V. - PACKAGE SALES OF MALT BEVERAGES AND WINES

Sec. 6-71. - Hours of operation, sales on election and Christmas Day.
(a)
The hours of operation of retail licensees for the sale of package malt beverages and wines shall be allowed daily (Sunday between the hours of 12:30 p.m. and 11:30 p.m.). Wholesale licensees may deliver to licensed retailers as needed.
(b)
The wholesale and retail sale of wine and malt beverages shall be lawful during the polling hours of any election provided.
(c)
The wholesale and retail sale of wine and malt beverages shall be lawful on Christmas Day.
(Ord. of 8-6-13, § 1; Res. of 2-6-18(2))
Sec. 6-72. - Age of employees.
No licensee shall allow or require a person in his employment who is under the age of 18 years to dispense, serve, sell or take orders for any malt beverages or wines. This restriction does not apply to persons under the age of 18 years employed in grocery stores or drugstores who are selling said beverages.
(Ord. of 8-6-13, § 1)
