ARTICLE XVIII. - SEVERABILITY

Sec. 28-553. - Severability.
Should any article, section or provision of this chapter be declared by a court of competent jurisdiction to be invalid, such action shall not affect the validity of this chapter as a whole or any part thereof other than the part so declared to be invalid; each article, clause and provision hereof being declared severable.
