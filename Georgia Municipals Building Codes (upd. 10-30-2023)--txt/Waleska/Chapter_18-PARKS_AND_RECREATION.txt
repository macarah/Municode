Chapter 18 - PARKS AND RECREATION
ARTICLE I. - IN GENERAL

Secs. 18-1—18-18. - Reserved.
ARTICLE II. - USE OF FACILITIES

Sec. 18-19. - Denial of permits for violations.
Any person convicted of a violation of this article shall be denied any park permit or other permission to utilize the facilities of any park or recreational facility in the city for a period of 60 days following any conviction for a violation of this article.
(Ord. of 8-18-1987(1), § 7)
Sec. 18-20. - Hours of operation; overnight camping.
(a)
No overnight camping shall be permitted in any park or recreational facility in the city.
(b)
The hours of operation for all parks and recreational facilities in the city shall be from sunrise until 11:00 p.m.
Sec. 18-21. - Motor vehicles.
No person shall operate any motorized vehicle, including motorcycles, mopeds or any other similar vehicle, in any part of or upon any road in any park or recreational facility in the city, except upon roads within such park or recreational facility and in designated parking areas. No motorized vehicle described in this section shall be parked or left standing at any place in any park or recreational facility in the city, except in areas designated for such purpose.
Sec. 18-22. - Open fires.
No open fires, except in picnic grills, shall be permitted at any time or at any place in any park or recreational facility in the city.
Sec. 18-23. - Firearms and explosives.
No person shall discharge any firearm or explosive, including firecrackers or fireworks, except with the express written permission of the city, in any park or recreational facility in the city.
