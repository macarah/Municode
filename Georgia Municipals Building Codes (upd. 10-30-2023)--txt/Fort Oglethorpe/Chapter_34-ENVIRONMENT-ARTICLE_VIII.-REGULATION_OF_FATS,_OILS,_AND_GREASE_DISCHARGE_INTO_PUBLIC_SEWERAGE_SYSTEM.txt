ARTICLE VIII. - REGULATION OF FATS, OILS, AND GREASE DISCHARGE INTO PUBLIC SEWERAGE SYSTEM[4]

Footnotes:
--- (4) ---
Editor's note— Ord. No. 2018-14, adopted August 13, 2018, repealed the former article VIII, §§ 34-341—34-354, and enacted a new article VIII as set out herein. The former article VIII pertained to similar subject matter and derived from Ord. No. 2008-11, adopted September 22, 2008.

Sec. 34-341. - Purpose.
The purpose of this article is to significantly limit fats, oils, and greases from entering the waste water collection and transmission system in order to reduce sanitary system overflows related to blockages caused by fats, oils, and greases.
(Ord. No. 2018-14, 8-13-2018)
Sec. 34-342. - Documents adopted by reference.
The documents identified in the following sections include any attachments, future additions, appendices, indexes, and amendments, including local amendments adopted by the City of Fort Oglethorpe.
(Ord. No. 2018-14, 8-13-2018)
Sec. 34-343. - FOG management program.
The Fort Oglethorpe Fats, Oils, And Grease (FOG) Management Program, dated July 2, 2018, is hereby adopted as the minimum standards for FOG management within the city.
(Ord. No. 2018-14, 8-13-2018)
