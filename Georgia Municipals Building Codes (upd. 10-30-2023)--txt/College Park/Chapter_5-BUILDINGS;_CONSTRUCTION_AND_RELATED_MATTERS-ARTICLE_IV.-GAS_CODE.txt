ARTICLE IV. - GAS CODE

Sec. 5-70. - Code adopted.
For the purpose of establishing rules and regulations for the installation, operation, maintenance and repair of gas piping systems and gas appliances and fixtures, in order to secure the public safety and consumer satisfaction, that certain gas code, known as the Standard Gas Code, 1997 edition, save and except such portions as are hereinafter stricken or modified, is hereby adopted and incorporated as fully as if set out at length herein. The provisions of such gas code shall control the installation, operation, maintenance and repair of gas piping systems and gas appliances and fixtures in the city, except where the provisions of such gas code are in conflict with this Code, in which case the provisions of this Code shall prevail. A current copy of the gas code, as amended, shall be maintained on file in the office of the city clerk where it shall be available for public inspection.
