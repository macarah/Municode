ARTICLE I. - IN GENERAL

Sec. 21-1. - Short title.
This chapter shall be known as the "College Park Secondhand Goods Ordinance."
(Ord. No. 2016-7, § 1(Exh. A), 2-15-16)
Sec. 21-2. - Findings and intent.
This chapter is adopted to address the interests of public safety, health and welfare of the community and to aid and assist in the recovery of stolen property, and to aid and assist local law enforcement in fulfilling their public safety functions.
(Ord. No. 2016-7, § 1(Exh. A), 2-15-16)
Sec. 21-3. - General policies and purposes.
(a)
Secondhand dealers, pawnbrokers, precious metals dealers, and secondary metal recyclers may operate only after the issuance of a license or permit for such operation by the city and only in the manner authorized by such license or permit. Transactions by these businesses may only occur through a license or permit holder who complies with the rules and regulations of this chapter and with the licensing, regulatory and revenue requirements of the state.
(b)
All licenses, permits, and work permit identification cards issued under this chapter are a mere grant or privilege subject to all terms and conditions imposed by this chapter and state law and are subject to being revoked by the city.
(c)
Each holder shall display the license or permit issued under this chapter prominently at all times at the outlet for which the license or permit is used. A separate license or permit must be issued for each outlet of sale and a separate application must be made for each outlet.
(Ord. No. 2016-7, § 1(Exh. A), 2-15-16)
