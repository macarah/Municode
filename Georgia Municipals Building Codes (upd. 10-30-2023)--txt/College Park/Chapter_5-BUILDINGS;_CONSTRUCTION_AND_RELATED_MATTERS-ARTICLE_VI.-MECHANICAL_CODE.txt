ARTICLE VI. - MECHANICAL CODE

Sec. 5-110. - Mechanical code adopted.
For the purpose of establishing rules and regulations for the construction, installation, alteration, maintenance and repair of heating, air conditioning, mechanical devices, and other similar work in the city, that certain mechanical code promulgated by the Southern Building Code Congress International, Inc., being particularly the Standard Mechanical Code, 1997 edition, save and except such portions as are hereinafter stricken or modified, is hereby adopted and incorporated as fully as if set out at length herein. A current copy of the mechanical code, as amended, shall be maintained on file in the office of the city clerk where it shall be available for public inspection.
(Code 1963, § 11-1; Ord. No. 87-1, § 1, 1-19-87; Ord. No. 88-11, § 9, 5-2-88; Ord. No. 91-13, § 1, 7-15-91; Ord. No. 2000-13, § 1, 5-15-00)
Sec. 5-111. - Amendments to code.
The mechanical code is amended in the following particulars:
(1)
Section 108.4 is amended to read as follows:
"Right of Appeal. All persons shall have the right to appeal the mechanical officer's decision to the Construction Board of Adjustments and Appeals."
(2)
Section 101.2.3 of the 1997 Standard Mechanical Code is deleted in its entirety and amended to read as follows:
"Fees for inspecting repairs, alterations and additions and new installations shall be established by a resolution of Mayor and Council."
(Code 1963, § 11-3; Ord. No. 87-1, § 2, 1-19-87; Ord. No. 91-13, § 2, 7-15-91; Ord. No. 93-1, § 3, 1-4-93; Ord. No. 2000-13, § 2, 5-15-00; Ord. No. 2010-25, § 6, 8-16-10)
Secs. 5-112—5-119. - Reserved.
