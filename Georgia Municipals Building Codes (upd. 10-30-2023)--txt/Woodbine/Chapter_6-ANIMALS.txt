Sec. 6-1. - Domesticated farm animals.
Domesticated farm animals shall not be permitted to be kept in the city. These shall include, but not be limited to, the equine family, cows and other members of the bovine family, the goat family, pigs, sheep and lambs, poultry (Roosters, bantams, guinea hens, quail, turkeys, peacocks, emus, and other such fowl from that family).
(Ord. of 08-05-2019(1))
