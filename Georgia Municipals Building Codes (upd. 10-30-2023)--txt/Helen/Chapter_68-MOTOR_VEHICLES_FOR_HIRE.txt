Chapter 68 - MOTOR VEHICLES FOR HIRE[1]

Editor's note— Ord. No. 15-08-01 , adopted Sept. 15, 2015, repealed the former Ch. 68, §§ 68-1—68-27, and enacted a new Ch. 68 as set out herein. The former Ch. 68 pertained to similar subject matter and derived from Ord. No. 14-08-02 , adopted Oct. 7, 2014.

Sec. 68-1. - Definitions.
The following words, terms, or phrases, when used in this chapter, shall have the meanings given in this section.
Vehicles for hire. Any and all automobiles, including taxicabs and ride share drivers, designed and used for the purpose of transporting less than 10 passengers for consideration or charges which are determined by agreement, contract, mileage, or length of time, not operating from a fixed terminal on a fixed route.
(Ord. No. 15-08-01 , 9-15-15)
Sec. 68-2. - Requirements.
Pursuant to O.C.G.A., § 40-5-39. No person shall operate a motor vehicle for hire within the city limits of Helen, Georgia unless such person:
(1)
Has a for-hire license endorsement pursuant to this Code section (O.C.G.A., § 40-5-39) and has liability insurance coverage in the amounts required by law for the class of motor vehicle being operated for hire and the requirements for limousine carriers, ride share networks and drivers, and taxi services, as applicable; or
(2)
Has a private background check certification pursuant to this Code section (O.C.G.A., § 40-5-39) and has liability insurance coverage in the amounts required by law for the class of motor vehicle being operated for hire and the requirements for limousine carriers, ride share networks and drivers, and taxi services, as applicable.
Any driver of a vehicle for hire, that fails to meet the requirements of O.C.G.A., § 40-5-39 shall be guilty of a misdemeanor, pursuant to O.C.G.A., § 40-5-39(g).
(Ord. No. 15-08-01 , 9-15-15)
