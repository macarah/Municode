ARTICLE VII. - EMPLOYEE BENEFITS

Sec. 2-168. - Worker's compensation insurance.
Certain elected officials of the county, while performing their elected duties, shall be covered under the Association of County Commissioner's of Georgia Group Self-Insured Worker's Compensation Fund.
(Res. of 12-9-1996)
Sec. 2-169. - Persons covered.
(a)
Those elected officials to be covered under the worker's compensation laws of the state are: all members of the board of commissioners, the sheriff, the probate judge, the clerk of the superior court, the tax commissioner, the coroner, and the magistrate judge.
(b)
Volunteer firemen shall be ensured under the worker's compensation laws of the state by a private insurance company. The county will assume payment of all premiums for said insurance.
(Res. of 12-9-1996)
