ARTICLE I. - IN GENERAL

Sec. 2-1. - Additional penalties for ordinance violations.
The provisions of the Jail Construction and Staffing Act (O.C.G.A. § 15-21-90 et seq.) are hereby placed into effect and the additional penalties provided for in such act are hereby imposed and shall be collected as provided for in such act. The funds collected pursuant to such article shall be expended for the purposes provided for in such act.
(Res. of 6-30-1990)
