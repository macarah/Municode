Sec. 3-1-1. - Disbursements.
All city disbursements shall be made only on the authorization and signature of the mayor, city manager and finance director, or any two thereof.
(Code 1977, § 3-1001)
Sec. 3-1-2. - Purchases and contracts.
(a)
No one may obligate the city on any contract unless it is by a contract or bond ordinance or pursuant to a purchase order issued by the city manager.
(b)
The city manager shall establish rules and regulations for all purchasing and procurement for the city, including bidding procedures as may be necessary, provided that these rules and regulations and any amendments thereto, shall be approved by the mayor and council prior to their effective date.
(Code 1977, § 3-1002)
Sec. 3-1-3. - Budget adoption.
(a)
The annual budget as adopted each year by the mayor and council is made a part hereof and incorporated into this code by reference as fully as if it were here set forth in full. Copies of the budget shall be available to the public in the office of the city clerk.
(b)
The budget shall take effect July 1 of each year and shall remain in effect through the following June 30.
State Law reference— Budgets and audits, O.C.G.A., § 36-81-1 et seq.
