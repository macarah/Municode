Sec. 8-7-1. - Reserved.
Editor's note— Formerly, § 8-7-1 provided for the incorporation of the zoning ordinance by reference. Inasmuch as such ordinance has been set out as App. A to this Code, § 8-7-1 was deleted.
Sec. 8-7-2. - Land use plan incorporated by reference.
The "1995 Land Use Plan, City of Morrow, Georgia," with the 1998 amendments, is hereby adopted and incorporated into this Code as fully as though set out at length herein. Copies of the land use plan are maintained on file in the office of the city clerk where they are available for public inspection.
(Ord. No. 98-16, § 1, 12-8-98)
