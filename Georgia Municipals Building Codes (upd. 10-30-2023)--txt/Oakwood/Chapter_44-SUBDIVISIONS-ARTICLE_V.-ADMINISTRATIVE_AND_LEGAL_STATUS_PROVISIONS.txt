ARTICLE V. - ADMINISTRATIVE AND LEGAL STATUS PROVISIONS

Sec. 44-193. - Administration and interpretation.
The zoning administrator shall administer and interpret the provisions of this chapter.
(Ord. No. 221, § 601, 4-11-1994)
Sec. 44-194. - Violation and penalty.
In any case where any land is, or is proposed to be, used in violation of this chapter, the city attorney may, in addition to other remedies provided by law, institute injunction, abatement, or any appropriate action of actions, or proceeding to prevent, enjoin or abate such unlawful use.
(Ord. No. 221, § 602, 4-11-1994)
Sec. 44-195. - Amendment.
This chapter may be amended. Before enacting such amendment, the city council shall hold a public hearing thereon, notice of which shall be published at least 15 days prior to such hearing in a newspaper of general circulation in the city.
(Ord. No. 221, § 603, 4-11-1994)
Sec. 44-196. - Variances.
The city council may approve variances to the provisions of this chapter, after a public hearing before the city council, notice of which shall be published at least 15 days prior to such hearing in a newspaper of general circulation in the city.
(Ord. No. 221, § 604, 4-11-1994; Ord. No. 2020-CO-12-810, § 16, 3-8-2021)
Secs. 44-197—44-215. - Reserved.
