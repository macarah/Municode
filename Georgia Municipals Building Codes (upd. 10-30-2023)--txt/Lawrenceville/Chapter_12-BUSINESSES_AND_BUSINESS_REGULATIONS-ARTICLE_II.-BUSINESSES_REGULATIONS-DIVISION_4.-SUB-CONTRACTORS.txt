DIVISION 4. - SUB-CONTRACTORS

Sec. 12-124. - License and fee.
(a)
License required. All sub-contractors of whatever nature shall be required to obtain a license from the City Clerk in the manner specified in this chapter.
(b)
Fee established. The annual license fee shall be $37.50.
(Code 2005, § 32-203)
