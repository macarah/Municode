DIVISION 3. - CONSTRUCTION CONTRACTORS

Sec. 12-105. - License and fee.
(a)
License required. All contractors of whatever nature shall be required to obtain a license from the City Clerk in the manner specified in this chapter.
(b)
Fee established. The annual license fee shall be $50.00.
(Code 2005, § 32-202)
