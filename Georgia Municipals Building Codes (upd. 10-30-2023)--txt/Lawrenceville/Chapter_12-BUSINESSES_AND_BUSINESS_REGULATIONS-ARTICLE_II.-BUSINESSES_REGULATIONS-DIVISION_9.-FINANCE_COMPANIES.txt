DIVISION 9. - FINANCE COMPANIES

Sec. 12-265. - License and fee.
(a)
License required. All finance companies shall be required to obtain a license from the City Clerk in the manner specified in this chapter.
(b)
Fee established. The annual license fee is $125.00.
(Code 2005, § 32-210)
