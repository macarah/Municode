Chapter 63 - SPECIAL DISTRICTS
ARTICLE I. - GENERAL PROVISIONS

Sec. 63-1. - Creation of special districts.
There is hereby created and established special districts for the provision of services within such districts. Fees, assessments and taxes may be levied and collected within such districts to pay, wholly or partially, the cost of providing such services therein and to construct and maintain facilities therefor.
(Ord. No. 2020-01-161, 2-25-2020)
Secs. 63-2—63-24. - Reserved.
ARTICLE II. - ENHANCED PUBLIC SAFETY MEASURES

Sec. 63-25. - Program established.
There is established an enhanced public safety program whereby eligible neighborhoods may petition the city to authorize installation of license plate readers, video cameras, lighting, and other such enhanced public safety measures within said neighborhoods. The city manager, or designee, shall create and maintain an enhanced public safety policy for the establishment of enhanced public safety districts and implementation of enhanced public safety measures.
(Ord. No. 2020-01-161, 2-25-2020)
Sec. 63-26. - Council approval.
Prior to the installation of any enhanced public safety measure, the city council shall approve by resolution the establishment of each such public safety district, pursuant to the policy created by the city manager or designee.
(Ord. No. 2020-01-161, 2-25-2020)
Secs. 63-27—63-29. - Reserved.
ARTICLE III. - TRAFFIC CALMING MEASURES

Sec. 63-30. - Program established.
There is established a traffic calming program whereby eligible neighborhoods may petition the city to authorize installation of traffic calming measures within said neighborhoods. The city manager, or designee, shall create and maintain a policy for the establishment of traffic calming districts and implementation of traffic calming measures.
(Ord. No. 2020-01-161, 2-25-2020)
Sec. 63-31. - Council approval.
Prior to the installation of any traffic calming measure, the city council shall approve by resolution the establishment of each traffic calming district, pursuant to the policy created by the city manager or designee.
(Ord. No. 2020-01-161, 2-25-2020)
