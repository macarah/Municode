Sec. 3.4.3.3. - Accessory uses and buildings.
The following accessory uses of land and buildings shall be authorized in the light industrial district, as subordinate to the principal use or building:
A.
Accessory buildings, subject to the standards of section 4.2.4, Non-residential accessory building standards.
B.
Night watchman living quarters not to exceed 600 square feet of heated floor area.
