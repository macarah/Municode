Sec. 2-231. - Departments and directors enumerated.
The following departments and their directors are created or continued in the executive branch of the city:
(1)
Finance department, director of finance;
(2)
Police department, chief of police;
(3)
Fire department, fire chief;
(4)
Public works department, director of public works;
(5)
Water and sewer department, director of utilities;
(6)
Recreation and parks department, director of recreation;
(7)
Inspection department, director of inspections;
(8)
Engineering department, city engineer;
(9)
Community development, director of community development; and
(10)
Human resources, director of human resources.
(Code 1976, § 2-4001)
Sec. 2-232. - Duties and responsibilities.
The duties and responsibilities of the departments and directors listed in section 2-231 shall be prescribed by ordinance and by administrative regulations of the city manager consistent with such ordinance.
(Code 1976, § 2-4001)
