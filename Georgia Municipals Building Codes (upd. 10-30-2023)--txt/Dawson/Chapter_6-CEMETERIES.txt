Charter reference— Authority to regulate, § 4-42.

Sec. 6-1. - Permission to install monuments, etc.
It shall be unlawful for any person to erect, install or cause to be erected or installed, any monument, tombstone, fence, railing or coping, or plant any tree, brush, or shrub, or do any work of any character, or place any object or ornament upon any cemetery lot in Cedar Hill Cemetery, in the city without first obtaining permission from the city council.
(Code 1974, § 31-101)
Sec. 6-2. - Permission to remove, etc., monuments, etc.
It shall be unlawful for any person to mutilate or in any manner damage, alter, deface, destroy, or remove any monument, tombstone, fence, railing, coping, marker, or any other object that has been placed on, or around any cemetery lot in Cedar Hill Cemetery in the city without first obtaining permission from the city council.
(Code 1974, § 31-102)
Sec. 6-3. - Grave work insurance; use of vaults; slab material; flower containers.
It shall be unlawful for any person to violate or disregard any of the following regulations:
(1)
Liability insurance on any one (1) opening and/or closing grave. City shall have a copy of insurance policy.
(2)
All vaults shall be placed under ground.
(3)
All bodies shall be placed in the vault.
(4)
All slabs shall be granite, marble or concrete.
(5)
All floral arrangements shall be in a suitable container.
(Code 1974, § 31-103)
