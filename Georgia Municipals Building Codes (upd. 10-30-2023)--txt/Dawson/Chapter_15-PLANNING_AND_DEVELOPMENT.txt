Sec. 15-1. - Planning commission—Purpose.
There is hereby created and established a planning commission to be known as City of Dawson Planning Commission, with powers and duties defined by law.
(Code 1974, § 3-901(1))
State Law reference— For zoning procedures act, see O.C.G.A. § 36-66-1 et seq.
Sec. 15-2. - Same—Members.
The planning commission shall consist of five (5) members who are to serve without compensation, and whose terms of office shall be for five (5) years, except that the first or initial terms of the members shall be staggered so that the term of one (1) member shall expire annually. The annual expiration date for the expiration of the terms of office of the members shall be March first.
(Code 1974, § 3-901(2), (3))
