ARTICLE V. - BOARDS

Sec. 2-283. - Existing boards.
The county has established certain boards, which shall have the duties as may be provided from time to time by the board of commissioners, including, but not limited to:
(1)
The county library board, consisting of eight members appointed by the board of commissioners for terms of three years.
(2)
The county tax assessor board, consisting of three members appointed by the board of commissioners for terms of three years.
(3)
The county board of health, consisting of seven members appointed by the board of commissioners for terms of three years.
(4)
The county parks and recreation advisory board, consisting of five members appointed by the board of commissioners for terms of three years.
(Ord. of 1-3-1972; Ord. of 1-10-1983)
