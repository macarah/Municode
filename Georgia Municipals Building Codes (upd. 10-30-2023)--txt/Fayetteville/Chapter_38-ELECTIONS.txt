Cross reference— Administration, ch. 2.

Sec. 38-1. - Applicability of state law.
All elections shall be held and conducted in accordance with O.C.G.A. § 21-2-1 et seq., which is titled the "Georgia Election Code" as now or hereinafter amended.
(Ord. No. 0-21-00, arts. I, II, 11-20-00)
Sec. 38-2. - Registration list.
Pursuant to O.C.G.A. § 21-2-45, the City of Fayetteville hereby elects to use the voter registration list maintained by the county.
(Ord. No. 0-21-00, arts. III, IV, 11-20-00)
