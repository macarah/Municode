ARTICLE XII. - REMEDIES

Sec. 74-361. - Actions to stop violations.
If any county land is used in violation of this chapter, the planning and zoning commission, the board of commissioners, the county attorney acting for either, or any adjacent property owners who would be damaged by such violation, in addition to other remedies, may institute injunction or other appropriate action in proceeding to stop the violation in the case of such land use.
(Res. No. 2001-08, § 1, 3-13-2001)
