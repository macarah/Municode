ARTICLE XIII. - PENALTIES FOR VIOLATION

Sec. 74-391. - Penalties.
Any person violating any provision of this chapter shall be guilty of violating a duly adopted resolution of the county. Upon conviction of violation of any provision of this chapter the offender shall be punished as provided in section 1-11.
(Res. No. 2001-08, § 1, 3-13-2001)
