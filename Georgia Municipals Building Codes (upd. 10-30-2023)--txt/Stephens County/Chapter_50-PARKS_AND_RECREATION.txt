ARTICLE I. - IN GENERAL

Sec. 50-1. - Hours of use.
All county public parks located within the unincorporated area of the county shall be open from 6:00 a.m. until 10:00 p.m. each day. The parks, except as hereinafter set out, shall be closed for use from 10:00 p.m. until 6:00 a.m. each day; provided, however, that the hours when public parks are closed to the general public shall not apply to persons using the parks for the purpose of putting in or removing a boat, using the parks for fishing, or camping in designated camping areas within the parks.
(Res. of 2-3-1981, § 1)
ARTICLE II. - POSSESSION OF ALCOHOL

Sec. 50-2. - Use and possession of alcohol prohibited.
No person shall use or possess alcoholic beverages within any public park located in the unincorporated area of the county. Alcoholic beverages shall include malt beverages, wine, and distilled spirits as the same are defined in O.C.G.A. title 3.
(Res. of 2-3-1981, § 2)
ARTICLE III. - RECREATION COMPLEX PET ORDINANCE

Sec. 50-3. - Recreation complex pet ordinance.
No pet belonging to or in the control of any person, including, but not limited to, any visitor, invitee, employee, participant or spectator in any sporting event or other event, or any other person on the premises shall be allowed in or on any portion of the Stephens County Recreation Complex located at 199 Roselane Park Drive, Toccoa, Georgia 30577, unless the pet is on a leash. This article shall not apply to specially trained animals assisting persons with disabilities. This article shall not apply to any county-approved or sponsored event in which pets are the main purpose of the activity or event, such as a pet show or contest.
(Res. of 3-27-2012; Ord. of 8-14-2012)
