Sec. 2-260. - Fee schedule.
The City Council shall pass a fee schedule by resolution to assess all fees under the City of Doraville, Georgia, Code, where such fees are referenced in any part of the Code. Such fees may be further amended by resolution of the City Council and such fee schedule, as amended at any time, shall be incorporated herein and kept on file by the City Clerk and available for inspection.
(Ord. No. 2012-3.5, § 1, 2-21-12)
