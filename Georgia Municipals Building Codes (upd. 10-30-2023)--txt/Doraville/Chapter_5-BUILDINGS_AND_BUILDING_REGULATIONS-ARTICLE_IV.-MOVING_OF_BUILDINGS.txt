ARTICLE IV. - MOVING OF BUILDINGS

Sec. 5-196. - Permit.
No person shall move any building through the City without first obtaining a permit from the City Clerk upon written application on forms provided by the City Clerk, supplying any information which the clerk shall find necessary to a fair determination of whether a permit should issue. The application shall be accompanied by the payment of a fee to cover the expenses of processing same, which fee shall be nonrefundable even if the permit is not issued. Such fee shall be determined by the City Council and listed in the schedule of fees and charges maintained in the City Clerk's office.

Sec. 5-197. - Police escort.
Upon the issuance of a permit the applicant shall apply to the police department for a police escort which shall escort the building through the city. No building shall be moved through the City without such police escort.
