Chapter 16 - HEALTH AND PUBLIC WELFARE

Sec. 16-1. - Health department regulations adopted.
(a)
The rules and regulations of the county board of health, as amended from time to time, to the extent applicable within the city are adopted and incorporated in this chapter by reference. A copy of all the adopted rules and regulations is on file in the office of the city. Violations of the adopted rules shall constitute a violation of this chapter, subject to penalties as provided in section 1-6.
(b)
Wherever a conflict exists between the adopted health department regulations and other provisions of this Code, the most stringent provision shall prevail and be controlling.
(c)
The municipal court shall have jurisdiction over violations of this chapter and procedure for enforcement of this chapter shall be as provided by state law. Complaints of violations shall be brought before the municipal court by designated representatives of the county board of health and shall be prosecuted through that court. A complaint shall be brought before the municipal court only after all notices and rights to a hearing before a hearing officer of the board of health shall have been provided.
