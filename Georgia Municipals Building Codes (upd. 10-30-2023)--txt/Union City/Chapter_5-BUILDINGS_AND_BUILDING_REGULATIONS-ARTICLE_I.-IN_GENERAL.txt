ARTICLE I. - IN GENERAL

Sec. 5-1. - Building permit fee schedule.
(a)
No building permit required by the city shall be transmitted to an applicant until the fees set forth in subsection (b) below have been paid. Nor shall an amendment to a permit be approved until the additional fee, if any shall be required, has been paid.
(b)
The fees for inspection of buildings, whether for construction, removal or demolition; electrical installation or work; plumbing installation or work; heating and air conditioning installation or work; grading; drainage; curb gutters; and commercial drives, as are required by this chapter, shall be the same as those presently in force and as hereafter amended by the board of commissioners of the county. A copy of this fee schedule shall be kept and maintained by the city clerk for public reference.
(Ord. No. 79-4, 5-15-79)
