ARTICLE I. - IN GENERAL

Sec. 15-1. - Inspection fees.
The inspection fees for all water or sewer service as required in this chapter shall be the same as those presently in force and as hereafter amended by the county board of commissioners. A copy of this fee schedule shall be kept and maintained by the city clerk for public reference.
(Ord. No. 79-5, 5-15-79)
