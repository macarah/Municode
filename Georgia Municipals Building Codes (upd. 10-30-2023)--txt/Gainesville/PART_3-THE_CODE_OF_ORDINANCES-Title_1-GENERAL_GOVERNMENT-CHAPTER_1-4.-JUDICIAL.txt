CHAPTER 1-4. - JUDICIAL[6]

Sec. 1-4-1. - Judgments and sentences to run consecutively.
All judgments and sentences imposed and ordered by the municipal court shall run consecutively unless otherwise specifically provided by the judge in such judgments and sentences.
(Code 1958, § 1-9)
State Law reference— Concurrent sentences for felony and misdemeanor offenses, OCGA § 17-10-10.
