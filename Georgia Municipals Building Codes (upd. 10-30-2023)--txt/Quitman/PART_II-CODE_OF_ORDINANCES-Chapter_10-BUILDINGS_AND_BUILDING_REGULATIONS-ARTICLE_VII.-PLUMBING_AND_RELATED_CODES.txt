ARTICLE VII. - PLUMBING AND RELATED CODES

Sec. 10-379. - Plumbing code adopted.
There is hereby adopted by the city, for the purpose of establishing rules and regulations for the construction, alteration, removal, maintenance and use of plumbing and plumbing fixtures, that certain code known as the International Plumbing Code, 2006 edition, as amended by the state department of community affairs, save and except such portions as are hereinafter deleted, modified or amended. Such code and revisions are hereby adopted and incorporated as fully as if set out at length herein.
(Code 1962, § 16-1; Code 1986, § 5-236; Ord. No. 309, § 1, 11-4-1985)
Sec. 10-380. - Office of plumbing inspector created; appointment and removal of inspector.
The office of plumbing inspector is hereby created. The plumbing inspector shall be appointed by the board of commissioners, as provided in this Code, and he or she may be removed at any time by the board, without cause.
(Code 1962, § 16-2; Code 1986, § 5-237)
Sec. 10-381. - General powers and duties of plumbing inspector.
It shall be the duty of the plumbing inspector to enforce the provisions of the plumbing code adopted in this article and to perform such other duties, and exercise such powers as are prescribed by the board of commissioners.
(Code 1962, § 16-3; Code 1986, § 5-238)
Sec. 10-382. - Standard mechanical code adopted.
(a)
The following code is hereby adopted by reference and incorporated as fully as if set out at length herein: the International Mechanical Code, 2006 edition, as amended by the state department of community affairs.
(b)
In the event there are any conflicting rules or regulations existing in any ordinances of the city, this section and the code incorporated herein shall prevail.
(Code 1986, § 5-240; Ord. No. 309, § 1, 11-4-1985)
