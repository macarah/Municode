DIVISION 6. - WORKING TEST

Sec. 2-188. - Determination of employability and work performance; certification.
As a final test of employability, the first six months of employment in positions of original appointment, promotion or transfer to other positions which have different qualification shall be a working test to determine if the employee can be considered as being capable of performing satisfactorily in that position. In cases where a position requires state certification, the employee will remain on working test until the certification is obtained even though it may exceed six months. If an employee is terminated during the working test period, such employee is not entitled to a hearing.
(Mo. of 12-4-2007, art. VI)
