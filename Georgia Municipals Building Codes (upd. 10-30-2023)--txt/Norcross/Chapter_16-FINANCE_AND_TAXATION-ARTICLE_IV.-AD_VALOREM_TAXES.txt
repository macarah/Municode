Sec. 16-98. - Levy of tax.
An annual ad valorem tax upon all real and personal property within the city is hereby levied. The millage rate therefor shall be established each year by ordinance of the Council.
(Code 1979, § 3-2-1; Code 1998, § 38-121)
Sec. 16-99. - Assessment.
All property subject to the ad valorem tax shall be assessed at 40 percent of its full fair market value as established by the county for county ad valorem tax purposes.
(Code 1979, § 3-2-2; Code 1998, § 38-122)
Sec. 16-100. - Tax due and payable.
(a)
All ad valorem taxes due the city other than for motor vehicles shall be billed in one payment.
(b)
Ad valorem tax bills, as adjusted to conform to the tax digest of the county, shall be payable on or before November 15, but upon adjustment by the state revenue commissioner the city shall, within such reasonable time thereafter as may be determined by the resolution of the City Council, after receipt from the county of the adjustment information, refund any over payments or bill for any underpayments resulting.
(c)
Any taxes not paid by the due date shall bear interest at the maximum rate allowed by state law.
(Code 1979, § 3-2-3; Code 1998, § 38-123)
