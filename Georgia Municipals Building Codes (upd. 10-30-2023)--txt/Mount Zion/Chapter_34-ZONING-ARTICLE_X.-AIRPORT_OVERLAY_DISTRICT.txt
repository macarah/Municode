ARTICLE X. - AIRPORT OVERLAY DISTRICT

Sec. 34-401. - Purpose.
The purpose of this article is to establish regulations for building height that ensure public safety in the airport overlay district.
(Res. of 10-12-2004(3), § 13.1)
Sec. 34-402. - Maximum building height.
All structures in the airport overlay district shall be one story and less than 35 feet.
(Res. of 10-12-2004(3), § 13.2)
