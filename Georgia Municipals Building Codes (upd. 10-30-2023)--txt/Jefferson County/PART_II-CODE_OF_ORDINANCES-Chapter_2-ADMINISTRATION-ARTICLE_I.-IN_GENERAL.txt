ARTICLE I. - IN GENERAL

Sec. 2-1. - Records management.
The county clerk is authorized and directed, according to guidelines established by the secretary of state, to develop and establish a formal records management program for the county. A copy of the records management program and policy is available in the office of the county clerk.
(Code 1996, § 2-1; Res. of 4-13-2010)
