Sec. 30-1. - Conduct.
City municipal elections shall be conducted pursuant to the city Charter, as amended, and in accordance with the state municipal election code.
(Code 1980, § 8-1)
Sec. 30-2. - Agreements on municipal elections.
The mayor, on behalf of the city, is authorized to enter into agreements with the county to perform any or all functions of conducting municipal elections permitted by state law under O.C.G.A. § 21-3-10(a).
(Ord. No. 677, § 8-2, 10-16-1997)
