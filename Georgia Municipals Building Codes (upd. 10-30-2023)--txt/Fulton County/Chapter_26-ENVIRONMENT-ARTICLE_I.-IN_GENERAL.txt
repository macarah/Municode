Editor's note— Res. No. 05-0566, Exh. A, adopted May 4, 2005, repealed art. I in its entirety. Formerly, said article pertained to the Chattahoochee River Corridor Tributary Protection Area; purpose of section; adoption of regulations; Reduction of phosphorous in state waters; mandated retail sale of low phosphorous laundry detergents; and duty of owner to clean property. The user's attention is directed to art. VII of this chapter for similar provisions.
