Sec. 40-1. - Unified Development Ordinance incorporated by reference.
The Unified Development Ordinance of the city, adopted by ordinance of the mayor and council on December 20, 2007, as now or hereafter amended, is hereby incorporated into this Code by reference as fully as if set out at length herein. Copies of the zoning regulations shall be maintained on file in the office of the city clerk and shall be available for public inspection.
(Code 2002, § 6-6-1)
