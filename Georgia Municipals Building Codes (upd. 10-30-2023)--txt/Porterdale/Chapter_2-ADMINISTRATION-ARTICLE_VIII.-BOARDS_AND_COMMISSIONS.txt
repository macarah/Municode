Sec. 2-400. - Creation authorized; restrictions on membership.
The council shall create by ordinance such boards, commissions, and authorities pursuant to section 3.11 of the City of Porterdale Charter. No immediate family members, and for the purpose of this section, immediate family members are defined as: spouse, child, parents, grandparents by blood or marriage, and/or brothers, sisters by blood, half-blood or marriage, of any serving elected city official shall be nominated for, appointed to, or qualified to serve on any board, authority, or commission created by the city council under the authority of section 3.11 of the City Charter, whether such board, authority, or commission now exists or is hereafter created. This disqualification from service shall be in addition to any and all other prohibitions from or qualifications for service on boards, authorities, or commissions that may be established in this Code of Ordinances or prescribed or proscribed by the City Charter, or applicable state or federal law, with regard to a particular board, authority or commission.
(Code 2003, art. VIII; Ord. of 6-5-2006; Ord. of 7-15-2021(1) , § 3)
