Sec. 90-1. - Provisions incorporated by reference.
The city's zoning ordinance and zoning map adopted March 28, 2011, as amended, are by this reference incorporated in and made a part of this Code in as full and complete a manner and with like effect as though set out in full in this chapter.
(Amend. 1-12-2015, § 2(Exh. B)
