Chapter 2 - ADMINISTRATION

Sec. 2-1. - Supervision of departments.
All departments of the city shall be under the oversight of a councilman designated by the mayor. No oversight action of a councilman shall be final unless approved by the council or the city manager.
Sec. 2-2. - Meetings.
The mayor and council shall hold a regular meeting on the first Tuesday in each month at such hours as may be provided by the rules of the council. And in addition, the mayor may call a meeting at any time he thinks necessary upon serving notice to each councilman.

Sec. 2-3. - Purchasing.
Purchases by city employees are authorized as follows:
(1)
Purchases of any budgeted item of $10,000.00 or less may be approved by the city manager.
(2)
Purchases of any unbudgeted item up to $5,000.00 may be approved by the city manager if sufficient contingency funds are available for such purchase.
(3)
All purchases over $10,000.00 require requests for proposals or sealed bids, as applicable, and approval of the mayor and council.
(4)
Emergency purchases are exceptions to this section, and emergency purchases shall be confirmed as soon as practicable by mayor and council.
(Ord. of 4-4-2017)
