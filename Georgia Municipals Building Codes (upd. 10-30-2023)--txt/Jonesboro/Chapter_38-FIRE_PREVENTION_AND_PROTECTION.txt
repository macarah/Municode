Sec. 38-1. - Adoption of Clayton County Fire Ordinance.
Chapter 42, Fire Prevention and Protection, of the Code of Ordinances of Clayton County, as may be amended from time to time by the Clayton County Board of Commissioners, is hereby adopted by reference thereto and incorporated herein, as the official fire prevention and protection code of the city. A copy of Chapter 42 of the Code of Ordinances of Clayton County shall be on file in the office of the City Clerk of the City of Jonesboro.
(Ord. No. 2010-006, § 1, 6-14-2010)
