Sec. 86-213. - NAICS 623110—Nursing care facilities, including nursing homes.
The following conditions are assigned in the O&I and C-2 district:
(1)
Must be located on a street having a classification of collector or greater.
(2)
Must be established on a lot having a minimum area of one acre.
(3)
On-site parking and/or drop-off space adequate to meet the needs of the proposed facility shall be provided.
(4)
Must be located in a stand-alone building (no planned centers or connected storefronts).
(5)
The operator of the facility shall provide the Jonesboro police department and Clayton County fire department with a current list of residents living in the facility who have disabilities, and information concerning special needs, to ensure each resident's well-being and safe evacuation from the premises in the event of a fire or similar emergency.
(6)
A minimum 50-foot wide buffer shall be maintained between built elements of the proposed facility and any adjacent, residentially-zoned property.
(Ord. No. 2019-003 , § 1, 2-11-2019; Ord. No. 2019-011 , § 1, 6-3-2019)
