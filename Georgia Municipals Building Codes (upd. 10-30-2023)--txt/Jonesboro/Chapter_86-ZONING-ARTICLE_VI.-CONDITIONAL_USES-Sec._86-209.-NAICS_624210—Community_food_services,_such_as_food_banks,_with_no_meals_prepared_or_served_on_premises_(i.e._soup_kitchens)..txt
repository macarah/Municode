Sec. 86-209. - NAICS 624210—Community food services, such as food banks, with no meals prepared or served on premises (i.e. soup kitchens).
modified
The following conditions are assigned in the O&I, C-2, and M-1 districts:
(1)
Must be located on a street having a classification of collector or greater.
(2)
Off-street parking and/or drop-off space adequate to meet the needs of the proposed facility shall be provided.
(3)
Must be established on a lot having a minimum area of one acre and a minimum frontage of 150 feet.
(4)
Must be located in a stand-alone building (no planned centers or connected storefronts).
(5)
A minimum 50-foot wide buffer shall be maintained between built elements of the proposed facility and any adjacent, residentially-zoned property.
(6)
Onsite pickup by customers shall be prohibited on evenings and on Sundays.
(Ord. No. 2019-003 , § 1, 2-11-2019; Ord. No. 2019-011 , § 1, 6-3-2019; Ord. No. 2021-014 , § 1(Exh. A), 6-14-2021; Ord. No. 2022-009 , § 1(Exh. A), 3-14-2022)
