ARTICLE I. - IN GENERAL

Sec. 58-1. - Drainage area standards.
(a)
All repairs to the public right-of-way drainage areas in the county damaged by the flood in July 1994 will be repaired according to state department of transportation standards and specifications for 25-year flood frequency.
(b)
All public right-of-way drainage areas constructed after September 6, 1994, will be constructed according to state department of transportation standards and specifications.
(Res. of 9-6-1994)
