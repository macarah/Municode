Article XIII - CARNIVALS AND CIRCUSES

Sec. 30-5-311. - Requirements additional to license fees and taxes.
In addition to payment of all of the license fees and taxes, each carnival or circus which shall operate in the city shall be required to provide, on the premises on which they are operating, security personnel, at their sole cost and expense, which is adequate for crowd control and/or protection of patrons, customers, and participants. The adequacy and sufficiency of such security personnel must be approved by the chief of police or his designee.
(Ord. No. 90-44, § 4, 9-11-90)
