DIVISION 23. - MU-5 (MIXED-USE VERY HIGH DENSITY) DISTRICT

Sec. 46-679. - District requirements, standards and criteria.
All provisions found in the MU-3 (Mixed-Use Medium Density) District shall also apply to the MU-5 (Mixed-Use Very High Density) District, except that height restrictions apply to MU-5 in accordance with table 2.13.
