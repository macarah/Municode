DIVISION 22. - MU-4 (MIXED-USE HIGH DENSITY) DISTRICT

Sec. 46-660. - District requirements, standards and criteria.
All provisions found in the MU-3 (Mixed-Use Medium Density) District shall also apply to the MU-4 (Mixed-Use High Density) District, except that height restrictions apply to the MU-4 (Mixed-Use High Density) District in accordance with table 2.11.
