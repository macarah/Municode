ARTICLE VIIC. - R-25C

Sec. 74-150. - Development standards.
(a)
Open space—25 percent, dedicated to the homeowners' association; ten percent must be able to be able to be built on.
(b)
Maximum lot area—2.5 dwellings per acre.
(c)
Minimum lot width at building line—60 feet.
(d)
Yard setbacks:
(1)
From all streets—20 feet;
(2)
Side yard setbacks—7.5 feet;
(3)
Rear yard setbacks—20 feet;
(e)
Maximum height of structures—35 feet.
(f)
Minimum square footage of principal structure—1,200 square feet.
(Ord. No. 22-02 , § 4, 1-13-2022)
Sec. 74-151. - Architectural reference.
(a)
Building materials.
(1)
For principal structures, allowable building materials (not including trim/accent) along the front and side facades are limited to the following:
a.
Brick;
b.
Brick veneer;
c.
Stone;
d.
Architectural precast concrete; and
e.
Natural wood and/or cement-based wood siding.
(2)
There shall be no more than two building materials used (not including trim/accent materials).
(Ord. No. 22-02 , § 4, 1-13-2022)
Secs. 74-152—74-154. - Reserved.
