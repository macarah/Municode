State Law reference— Smoking in public places, O.C.G.A. § 16-12-2.

Sec. 46-26. - Smoking in county offices.
It shall be unlawful for any person to smoke tobacco in any form in any county office or any public area within a county office building.
(Ord. No. 69, § 1, 2-19-1993)
Sec. 46-27. - Penalty for violation.
Violation of this article shall, upon conviction, be punishable as provided in section 1-11.
(Ord. No. 69, § 2, 2-19-1993)
