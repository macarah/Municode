BARTOW COUNTY ZONING ORDINANCE
An ordinance regulating within the county the location, height, bulk, number of stories and size of buildings and other structures; the sizes of yards, courts, and other open spaces; the density and distribution of population; the uses of buildings, structures, and land for trade, industry, residence, recreation, mining, agriculture, sanitation, public activities, and boundaries thereof; defining certain terms used herein; providing for the method of administration and amendment; establishing and defining the powers and duties of the board of appeals; establishing and defining the powers and duties of the planning commission; providing penalties for violations; repealing conflicting ordinances; and for other purposes.
(Ord. of 7-21-2021(4) )
