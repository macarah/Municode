Sec. 6-81. - Standard Gas Code—Adopted.
The Standard Gas Code, to adopt and enforce the latest edition, as adopted and amended by the Georgia Department of Community Affairs.
(Code 1976, § 8-1051; Ord. No. 77-88, 12-29-88; Ord. No. 35-91, § 7, 9-19-91; Ord. No. 41-95, § 6, 9-28-95; Ord. No. 37-01, § 8, 9-6-01)
Sec. 6-82. - Same—Insertions, deletions and changes.
In the Standard Gas Code, as adopted, the phrase "Construction Board of Adjustments and Appeals" and all provisions related thereto shall be construed to mean the construction board of adjustment and appeals as defined in Division 3 of Article II of Chapter 6 and all provisions therein shall supercede the requirements of the Standard Gas Code.
(Code 1976, § 8-1052; Ord. No. 35-91, § 8, 9-19-91; Ord. No. 41-95, § 7, 9-28-95; Ord. No. 37-01, § 9, 9-6-01)
Sec. 6-83. - Interference with inspections.
Any person attempting to prevent an inspection, whether owner, lessee, or his agent, shall be in violation of this article and shall be guilty of an offense.
(Code 1976, § 8-1057)
