ARTICLE IX. - R-1, RESTRICTED OFFICE-INSTITUTIONAL DISTRICT

Sec. 9.01. - Statement of purpose.
The intent of this District is to encourage and provide suitable areas within the City for offices, businesses, institutional and related activities with a minimum of interference from excess traffic and conflicting land uses.
Sec. 9.02. - Permitted uses.
a.
Professional offices, hospitals, clinics (other than veterinary) and clinic laboratories, nursing rest homes, group homes, convalescent homes and personal care homes.
b.
Pharmacies, florist shops, mortuaries.
c.
Day Care Facilities provided that no play equipment be located in the front yard and that signage be compatible to the neighborhood and not larger than regulated for home occupations. All State licensing requirements and regulations will also apply.
d.
Adult Care Facilities.
e.
Financial institutions.
Sec. 9.03. - Conditional uses.
a.
Beauty and/or barber shops and similar personal service establishments.
b.
Churches. (See Section 5.03a.)
c.
Public buildings, structures and other public land uses.
d.
Retail sales of goods and services.
e.
Boarding houses.

g.
Other uses similar to the above and compatible with the particular neighborhood and environment as approved by the Mayor and City Council.
