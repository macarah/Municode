ARTICLE I. - IN GENERAL

Sec. 62-1. - Uniform Rules of the Road adopted.
(a)
Pursuant to O.C.G.A. §§ 40-6-372—40-6-376, O.C.G.A. tit. 40, ch. 6 (O.C.G.A. § 40-6-1 et seq.), known as the Uniform Rules of the Road, including the definitions contained in O.C.G.A. § 40-1-1, are adopted as and for the traffic regulations of the county with like effect as if recited in this section.
(b)
Unless another penalty is expressly provided by state law, every person convicted of a violation of any provision of this section shall be punished in accordance with section 1-12.
(Code 2002, § 66-31)
