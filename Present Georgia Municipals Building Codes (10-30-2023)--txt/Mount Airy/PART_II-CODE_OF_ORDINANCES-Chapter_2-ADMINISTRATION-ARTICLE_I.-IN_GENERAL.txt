ARTICLE I. - IN GENERAL

Sec. 2-1. - Official map.
The original town boundary was circular, extending three-fourths of a mile in all directions from a center point that was close to the railroad underpass on Sixth Street. The current boundaries of the town are as reflected in the "Official Map of the Corporate Limits of the Town of Mount Airy, Georgia," as amended from time to time by the town council. The official map is available for inspection in the office of the town clerk.
(Code 1947, § 1-34)
State law reference— Terms town, city, and municipality declared synonymous, O.C.G.A. § 36-30-1.
Sec. 2-2. - Administration of oaths.
All oaths required to be administered to any officer or person by these ordinances, except the oath required to be administered to taxpayers that shall be administered by the clerk and treasurer, may be administered by any officer authorized by law to administer oaths.
(Code 1947, § 1-16)
State law reference— Oath of public officers, O.C.G.A. § 45-3-1 et seq.; persons before whom oath is taken, O.C.G.A. § 45-3-3.
