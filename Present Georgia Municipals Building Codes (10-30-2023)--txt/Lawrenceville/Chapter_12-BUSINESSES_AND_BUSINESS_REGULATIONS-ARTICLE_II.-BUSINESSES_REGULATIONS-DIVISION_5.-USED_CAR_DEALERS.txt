DIVISION 5. - USED CAR DEALERS

Sec. 12-147. - License required.
Any used car or used motor vehicle dealer, as such terms are defined in O.C.G.A. § 43-47-1, who does business within this Municipality shall be required to obtain a license from the City Clerk in the manner specified in this chapter.
(Code 2005, § 32-204(1))
State Law reference— Local regulation of used motor vehicle dealers, O.C.G.A. § 43-47-13.
Sec. 12-148. - Fee established.
The annual business license fee for each used car or used motor vehicle dealer doing business in the City shall be $75.00.
(Code 2005, § 32-204(2))
State Law reference— Local regulation of used motor vehicle dealers, O.C.G.A. § 43-47-13.
Sec. 12-149. - Review of application.
No action on any application for a license under this division shall be taken by the City Council until the Police Chief has reviewed such application and forwarded his recommendation thereon to the City Clerk in the manner specified in this chapter.
(Code 2005, § 32-204(3))
State Law reference— Local regulation of used motor vehicle dealers, O.C.G.A. § 43-47-13.
Sec. 12-150. - Restriction on issuance.
No license under this division shall be issued to any applicant who has not been licensed by the State Board of Registration of Used Car Dealers.
(Code 2005, § 32-204(4))
State Law reference— Local regulation of used motor vehicle dealers, O.C.G.A. § 43-47-13.
Sec. 12-151. - Records.
Each used car dealer or used motor vehicle dealer licensed under this division shall keep a record of all motor vehicles offered for sale, exchange, or disposal to the public, which record shall show the make of said motor vehicle, the year of its manufacture, its serial number, and its engine number, and which record shall always be kept available, for the Police Chief, the presence in his place of business of any motor vehicle on which the serial or engine number has been defaced or altered.
(Code 2005, § 32-204(5))
State Law reference— Local regulation of used motor vehicle dealers, O.C.G.A. § 43-47-13.
Sec. 12-152. - Inspections.
It shall be the duty of the Police Chief to make inspections from time to time for the purpose of seeing that the records required in this division are being kept.
(Code 2005, § 32-204(6))
State Law reference— Local regulation of used motor vehicle dealers, O.C.G.A. § 43-47-13.
Sec. 12-153. - Exceptions.
Nothing in this division shall be deemed to apply to any individual making an isolated sale of his own vehicle.
(Code 2005, § 32-204(7))
State Law reference— Local regulation of used motor vehicle dealers, O.C.G.A. § 43-47-13.
Secs. 12-154—12-175. - Reserved.
