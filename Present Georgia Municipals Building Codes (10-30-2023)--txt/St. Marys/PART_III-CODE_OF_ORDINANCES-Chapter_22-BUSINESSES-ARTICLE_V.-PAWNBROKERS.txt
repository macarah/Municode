ARTICLE V. - PAWNBROKERS[4]

Footnotes:
--- (4) ---
State Law reference— Pawnbrokers, O.C.G.A. § 44-12-130 et seq.

Sec. 22-151. - License issuance.
The city is authorized to issue a city license to a pawnbroker to operate such designated business within the legal limits of the city.
(Ord. of 2-2-61, § I)
Sec. 22-152. - Conditions of license.
A licensee, under the provisions of this article, shall agree as a condition precedent to receiving such license:
(1)
That he will not knowingly accept any article or articles of property proposed to be pawned that such licensee knows to be stolen.
(2)
That such licensee shall not engage in the business of making loans or lending money to any degree and that such business shall be confined to the strictest terms of accepting property to be pawned or pledged for a fair and reasonable sum of money.
(3)
That the licensee shall report to the city police department a full description sufficient for identification or in possession of the licensee.
(Ord. of 2-2-61, § III)
Sec. 22-153. - Revocation of license.
Upon conviction for the violation of any of the terms or provisions of this article such license shall be revoked.
(Ord. of 2-2-61, § IV)
