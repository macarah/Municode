ARTICLE III. - PRIVATE WATER SYSTEMS[3]

Sec. 25-126. - Generally.
Private water systems and vendors shall be governed by all criteria in this chapter relating to water purity, protection of supply and system sanitation, and shall be licensed by the county.
(Code 1976, § 6-1081)
Sec. 25-127. - Approval of plans.
All plans for private water systems shall be prepared by an engineer of the county and approved by the county prior to construction. Materials of construction shall meet minimum standards set by the county for the public water system.
(Code 1976, § 6-1082)
Sec. 25-128. - Connection to public water system.
No connection shall be made between the county water system and a private water system except by approval of the board of commissioners. Such approved connections shall be metered and equipped with backflow prevention devices approved by the county and subject to all conditions of this chapter.
(Code 1976, § 6-1083)
Sec. 25-129. - No county warranty.
By its regulation of private water systems and suppliers, the county assumes no liability for the purity of such water or for damages caused by operators of private facilities.
(Code 1976, § 6-1084)
