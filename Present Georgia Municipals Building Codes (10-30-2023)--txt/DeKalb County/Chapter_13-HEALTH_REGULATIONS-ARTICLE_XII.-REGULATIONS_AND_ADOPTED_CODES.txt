ARTICLE XII. - REGULATIONS AND ADOPTED CODES

Sec. 13-350. - Adoption of the rules and regulations certifying portable sanitation contractors and for other purposes.
Pursuant to O.C.G.A. § 31-2A-6 et seq., and now and as it may hereinafter be amended, there is hereby adopted as if fully set forth herein the state regulations currently codified as Ga. Comp. R. Regs. R. 511-3-6-.01 et seq., relating to portable sanitation units now as may hereafter be promulgated by the Georgia Department of Public Health that mandate the permit requirements, certification of contractors, companies, providers, and individuals involved in the rental, servicing, disposal, maintenance, construction, and installation of portable sanitation units, and requires certified contractors to supply a minimum number of units during anticipated peak attendance at construction sites and for special events, now and as may be amended hereafter by the public health department.
(Ord. No. 16-10, Pt. I, 9-27-16)
State Law reference— Similar provisions, O.C.G.A. §§ 31-2A-6, 31-12-8, 31-27-9, 12-8-1.
