27-3.32 DIVISION 32. - FAIRSTONE OVERLAY DISTRICT

Sec. 3.32.1. - Scope of the regulations, applicability and boundaries.
This division establishes standards and procedures that apply to any development that lies, in whole or in part, within the Fairstone Overlay District. The boundaries of the Fairstone Overlay District shall be established by a zoning map amendment adopted pursuant to this chapter which amendment is incorporated herein and made a part of this chapter. The provisions of this Code, including, but not limited to, the provisions of division 8 shall govern the demolition, new construction, and residential development in whole or in part with any area of the Fairstone Overlay District.
( Ord. No. 15-06 , 8-25-2015)
