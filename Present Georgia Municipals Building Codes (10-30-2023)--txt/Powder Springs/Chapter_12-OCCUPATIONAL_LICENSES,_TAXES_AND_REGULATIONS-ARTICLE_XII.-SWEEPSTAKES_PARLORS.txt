ARTICLE XII. - SWEEPSTAKES PARLORS[12]

Footnotes:
--- (12) ---
Editor's note— Ord. No. 2011-27, adopted Jan. 2, 2012, supplied provisions to be added to this Code as §§ 12-303 and 12-304. In order to maintain the existing numbering style of this Code, at the discretion of the editor, these provisions have been redesignated as §§ 12-341 and 12-342 to read as set out.

Sec. 12-341. - Definitions.
For the purposes of this article, the following definition shall apply:
Sweepstakes parlors shall mean any premises upon which patrons may use devices that provide the ability of a player to win a cash or non-cash prize for participation in a no skill game after the payment of cash or other consideration or the payment for merchandise, including, but not limited to, phone cards or internet time or payment for time to use such devices, even if others can participate for free. Sweepstakes parlors include internet sweepstakes cafes, phone card sweepstakes cafes or any variation thereof.
( Ord. No. 2011-27 , 1-3-12)
Sec. 12-342. - Use prohibited.
(a)
Any method of operating a sweepstakes parlor is prohibited.
(b)
No business license or other use permit or license may be issued to a sweepstakes parlor.
( Ord. No. 2011-27 , 1-3-12)
