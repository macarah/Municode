ARTICLE VII. - PERSONNEL POLICY

Sec. 2-151. - Adopted.
The City of Powder Springs hereby adopts a personnel policy to govern personnel matters for the city. Except as otherwise provided herein, the whole of the personnel policy manual shall be adopted and incorporated as if fully set out herein. A copy of the personnel policy manual is on file and available for inspection in the office of the city clerk.
(Ord. No. 2004-20, 6-21-04)
