Sec. 2-92. - Department rules.
Department directors may promulgate and publish rules of conduct and performance to govern operations which are peculiar to their individual departments. Copies of all such departmental rules or amendments thereto shall be delivered to the mayor and human resources director at least twenty (20) working days prior to the effective date thereof, and any such rules which go into effect shall be published and made known to all employees of the department.
(Code 1977, § 2-7031; Ord. No. 34-94, 8-1-94)
