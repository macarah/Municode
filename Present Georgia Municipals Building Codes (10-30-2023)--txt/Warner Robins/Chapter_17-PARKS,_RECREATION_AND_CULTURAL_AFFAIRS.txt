Sec. 17-1. - Auditorium; rates; concession stand.
(a)
The rates for the rental of the city auditorium, hereby incorporated herein, shall be kept on file in the office of the city clerk.
(b)
The concession stand shall be operated only by auditorium personnel.
(Code 1977, § 6-1001)
Sec. 17-2. - Park curfew.
As contained in this section, the term "park" is a park, playground, recreation area or any other area owned or used by the city and devoted to active or passive recreation. All city parks shall be closed between the hours of dusk and 6:00 a.m. with the exception of city-sponsored organized sporting events or other recreation activities held on lighted facilities which may continue past dusk but only until their conclusion. Any persons found to be in a city park between these hours shall be deemed to be in violation of this section and subject to penalties as set forth in the City Code. The chief of police or designee is authorized to issue special event permits to allow use of the parks during the otherwise prohibited hours, and the mayor or the chief of police or their designees are authorized to close parks earlier than dusk if deemed necessary for public safety purposes.
(Ord. No. 27-93, § 1, 4-19-93; Ord. No. 30-95, § 1, 6-5-95; Ord. No. 12-17, § 1, 3-20-17)
