ARTICLE X. - CARNIVAL AND CIRCUS

Sec. 10-190. - Compliance with article.
All holders of license for the operators of carnivals or circus[es] shall comply with the provisions in this article. Fee for carnival or circus license is three hundred twelve dollars ($312.00) for the first week — one hundred eighteen dollars ($118.00) for each additional week.
(1)
Proof of liability insurance;
(2)
Hours 12:00 p.m. to 11:00 p.m.;
(3)
Proof of state safety inspection; and
(4)
Two hundred fifty (250) feet from residential area.
(Ord. No. 48-94, 11-21-94)
