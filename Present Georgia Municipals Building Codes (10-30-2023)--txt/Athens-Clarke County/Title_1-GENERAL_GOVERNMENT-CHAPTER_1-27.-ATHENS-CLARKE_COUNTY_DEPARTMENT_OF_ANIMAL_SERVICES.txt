Editor's note— Ord. of 10-1-2019(3) set out provisions intended for use as chapter 1-26. To avoid duplication of chapter numbers, and at the discretion of the editor, these provisions have been included as chapter 1-27.

Sec. 1-27-1. - Establishment.
Pursuant to Athens-Clarke County Charter section 4-201 et seq., there is hereby created the Department of Animal Services as part of the administrative and service departments of the Unified Government under the supervision and direction of the Manager. The employees of said office shall be employees of Athens-Clarke County appointed by the Manager under the personnel system with the Director of Animal Services as a professional class position.
( Ord. of 10-1-2019(3), § 1 )
Sec. 1-27-2. - Purpose.
The purpose of the Department of Animal Services is to protect the public safety and health from at-large and dangerous animals, protect animals from inhumane treatment and provide education, provide housing for the animals in the County's possession, and to convey animals to caring homes or alter them and return them to their territory or colony.
( Ord. of 10-1-2019(3), § 1 )
