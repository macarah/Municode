ARTICLE VII. - PACKAGE DISTILLED SPIRITS; LICENSE REQUIREMENTS AND REGULATION

Sec. 6-100. - Applicability of chapter to those businesses operating both the retail sale of distilled spirits and package malt beverages and wine.
For licensees who wish to operate a business that conducts both the retail sale of distilled spirits and package malt beverages and wine, in the case of conflict, the provisions of this chapter regulating the sale of distilled spirits shall prevail, and compliance with those provisions, rather than the provisions applicable to the license permitting the sale of package malt beverages and wine, is required. The applicant shall be required to complete the application(s) required by this chapter.
(Ord. of 8-14-2017)
Sec. 6-101. - Separation of alcoholic beverages from nonalcoholic merchandise.
All holders of licenses under this article shall separate alcoholic beverages from their nonalcoholic merchandise so that the alcoholic beverages may be closed off from the rest of the premises at all times when sale thereof is prohibited, and the same shall be so closed off during the prohibited times for sale.
(Ord. of 8-14-2017)
Sec. 6-102. - Consumption prohibited.
It shall be unlawful for any person to consume any alcoholic beverage on premises licensed for the sale of package alcoholic beverages. For the purpose of this section only, "premises" shall include the parking area immediately adjoining the premises licensed for the sale of package alcoholic beverages and available for the use of the customers of the licensed premises, whether or not the same are owned or leased by the license holder. It shall be unlawful for any licensee to permit the consumption of alcoholic beverages on said premises or to sell unsealed (broken) alcoholic beverages.
(Ord. of 8-14-2017)
Sec. 6-103. - Sale or delivery off premises.
It shall be unlawful for any licensee of a retail establishment permitted to sell distilled spirits hereunder to make deliveries of any alcoholic beverages by the package beyond the boundaries of the premises covered by the license.
(Ord. of 8-14-2017)
