Sec. 50-1. - Fishing, boating in city reservoir.
Baskets or trot lines shall not be used at the city reservoirs. All boats with internal combustion motors or over 16 feet in length are prohibited.
(Code 1989, § 12-15)
Sec. 50-2. - Parks maintained by city.
The city maintains Mercer Park and Fort Washington Park. All other parks and recreation facilities are maintained and regulated by the Washington Wilkes Parks and Recreation Commission.
