ARTICLE I. - IN GENERAL

Sec. 2-1. - Agent for service of process; designated.
(a)
The county manager of the county, is hereby designated as the agent for service of process with respect to any and all civil actions against the county, or against any member of the board of commissioners of the county named in his or her official capacity as such.
(b)
In the absence of the county manager, the county clerk is hereby designated as the agent for service of process with respect to any and all civil actions against the county, or against any member of the board of commissioners of the county named in his or her official capacity as such.
(c)
This section shall have no application to civil actions filed against any county official or employee named in his or her individual capacity. In the event a county official or employee is named in his or her individual capacity then service of process upon said individual shall be obtained as required by law.
(d)
This section is adopted pursuant to the authority expressed in O.C.G.A. § 9-11-4(d)(5).
(Ord. of 8-2-1999(4), §§ 1—4; Res. No. 2020-12, 7-13-2020)
