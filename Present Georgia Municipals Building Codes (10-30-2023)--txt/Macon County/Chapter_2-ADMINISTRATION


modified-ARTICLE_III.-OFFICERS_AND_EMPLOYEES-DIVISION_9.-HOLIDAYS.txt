DIVISION 9. - HOLIDAYS

Sec. 2-286. - Holidays allowed.
The following are the official holidays that will be observed by eligible employees:
New Year's Day
Martin Luther King Day
Memorial Day
Independence Day
Labor Day
Thanksgiving Day
Day after Thanksgiving
Christmas Eve
Christmas Day
Good Friday
(Mo. of 12-4-2007, § 9.01)
Sec. 2-287. - Observation of holidays.
(a)
Employees may be required to work during the above holidays.
(b)
Employees on a five-day, eight-hour schedule who are required to work on a designated holiday will receive 2½ times such employee's regular pay or may elect to take an equivalent amount of time off at another time as determined by the employee and the employee's supervisor. If a holiday falls on Saturday, it will generally be observed on the preceding Friday. If a holiday falls on Sunday, it will be generally observed on the following Monday.
(c)
A deputy sheriff, EMS employee or other employee on a seven-day, 24-hour schedule, who is required to work on a calendar holiday will receive 2½ times such employee's regular pay or may elect to take an equivalent amount of time off at another time as determined by the employee and the employee's supervisor.
(d)
Employees not working on a holiday will receive straight time for the holiday.
(Mo. of 12-4-2007, § 9.02)
