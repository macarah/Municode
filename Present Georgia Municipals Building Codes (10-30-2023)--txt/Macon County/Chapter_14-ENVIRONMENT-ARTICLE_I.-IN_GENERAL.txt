ARTICLE I. - IN GENERAL

Sec. 14-1. - Permitting officer.
Unless otherwise stated, for purposes of this chapter, the permitting officer is the department, office or individual as designated by the board of county commissioners who has the responsibility of administering and enforcing the provisions of this chapter.
Editor's note— As of the date of adoption of this Code of Ordinances, the State of Georgia Department of Natural Resources has been designated by the board of county commissioners as the permitting officer for the regulations promulgated under this chapter. In addition, the County Code of Ordinances has another chapter that contains alternative enforcement procedures for this subject matter that parallels those proscribed under this chapter.
