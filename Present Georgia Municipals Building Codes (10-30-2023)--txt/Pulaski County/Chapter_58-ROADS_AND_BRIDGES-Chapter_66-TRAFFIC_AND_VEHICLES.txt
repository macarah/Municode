ARTICLE II. - UNIFORM RULES OF THE ROAD[2]

Footnotes:
--- (2) ---
State Law reference— Adoption of uniform rules of the road by local authorities, O.C.G.A. § 40-6-372.

Sec. 66-31. - Adopted.
(a)
Pursuant to O.C.G.A. §§ 40-6-372—40-6-376, O.C.G.A. §§ 40-6-2—40-6-395, known as the Uniform Rules of the Road, and the definitions contained in O.C.G.A. § 40-1-1 are adopted as and for the traffic regulations of the county with like effect as if recited in this section.
(b)
Unless another penalty is expressly provided by state law, every person convicted of a violation of any provision of this section shall be punished in accordance with section 1-11.

ARTICLE III. - HEAVY VEHICLE/RESIDENTIAL STREET AND ROAD

Sec. 66-51. - Title.
This article shall be known and cited as the Pulaski County Heavy Vehicle/Residential Street and Road Ordinance.
(Ord. of 1-4-2011(1), § 1)
Sec. 66-52. - Purpose.
It has come to the attention of the commissioner that drivers of heavy vehicles (for purpose of this article defined as vehicles having in excess of six wheels and gross combined net weight 10,000 lbs.) are using certain streets within what is commonly known as the "Massee Subdivision" in Pulaski County for access from Industrial Boulevard to U.S. Hwy 341; and
Whereas, the aforesaid subdivision streets are primarily residential, densely populated and not designed for heavy tonnage vehicles; and
Whereas, the aforesaid heavy vehicles have ample access to Industrial Boulevard from U.S. Hwy. 341 along a paved county road what is known as "Fetchman Road";
Wherefore, vehicles "heavy vehicles" as defined hereinabove are prohibited from travelling upon the following streets:
EXPAND
North Street From its intersection with East Street to the intersection of North Street with U.S. Hwy 129;
Boyer Street From its intersection with North Street to its intersection with East Street;
East Street From its intersection with U. S. Hwy 341 to its intersection with North Street;
Boeing Street From its intersection with U. S. Hwy 129/GA Hwy 247 to its intersection with North Street.
Harrington Street (Entire Length)
Curtis Street (Entire Length)
Douglas Street (Entire Length)
Piper Street (Entire Length)
  (Ord. of 1-4-2011(1), § 2)
Sec. 66-53. - Signage.
Signage has been erected on North Street, Boeing Street, Boyer Street and East Street indicating such prohibition.
(Ord. of 1-4-2011(1), § 3)
Sec. 66-54. - Penalty.
Penalty for violation of this article shall be $500.00.
(Ord. of 1-4-2011(1), § 4)
