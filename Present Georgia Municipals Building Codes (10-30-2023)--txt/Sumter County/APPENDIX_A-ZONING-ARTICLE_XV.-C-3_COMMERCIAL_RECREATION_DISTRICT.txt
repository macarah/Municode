ARTICLE XV. - C-3 COMMERCIAL RECREATION DISTRICT

Sec. 15.01. - Statement of purpose.
The C-3 district as established in this article is intended to provide areas for recreational facilities developed as commercial ventures.
Sec. 15.02. - Permitted uses.
The following uses are permitted in the C-3 district:
A.
Archery ranges.
B.
Amusement parks.
C.
Campgrounds.
D.
Camps, day or overnight.
E.
Commercial swimming pools, tennis courts and the like.
F.
Country clubs.
G.
Educational facilities such as zoos and botanical gardens.
H.
Fairgrounds.
I.
Golf courses of all types and all accessory uses such as driving ranges and practice putting greens.
J.
Gun clubs, outdoor (shooting ranges must meet all applicable federal, state and local government requirements).
K.
Marinas.
L.
Race tracks and drag strips.
M.
Recreational fish ponds.
N.
Reservoirs.
O.
Uses permitted in R-1 and R-2 districts.
Sec. 15.03. - Conditional uses.
The following are conditional uses in the C-3 district:
A.
Radio, cellular phone or similar transmission and receiving towers.
B.
Recreation vehicle park.
