Sec. 58-1. - Comprehensive plan.
The comprehensive plan, as now or hereafter amended, is approved and adopted, along with the accompanying maps, goals, policies, objectives and programs, as a general guide for the future growth, change, development and progress of the county.
(Res. of 9-6-1994)
