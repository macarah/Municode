Sec. 30-1. - Imposition of penalties for jail construction and staffing.
The provisions of O.C.G.A. §§ 15-21-90—15-21-95 shall be in effect in the county in accordance with the provisions thereof, and the board of commissioners agrees to expend the funds collected thereunder for the purposes provided for in O.C.G.A. §§ 15-21-90—15-21-95. The additional penalties provided for therein shall be imposed and collected in the cases to which they are applicable.
(Res. of 10-17-1989)
Sec. 30-2. - Imposition of court fees to provide for county court functions.
Any person against whom a fine is imposed or community service work imposed by the magistrate, state and superior courts of Stephens County, Georgia for the violation of any of the laws or ordinances of Stephens County, Georgia or the laws of the State of Georgia shall be subject to and have assessed a fee in the amount of $10.00 per violation. Said fee to be in addition to all other fines and fees imposed by magistrate, state and superior courts.
(Ord. of 11-22-2016)
