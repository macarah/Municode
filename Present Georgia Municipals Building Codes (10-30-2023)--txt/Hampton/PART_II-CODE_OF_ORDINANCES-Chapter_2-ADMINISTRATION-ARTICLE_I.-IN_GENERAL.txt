ARTICLE I. - IN GENERAL

Sec. 2-1. - Removal of city books, records from offices.
(a)
It shall be unlawful for any person, whether an officer of the city or otherwise, to remove from any municipal office any book, record or other valuable documents without an order from the mayor and council.
(b)
Any person knowingly permitting any of the city records to be removed without the order of the mayor shall be liable to the same punishment for permitting them to be removed.
(Ord. No. 1, § 21, 3-4-75)
Sec. 2-2. - Records retention.
(a)
The city manager, or his designee, is hereby designated as the records manager for the city. This Code section shall in no way replace the records custodian identified by the city in accordance with O.C.G.A. § 50-18-71(b)(1)(B).
(b)
All city records shall be maintained in accordance with the University of Georgia's Records Retention Schedule and the city shall conform to the guidelines adopted from time to time by the state technology authority for the maintenance and security of its records.
(Ord. No. 247, § 1, 9-12-06; Ord. No. 537 , § 1, 3-9-21)
