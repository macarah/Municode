Sec. 78-1. - Food vendors.
It shall be unlawful for any private vendors of candy, soft drinks, foods of any kind and sundries to roll any handwagon or any motorized vehicle through the city parks and city recreation centers for the purpose of soliciting business and of selling any of the products described in this section without the express permission of the Dalton Parks and Recreation Commission.
(Code 1983, § 12-1)
