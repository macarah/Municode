Sec. 42-1. - Conduct of elections; voting machines; returns.
In all city elections as fixed by law, the use of automatic voting machines is authorized. All city elections will be held in the same manner as prescribed by law, and the returns shall be made in the same manner as prescribed by law.
(Code 1983, § 9-1)
State Law reference— Authority for use of voting machines, O.C.G.A. § 21-2-320.
Sec. 42-2. - Voting precincts.
(a)
Map adopted by reference. The voting districts or precincts within the city for primaries and elections within the city shall be altered, amended, reconstituted and established in accordance with that map shown and attached to the ordinance or resolution from which this subsection derives as exhibit A and which is made a part of this subsection by reference.
(b)
Authority of superintendent of elections. The superintendent of elections is directed to take all actions required and proper under the law for promulgating that map adopted in subsection (a) of this section by reference as exhibit A and ordered to be placed in the permanent minutes and proceedings of the mayor and council reconstituting voting precincts within the city in conformity with the geographical standards and requirements of O.C.G.A. § 21-2-261.1, and to give public notice thereof as required by such law and to call such public meetings or hearings as may be required by such law for the mayor and council and to take all other acts necessary and proper to effect compliance with such law, including but not limited to making required filings with the secretary of state and the United States Department of Justice.
(Code 1983, § 9-3)
Sec. 42-3. - Qualifying period for municipal candidates.
The qualifying period for candidates for elective office in the city shall be as provided by state law.
(Code 1983, § 9-2; Ord. of 6-19-1995, § 1(9-2); Ord. No. 10-08, § 1, 6-7-2010)
Sec. 42-4. - Qualifying fees.
The qualifying fees for candidates for elective office in the city shall be as provided by state law.
(Ord. of 6-19-1995, § 2(9-4); Ord. No. 10-08, § 2, 6-7-2010)
