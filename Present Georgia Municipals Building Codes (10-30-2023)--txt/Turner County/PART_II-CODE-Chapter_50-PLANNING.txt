Sec. 50-1. - Comprehensive plan adopted.
The county board of commissioners adopt the 2025 Greater Turner Comprehensive Plan and transmit notification to the South Georgia Regional Development Center and the state department of community affairs for certification of completion. Such plan, as now or hereafter amended, is incorporated in this section as if set out at length in this section.
(Res. of 12-21-1994; Res. No. 2005-03-01, 3-10-2005)
