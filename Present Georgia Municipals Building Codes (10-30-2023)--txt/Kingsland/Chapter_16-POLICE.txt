Sec. 16-1. - False report of crime.
No person in the city shall make to or file with the police department of the city any false, misleading or unfounded statement or report concerning the commission or alleged commission of any crime occurring within the city.
(Code 1968, § 7-504(4))
State Law reference— Stirring up of groundless actions; encouraging legal proceedings, O.C.G.A. § 16-10-95.
Sec. 16-2. - Resisting or interfering with officer.
No person in the city shall resist any police officer, any member of the police department or any person duly empowered with police authority while in the discharge or apparent discharge of his duty, or in any way interfere with or hinder him in the discharge of his duty.
(Code 1968, § 7-504(5)(a))
State Law reference— Hindering or obstructing law enforcement officers, resisting arrest, O.C.G.A. § 16-10-24.
Sec. 16-3. - Escape from jail.
No person in the city shall, while a prisoner in the city jail or in any other place where prisoners are confined, or while otherwise in custody of and confined by the city, escape or attempt to escape or assist others to escape or attempt to escape from such custody or confinement.
(Code 1968, § 7-504(5)(b))
State Law reference— Escape, O.C.G.A. § 16-10-52; aiding or permitting escape, O.C.G.A. § 16-10-53.
