ARTICLE VI. - LICENSES IN DOWNTOWN OVERLAY DISTRICT

Sec. 3-140. - Bar, pub, tavern, public entertainment facility and brew pub licenses.
Any owner of a bar, pub, tavern, public entertainment facility or a brew pub wishing to sell or offer alcoholic beverages for consumption on the premises and located within the downtown overlay district shall obtain a downtown overlay district license which shall be supplemental to and in addition to any other alcoholic beverage license required by this chapter.
(Ord. (W-10-12) of 9-11-12)
Sec. 3-141. - Hours of operation.
Bars, pubs, taverns or public entertainment facilities that do not otherwise qualify as a restaurant or brew pub must be closed between the hours of 1:55 a.m. on Sunday morning and 6:00 a.m. the following Monday morning.
(Ord. (W-10-12) of 9-11-12)
Sec. 3-142. - State law.
No license shall be issued under this article if the granting of such license would constitute a violation of state law or regulations.
(Ord. (W-10-12) of 9-11-12)
