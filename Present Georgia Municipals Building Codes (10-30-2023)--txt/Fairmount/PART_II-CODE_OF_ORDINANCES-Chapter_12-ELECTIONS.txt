Chapter 12 - ELECTIONS

Sec. 12-1. - Voter registration.
Any person who is now a legal registered voter of the city is now a permanent registered voter and eligible to vote in any future general or special election of the city, if he is still qualified in other respects to do so; and any person who is qualified to vote and shall register to vote in the city shall not be required to reregister to vote so long as that person is qualified to vote in other respects.

Sec. 12-2. - Polling times.
The voting polls shall be open from 7:00 a.m. to 7:00 p.m. for each election held.
