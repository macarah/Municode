Charter reference— Mayor and council, art. II.

Sec. 2-1-1. - Meetings; regular; special.
(a)
Regular meetings of the mayor and council shall be held in the council chamber of the city hall on the second and fourth Mondays of each month at 6:30 p.m.
(b)
Special meetings of the mayor and council may be held on call as provided in the City Charter.
(Ord. No. 86-2, § 1, 1-27-86)
Charter reference— [Powers of city vested in] mayor and council[; regular and special meetings; notice of special meetings; quorum], § 2-4.
Sec. 2-1-2. - Rules of procedure; order of business.
Unless otherwise provided in this Code, rules of procedure for meetings of the mayor and council shall follow an edition of Roberts' Rules of Order prescribed by the mayor, and all matters to be discussed at a meeting shall appear in the meeting agenda which shall be prepared by the city manager.
