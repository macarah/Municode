Sec. 50-201. - Plumbers.
No master, contracting, or journeyman plumber, as such terms are defined in O.C.G.A. § 43-14-2 shall be permitted to engage in any plumbing business in this city unless such person shall hold a valid certificate of qualification issued to him by the division of master plumbers and journeyman plumbers.
(Code 1982, § 5-203(1))
Sec. 50-202. - Electrical contractors.
No electrical contractor, as such term is defined in O.C.G.A. § 43-14-2 shall be permitted to engage in any electrical contracting business in this city unless such person shall hold a valid license issued to him by the division of electrical contractors.
(Code 1982, § 5-203(2))
Sec. 50-203. - Conditioned air contractors.
No conditioned air contractor, as such term is defined in O.C.G.A. § 43-14-2, shall be permitted to engage in any warm air contracting business in this city unless such person shall hold a valid certificate of qualification issued to him by the division of conditioned air contractors or by the county in which this city is located.
(Code 1982, § 5-203(3))
Sec. 50-204. - General contractors.
All general contractors who contract with this city for any public work exceeding $1,000.00 in amount shall be required to obtain a performance and payment bond in the amount and manner specified by law.
(Code 1982, § 5-203(4))
