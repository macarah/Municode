ARTICLE I. - IN GENERAL

Sec. 10-1. - Liability insurance required for operators of certain amusement devices.
All persons shall, prior to operating an amusement device where human beings are conveyed, obtain a permit from the city. Upon requesting a permit, the applicant must show evidence of personal liability insurance in the amount of $500,000.00 for each person and $1,000,000.00 for each accident plus $100,000.00 property damages. The applicant shall provide the name of a person domiciled in the county as agent for service of process.
(Code 1986, § 9-1-5)

ARTICLE II. - ADULT ENTERTAINMENT

Sec. 10-31. - Provisions saved from repeal.
The ordinance adopted October 11, 1993, on the subject of adult entertainment is not repealed and shall continue in full force and effect pending a revision of the subject matter by the city.
