Sec. 50-1. - Ordinance incorporated by reference.
The Land Development Ordinance of the City of Woodstock, Georgia, January 24, 1995, or as hereafter amended," adopted by the city council on January 24, 1995, is incorporated into this chapter as fully as though set out at length in this chapter. Copies of the land development ordinance are maintained on file in the office of the planning and zoning administrator where they are available for public inspection.
(Code 1989, § 8-3-1)
