ARTICLE II. - AREA PLANNING AND DEVELOPMENT COMMISSION[2]

Footnotes:
--- (2) ---
Cross reference— Administration, ch. 2.

Sec. 30-31. - Name.
The Middle Georgia Area Planning Commission shall be known as the Middle Georgia Area Planning and Development Commission.
(Ord. of 2-5-1972, § 1; Code 1987, § 16-20)
Sec. 30-32. - Membership as provided by law.
The area planning and development commission, by bylaw, shall provide for its membership and for the selection of commission representatives by the member governments, subject, of course, to the requirements and limitations of this article and of applicable state law. Commission members shall serve without compensation.
(Ord. of 2-5-1972, § 2; Code 1987, § 16-21)
Sec. 30-33. - Officers and management of affairs.
The area planning and development commission is hereby authorized to elect its officers and manage its affairs in accordance with its bylaws, subject, of course, to the requirements and limitations of this article and of applicable state law.
(Ord. of 2-5-1972, § 3; Code 1987, § 16-22)
Sec. 30-34. - Powers, duties and obligations.
The area planning and development commission shall be a joint planning commission as defined in and with all of the powers, duties, and obligations of a joint planning commission set forth in state law.
(Ord. of 2-5-1972, § 4; Code 1987, § 16-23)
State Law reference— Planning and zoning power, Ga. Const. 1983, art. 9, § 2, ¶ IV.
Sec. 30-35. - Bylaws on file.
The initial bylaws of the area planning and development commission are attached to the ordinance from which this article is derived and are on file in the city clerk's office. Such bylaws are hereby approved and consented to.
(Ord. of 2-5-1972, § 5; Code 1987, § 16-24)
Sec. 30-36. - Advisory capacity.
The area planning and development commission shall act in an advisory capacity only, and any plan, or part thereof, or amendment, extension or addition thereto adopted by the commission shall constitute a recommendation only and shall have no binding effect on the member governments in implementing any plans or zoning laws.
(Ord. of 2-5-1972, § 7; Code 1987, § 16-25)
Sec. 30-37. - Other commissions not affected.
The passage of this article shall not affect the present or future powers and duties granted to any existing local planning and zoning commission or in any way affect the future creation of any local planning and zoning commission.
(Ord. of 2-5-1972, § 8; Code 1987, § 16-26)
