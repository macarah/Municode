ARTICLE IX - SCHOOLS
Cross reference— Other state special laws, Division I, Sec. 2-101 et seq

Section 9.1 - School system continued.
The city school system shall be and remain as now provided by the laws creating the same.
The adoption of this charter shall in no way be construed to repeal the act of the General Assembly creating the city school system, or any act amendatory thereof nor shall it affect the millage rates applicable to the said school system.
(Ga. L. 1977, p. 3541, Sec. 9.1)
Cross reference— Board of education, Division I, Part 2, Sec. 2-201 et seq.
Section 9.2 - Powers which may be granted to board of education as to construction and equipment.
The council shall have the full right to delegate to the board of education the right and authority to supervise the construction and equipment of all school buildings and improvements incident thereto, which may be constructed with funds belonging to the city or provided through bond issue.
(Ga. L. 1977, p. 3541, Sec. 9.2)
Section 9.3 - No liability of city.
The council and/or the city shall not, in any way or manner, be liable for any contract or obligation which may be made by the board of education of the city, unless such liability is expressly incurred.
(Ga. L. 1977, p. 3541, Sec. 9.3)
