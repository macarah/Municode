"City of Marietta"

"The undersigned, as Planning and Zoning Director of the City of Marietta, Georgia, hereby approves this plat for the recording of same in the Office of the Clerk of the Superior Court of Cobb County.
"Date"
"Planning and Zoning Director"
"City of Marietta, Georgia"
