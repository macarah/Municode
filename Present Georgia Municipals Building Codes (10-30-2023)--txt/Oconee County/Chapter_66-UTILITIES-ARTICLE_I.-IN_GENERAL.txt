ARTICLE I. - IN GENERAL

Sec. 66-1. - Water and wastewater standards.
(a)
The "Water and Wastewater Standards" adopted January 4, 2011, and incorporated therein by reference are adopted as The Water and Wastewater Standards of Oconee County, Georgia, and referred to in this section as "standards." A copy of such standards are on file in the office of the county clerk.
(b)
The standards adopted in subsection (a) of this section are supplemental to other local, state and federal regulations and laws applicable to the matters addressed by this section and compliance with the same is required as a condition precedent to any actions under this section. To the extent there is a direct conflict between any other county ordinance and the provisions of these water and wastewater standards, such earlier provisions are repealed.
(c)
To the extent any of the standards provide for a different procedure than is required in the intergovernmental agreement between the county and the City of Watkinsville dated January 8, 1991, the provisions of the intergovernmental agreement shall control unless and until the mayor and council of the City of Watkinsville provides otherwise.
(Ord. of 12-19-2000; Ord. of 1-4-2011)
