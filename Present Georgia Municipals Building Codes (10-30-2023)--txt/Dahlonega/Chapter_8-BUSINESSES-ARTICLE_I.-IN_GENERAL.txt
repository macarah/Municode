ARTICLE I. - IN GENERAL

Sec. 8-1. - Nontraditional tobacco paraphernalia.
(a)
The following words, terms and phrases, when used in this section, shall have the meanings ascribed to them in this subsection, except where the context clearly indicates a different meaning:
Nontraditional tobacco paraphernalia means nontraditional instruments (such as bongs and hookah pipes) designed so as to facilitate the smoking, consumption or ingestion of tobacco leaf in any form, provided that the term "nontraditional tobacco paraphernalia" shall exclude cigarette papers or wrappers, blunt wraps, traditional tobacco pipes (such as brand names Briar and Meerschaum), holders, cigarette rolling machines, or other products, devices, or substances used for the purpose of making cigarettes or tobacco in any form whatsoever.
(b)
Any person or entity subject to the occupation tax imposed by the city who offers for sale any item of nontraditional tobacco paraphernalia shall be subject to the provisions of this section.
(c)
Each person or entity subject to this section shall maintain all entire inventory of nontraditional tobacco paraphernalia in a location completely screened from view by the public. It shall be unlawful for a licensee to allow any item of nontraditional tobacco paraphernalia to be in view of the public, except during actual sales of such items of nontobacco paraphernalia to customers.
(d)
Any person found to be in violation of this section shall be subject to the following penalties:
(1)
For the first violation, a fine not less than $250.00;
(2)
For a second violation within one year, a fine not less than $250.00 but not to exceed $500.00; and
(3)
For each additional violation within one year, a fine not less than $500.00 but not to exceed $1,000.00.
