Footnotes:

Cross reference— Civil service system act, ch. 2.120.

2.100.010. - Adopted.
A retirement program for the employees and elected officials of the county, not participating in a pension plan partially or wholly funded with public funds, is created.
(Res. of 12-26-72; Code 1991, § 2.40.010)
2.100.020. - Conformance of plan to county regulations.
The retirement program shall be in accordance with and conform to the plan approved by the board of managers of the Association of County Commissioners of Georgia, and all future amendments to the plan; and in accordance with and in compliance and pursuant to the Declaration of Trust dated October 23, 1968, establishing the Association of County Commissioners of Georgia Pension Trust.
(Res. of 12-26-72, § B; Code 1991, § 2.40.020)
2.100.030. - Plan is noncontributory.
The program shall be on a noncontributory basis.
(Res. of 12-26-72, § C; Code 1991, § 2.40.030)
