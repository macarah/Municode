3.80.010. - Scope.

3.80.020. - Exemptions.
1.
No execution shall be transferred where a valid payment agreement between the taxpayer and the county exists and payments under such agreement are current.
2.
No execution shall be transferred where the subject property is entitled to a senior citizen or veterans' exemption.
3.
No discounts as to the principal amount or interest then due shall be authorized on any execution transferred by the Hall County Tax Commissioner.
4.
No execution shall be transferred by the Hall County Tax Commissioner prior to 120 days after the last date upon which payment of the tax is due.
