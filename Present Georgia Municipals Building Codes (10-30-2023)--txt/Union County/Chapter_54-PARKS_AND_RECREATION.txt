ARTICLE II. - CAMPGROUND PARKING FEES

Sec. 54-31. - Established.
A parking fee of $1.00 per 24-hour period shall be charged for each motor vehicle parking in the Poteete Creek Campground.
(Ord. No. O-99-02, § 1, 12-22-1998)
Sec. 54-32. - Failure to pay; exemptions.
It shall be a violation of this article for any person to park a motor vehicle in the Poteete Creek Campground without paying the parking fee imposed by section 54-31, except authorized county personnel and employees.
(Ord. No. O-99-02, § 2, 12-22-1998)
Sec. 54-33. - Violation; penalty.
Any person violating any provision of this article shall be guilty of violating a duly adopted county ordinance and, upon conviction, such violation shall be punished as provided in section 1-12. O.C.G.A. § 15-10-60 et seq., shall govern the trial of any violation of this article, and any violation of this article may be tried upon citation as contemplated by O.C.G.A. § 15-10-63.
(Ord. No. O-99-02, § 3, 12-22-1998)
