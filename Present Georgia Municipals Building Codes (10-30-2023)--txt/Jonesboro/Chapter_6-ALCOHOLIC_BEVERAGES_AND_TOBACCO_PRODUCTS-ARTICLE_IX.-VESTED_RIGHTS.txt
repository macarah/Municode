ARTICLE IX. - VESTED RIGHTS

Sec. 6-201. - Vested right status for existing establishments.
No establishment, by virtue of being in operation prior to January 10, 2005, shall be allowed to sell distilled spirits by the drink without meeting all requirements of this chapter.
(Ord. No. 2015-006, § 1(Exh. A), 8-10-2015)
