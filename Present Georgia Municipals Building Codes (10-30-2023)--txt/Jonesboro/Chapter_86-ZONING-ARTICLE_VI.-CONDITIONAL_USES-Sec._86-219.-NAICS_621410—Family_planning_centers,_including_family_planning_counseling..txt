Sec. 86-219. - NAICS 621410—Family planning centers, including family planning counseling.
The following conditions are assigned in the H-2, O&I, and C-2 districts:
(1)
Must be located on a street having a classification of collector or greater.
(2)
Must be located in a stand-alone building (no planned centers or connected storefronts).
(Ord. No. 2019-003 , § 1, 2-11-2019; Ord. No. 2019-011 , § 1, 6-3-2019)
