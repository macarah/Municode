Sec. 86-196. - NAICS 6114—Business schools and computer and management training.
The following conditions are assigned in the H-2, O&I, MX and C-2 districts:
(1)
Must be located on a street having a classification of collector or greater.
(2)
Must be established on a lot having a minimum area of one acre and a minimum frontage of 150 feet.
(Ord. No. 2019-003 , § 1, 2-11-2019; Ord. No. 2019-011 , § 1, 6-3-2019)
