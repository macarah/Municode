Sec. 46-1. - Zoning ordinance incorporated by reference.
The zoning ordinance of the city, adopted by the council on March 21, 1985, as now or hereafter amended, is incorporated into this chapter and made a part of this chapter. A copy of the zoning ordinance shall be maintained on file in the office of the city clerk where it shall be available for public inspection.
(Code 1982, § 6-3-1)
