Editor's note— Art. II has been superseded by House Bill No. 685, passed by the Georgia Legislature in its 2012 legislative session and signed into law by the Governor on May 3, 2012, effective July 1, 2012, regarding vicious and dangerous dogs. Former art. II, §§ 14-31—14-39 derived from an ordinance adopted June 22, 1989. An amendment dated February 7, 2013, states that, "Now therefore, be it ordained by the board of commissioners, that the Ordinance previously adopted on the 22 nd day of June, 1989, and known as the Brantley County Dog Control Ordinance is hereby amended so as to strike such definitions and provisions contained in said Ordinance that are in conflict with those definitions and provisions as have been made the general law of the State of Georgia pursuant to the language of House Bill 685 and to retain such definitions and provisions that are not otherwise in conflict with the said general law."

Secs. 14-31—14-39. - Reserved.
