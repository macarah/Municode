Sec. 2-240. - Personnel policy.
The City of Doraville Personnel Policy shall be as created and adopted by the City Manager and kept on file with the City Manager's Office and shall govern all employment and personnel matters of the City as authorized by the City Charter.
(Ord. No. 2013-18, § 1, 11-4-13)
