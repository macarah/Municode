Chapter 15.20 - PLUMBING CODE
Sections:

15.20.010 - Adopted.
There is adopted by and for the city the Standard Plumbing Code recommended by the Southern Building Code Congress, 1991 Edition, including Chapter 1, with revisions as adopted by the Georgia Department of Community Affairs, which is referred to, incorporated in this section and made a part hereof for all purposes, a copy of which code is filed of record in the office of the city clerk.
