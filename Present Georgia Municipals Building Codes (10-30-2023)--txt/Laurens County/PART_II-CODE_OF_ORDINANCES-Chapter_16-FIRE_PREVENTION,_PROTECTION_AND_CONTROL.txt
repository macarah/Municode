ARTICLE II. - RURAL FIRE DEFENSE GROUP EXECUTIVE COMMITTEE

Sec. 16-19. - Established.
The board of commissioners has established an executive committee of the volunteers of the county rural fire defense group.
(Code 1994, § 38-26; Res. of 2-17-1981)
Sec. 16-20. - Composition; terms.
(a)
The committee will be composed of seven members whose terms will be staggered as follows:
(1)
Two members will be appointed for one-year terms.
(2)
Three members will be appointed for two-year terms.
(b)
When the terms of the committee members expire, they may be reappointed by a majority vote of the executive committee, or a new member may be appointed by a majority vote of the committee.
(Code 1994, § 38-27; Res. of 2-17-1981)
Sec. 16-21. - Election of officers; reports; duties.
(a)
Members shall elect officers and shall decide by majority vote which of them shall have one-year terms and which shall be appointed for two-year terms.
(b)
The executive committee will report directly to the county fire chief at regular intervals concerning the overall operations of the rural fire defense group.
(c)
The committee will be responsible for planning and recommending to the fire chief future expense, purchasing equipment for the rural fire services, and furnishing guidelines for day-to-day operations of the rural fire volunteers.
(Code 1994, § 38-28; Res. of 2-17-1981)
