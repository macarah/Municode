ARTICLE I. - IN GENERAL

Secs. 26-1—26-25. - Reserved.
ARTICLE II. - TRUST FUND

Sec. 26-26. - Established.
There is hereby established a cemetery trust fund for the city to be administered by the city administrator or other designee of the mayor and council. This trust fund is to be used for payment of services to maintain all cemeteries within the city except those privately owned or presently maintained by other sources.
(Ord. No. 93-07, § 1, 8-2-93)
Sec. 26-27. - Annual audit.
An audit of the cemetery trust fund will be conducted each year by the city auditor at such time as the regular audit of the city is made.
(Ord. No. 93-07, § 2, 8-2-93)
Sec. 26-28. - City contributions.
In light of the fact that it is in the best interest of the citizens of the city, and conducive to their health and general welfare to have clean, orderly, well kept burial sites, the city is hereby authorized to contribute such amounts or services as from time to time may become prudent to a not for profit organization formed for the purposes of maintaining cemeteries within the limits of the city.
(Ord. No. 93-07, § 4, 8-2-93)
