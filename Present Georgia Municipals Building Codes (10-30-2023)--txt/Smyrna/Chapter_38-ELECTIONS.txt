Sec. 38-1. - Registration of voters.
For the registration of voters in the city the county books shall be used, the current registration therein of any resident of the city constituting registration to vote in city elections.
(Code 1977, § 2-4)
State Law reference— Registration duties of county registrar, O.C.G.A. § 21-3-123.
Sec. 38-2. - Filing notice of candidacy.
Each candidate for municipal office shall file a notice of candidacy during qualifying period in accordance to O.C.G.A. 21-2-132 as amended.
(Code 1977, § 2-3; Ord. No. 2007-11, 8-6-07)
State Law reference— Filing notice of candidacy, O.C.G.A. § 21-3-91.
