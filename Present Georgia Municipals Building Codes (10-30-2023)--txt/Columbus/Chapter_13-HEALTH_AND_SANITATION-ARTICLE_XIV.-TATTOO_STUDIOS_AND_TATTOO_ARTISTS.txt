ARTICLE XIV. - TATTOO STUDIOS AND TATTOO ARTISTS

Sec. 13-541. - State rules and regulations for tattoo studios and tattoo artists adopted by reference.
The Rules and Regulations for Tattoo Studios and Tattoo Artists as adopted by the Columbus Board of Health, and further identified as the proposed Rules and Regulations at Chapter 290-31-40 et seq. are hereby adopted and incorporated herein by reference and a copy of the same is attached hereto.
(Ord. No. 04-43, § 1, 6-8-04)
Sec. 13-542. - Amendments.
(a)
290-31-40.18 of said Rules and Regulations adopted by the Columbus Board of Health shall be amended by adding the following at the end:
(7)
Before a tattoo studio and tattoo artist permit is granted pursuant to this chapter by the health authority, each applicant shall pay a permit fee in the amount established as shown on the attached fee table that is incorporated herein by reference and a copy of the same attached hereto. A copy of said fee table is on file in the office of the clerk of said Council of Columbus.
(8)
Tattoo studio and tattoo artist permits granted by the health authority under this chapter shall expire on June 30 of each year. Permit holders do not have an automatic right to renewal and must be in satisfactory compliance with the provisions of the rules of this chapter for the granting of permits. Permit renewal fee(s) must be received by the health authority within 30 days of the expiration of the permit.
(9)
Permit holders who fail to satisfy compliance with the rules of this chapter at the time of renewal or fail to submit the permit renewal fee as required, are subject to administrative closure of the establishment.
(b)
290-31-40-.22 of said Rules and Regulations shall be deleted in its entirety and there substituted a new section so numbered as stated to read as follows:
290-31-40-.22Penalty. Any person who violates any provision of this Article shall be punished pursuant to section 1-8 of the Columbus Code. Citations for such violations shall be heard in the environmental court division of recorders court.
(Ord. No. 04-43, §§ 2, 3, 6-8-04)
