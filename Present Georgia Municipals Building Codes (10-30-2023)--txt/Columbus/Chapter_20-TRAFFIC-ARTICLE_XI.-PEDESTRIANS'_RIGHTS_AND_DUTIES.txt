Said sections pertained to pedestrian's rights and duties and provided that drivers should exercise due care with regard to pedestrians.
Sec. 20-11.6. - Prohibited crossing.
(a)
Between adjacent intersections at which traffic-control signals are in operation, pedestrians shall not cross at any place except in a crosswalk.
(b)
No pedestrian shall cross a roadway other than in a crosswalk (in the central traffic district or) in any business district.
(c)
No pedestrian shall cross a roadway other than in a crosswalk upon any through street or parkways.
(d)
No pedestrian shall cross a roadway intersection diagonally unless authorized by official traffic-control devices; and when authorized to cross diagonally pedestrians shall cross only in accordance with the official traffic-control devices pertaining to such crossing movements.
(e)
It shall be unlawful for pedestrians to cross streets at street level where overhead crosswalks are available.
(Ord. No. 71-198, § 12, 7-20-71)
