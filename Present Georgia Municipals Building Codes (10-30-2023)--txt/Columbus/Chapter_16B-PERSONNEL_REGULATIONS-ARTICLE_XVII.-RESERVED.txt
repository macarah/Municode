Editor's note— Ord. No. 99-21, § 1, adopted April 13, 1999, repealed former Art. XVII of this chapter in its entirety. Former Art. XVII, §§ 16B-17-1—16B-17-4, pertained to appeals and grievance procedures and derived from Ord. No. 71-213, Ch. 17, §§ 1, 2, adopted Aug. 17, 1971; Ord. No. 71-287, § 3, adopted Nov. 2, 1971; Ord. No. 78-46, §§ 1, 2, adopted May 2, 1978; Ord. No. 78-78, § 1, adopted June 30, 1978; Ord. No. 80-150, § 1, adopted Dec. 30, 1980; Ord. No. 81-1, § 1, adopted January 13, 1981; and Ord. No. 89-26, adopted March 21, 1989. Section 2 of Ord. No. 99-21 adopted Human Resources Policies Numbered 220-501 Work Rules, 220-502 Disciplinary Action, 220-503 Involuntary Termination of Employment, 220-504 Fair Treatment Policy, and 220-505 Personnel Review Board, dated April 1, 1999; official copies of said policies shall be maintained in the office of the city manager.

Secs. 16B-17-1—16B-17-4. - Reserved.
