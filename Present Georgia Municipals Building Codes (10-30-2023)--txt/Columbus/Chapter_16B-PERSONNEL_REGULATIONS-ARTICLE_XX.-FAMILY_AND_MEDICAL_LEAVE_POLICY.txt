ARTICLE XX. - FAMILY AND MEDICAL LEAVE POLICY[6]

Footnotes:
--- (6) ---
Editor's note— Ordinance No. 93-75, adopted Sept. 7, 1993, did not specifically amend this Code; hence, inclusion as Ch. 16B, Art. XX, §§ 16B-20-1—16B-20-4 was at the discretion of the editor.

Sec. 16B-20-1. - Policy adopted.
The attached Family and Medical Leave Policy is hereby adopted. Such policy is not included herein but is on file in the offices of the city.
(Ord. No. 93-75, 9-7-93)
Sec. 16B-20-2. - City manager to enforce policy.
The city manager is authorized to define, implement, maintain and enforce programs consistent with this policy to provide for the mandated family and medical leave.
(Ord. No. 93-75, 9-7-93)
Sec. 16B-20-3. - Personnel division to proceed with plans.
The personnel division is directed to proceed with implementation plans to include provisions for notification, eligibility, record keeping, confidential medical information, other leave and benefit coordination, reinstatements, and general administration necessary for immediate implementation.
(Ord. No. 93-75, 9-7-93)
Sec. 16B-20-4. - Department heads, program managers and elected officials subject to policy.
All department heads, program managers and elected officials with covered employees are subject to the policy hereby adopted.
(Ord. No. 93-75, 9-7-93)
