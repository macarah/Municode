Sec. 60-100. - Qualifications for all directors, coaches and supervisors.
Any person who has been convicted of a felony under the laws of the State of Georgia, the federal government, or any other state or foreign jurisdiction, which shall include all felonies related to sexual crimes, shall be ineligible to serve as a director, coach, or supervisor or supervise either directly or indirectly any child in any Rincon Recreation Program.
(Ord. No. O-2007-018 , 11-26-07)
