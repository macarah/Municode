Chapter 58 - PLANNING[1]

Sec. 58-1. - Adoption of growth management code.
The growth management code of the city, adopted by the mayor and council on September 25, 1989, as Ordinance Number 89-014, as now or hereafter amended, is hereby incorporated into this chapter and made a part of this chapter. A copy of the growth management code shall be maintained on file in the office of the city clerk.
