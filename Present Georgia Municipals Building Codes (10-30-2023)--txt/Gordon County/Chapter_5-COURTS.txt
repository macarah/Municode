Sec. 5-1. - Ten-percent penalty on levied fines.
(a)
There is imposed on every fine levied in the superior court, state court, probate court, magistrate court, municipal court or other court of the county a ten-percent penalty as provided by the Jail Construction and Staffing Act (O.C.G.A. § 15-21-90 et seq.).
(b)
O.C.G.A. §§ 15-21-90 through 15-21-95 are adopted as a part of this section as if quoted herein verbatim.
(Res. of 11-21-89)
Sec. 5-2. - Reserved.
Editor's note— A motion passed Jan. 11, 2001, repealed in its entirety § 5-2 which pertained to the defense of civil actions against sheriff's department employees and derived from a resolution adopted Sept. 5, 1989.
