ARTICLE IV. - FINANCIAL INSTITUTIONS

Sec. 10-114. - Tax levied.
Pursuant to O.C.G.A. § 48-6-93 there is hereby levied an annual business license tax upon state and national banking associations, federal savings and loan associations and state building and loan associations at the rate of $1,000.00 per year.
(Ord. No. 30-100, § 1, 12-14-1982)
Sec. 10-115. - Due date.
The due date of taxes levied by the ordinance from which this article is derived shall be April 1 of each year.
(Ord. No. 30-100, § 2, 12-14-1982)
