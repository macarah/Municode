Sec. 2-4-1. - Incorporation of resolutions by reference.
The following resolutions of the board of commissioners, regulating the matter herein specified, constituting legislation enacted jointly with the City of Albany, and as amended, are hereby incorporated into and made a part of this Code by reference, as fully as if set out at length herein:
(1)
Building: All matters pertaining to building, construction, alterations, repairs, moving, removal, demolition, and all materials and appliances used in connection with the same within the unincorporated area of the county. (Res. No. 82-035, § 1.1, 12-6-82)
(2)
Plumbing: All matters pertaining to plumbing work within the unincorporated area of the county. (Res. No. 82-037, § 1.1, 12-6-82)
(3)
Gas: All matters pertaining to gas work within the unincorporated area of the county. (Res. No. 82-038, § 1.1, 12-6-82)
(4)
Housing: All matters pertaining to the basic minimum housing standards within the unincorporated area of the county. (Res. No. 82-039, § 1, 12-6-82)
(5)
Air conditioning, heating and ventilation: All matters pertaining to air conditioning, heating and ventilation within the unincorporated area of the county. (Res. No. 85-014, § 1.2, 7-23-85)
(6)
Electrical: All matters pertaining to electrical installations, including alterations, repairs, and equipment, within or connected to the electrical system serving the county, within the unincorporated area of the county. (Res. No. 85-015, § 1.2, 7-23-85)
