ARTICLE XV. - TAXICABS[10]

Footnotes:
--- (10) ---
State Law reference— Taxicabs generally, certificate of public necessity, O.C.G.A. § 36-60-25.

Sec. 10-602. - Applicability of article.
This article shall apply to all business licenses or permits issued to taxicab businesses.
(Code 1976, § 7-3041; Code 1985, § 6-70)
Sec. 10-603. - Chauffeur's license required.
It is unlawful for any taxicab to be operated by any person who does not hold a valid state chauffeur's license.
(Code 1976, § 7-3042; Code 1985, § 6-71)
Sec. 10-604. - Insurance required.
It is unlawful for any person to operate a taxicab without first obtaining insurance for personal injury and property damage in the respective limits of $10,000.00 for personal injury, $20,000.00 per accident and $5,000.00 property damage.
(Code 1976, § 7-3043; Code 1985, § 6-72)
Sec. 10-605. - Prior convictions.
It is unlawful for any person to operate a taxicab within the city who has a prior record of conviction, guilty plea or nolo contendere plea, for driving under the influence of intoxicating beverages within the preceding five years.
(Code 1976, § 7-3044; Code 1985, § 6-73)
Sec. 10-606. - Permit number.
It is unlawful for any person to operate a taxicab within the city without first obtaining a permit number for each taxicab from the community affairs department, to be displayed on the right lower corner of the rear windshield of each taxicab; as a condition of obtaining a permit number, the applicant must exhibit to the clerk a chauffeur's license as above provided and insurance as above provided and must submit to a record investigation by the police chief to determine whether or not there are any previous convictions for driving under the influence of intoxicating beverages or any other moving traffic violation. If such a person has an accumulation of points under the motor vehicle laws of the state equal to five or more, that shall be grounds for the denial of such permit.
(Code 1976, § 7-3045; Code 1985, § 6-74)
Sec. 10-607. - Driver registration.
Each driver of a taxicab shall register with the community affairs department in addition to the registration of the individual taxicabs and be subject to police investigation.
(Code 1976, § 7-3046; Code 1985, § 6-75)
Sec. 10-608. - Penalties.
If any taxicab owner or driver is found to be in violation of this article, such person shall be subject to suspension of the business license and permits, and punishment as provided in section 1-9.
(Code 1976, § 7-3047; Code 1985, § 6-76)
