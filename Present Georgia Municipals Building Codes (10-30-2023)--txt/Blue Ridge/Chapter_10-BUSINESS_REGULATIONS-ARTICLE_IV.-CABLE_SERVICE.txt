ARTICLE IV. - CABLE SERVICE

Sec. 10-161. - Franchise fee applicable to holders of cable and video state franchises.
The mayor and council of the city hereby re-state by resolution that the city requires a franchise fee of five percent of any cable or video state franchise holder's gross revenues received from the provision of cable or video service generated within the corporate boundaries of the city, pursuant to a franchise issued by the state pursuant to O.C.G.A. § 36-76-1 et seq., known as the "Consumer Choice for Television Act" of 2007.
(Res. No. BR2018-02, § 1, 2-13-2018)
