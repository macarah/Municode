Sec. 2-61. - Compensation of mayor, councilmembers and municipal judge.
The salary and compensation for the mayor, councilmembers and municipal judge of the city shall be as prescribed from time to time by the governing body, which enactments are on file in the office of the city clerk.
(Code 1988, § 2-3)
Charter reference— Fixing compensation, § 2-103.
Sec. 2-62. - Compensation of city attorney.
The salary of the city attorney shall be fixed by the governing body each year. If the city is a party to, or interested in, litigation which will require the services of the city attorney, such attorney shall be paid additional compensation therefor.
(Code 1988, § 2-4)
Charter reference— City attorney compensation, § 4-202.
