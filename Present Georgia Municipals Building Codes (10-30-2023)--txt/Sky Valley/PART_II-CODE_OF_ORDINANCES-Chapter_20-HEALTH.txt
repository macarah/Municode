Sec. 20-1. - State rules adopted.
(a)
The rules of the Department of Public Health, Chapter Subtitle 511-6-2 (Ga. Comp. Rules and Regs. ch. 511-6-2), Tourist Accommodations, as the same exists or as amended, are adopted by reference as if fully set out herein.
(b)
The rules of the Department of Public Health, Subtitle 511-3-5 (Ga. Comp. Rules and Regs. ch. 511-3-5), Swimming Pools, Spas, and Recreational Water Parks, as the same exists or as amended, are adopted by reference as if fully set out herein.
(c)
The rules of the Department of Public Health, Subtitle 511-3-1 (Ga. Comp. Rules and Regs. ch. 511-3-1), On-Site Sewage Management Systems, as the same exists or as amended, are adopted by reference as if fully set out herein.
(d)
The rules of the Department of Human Resources, Public Health, Chapter 511-6-1 (Ga. Comp. Rules and Regs. ch. 511-6-1), Food Service, as the same exists or as amended, are adopted by reference as if fully set out herein.
(Ord. No. 03-13, § I, 10-16-2003)
