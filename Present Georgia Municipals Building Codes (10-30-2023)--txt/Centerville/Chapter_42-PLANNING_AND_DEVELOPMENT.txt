Sec. 42-1. - Adoption of comprehensive plan.
The city's comprehensive plan, dated January 2,2007, as amended from time to time by the city council, is incorporated as though fully set forth in this chapter.
Secs. 42-2—42-20. - Reserved.
ARTICLE II. - MIDDLE GEORGIA REGIONAL DEVELOPMENT COMMISSION

Sec. 42-21. - Name designated.
The Middle Georgia Area Planning Commission shall henceforth be known as the Middle Georgia Regional Development Commission.
(Code 1992, § 12-26; Ord. of 1-2-1973, § 1)
Sec. 42-22. - Membership.
The Middle Georgia Regional Development Commission by bylaw shall provide for its membership and for the selection of representatives by the member governments, subject, of course, to the requirements and limitations of this article and of applicable state law. Members shall serve without compensation.
(Code 1992, § 12-27; Ord. of 1-2-1973, § 2)
Sec. 42-23. - Organization.
The Middle Georgia Regional Development Commission is hereby authorized to elect its officers and manage its affairs in accordance with its bylaws, subject, of course, to the requirements and limitations of this article and of applicable state law.
(Code 1992, § 12-28; Ord. of 1-2-1973, § 3)
Sec. 42-24. - Powers, duties.
The Middle Georgia Regional Development Commission shall be a joint planning commission as defined in and with all of the powers, duties and obligations of a joint planning commission set forth in that certain Act approved March 13, 1957, (1957 Ga. Laws (Act No. 358, p. 420 et seq.), as heretofore or hereafter amended, and an area planning and development commission as defined in and with all of the powers, duties, and obligations of an area planning and development commission set forth in that certain Act approved March 18, 1970, (1970 Ga. Laws (Act No. 1066, p. 321 et seq.), as heretofore or hereafter amended. The Middle Georgia Regional Development Commission shall also have all the powers, duties and obligations of a joint planning commission and area planning and development commission set forth in any other law of general application pertaining to joint planning commissions and area planning and development commissions.
(Code 1992, § 12-29; Ord. of 1-2-1973, § 4)
Sec. 42-25. - Initial bylaws approved.
The initial bylaws of the Middle Georgia Regional Development Commission are on file in the city clerk's office where they are available for reference and review.
(Code 1992, § 12-30; Ord. of 1-2-1973, § 5)
Sec. 42-26. - Advisory capacity.
The Middle Georgia Regional Development Commission shall continue to act in an advisory capacity only, and any plan, or part thereof, or amendment, extension or addition thereto, adopted by the center shall constitute a recommendation only and shall have no binding effect on the member governments in implementing any plans and/or zoning laws.
(Code 1992, § 12-31; Ord. of 1-2-1973, § 7)
Sec. 42-27. - No effect on local planning and zoning commission.
This chapter shall not affect the present or future powers and duties granted to any existing local planning and zoning commission or in any way affect the future creation of any local planning and zoning commission.
(Code 1992, § 12-32; Ord. of 1-2-1973, § 8)
