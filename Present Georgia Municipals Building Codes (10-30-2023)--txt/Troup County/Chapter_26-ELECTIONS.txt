Sec. 26-1. - Adoption of state rules and regulations.
The rules and regulations promulgated by the state election board that pertain to local elections, together with the provisions of the state election code contained in O.C.G.A. § 21-2-1 et seq., are adopted as the rules, regulations and provisions that shall govern the conduct of local general and special elections in the county.
Sec. 26-2. - Expenses.
Such funds as are necessary for the conduct of elections and for the performance of the duties that are specified by this chapter shall be budgeted and appropriated annually, and at other times when required.
Sec. 26-3. - Polling places and voting precincts; as shown on official map.
The polling places and voting precincts of the county shall be as shown on the official map on file in the office of the county clerk.
