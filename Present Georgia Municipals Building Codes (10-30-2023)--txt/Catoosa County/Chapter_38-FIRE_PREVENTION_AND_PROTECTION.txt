Chapter 38 - FIRE PREVENTION AND PROTECTION

Sec. 38-1. - Countywide fire protection provided.
The county shall provide fire protection for the unincorporated area of the county. The county shall have the authority to provide fire protection in the incorporated areas of the county provided that an agreement for fire protection and payment for the service is executed between the board of commissioners and the governing authority of the respective municipalities.
(Res. of 9-21-93, ยง I)
Sec. 38-2. - Fire protection service contracts and authority.
The board of commissioners shall have authority to:
(1)
Purchase firefighting equipment, vehicles, supplies and appliances;
(2)
Acquire land and construct fire departments;
(3)
Provide fire protection through a public fire department, by providing equipment and fire stations to a private nonprofit corporation (by contract), by contracting with a private corporation or a private nonprofit corporation to provide fire protection services, or by contracting with other governmental entities to provide fire service;
(4)
Enter into contracts to provide fire protection with any person or entity that it deems appropriate to provide fire protection for the citizens of the county.
(Res. of 9-21-93, ยง II)
Sec. 38-3. - Fire protection services.
Fire protection shall be provided for the unincorporated area of the county by the fire protection department, which shall be under the supervision and direction of the county manager or his designee. Nothing contained in this section shall alter or otherwise affect the powers established and conferred in section 38-2 hereof, including, but not limited to, the authority to contract with such agencies as Catoosa County Fire and Rescue, POST Volunteer Fire Department and/or other agencies for fire protection services.
