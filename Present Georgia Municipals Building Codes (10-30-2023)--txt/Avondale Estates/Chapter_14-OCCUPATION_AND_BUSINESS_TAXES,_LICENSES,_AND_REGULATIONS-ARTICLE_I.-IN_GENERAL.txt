ARTICLE I. - IN GENERAL

Sec. 14-1. - Display of merchandise outside of building; prohibited.
(a)
It shall be unlawful for any person to display merchandise for sale, lease or rental on the exterior of any building.
(b)
No city license or permit shall be issued to any person who intends to display merchandise for sale, lease or rental on the exterior of any building in violation of this section.
(Code 1973, § 2-7190; Ord. No. 780, § 2, 4-26-82)
Sec. 14-2. - Weights and measures—Selling by false representation.
Any person who, personally or by servant or agent, shall sell, offer or expose for sale any quantity of any commodity which by weight, measure or numerical count is less than the quantity which such person represents same to be shall be guilty of a misdemeanor.
(Code 1973, § 2-7112)
Sec. 14-3. - Same—Possession or sale of false instruments.
Any person who, personally or by servant or agent, or as the servant or agent of another, shall offer or expose for sale, sell or retain in such person's possession a false weight or measure, or weighing or measuring device, which has not been sealed by a state, county or city sealer of weights and measures within one (1) year, or use same in buying or selling of any commodity or thing or for hire or reward, or shall dispose of any condemned weight, measure, weighing device or measuring device contrary to law, or remove any tag placed thereon by any sealer of weights and measures, or shall, personally or by servant or agent, or as the servant or agent of another, offer or expose for sale, or have in such person's possession for the purpose of selling, any device or instrument to be used to or calculated to falsify weight or measure shall be guilty of a misdemeanor.
(Code 1973, § 2-7113)
Sec. 14-4. - Same—Standard thereof.
The standard of weights and measures as provided in O.C.G.A. § 10-2-1 et seq. shall be the standard of weights and measures for the city.
(Code 1973, § 2-7114)
Sec. 14-5. - Street vending prohibited.
No person shall place or keep any table, stall, booth or other device, implement or structure on any street or sidewalk in the city for the sale of fruit or other thing.
(Code 1973, § 2-7177)
