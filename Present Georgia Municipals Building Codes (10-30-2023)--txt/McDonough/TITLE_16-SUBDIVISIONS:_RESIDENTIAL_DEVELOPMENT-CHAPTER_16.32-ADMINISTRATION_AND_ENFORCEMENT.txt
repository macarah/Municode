CHAPTER 16.32 - ADMINISTRATION AND ENFORCEMENT

Sections:
16.32.010 - Administration.
The community development department director shall administer the process for subdivision plat approval. The community development department director shall:
1.
Receive all subdivision plats and distribute copies of plats to the police department, fire department, and public works department for their review to determine conformity with the provisions of all applicable regulations.
2.
Modify or reject any plats that fail to conform to the zoning and subdivision ordinances and make recommendations for improving the layout and design of subdivisions.
(Ord. No. 04-12-06-A, § I, 12-6-2004; Ord. No. 06-05-01(A), § 68, 5-1-2006)
16.32.020 - Enforcement.
The City Administrator may authorize enforcement action if the developer, or any contractor or subcontractor employed by the developer, fails to comply with any of the provisions of this title.
(Ord. No. 04-12-06-A, § I, 12-6-2004)
16.32.030 - Penalties for violation.
Violations of the provisions of this ordinance or failure to comply with any of its requirements, including violation of conditions and safeguards in connection with grants of variances shall, upon conviction, be punishable by a fine not to exceed one thousand dollars ($1,000.00), or by imprisonment of not more than sixty (60) days, or both; and in addition the violator may be required to pay any court costs which lawfully may be imposed. The imposition of one penalty for any violation shall not excuse the violation or permit it to continue. Each day such violation continues shall be considered a separate offense. Nothing herein shall prevent the City from taking other legal action as determined necessary to prevent or remedy any violation.
(Ord. No. 04-12-06-A, § I, 12-6-2004)
16.32.040 - Variances.
Where it can be shown that the application of any regulation of this ordinance would cause unnecessary hardship if strictly adhered to, or where, because of topographical or other conditions unique to the property would create practical difficulty in its development, the board City Council may authorize a variance after notice and a public hearing.
(Ord. No. 99-10-04, 10-4-1999; Ord. No. 04-12-06-A, § I, 12-6-2004)
