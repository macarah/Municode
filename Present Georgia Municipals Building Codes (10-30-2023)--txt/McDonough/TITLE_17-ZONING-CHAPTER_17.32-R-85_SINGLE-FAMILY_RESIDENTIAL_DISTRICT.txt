CHAPTER 17.32 - R-85 SINGLE-FAMILY RESIDENTIAL DISTRICT

Sections:
17.32.010 - Purpose and permitted uses.
A.
The R-85 single-family residential district is intended to provide suitable areas for single-family, detached dwellings at medium densities, with access to both public water and sewerage.
B.
Within any R-85 Single-Family Residential District, the following uses shall be permitted:
1.
Single-family detached dwellings.
2.
Customary residential accessory buildings or uses, provided such shall be permitted only in a rear yard and shall be situated not less than thee (3) feet from any property line; and further provided that in the case of corner lots, such buildings or structures shall be set back at least forty (40) feet from any side street right-of-way line.
3.
Residential subdivision amenity centers provided by developers or homeowner's associations.
(Zoning Ord. § 810, 1979; Ord. of 10-4-1982; Ord. of 4-4-1983; Ord. No. 04-12-06-B, § X, 12-6-2004; Ord. No. 08-01-22(C), § XII, 1-22-2008)
17.32.020 - Special Uses.
Within any R-85 Single-Family Residential District, the following uses may be allowed upon obtaining a Special Use Permit from the City Council:
A.
Senior Condominium Center.
B.
Churches.
C.
Cemeteries.
D.
Schools.
E.
Public buildings.
(Ord. No. 04-12-06-B, § XI, 12-6-2004; Ord. No. 08-01-22(C), § XIII, 1-22-2008)
