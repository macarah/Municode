CHAPTER 2.34 - SERVICE ON APPOINTED BODIES

Sections:
2.34.010 - Limitation on membership on commissions, committees and other such groups.
(a)
No person shall serve as a member on more than one (1) commission, committee, panel, board, or similar group, body or entity created by this code, by the governing body of the City, by resolution or ordinance of the mayor and Council, or by any agent, officer or employee of the City or member of any such group.
(b)
This Section shall not apply to the mayor or any member of the city council.
(Ord. No. 00-12-11, 12-11-2000)
2.34.020 - Repeal of conflicting ordinance.
The sections, paragraphs, sentences, clauses and phrases of this Ordinance are severable, and if any phrase, clause, paragraph or section of this Ordinance shall be declared illegal by the valid judgment or decree of any court of competent jurisdiction, such illegality shall not affect any of the remaining phases, clauses, sentence, paragraphs and sections of this ordinance. All ordinances and parts of ordinances in conflict herewith are hereby expressly repealed.
2.34.030 - Penalties.
Penalties in effect for violations of the Code of Ordinance, City of McDonough, Georgia at the time of the effective date of this Ordinance shall be and are hereby made applicable to this Ordinance and shall remain in full force and effect.
(Ord. No. 00-12-11, 12-11-2000)
