CHAPTER 2.32 - CITY EMPLOYEE RETIREMENT PLAN

Sections:
2.32.010 - Purpose.
The purpose of the ordinance codified in this chapter is to establish a retirement plan for the benefit of the employees of the city, as authorized and provided by the laws of the State of Georgia.
(Ord. of 11-30-1981, § 1)
Editor's note— An editorial note in the previous publication indicates that this section was amended during the 1997 codification.
2.32.020 - Retirement plan.
The retirement plan, comprising the Table of Contents and twenty-five (25) pages beginning with Article I and ending with Article XII, attached to the ordinance codified in this chapter as "Exhibit A," a copy of which can be found in the office of the city clerk, and each and every provision of the plan is incorporated in this chapter by reference and made a part hereof.
(Ord. of 11-30-1981, § 2)
Editor's note— An editorial note in the previous publication indicates that this section was amended during the 1997 codification.
2.32.030 - Amendment.
This chapter shall be subject to amendment from time to time by the mayor and council and by subsequent amendment of retirement plans by the laws of the state.
(Ord. of 11-30-1981, § 3)
