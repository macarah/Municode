ARTICLE I. - IN GENERAL

Sec. 8-1. - Firearms dealers.
No business license shall be issued for the sale of firearms and ammunition until the applicant submits proof of a federal firearms license to the city.
(Code 1979, § 9-2-4; Code 1998, § 14-1; Ord. No. 23-95, § I, 12-4-1995; Ord. No. 08-96, § 9-2-4, 4-1-1996)
Sec. 8-2. - Blasting contractors.
No business license shall be issued to a blasting contractor, unless the applicant provides proof of a valid state registration card and written approval of the Police Department and county Fire Department. The fee for such license shall be as provided in the business license schedule. All operations of such contractor shall comply with all applicable federal and state requirements.
(Code 1979, § 9-2-5; Code 1998, § 14-2)
