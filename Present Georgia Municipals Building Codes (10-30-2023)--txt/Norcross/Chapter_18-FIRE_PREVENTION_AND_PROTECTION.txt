Sec. 18-1. - Minimum fire safety standards.
The city adopts by reference, incorporates herein as if fully set out, and will enforce the state minimum fire safety standards set forth in O.C.G.A. § 25-2-12. The city authorizes the county fire prevention bureau to enforce all codes pursuant to this chapter for the city; provided, however, pursuant to county code section 46-28, all authorized officers of the Police Department may enforce the provisions within the city.
(Code 1979, § 4-2-1; Code 1998, § 42-1; Ord. No. 24-96, 12-2-1996)
