Chapter 22 - HEALTH CODES

Sec. 22-1. - County vector ordinance adopted.
(a)
The Gwinnett County Vector Control Ordinance, as adopted by resolution of the Gwinnett County Board of Commissioners on July 7, 1998, is hereby adopted by reference and incorporated herein as if fully set out as a general ordinance of the city to the extent that it is not inconsistent with the city's local amendments under this article. A copy of the current vector control ordinance and any additions, deletions, or local amendments thereto, shall be maintained in the Office of the Community Development Department and be available for inspection by the public.
(b)
For the purposes of this section, the following sections of the vector control ordinance are amended as follows:
Section 290-5-58.04 Enforcement. The County Vector Control Ordinance, upon adoption by the City Council of the city shall be enforced in the city by the Gwinnett County Board of Health, Environmental Health Section.
(Code 1998, § 34-112; Ord. No. 15-99, 10-4-1999)
