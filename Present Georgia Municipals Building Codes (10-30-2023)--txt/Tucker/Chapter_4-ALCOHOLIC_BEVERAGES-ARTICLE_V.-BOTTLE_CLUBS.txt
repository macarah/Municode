ARTICLE V. - BOTTLE CLUBS

Sec. 4-361. - Bring your own bottle establishments.
Bring your own bottle establishments cannot hold a license to sell and may not sell or serve any alcoholic beverages. All bring your own bottle establishments must be closed and have no customers in or on the premises by 11:00 p.m.
(Ord. No. O2018-05-17, § 4.251, 5-29-2018)
Sec. 4-362. - Permits.
It shall be unlawful to operate an establishment governed under the provisions of this article without first obtaining a license from the finance department and paying such fees and taxes as may be required by the ordinances, rules and regulations of the city. A bring your own bottle establishment shall be subject to all general licensing and regulations as well as the licensing and regulations for consumption-on-the-premises establishments.
(Ord. No. O2018-05-17, § 4.252, 5-29-2018)
