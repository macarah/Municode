DIVISION 1. - GENERALLY

Sec. 2-2-101. - Investment of funds.
Funds not needed for monthly bills and payroll shall be invested in certificates of deposit. The certificates shall be divided so that the dates of maturity are staggered.
(Mo. of 1-3-84, § 2)
Sec. 2-2-102. - Checks and warrants.
Checks and warrants on the county will be issued and signed by the chairman and the county clerk/administrator. In the absence of the chairman, the vice-chairman and the clerk will sign them. In the absence of the clerk/administrator, the deputy clerk will sign.
(Mo. of 1-3-84, § 1)
