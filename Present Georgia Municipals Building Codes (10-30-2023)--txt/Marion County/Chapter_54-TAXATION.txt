Chapter 54 - TAXATION

Sec. 54-1. - Gross premiums tax on insurance other than life insurance.
In accordance with O.C.G.A. § 33-8-8.2, there is levied for the year 1984, and for each year thereafter, an annual tax upon each insurer doing insurance business within the unincorporated area of the county, in an amount equal to 2.5 percent of the gross direct premiums received during the calendar year on all insurance, other than life insurance, as defined in O.C.G.A. § 33-3-5(2)—(5). The term "gross direct premiums" means gross direct premiums as used in O.C.G.A. § 33-8-8.2(a).
(Res. of 9-6-1983, § 1)
Sec. 54-2. - Sales and use tax for roads, streets, bridges and water and sewer projects.
The county hereby imposes a special county one percent sales and use tax pursuant to the provisions of O.C.G.A. §§ 48-8-110—48-8-121, subject to a referendum approval by the voters of the county, which will be a continuation of the sales and use tax imposed by the voters approximately five years ago and ending December 31, 2003. Such sales and use tax is imposed for the purpose of being used for roads, streets, bridges and water and sewer projects, and shall be imposed for a period of four years for road, street and bridge purposes and for five years for the water and sewer projects. The total amount not to exceed $4,000,000.00. The amount designated for roads, streets, bridges is $3,000,000.00. The amount designated for water and sewer is $1,000,000.00.
(Res. of 10-14-2003; Res. of 11-20-2003)
Sec. 54-3. - Board of tax assessors.
The term length of the board of tax assessors of the county, beginning with January 1, 2008, shall be three years.
(Ord. of 12-11-2007(02))
