Sec. 8-1. - Definitions.
The following words, terms and phrases, when used in this chapter, shall have the meanings ascribed to them in this section, except where the context clearly indicates a different meaning:
Building official or building inspector means the qualified building official or qualified building inspector as designated by the city.
Code enforcement officer means the qualified code enforcement official as designated by the city.
Sec. 8-2. - Enforcement.
(a)
The building official and the code enforcement officer are hereby authorized to enforce within the city the provisions of this chapter and the state minimum codes adopted herein.
(b)
The building official and the code enforcement officer are further authorized to render interpretations of this Code and the adopted state codes and to adopt policies and procedures in order to clarify the application of their provisions. Such interpretations, policies and procedures shall be in compliance with the intent and purpose of this chapter. Such policies and procedures shall not have the force and effect of law and shall not have the effect of waiving requirements specifically provided for in this Code or in the adopted state codes.
Sec. 8-3. - Codes adopted.
(a)
The following state minimum standards codes and permissive codes, as set forth in O.C.G.A. §§ 8-2-20 and 8-2-21, are adopted by the city as though fully set forth herein, as supplemented and amended by the state department of community affairs, and as may be amended by provisions of this chapter or of this Code, generally:

(2)
International Residential Code, 2012 Edition, with Georgia Amendments (2014)(2015)(2018).
(3)
International Fire Code, 2012 Edition, with Georgia Amendments (2014).
(4)

(5)
International Mechanical Code, 2012 Edition, with Georgia Amendments (2014)(2015).
(6)
International Fuel Gas Code, 2012 Edition, with Georgia Amendments (2014)(2015).
(7)

(8)
International Energy Conservation Code, 2009 Edition, with Georgia Supplements and Amendments (2011) (2012).
(9)
International Swimming Pool and Spa Code, 2012 Edition, with Georgia Amendments (2014).
(10)
Disaster Resilient Building Code IBC Appendix (2013).
(11)
Disaster Resilient Building Code IRC Appendix (2013).
(12)
International Property Maintenance Code, 2012 Edition, with Georgia Amendments (2015).
(13)
International Existing Building Code, 2012 Edition, with Georgia Amendments (2015).
(14)
National Green Building Standard, 2008 Edition, with Georgia Amendments (2011).
(b)
Within said codes, when reference is made to the duties of certain officials named therein, a designated official of the county who has duties corresponding to those of the named official in said code shall be deemed to be the responsible official insofar as enforcing the provisions of said code are concerned.
