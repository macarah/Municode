Sec. 22-1. - Adoption of state rules, regulations.
The rules and regulations promulgated by the state election board that pertain to municipal elections, together with the provisions of the state municipal election code contained in O.C.G.A. § 21-3-1 et seq., are adopted as the rules, regulations and provisions that shall govern the conduct of municipal, general and special elections in the city.
Sec. 22-2. - Qualifying fees.
A qualifying fee, as set forth in the schedule of fees and charges on file in the office of the city clerk, shall be paid by all candidates qualifying for any office.
(Ord. of 8-1-95)
Sec. 22-3. - Voting machines.
The use of vote records and tabulating machines for recording and computing the vote at elections held within the city is authorized in city elections, except when the use of voting machines is impracticable.
(Ord. of 9-9-93)
Sec. 22-4. - Expenses.
Such funds as are necessary for the conduct of elections and for the performance of the duties that are specified by this chapter shall be budgeted and appropriated annually, and at other times when required.
Sec. 22-5. - Polling places, voting precincts.
The polling places and voting precincts of the city shall be as shown on the official map on file in the office of the city clerk.
