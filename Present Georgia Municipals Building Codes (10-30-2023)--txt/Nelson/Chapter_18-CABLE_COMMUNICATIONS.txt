State Law reference— Avoiding or attempting to avoid charges for use of cable television service, O.C.G.A. § 46-5-2; municipalities prohibited from granting franchise for cable television within unincorporated area of county, O.C.G.A. § 36-18-3.

Sec. 18-1. - Basic service tier rates and charges.
(a)
The city will follow the FCC rate regulations in its regulation of the basic service rates and charges of the company and any other cable television system operating in the city, notwithstanding any different or inconsistent provisions in the franchise.
(b)
In connection with such regulation, the city will ensure a reasonable opportunity for consideration of the views of interested parties.
(c)
The mayor is authorized to execute on behalf of the city and file with the FCC such certification forms or other instruments as are now or may hereafter be required by the FCC rate regulations in order to enable the city to regulate basic service rates and charges.
(Ord. of 10-5-1993)
