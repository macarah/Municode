ARTICLE I. - IN GENERAL

Sec. 90-1. - Installation of driveway pipe.
Requirements for installation of driveway pipe for the purposes of providing access from private property to county roads are as follows:
(1)
All driveway pipe will be furnished by the landowner.
(2)
A county permit form can be picked up at the tax assessor's office or the county road department office.
(3)
The county engineer or director of roads and public works will determine the size of driveway pipe needed.
(4)
On state rights-of-way a DOT permit will be secured from the DOT by the landowner. The county will act as a contractor for the landowner and install the driveway pipe. The county will not do any paving or other maintenance work on the state rights-of-way.
(5)
On county rights-of-way the county road department will install the driveway pipe furnished by the landowner at no additional cost to the landowner.
(6)
There will be no headwalls on driveway pipe that extend above ground level.
(Mo. of 3-12-90(1))
Sec. 90-2. - Provisions saved from repeal.
The provisions regarding the naming of county roads, as set forth in a motion adopted August 9, 1993, are not repealed and shall continue in full force and effect pending a revision of the subject matter by the county.
