Sec. 14-1. - County provisions adopted.
Pursuant to chapter 10 of the Gwinnett County Code, as may be amended from time to time, such chapter is adopted as and for the regulations of this city, with like effect as if recited in full. The division within the county department of public safety known as the animal control unit has the full authority and responsibility for the enforcement of chapter 10 of the Gwinnett County Code within the city.
(Ord. of 9-25-2000(1); Ord. No. 2019-04, § 1(Exh. A), 4-22-2019)
Sec. 14-2. - Enforcement.
Any authorized officer of the city as well as the county animal control officer and county board of health are given the power to enforce the county animal control ordinance in the city.
