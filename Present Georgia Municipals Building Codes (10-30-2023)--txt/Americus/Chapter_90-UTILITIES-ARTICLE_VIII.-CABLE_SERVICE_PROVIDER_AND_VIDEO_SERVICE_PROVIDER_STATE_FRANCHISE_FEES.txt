ARTICLE VIII. - CABLE SERVICE PROVIDER AND VIDEO SERVICE PROVIDER STATE FRANCHISE FEES

Sec. 90-456. - [State franchise fees.]
Pursuant to the provisions of O.C.G.A. § 36-76-6, there is hereby established within the corporate limits of the City of Americus an annual five percent franchise fee payable to the City of Americus by the holders of state cable service franchises and state video service franchises who operate or provide services within the incorporated area of the city. Such annual five percent franchise fee shall be paid by the holder of each state cable service franchise and the holder of each state video service franchise upon the gross annual revenues received by such state franchise holder from customers residing within the incorporated areas of the city. Such franchise fees shall be paid directly to the city within 30 days after the last day of each calendar quarter. In the event that such franchise fees are not paid in a timely manner, then the city shall have the authority to take such steps that are authorized under O.C.G.A. § 36-76-6(b) in order to collect such franchise fees.
(Ord. No. O-08-03-06, 3-20-2008)
