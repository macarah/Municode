ARTICLE I. - IN GENERAL

Sec. 24-1. - Debt collection.
The city may take necessary steps to collect any outstanding debt or claim due to the city through any means provided by federal, state, or local law which may include the use of the small claims court, a collection agency, or any other means of collection. The cost associated with the processing and collection of the outstanding debt shall be the responsibility of the debtor.
(Ord. No. 08-02, § 1, 6-2-08)
