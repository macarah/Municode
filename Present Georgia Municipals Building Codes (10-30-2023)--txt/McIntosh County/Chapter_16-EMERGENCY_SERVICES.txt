ARTICLE II. - EMERGENCY PREPAREDNESS

Sec. 16-70. - Georgia Mutual Aid Resource Pact.
(a)
The county resolves to join the Georgia Mutual Aid Resource Pact.
(b)
The chair of the county board of commissioners or his designee is designated to act on behalf of the county on all matters relating to the activities and functions of the Georgia Mutual Aid Resource Pact.
(c)
The county may withdraw its membership by delivering written notice to an officer of the Pact giving 90 days' notice.
(d)
There shall be no liability imposed on the county for any action or failure to act as a party of the Georgia Mutual Aid Resource Pact. (Ga. L. 25-6-5)
(Res. of 6-11-2002(2))
Sec. 16-71. - Adoption of NIMS.
The county board of commissioners hereby adopts the National Incident Management System (NIMS) as established under HSPD 5 and the Unified Command System as established under O.C.G.A. § 38-3-57 as its system of preparing for and responding to disaster incidents and directs all incident managers and response organizations in the county train and exercise and use these systems in their response operation.
(Res. of 11-15-2005)
