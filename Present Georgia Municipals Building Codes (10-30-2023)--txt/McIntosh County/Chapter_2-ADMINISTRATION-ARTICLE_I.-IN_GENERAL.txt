ARTICLE I. - IN GENERAL

Sec. 2-1. - Board of commissioners.
At or after the board of commissioners are sworn in and qualified to act on behalf of the citizens of McIntosh County, and on the first Monday in January that has not been declared a national holiday, the board of commissioners shall met for all purposes allowable under the law, including but not limited to the appointment of the clerk of the board of commissioners, the designation of the depository and the legal organ, as well as the election of officers. Currently, the board elects a chair and vice-chair.
