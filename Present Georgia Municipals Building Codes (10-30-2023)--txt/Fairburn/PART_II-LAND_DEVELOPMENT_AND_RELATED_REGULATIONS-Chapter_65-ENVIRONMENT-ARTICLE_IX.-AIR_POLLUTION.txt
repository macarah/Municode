ARTICLE IX. - AIR POLLUTION

Sec. 65-481. - County board of health Regulation No. 24 adopted by reference.
The county board of health Regulation No. 24, entitled Air Pollution Control, is hereby adopted by the city as if fully set forth herein. A copy of the regulation is available for inspection in the office of the city clerk.
(Code 1966, § 13-26; Ord. No. 69-3, § 1, 10-27-1969)
Sec. 65-482. - Penalty for violation.
Any person convicted in the municipal court of the city of the violation of any provision of this article or the regulations adopted hereunder shall be punished by fine not to exceed $100.00 and/or 30 days in prison. In case of continuing violations, each day's violation shall constitute a separate offense and may be punished as such.
(Code 1966, § 13-27; Ord. No. 69-3, § 2, 10-27-1969)
