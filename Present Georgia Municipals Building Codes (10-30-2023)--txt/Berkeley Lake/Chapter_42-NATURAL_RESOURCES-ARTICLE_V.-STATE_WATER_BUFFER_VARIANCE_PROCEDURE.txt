Sec. 42-119. - Adoption.
The city adopts the state water buffer variance procedure in effect as of the date of the ordinance from which this article is derived. A copy shall be maintained in the office of the city clerk for inspection and purchase by the public.
(Code 2004, § 42-101)
Sec. 42-120. - Amendments and supplements.
Deletions and additions to, or the adoption of the latest supplement to the state water buffer variance procedure may be adopted by ordinance of the council and thereby incorporated herein; provided, copies of any changes in the standard text shall be maintained in the office of the city clerk and shall be made available for inspection by the public.
(Code 2004, § 42-102)
