ARTICLE I. - IN GENERAL

Sec. 2-1. - Authorized authority for performance bonds.
Each time there is a requirement or option for a performance bond in this Code, the surety for such bond shall be a surety company licensed and authorized to transact business in the state and approved by the county planning commission.
