ARTICLE I. - IN GENERAL

Sec. 2-1. - Fiscal year.
The fiscal year of the city for accounting and reporting systems is hereby changed from a calendar year to a fiscal year beginning July 1, in one year and ending June 30, the following year.
(Ord. No. 665, § 1, 6-5-1974)
State Law reference— Municipal authority to establish fiscal year, O.C.G.A. § 36-81-3.
