Sec. 2-415. - Coordinantor designated.
The county clerk shall be the coordinator of the county's records management plan and authorized to act for and on behalf of the county council in directing and coordinating all records management matters under the Georgia Records Act, O.C.G.A. § 50-18-90 et seq., the records retention schedules approved by the state, and the provisions of this article, except as limited in this article.
(Res. of 10-9-1986)
Sec. 2-416. - destruction of records.
(a)
No record shall be destroyed except as authorized by the state records retention schedule. Records shall maintained for at least the retention period provided in the state records retention schedule, but may be maintained for such longer period as is deemed necessary or advisable by the county clerk.
(b)
In no event shall any county record maintained under this section be destroyed without the express approval of the county clerk.
(Res. of 10-9-1986)
