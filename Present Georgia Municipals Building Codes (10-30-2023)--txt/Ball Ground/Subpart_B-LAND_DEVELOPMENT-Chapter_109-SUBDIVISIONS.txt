Chapter 109 - SUBDIVISIONS

Sec. 109-1. - Subdivision regulations incorporated by reference.
The subdivision regulations of the city, as prepared by the county-municipal planning commission, and as adopted by ordinance of the mayor and council on March 14, 1976, as now or subsequently amended, are hereby incorporated into this Code and made a part hereof as fully as if set out at length herein. A current copy of the subdivision regulations shall be maintained on file in the office of the city clerk.
