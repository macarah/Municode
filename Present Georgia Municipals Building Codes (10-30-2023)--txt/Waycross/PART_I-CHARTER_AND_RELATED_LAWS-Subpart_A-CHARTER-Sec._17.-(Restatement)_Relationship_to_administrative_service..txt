Sec. 17. - (Restatement) Relationship to administrative service.
Except for the purpose of inquiry, the commission and its members shall deal with the administrative service through the city manager, and neither the commission nor any member thereof shall give orders or instructions to any of the subordinates of the city manager either publicly or privately.
