Sec. 52. - (Revision) Ad valorem taxes; due dates.
The commission of the City of Waycross, by ordinance, shall establish a millage rate for the city property tax, a due date, and the time period within which these taxes must be paid. The commission of the City of Waycross, by ordinance, may provide for the payment of these taxes by installments or in one lump sum, as well as authorize the voluntary payment of taxes prior to the time when due.
