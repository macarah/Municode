Sec. 292. - (Restatement) Social security.
In the event the present federal social security act is amended to include municipal employees, then the said City of Waycross may, in its discretion, substitute, in whole or in part, said social security payments by the municipality and employees, and the benefits accruing therefrom, in lieu of said pension plan as outlined herein.
