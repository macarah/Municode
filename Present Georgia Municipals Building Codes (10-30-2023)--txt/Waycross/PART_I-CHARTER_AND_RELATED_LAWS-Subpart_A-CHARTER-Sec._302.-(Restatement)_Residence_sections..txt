Sec. 302. - (Restatement) Residence sections.
Be it further enacted by the authority aforesaid, that for the reasons above stated, the city commission of the City of Waycross shall have the further right and power in any districts proposed to be set aside primarily for residence purposes to further classify the use thereof and to provide therein the class or classes of residents to be housed therein and to provide therein such other and similar regulations and restrictions as shall secure the peace and good order of the city and the residents thereof. No such ordinance or amendments thereto authorized by either of the above sections shall be adopted except by a majority vote of the city commission.
