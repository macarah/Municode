Sec. 9.1. - (Restatement) Same—Election procedures.
Except as provided otherwise, all elections shall be held and conducted in accordance with the provisions of Chapter 3 of Title 21 of the O.C.G.A., known as the "Georgia Municipal Election Code" [O.C.G.A. § 21-3-1 et seq.], and the commission shall, by ordinance, prescribe such rules and regulations as it deems appropriate to fulfill any options and duties under that chapter.
(1986 Ga. Laws (Act No. 1243), p. 4843, § 1)
