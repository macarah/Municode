Sec. 69. - (Restatement) Double tax.
Be it further enacted, that all property in said city which is required by the laws and ordinances of the city to be returned for taxation, shall be double taxed, if not returned before March 15 of each year; provided, however, that the tax assessors or the city commission shall have power in their discretion to relieve from said double tax for good cause shown.
