Sec. 230. - (Restatement) Tax for street improvements.
In addition to the taxes and assessments now authorized by law to be levied and collected subject to the restrictions now made by law, the mayor and commission shall have full power in the same manner other taxes are levied and collected, to levy and collect annually a special tax not to exceed one-fourth of one per centum on all property, real and personal, within the corporate limits of said city, as now or hereafter laid out, which is taxable under the laws of the state for the purpose of making permanent street improvements.
