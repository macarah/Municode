DIVISION 1. - GENERALLY

Sec. 2-238. - Boards and commissions established by city council.
The city council shall establish such boards and commissions as may be required by state law or as the city council may determine are necessary. The city council may establish membership requirements and procedures for any board or commission.
(Ord. No. 2021-1216-01 , § 1, 12-16-2021)
Sec. 2-239. - Tree commission and parks and recreation advisory board.
(a)
The tree commission shall continue to exist and shall operate according to the provisions found in sections 2-268 through 2-275 as the provisions existed as of December 16, 2021, the deletion of those provisions notwithstanding.
(b)
The parks and recreation advisory board shall continue to exist and operated according to the provisions found in sections 2-276 through 2-281 as the provisions existed as of December 16, 2021, the deletion of those provisions notwithstanding.
(Ord. No. 2021-1216-01 , § 1, 12-16-2021)
