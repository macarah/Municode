Sec. 2-181. - Duties of city clerk.
In addition to the duties of the city clerk under the city Charter, the city clerk shall perform the following duties:
(1)
Be the custodian of the city seal and affix its impression on documents whenever required;
(2)
Preserve the codes, records and documents belonging to the city and maintain a proper index to all records and documents; and
(3)
Perform any other duties as may be required by the mayor and city council.
(Ord. No. 2021-1216-01 , § 1, 12-16-2021)
Sec. 2-182. - Duties of city attorney.
In addition to the duties of the city attorney under the city Charter, the city attorney shall have the following duties:
(1)
Prepare ordinances when so requested by the mayor and city council;
(2)
Prepare for execution all contracts and instruments to which the city is a party when so requested and approve, as to form, all ordinances, bonds and city contracts;
(3)
Render any other legal services as may be required by the mayor and city council; and
(4)
Assist city staff on legal requirements.
(Ord. No. 2021-1216-01 , § 1, 12-16-2021)
