ARTICLE E. - GAS

Sec. 8-2-41. - Fuel gas code adopted.
There is hereby adopted by and for the city, a gas code, known as the Standard Gas Code (International Fuel Gas Code), 2000 edition published by the Southern Building Code Congress International, Inc. (International Code Council), with Georgia amendments, together with any subsequent amendments, additions, or deletions thereto and future updated editions thereof, including any fee schedule included therein, and which is referred to, incorporated herein, and made a part hereof for all purposes, a copy of which code is on file in the office of the city clerk. In the event of any conflict between the provisions of the gas code adopted by this section and any provisions of this Code, state law or ordinances, rules or regulations, the provisions of this Code, state law or ordinances, rules or regulations shall prevail and be controlling.
(Ord. No. 92-004, § 1, 3-16-92; Ord. No. 04-76, § 1, 12-20-2004)
