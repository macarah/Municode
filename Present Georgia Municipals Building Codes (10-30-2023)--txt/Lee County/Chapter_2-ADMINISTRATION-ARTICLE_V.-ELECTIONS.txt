Related laws reference—Board of elections, art. II.

Sec. 2-196. - Powers.
(a)
The Board of Elections of Lee County, Georgia, is authorized to conduct all elections held pursuant to O.C.G.A. § 21-3-1 et seq.
(b)
The board of elections is authorized to and shall perform all duties as superintendents of elections as specified under O.C.G.A. § 21-3-1 et seq., with the exception of the qualifying of candidates.
(Ord. of 6-27-1996, §§ 1, 2)
Sec. 2-197. - Joint contract.
This division shall be valid once a contract is entered into between the county and the City of Smithville in compliance with the laws of the state, which contract shall include a provision requiring the City of Smithville to pay to the county all costs incurred in performing the functions authorized under this division.
(Ord. of 6-27-1996, § 3)
Editor's note— The contract referred to in this section was executed June 27, 1996. An official copy of the contract is on file in the office of the clerk of the board of commissioners.
