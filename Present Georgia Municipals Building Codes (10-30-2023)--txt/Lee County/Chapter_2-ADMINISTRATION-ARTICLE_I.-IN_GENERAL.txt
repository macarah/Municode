ARTICLE I. - IN GENERAL

Sec. 2-1. - Business hours for county courthouse and county offices.
Pursuant to the provisions of O.C.G.A. § 36-1-12, the county courthouse and county offices shall be open during normal working hours for the transaction of the public's business. As used in this section, "normal working hours" shall mean a minimum of 40 working hours during each calendar week, except for those weeks during which public and legal holidays, which are recognized and designated as such by Georgia law, or by the governing authority of Lee County, are observed. No deviation, change, or modification of such normal hours shall be authorized except by majority vote of the governing authority of the county.
(Ord. of 1-25-2011)
Editor's note— Ord. of 1-25-2011 set out provisions intended for use as Chapter 2, Article IX. For purposes of classification, and at the editor's discretion, these provisions have been included as section 2-1.
