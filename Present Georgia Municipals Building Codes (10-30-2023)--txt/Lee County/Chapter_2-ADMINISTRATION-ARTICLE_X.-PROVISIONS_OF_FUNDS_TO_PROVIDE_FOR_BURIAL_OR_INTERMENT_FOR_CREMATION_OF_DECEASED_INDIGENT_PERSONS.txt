ARTICLE X. - PROVISIONS OF FUNDS TO PROVIDE FOR BURIAL OR INTERMENT FOR CREMATION OF DECEASED INDIGENT PERSONS

Sec. 2-450. - Funding to provide interment or cremation for deceased indigent persons.
Pursuant to the provisions of O.C.G.A. § 36-12-5, Lee County shall pay an amount not to exceed $500.00 to provide a decent interment or cremation of a deceased indigent person or to reimburse such person as may have expended the cost of such interment or cremation voluntarily; provided, however, that any such amount paid pursuant to this article shall not exceed the lessor of the actual cost of interment or cremation.
( Ord. of 6-9-2015 )
Sec. 2-451. - Annual appropriation of funds or indigent burials.
The board of commissions shall, in their annual general fund budget, provide appropriation for the payment of such amounts during the fiscal year covered by such budget.
( Ord. of 6-9-2015 )
Sec. 2-452. - Approval of payment.
No payment under this article shall be made without the written approval of the county manager, and in no event shall any such payment under this article exceed $500.00 per burial or cremation.
( Ord. of 6-9-2015 )
Secs. 2-453—2-459. - Reserved.
