ARTICLE II. - SOCIAL SECURITY

Sec. 82-26. - Declaration to participate in retirement system.
It is hereby declared to be the policy and purpose of the city to extend at the earliest date possible under the agreement entered into by the state and the federal security administrator, to the employees and officials thereof, and its instrumentalities, not excluded by law, the benefits of the system of old age and survivors insurance as authorized by the Federal Social Security Act, and amendments thereto, including Public Law 734 of the 81st Congress, and as provided for in O.C.G.A. title 47, ch. 18 (O.C.G.A. § 47-18-1 et seq.). In pursuance of such policy, and for that purpose, the city shall take such action as may be required by applicable state or federal laws or regulations.
(Code 1974, § 17-1; Code 1995, § 78-26)
Sec. 82-27. - Execution of agreements.
The mayor is hereby authorized and directed to execute all necessary agreements and amendments thereto with the Georgia Municipal Employee Benefit System (GMEBS) and officials as provided for in section 82-26.
(Code 1974, § 17-2; Code 1995, § 78-27)
State Law reference— "State agency" defined, O.C.G.A. § 47-18-2(8).
Sec. 82-28. - Withholdings from salaries and wages.
Withholdings from salaries or wages of employees and officials for the purpose provided for in section 82-26 are hereby authorized to be made in the amounts and at such times as may be required by applicable state or federal laws or regulations, and shall be paid over to the state agency.
(Code 1974, § 17-3; Code 1995, § 78-28)
State Law reference— Contributions by employees, O.C.G.A. § 47-18-41(c)(2).
Sec. 82-29. - Employer contributions.
There shall be appropriated from general funds such amounts at such times as may be required by applicable state or federal laws or regulations for employer's contributions, administrative expenses, and a guaranty fund to assure that the state will not incur any debt or loss as a result of the agreement referred to in section 82-27. Such funds shall be paid over to the Georgia Municipal Employee Benefit System (GMEBS) in accordance with regulations established by such agency.
(Code 1974, § 17-4; Code 1995, § 78-29)
State Law reference— Contributions by political subdivisions, O.C.G.A. § 47-18-41(c)(1).
Sec. 82-30. - Records and reports.
The city shall keep such records and make such reports as may be required by applicable state or federal laws or regulations.
(Code 1974, § 17-5; Code 1995, § 78-30)
Sec. 82-31. - Exclusions.
There is hereby excluded from the coverage provided under this article any authority to make any agreement with respect to any position, or any employee or official, not authorized to be covered by applicable state or federal laws or regulations.
(Code 1974, § 17-6; Code 1995, § 78-31)
Secs. 82-32—82-41. - Reserved.
ARTICLE III. - PERSONNEL POLICIES

Sec. 82-42. - Adoption of personnel policy.
The adoption of this Code of Ordinances shall not be construed to repeal the Personnel Policy Ordinance, entitled "Personnel Policy and Procedures Manual," enacted on August 16, 1999, nor shall any amendments thereto be deemed repealed. Such ordinance and any amendments are on file in the office of the city manager, and are hereby expressly continued in full force and effect.
(Ord. No. 99-7-2, 8-16-1999)
