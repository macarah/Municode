ARTICLE XIII. - CABLE AND VIDEO SERVICE PROVIDERS

Sec. 22-500. - Incorporation of the Garden City Cable Communications Franchise Ordinance.
The terms and provisions of the Garden City Cable Communications Franchise Ordinance passed on November 20, 2006, are incorporated herein by reference and made a part hereof, the full text of which is set forth in Appendix ___ of the Code.
(Ord. No. 2008-7, § 1, 2-4-08)
Sec. 22-501. - Franchise fee for state-issued cable or video franchises.
The city hereby requires a franchise fee of five percent of gross revenues generated within the city for any cable or video state franchise issued in its corporate boundaries by the State of Georgia. The city hereby authorizes the city administrator, upon receipt of notice to the city of its rights to designate a franchise fee for an applicant for or holder of an existing state franchise, to provide written notice to the secretary of state and each applicant for or holder of a state franchise.
(Ord. No. 2008-7, § 1, 2-4-08)
