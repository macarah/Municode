DIVISION 13. - LEGAL STATUS PROVISIONS

Sec. 38-584. - Conflict with other laws.
When the provisions of this article specify more restrictive standards than required by any other statute, the requirements of this article shall govern. Whenever the provisions of any other statute require more restrictive standards, the provisions of such statute shall govern.
(Ord. of 5-2016(2), § 14.1)
