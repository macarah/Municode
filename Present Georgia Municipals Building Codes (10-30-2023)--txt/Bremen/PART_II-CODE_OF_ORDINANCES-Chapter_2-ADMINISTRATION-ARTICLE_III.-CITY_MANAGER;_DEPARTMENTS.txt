ARTICLE III. - CITY MANAGER; DEPARTMENTS

Sec. 2-90. - City manager.
In accordance with the requirements of article II, sections 2.18—2.21 of the Charter of the City of Bremen, as enacted June 2, 2010, there is created the position of city manager, whose appointment, term, qualifications, removal, powers and duties, shall be as outlined in article II, section 2.18; section 2.19 and section 2.21 of the Charter of the City of Bremen, dated June 2, 2010. In the event the city manager shall be unable to exercise or perform the duties of the office, the designated acting city manager shall exercise and perform the duties of manager during the manager's temporary absence or physical or mental disability, subject to revocation of designation by the council per the provisions of article II, section 2.20 of the above referenced Charter.
(Ord. No. 2016-O-005, § 2.3.1, 7-18-2016)
Sec. 2-91. - Authorization.
The following departments have been established by prior city councils of the city and may be modified, revised, or eliminated by majority vote of the city council:
(1)
Administration.
(2)
Community development, office of codes enforcement and office of planning and zoning.
(3)
Public works.
(4)
Police.
(5)
Fire.
(6)
Parks and recreation.
(7)
Library.
(Ord. No. 2016-O-005, § 2.3.2, 7-18-2016)
Sec. 2-92. - Right of contract.
The city may contract with third parties to provide all or portions of the functions of any municipal department.
(Ord. No. 2016-O-005, § 2.3.3, 7-18-2016)
Sec. 2-93. - Oversight.
There shall be a director of each department or agency who shall be its principal officer. The city manager shall be the director of the administration department and the city manager shall act as the director of any department for which no active appointment exists for director of that department. Each director shall, subject to the direction and supervision of the city manager, be responsible for the administration and direction of the affairs and operations of the director's department or agency. A single individual may be the director of one or more department.
(Ord. No. 2016-O-005, § 2.3.4, 7-18-2016)
