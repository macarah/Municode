ARTICLE II. - EMERGENCY ACCESS TO PRIVATE PROPERTY

Sec. 16-19. - Entering onto private property in emergency situations.
Officers and employees of the county shall have the authority to enter upon private property to bury dead animals, to close open wells, and in other emergency situations where adequate safety measures have not been taken by other persons in situations where it is necessary to protect public health, safety, and welfare.
(Code 2008, § 13-101)
