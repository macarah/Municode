Sec. 74-1. - Public hearings.
The city council is authorized to hold all public hearings in connection with amendments to the city zoning ordinance and the maps thereof.
(Res. of 4-2-1973; Amend. of 3-17-1998; Ord. No. 1-12-2007, § 1, 12-27-2007)
Sec. 74-2. - Provisions saved from repeal.
The city's zoning rules and regulations, as amended, adopted December 16, 1985, are not repealed and shall continue in full force and effect. The city adopted a revision of the Jackson Zoning Ordinance on December 27, 2007, effective January 1, 2008. A copy of the City of Jackson Zoning Ordinance may be obtained from city hall.
(Amend. of 3-17-1998; Ord. No. 1-12-2007, § 2, 12-27-2007)
