Chapter 26 - PEDDLERS AND SOLICITORS

Sec. 26-1. - Solicitor's permit; application; investigation.
(a)
Permit required. No person except duly organized charitable organizations shall be allowed to solicit within the city without first having obtained a permit from the mayor and council after making written application to the mayor and council stating the nature and purpose of such solicitation. The city may appoint any person to investigate and advise the mayor and council as to the necessity or need for said permit.
(b)
Denial or issuance of permit; violations. Upon authorization, after due hearing, the mayor and council has the right to grant or refuse a solicitor's permit. Should said permit be refused and such person solicit after refusal of said permit, it shall be a violation of this Code. Each solicitation shall be a separate and distinct offense and punishable as such in the mayor's court.
(Code 1986, §§ 15-1, 15-2)
