Sec. 26-1. - Office of municipal court judge established.
It is ordained by the mayor and council to establish the official title and position of judge of the municipal court for the city. The judge of the municipal court shall be elected by majority vote of the mayor and council.
(Code 1980, § 2-3-1(a))
Sec. 26-2. - Removal of municipal judge.
Removal of the municipal judge shall be by majority vote of the mayor and council.
(Code 1980, § 2-3-1(b))
Sec. 26-3. - Compensation of municipal judge.
The salary of the municipal judge shall be as provided by the mayor and council from time to time.
(Code 1980, § 2-3-1(c))
Sec. 26-4. - Hours of operation.
The municipal judge of the court is hereby authorized to impose a technology fee to each citation or accusation brought before said court in which a bond forfeiture, plea of guilty, plea of nolo contendere, or a guilty finding after trial by the municipal court. The technology fee shall be in an amount not to exceed $35.00 per citation and/or accusation. Said technology fee may be amended by the municipal judge of the court when the third party fees are increased.
(Ord. of 4-14-14(2), § 1 )
Sec. 26-5. - Failure to appear warrant costs.
The municipal judge of the court is hereby authorized to impose the cost of service to each failure to appear warrant issued by said court upon failure of the defendant to appear as summoned to an accusation or by warrant, in an amount not to exceed $100.00 per failure to appear warrant issued and served. Said failure to appear warrant cost may be amended by the municipal judge of the court as deemed necessary and allowed by Charter of the City of Vienna.
(Ord. of 4-14-14(1), § 1)
