DIVISION 2. - VENDING MACHINES

Sec. 38-51. - Findings.
(a)
Vending machines are located in jail property in the county and the question has arisen as to the legality of the spending of funds (profits) generated by the operation of these vending machines.
(b)
The attorney general of the state has indicated that the board of commissioners of the county is in control of all county matters.
(c)
To authorize the county sheriff to expend profits from vending machines, the county should give prior authorization.
(Ord. No. 11, §§ 1—3, 4-11-2002)
Sec. 38-52. - Authorization.
(a)
The county sheriff is hereby authorized and permitted to expend funds generated by the operation of vending machines located on jail property in the county, for those purposes for which the county would otherwise have to spend its funds to provide for the prisoners confined in the jail.
(b)
The county sheriff shall keep a written record of profits received and a written record of the expenditure of the funds and exhibit the same to the board of commissioners on the meeting date of the board of commissioners each consecutive month.
(Ord. No. 11, § 4, 4-11-2002)
