ARTICLE 17. - LEGAL STATUS

Sec. 17.1. - Introduction.
This appendix is not intended to interfere with or annul any easement, covenant, or other agreement between parties. However, where this appendix imposes a greater restriction upon the use of property or by private easements, covenants, or agreements, the provisions of this appendix shall govern. Whenever other ordinances or parts of ordinances require greater restrictions than those required by this appendix, such ordinances or parts of ordinances shall govern.
(Ord. No. 2001-07, § 17.1, 4-10-2001)
Sec. 17.2. - Conflicting regulations.
All ordinances and parts of ordinances, regulations, or laws passed by the City Council of Luthersville, Georgia, in conflict herewith, are hereby repealed. All ordinances or parts of ordinances not specifically in conflict herewith are hereby continued in force and effect.
(Ord. No. 2001-07, § 17.2, 4-10-2001)
Sec. 17.3. - Validity.
Should any part, section, or provision of this appendix be declared invalid or unconstitutional by any court of competent jurisdiction, such decision shall not affect the validity of any remaining portion of this appendix.
(Ord. No. 2001-07, § 17.3, 4-10-2001)
