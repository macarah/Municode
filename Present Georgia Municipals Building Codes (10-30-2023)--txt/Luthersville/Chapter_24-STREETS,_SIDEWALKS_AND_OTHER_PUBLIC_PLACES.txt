ARTICLE II. - PARKS

Sec. 24-19. - Parks and recreation.
(a)
All parks, sports fields, and city recreational facilities will be open to the public at 8:00 a.m. and close at dark (at the discretion of the police department). Under special circumstances, a permit may be obtained from city hall for use of these facilities outside of normal hours.
(b)
The following rules apply at all times for the parks, sports fields, and recreation facilities:
(1)
No alcoholic beverages permitted.
(2)
No drugs or drug paraphernalia.
(3)
No lewdness of any kind.
(4)
No weapons (knives, guns, etc.).
(5)
No loud music.
(6)
No horse-playing or climbing of fences.
(7)
No glass items of any kind permitted on courts or fields.
(8)
All trash will be removed or placed in the appropriate receptacles.
(9)
All subjects using these facilities shall at all times act and behave in a respectful and courteous manner towards others.
(10)
Other rules may be enacted at the time of events by city employees, city police officers, or their designee.
(c)
Any violation of the rules governing parks, sports fields, or recreational facilities may result in the person being asked to leave the facility, and being banned from use of the facilities by the municipal court, and could include criminal prosecution.
(d)
The parks, sports fields, and recreational facilities are provided by the city for use by its citizens and the general public. The facilities and the rules governing them were established to provide a safe and friendly place for people, both young and old, to exercise, have fun, and spend time with family and friends.
(e)
Please enjoy these facilities and help keep them clean and safe.
(Ord. No. 2006-3, 8-8-2006)
