CHAPTER VIII. - GENERAL PROVISIONS

Sec. 8.10. - Severability.
In the event any section, subsection, sentence, clause or phrase of this Charter shall be declared or adjudged invalid or unconstitutional, such adjudication shall in no manner affect the other sections, subsections, sentences, clauses or phrases of this Charter, which shall remain of full force and effect, as if the section, subsection, sentence, clause or phrase to declared and adjudged invalid or unconstitutional was not originally a part hereof. The General Assembly hereby declares that it would have passed the remaining parts of this Charter if it had known that such part or parts hereof would be declared or adjudged invalid or unconstitutional.
Sec. 8.11. - Repealers.
(a)
Specific. An Act incorporating the City of Tallapoosa, Georgia, in the County of Haralson, approved December 23, 1896 (1896 Ga. Laws, page 247), as amended, is hereby repealed in its entirety.
(b)
General. All laws and parts of laws in conflict with this Charter are hereby repealed.
Sec. 8.12. - Effective date.
This Charter shall become effective on April 15, 1976.
