ARTICLE 98-1. - INTRODUCTION

Sec. 98-1.1. - Purpose.
This chapter 98 regulates the location, height, bulk, number of stories and size of buildings; the percentage of a lot that may be occupied; the sizes of yards, and other open spaces; the density and distribution of population; the uses of buildings and land for commerce, industry, neighborhoods, public activities, recreation and other purposes; creating districts for such purposes and establishing the boundaries thereof; defining certain terms used in this chapter; providing for the method of administration, amendment and enforcement and for imposition of penalties for violation; providing for a board of zoning appeals and defining the authority and responsibility of the board; providing for a zoning commission and defining the powers and duties of the commission; repealing conflicting ordinances; and for other purposes.
(Ord. No. 1166 , ยง 1(Exh. A), 4-5-22)
Sec. 98-1.2. - Preamble and enactment clause.
In pursuit of authority conferred by Georgia General Planning and Zoning Enabling Act of 1957 (Georgia Laws, 1957, p. 420 as amended), for the purpose of promoting the health, safety, morals, convenience, order, prosperity and welfare of present and future residents of the city in accordance with a comprehensive plan designed to minimize congestion in the streets; to secure safety from fire, panic and other dangers; to provide adequate light and air; to prevent overcrowding of land; to avoid undue concentration of population; to facilitate adequate provision of transportation, water, sewerage, schools, parks and other public improvements, being made with reasonable consideration, among other requirements, of the character of the various zoning districts and their unique suitability for specific uses with the intent to promote desirable living conditions and the stability of neighborhoods; protect property against blight and depreciation; secure economy in public expenditures; conserve the value of buildings and encourage the most appropriate use of land and buildings throughout the municipality, the governing body of the city does ordain and enact into this chapter the articles and sections contained herein.

Sec. 98-1.3. - Title.
This chapter shall hereafter be known and cited as the "City of Thomaston Zoning Ordinance."

Sec. 98-1.4. - Jurisdiction.
This chapter shall govern the use and development of all private property within the corporate limits of the city. However, all public buildings and public property shall be immune to this chapter.
