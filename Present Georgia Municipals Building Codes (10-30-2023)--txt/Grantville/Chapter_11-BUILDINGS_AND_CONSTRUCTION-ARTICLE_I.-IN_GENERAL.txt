ARTICLE I. - IN GENERAL

Sec. 11-1. - Fees established.
The city hereby adopts the schedule of fees for development services associated with requests for annexations, rezonings, variances and conditional uses, and for subdivision plan/plat reviews, subdivision inspections, site plan reviews and site development inspections.
(Ord. No. 05-2006, § 1, 6-12-2006)
