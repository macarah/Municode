ARTICLE VIII. - LOITERING

Sec. 6-211. - Loitering prohibited.
It shall be an offense punishable as provided in section 1-12 of this Code for any person or persons to remain outside the building for more than five minutes of a premises licensed for on-premises consumption.
(Ord. No. 2012-07, ยง 2(Exh. A), 12-6-2012)
Sec. 6-212. - Congregating prohibited.
It shall be an offense punishable as provided in section 1-12 of this Code for any person or persons to congregate outside any building or building portion of a premises licensed for on-premises consumption. The term "congregate" as used herein means any assembly of two or more persons who remain in company with each other for more than five minutes, or who cause a disturbance, or violate any federal or state laws of local ordinances.
