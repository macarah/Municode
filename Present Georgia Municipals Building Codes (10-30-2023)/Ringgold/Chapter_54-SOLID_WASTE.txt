Sec. 54-1. - Garbage disposal.
(a)
No person shall transfer garbage to any place within the city for the purpose of disposal at a location other than that person's own residence.
(b)
The penalty for each violation of this section shall be as prescribed in section 1-11.
(c)
The chief of police or his lawful assistant, the mayor, or the city manager shall be authorized to issue a citation for a violation of this section, requiring an offender to show cause in the municipal court why the penalties provided for in subsection (b) should not be imposed.
(d)
Appeal from the decision of the municipal court may be had as provided by law.
(Ord. of 12-12-94, §§ 1, 3, 5, 6)
