Sec. 34-1. - Adoption of state rules, regulations.
The rules and regulations promulgated by the state election board that pertain to municipal elections, together with the provisions of the state municipal election code contained in O.C.G.A. § 21-3-1 et seq., are adopted as the rules, regulations and provisions that shall govern the conduct of municipal, general and special elections in the city.
Sec. 34-2. - Expenses.
Such funds as are necessary for the conduct of elections and for the performance of the duties that are specified by this chapter shall be budgeted and appropriated annually, and time to time when required.
