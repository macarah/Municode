Chapter 28 - SANITARY SEWERAGE

Sec. 28-1. - Private systems required.
The responsibility for the sanitary and efficient disposal of human waste (i.e., feces and urine of human beings) shall be on the individual landowner.
(Code 1991, ยง 21-101)
Sec. 28-2. - Acceptable methods of disposal.
(a)
It shall be unlawful to dispose of any human waste except by way of a sanitary sewer or septic tank or a sanitary privy of the kind which has been approved by the public health departments of this state and county.
(b)
Any type of privy or other disposal method not in compliance with this chapter shall be declared a public nuisance, subject to abatement.
(Code 1991, ยง 21-102)
Sec. 28-3. - Habitation of noncomplying buildings.
It shall be unlawful to own, lease, or otherwise reside in a residence or building which has no proper sanitary disposal system.
(Code 1991, ยง 21-103)
