Sec. 5-190. - Sign ordinance incorporated by reference.
The sign ordinance of the City of College Park, as adopted by the mayor and council by Ordinance No. 85-13 adopted August 5, 1985, as amended, is hereby incorporated into this Code by reference as fully as though set out at length herein. A current copy of the sign ordinance shall be maintained on file in the office of the city clerk where it shall be available for public inspection.
(Ord. No. 88-11, § 12, 5-2-88)
