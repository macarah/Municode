Sec. 2-36. - Regular meetings.
(a)
The board of commissioners shall meet at some public place in Perry on the first Tuesday of each month at 9:00 a.m.
(b)
The board shall meet at the county annex building in Warner Robins on the third Tuesday of each month at 5:00 p.m.
(Code 1976, § 2-1001; Min. of 7-20-93(1), § 2; Mo. of 1-21-97; Amd. of 12-4-17; Amd. of 1-5-21)
State Law reference— Requirement to prescribe the time, place and dates of regular meetings of board of commissioners, O.C.G.A. § 50-14-1(d); 1993 GA Law (Act #68).
Sec. 2-37. - Special meetings.
The chairman or a majority of the members of the board of commissioners may call a special meeting upon reasonable notice to the other members. No special meeting shall be called unless the notice given shall specify the subject matter to be considered or unless, after the meeting is assembled and all are present, all members agree to transact business not specified in the call, subject to the provisions of law.
(Code 1976, § 2-1002)
State Law reference— Meetings to be open to public, O.C.G.A. § 50-14-1; due notice requirements for other than regular meetings, O.C.G.A. § 50-14-1(d); excluded proceedings, O.C.G.A. § 50-14-3.
Sec. 2-38. - Agenda.
There shall be a written agenda prepared prior to each meeting of the board of commissioners. Persons who desire to address the members of the board at a meeting shall be required to notify the clerk of the board 24 hours prior to commencement of each meeting. Each person who desires to address the board shall be limited in his remarks to five minutes in duration. The chairman of the board of commissioners retains the discretion to recognize any person attending a meeting of the board.
Sec. 2-39. - Rules of procedure.
Robert's Rules of Order, Newly Revised, shall generally be adhered to in all meetings of the board of commissioners.
Sec. 2-40. - Distribution of minutes.
A copy of all minutes shall be delivered or mailed to all members of the board of commissioners within five days of each meeting, and a copy furnished to any other person as designated by the board.
(Code 1976, § 2-1003)
