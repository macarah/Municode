ARTICLE I. - IN GENERAL

Sec. 2-1. - County boundaries.
The boundaries of the county at all times shall be shown on a map or a written description, or any combination thereof, to be retained permanently in the office of the county clerk and to be designated, as the case may be, "official map (or description) of the county boundaries of Houston County, Georgia." Photographic, typed or other copies of such map or description certified by the county clerk shall be admitted as evidence in all courts and shall have the same force and effect as the original map or description.
(Code 1976, §§ 1.101, 1.102)
State Law reference— Extent of jurisdiction of counties divided by water, O.C.G.A. § 36-1-2; county boundaries, O.C.G.A. § 36-3-1 et seq.
