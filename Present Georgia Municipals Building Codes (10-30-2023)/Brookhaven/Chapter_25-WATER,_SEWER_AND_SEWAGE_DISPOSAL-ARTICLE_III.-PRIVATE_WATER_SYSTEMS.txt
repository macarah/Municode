ARTICLE III. - PRIVATE WATER SYSTEMS

Sec. 25-236. - Water purity, protection and sanitation criteria to govern.
Private water systems and vendors shall be governed by all criteria in this chapter relating to water purity, protection of supply and system sanitation, and shall be licensed by the water service provider or its designee.
(Ord. No. O2013-01-55, § 25-126, 1-22-2013)
Sec. 25-237. - Approval of plans.
All plans for private water systems shall be prepared by an engineer of the county and approved by the water service provider prior to construction. Materials of construction shall meet minimum standards set by the county for the public water system.
(Ord. No. O2013-01-55, § 25-127, 1-22-2013)
Sec. 25-238. - Connection to public water system.
No connection shall be made between the public water system and a private water system except by approval of the water service provider. Such approved connections shall be metered and equipped with backflow prevention devices approved by the water service provider and subject to all conditions of this chapter.
(Ord. No. O2013-01-55, § 25-128, 1-22-2013)
Sec. 25-239. - No city or water service provider warranty.
By its regulation of private water systems and suppliers, the city or water service provider assumes no liability for the purity of such water or for damages caused by operators of private facilities.
(Ord. No. O2013-01-55, § 25-129, 1-22-2013)
