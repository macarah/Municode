Sec. 106-1. - Ordinance continued in force.
The adoption of this Code of Ordinances shall not be construed to repeal the subdivision ordinance, entitled "Land Subdivision Regulations," enacted on May 19, 2003; nor shall any amendments thereto be deemed repealed. Such ordinance and any amendments are on file in the office of the city manager, and are hereby expressly continued in full force and effect.
(Ord. No. 2003-04-02, 5-19-2003)
