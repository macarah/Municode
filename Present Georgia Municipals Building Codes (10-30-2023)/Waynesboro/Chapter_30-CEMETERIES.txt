Sec. 30-1. - Sale of lots.
(a)
Cemetery lots sold in both Magnolia Cemetery and the Pines Cemetery shall measure four feet by ten feet for each individual grave space or any multiple thereof. The cost of each grave space shall be established by a fee schedule as fixed from time to time by the mayor and council. Lots shall be sold only to individuals or to a family member of the deceased. Lots may not be purchased by funeral homes or other entities for the purpose resale or to include in funeral services packages.
(b)
The city reserves the right to repurchase any lots at the sale price actually paid by the purchaser, if the purchaser desires to terminate his interest in such spaces. Such lots shall not be resold to any person without express permission of the mayor and council.
(Ord. No. 2016-02-05, § 2, 3-21-2016)
Sec. 30-2. - Vaults required; minimum depth.
Vaults will be required in all burials in the city cemeteries. The vaults and vault tops will be constructed of concrete, steel or similar strength material. The vault tops will be at least 18 inches below the surface of the ground, unless the vault top is used as slab and is at least four inches thick reinforced concrete, steel or similar strength material.
(Ord. No. 2016-02-05, § 2, 3-21-2016)
Sec. 30-3. - Cemetery and cemetery lot care and maintenance.
City cemeteries are not perpetual care cemeteries. City personnel will perform basic yard maintenance on the cemetery grounds on a periodic basis. However, the owner or the family members of the owner of each cemetery lot is responsible for the care and maintenance of such lot.
(Ord. No. 2016-02-05, § 2, 3-21-2016)
