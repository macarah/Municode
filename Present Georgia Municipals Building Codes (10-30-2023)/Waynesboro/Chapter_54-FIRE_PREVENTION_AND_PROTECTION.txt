Sec. 54-26. - Interference with fire apparatus.
It shall be unlawful for any person to ride, climb upon, or in any way handle or obstruct the fire apparatus of the city, except members of the fire department and authorized city officials, unless requested to do so by the fire chief.
(Code 1974, § 9-5; Code 1995, § 58-27)
