ARTICLE XVII. - AMENDMENTS AND MODIFICATIONS

Sec. 26-480. - Procedure.
These regulations may be amended or modified from time to time as needed. Before enacting an amendment or a modification to the regulations, the planning commission or the board of commissioners shall hold a public hearing thereon, notice of which shall be published at least 15 days prior to such hearing in a newspaper of general circulation in the county. The board of commissioners may then adopt the changes.
