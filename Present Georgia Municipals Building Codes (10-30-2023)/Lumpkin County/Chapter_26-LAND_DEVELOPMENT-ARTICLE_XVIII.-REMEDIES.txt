ARTICLE XVIII. - REMEDIES

Sec. 26-499. - Right to legal proceedings.
If any land is used in violation of these regulations, the planning commission, the planning director, the public works director, the county attorney, and the county board of commissioners, any adjacent property owners who would be damaged by such violation, or any other citizen of the county in addition to other remedies, may institute legal proceedings to obtain an injunction or other appropriate remedy to stop the violation or to prevent any act which would constitute a violation.
(Res. No. 2002-44, 12-1-2002; Res. No. 2005-60, 10-21-2005; Res. No. 2005-61, 10-21-2005; Res. No. 2007-44, 9-20-2007; Res. No. 2007-55, 11-15-2007; Res. No. 2009-50, § 1700, 7-16-2009)
