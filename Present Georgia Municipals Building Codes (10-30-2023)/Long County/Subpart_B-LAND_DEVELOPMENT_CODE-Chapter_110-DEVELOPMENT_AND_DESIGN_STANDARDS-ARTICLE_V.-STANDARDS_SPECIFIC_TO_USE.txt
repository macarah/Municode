ARTICLE V. - STANDARDS SPECIFIC TO USE

Sec. 110-111. - Horse stables.
Where horse stables, both commercial and noncommercial, are a permitted use, the following requirements shall apply:
(1)
Must have a minimum of five acres; and
(2)
Must not exceed one horse per one acre.
(Ord. of 2-3-2015, § 8.8.1)
Sec. 110-112. - Kennels.
Where kennels are a permitted use, the following requirements shall apply:
(1)
Must have a minimum of five acres; and
(2)
Dogs must be kept a minimum of 50 feet from all property lines.
(Ord. of 2-3-2015, § 8.9.1)
