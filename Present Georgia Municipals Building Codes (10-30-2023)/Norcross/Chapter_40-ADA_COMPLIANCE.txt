Chapter 40 - ADA COMPLIANCE

Sec. 40-1. - Intent.
It is the policy of the city to not discriminate against Americans with disabilities in conformity with the rules and regulations as promulgated and set forth in 28 CFR 35 and 29 CFR 1602, 1627 and 1630.
(Ord. No. 08-2019 , § II, 6-3-2019)
Sec. 40-2. - Definitions.
The city hereby incorporates into this chapter by reference the definitions set forth in 28 CFR 35.104.
(Ord. No. 08-2019 , § II, 6-3-2019)
Sec. 40-3. - ADA Coordinator.
The ADA Coordinator shall be appointed by the Mayor and Council. It shall be the duty of the ADA Coordinator to meet the requirements of and ensure compliance with 28 CFR 35.107(a).
(Ord. No. 08-2019 , § II, 6-3-2019)
Sec. 40-4. - Grievance procedure for the disabled.
The city shall maintain a formal grievance procedure for the redress of complaints concerning discrimination against the disabled on file with the City Clerk.
(Ord. No. 08-2019 , § II, 6-3-2019)
Sec. 40-5. - Conflicts.
All existing parts of this Code in conflict with 28 CFR 35 are expressly repealed.
(Ord. No. 08-2019 , § II, 6-3-2019)
