ARTICLE N. - STANDARD EXISTING BUILDING CODE

Sec. 8-2-131. - Existing building code adopted.
For the purpose of establishing rules and regulations for the construction, alteration, use, demolition and removal of buildings or other structures, or any appurtenances connected or attached thereto, there is hereby adopted the Existing Building Code, being particularly the 2003 edition thereof, with any subsequent amendments, additions, deletions thereto and future updated editions thereof, including any fee schedule included therein, as published by the International Code Council, and the whole thereof, except the portions as may be hereafter deleted, modified or amended, a copy of which has been and is now filed in the office of the city clerk, and is hereby adopted and incorporated by reference as fully as if set out at length therein, and provisions thereof shall be controlling as to all subjects therein contained, within the corporate limits, except that in the event that any of the provisions are in conflict with other provisions of this Code, state law or ordinances, rules or regulations, the provisions of this Code shall prevail and be controlling.
(Ord. No. 92-011, § 1, 3-16-92; Ord. No. 04-82, § 1, 12-20-2004)
Editor's note— Ord. No. 92-011 added § 8-2-131, adopting the standard existing building code to be used by the city.
