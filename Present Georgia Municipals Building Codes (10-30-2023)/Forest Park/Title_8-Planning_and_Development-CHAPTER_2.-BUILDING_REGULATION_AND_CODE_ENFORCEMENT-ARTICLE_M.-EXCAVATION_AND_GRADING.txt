ARTICLE M. - EXCAVATION AND GRADING

Sec. 8-2-121. - Standard excavations and grading code adopted.
There is hereby adopted by and for the city the Standard Excavation and Grading Code, being particularly the 1978 edition thereof, and any subsequent amendments, additions or deletions thereto, or future updated editions thereof, including any fee schedule included therein, as published by the Southern Building Code Congress International, Inc., which is referred to, incorporated herein and made a part hereof for all purposes, a copy of which code is filed on record in the office of the city clerk, the provisions of which shall be controlling within the city. In the event of any conflict between the provisions of the Standard Excavation and Grading Code and the provisions of this Code, state law or ordinances, rules or regulations, the provisions of this Code, state law or ordinances, rules or regulations shall prevail and be controlling.
(Ord. No. 92-010, § 1, 3-16-92)
Editor's note— Ord. No. 92-010 added § 8-2-121, adopting the standard excavation and grading code to be used by the city.
