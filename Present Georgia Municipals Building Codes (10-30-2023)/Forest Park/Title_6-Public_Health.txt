CHAPTER 1 - PUBLIC HEALTH CODES, RULES, AND REGULATIONS

Sec. 6-1-1. - Clayton County Board of Health On-Site Sewage Management Systems Rules Adopted.
(a)
For the purpose of establishing rules and regulations pertaining to on-site sewage management systems in the city, there is hereby adopted the "On-Site Sewage Management Systems Rules" adopted and approved by the Clayton County Board of Health on or about July 19, 2006, including but not limited to the Regulations attached thereto as Attachment A, which were adopted and approved by the Clayton County Board of Health on or about October 18, 2006, together with all amendments thereto, if any, adopted by the Clayton County Board of Health through and including the effective date of this chapter, a true and correct copy of all of which is currently on file in the Office of the city clerk, and the same is hereby adopted and incorporated herein by reference as fully as if set forth herein in its entirety, and the provisions thereof shall be controlling as to all subjects contained therein within the corporate limits of the City of Forest Park, Georgia, except that in the event that any of the provisions thereof are in conflict with any other provisions of this City Code or any state law or regulation, then in such event the other provisions of this City Code or the state law or regulation shall prevail and be controlling.
(b)
In addition to and independently of subsection (a) of this section, there is hereby adopted and incorporated herein by reference as fully as if the same were set forth herein in their entirety, any future amendments, additions, deletions, and editions of said "On-Site Sewage Management Systems Rules" which may hereafter be adopted and approved by the Clayton County Board of Health, copies of all of which shall be filed with the office of the city clerk and available for inspection and copying by the general public.
(Ord. No. 10-09, § 1, 5-3-2010)
