Sec. 12-1. - Clerk.
There shall be a clerk of the municipal court, who may be an employee of the court who shall be appointed subject to approval of the judge. It shall be the responsibility of the clerk to attend all sessions of the court and keep the records thereof. A clerk pro tempore may be designated to serve in the temporary absence of the clerk, subject to the approval of the judge.
(Code 2018, § 4.101; Ord. No. 2008-1, 8-4-2008)
