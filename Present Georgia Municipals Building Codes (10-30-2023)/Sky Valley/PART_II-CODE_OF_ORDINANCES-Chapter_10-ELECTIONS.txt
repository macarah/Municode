State Law reference— Elections generally, O.C.G.A. § 21-2-1 et seq.

Sec. 10-1. - Election records.
All primary or election documents utilized or maintained by the city shall be kept on file with the other city records for at least 24 months.
(Ord. of 5-1-1978, § 2)
Sec. 10-2. - Polling place.
The city will hold its voting for local, state, and federal elections at the location of 201 West Savannah Street, Clayton, GA 30525.
(Ord. No. 19-02, § 1, 6-23-2019)
State Law reference— Authority to select polling places, O.C.G.A. § 21-2-265.
Sec. 10-3. - Election superintendent authorized to appoint assistants and clerks.
The election superintendent is authorized to appoint such assistants and clerks as he may deem proper for the conducting of all elections, primaries and referendums.
(Ord. of 5-1-1978, § 8)
Sec. 10-4. - Counting of ballots, certifying and declaring results.
The election superintendent and some other person appointed by him shall count the ballots in all elections and certify the election return to the mayor and council and the mayor shall by executive order declare the results of such election.
(Ord. of 5-1-1978, § 9)
