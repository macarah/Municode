ARTICLE I. - IN GENERAL

Sec. 2-1. - Payment of attorney's fees.
The county shall pay no attorney's fees or reimburse any person for the payment of attorney's fees for legal representation of any employee of the county, elected officials of the county, or appointed officials of the county, or any other person other than for legal representation by the county attorney as designated by the board of commissioners or members of its office or staff; or by any attorney having been properly appointed by the court or the indigent defense coordinator's office for the representation of indigent criminal defendants; or as ordered by a court having competent jurisdiction to order the payment of attorney's fees by the county; unless, prior to the employment of any other attorney by any employee, elected officials or appointed officials, or other person the board of commissioners shall have agreed to pay or reimburse for the payment of such attorney's fees and shall have approved the basis by which such fee would be charged or the rate at which such fee would be charged.
(Ord. of 11-15-88)
Sec. 2-2. - Coverage of firefighters.
County volunteer firefighters shall be covered under the Association of County Commissioners of Georgia Group Self-Insured Workers' Compensation Fund while performing their duties as volunteer firefighters.
