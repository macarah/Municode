ARTICLE V. - PLACE NUMBERING

Sec. 2-256. - Title.
This article shall be known and may be cited as "The St. Marys Place Numbering Ordinance."
(Ord. of 11-13-89, § 1)
Sec. 2-257. - Required.
All houses or places of dwelling, business, profession, establishment or other location situated on city streets, alleys or other ways of access in the city shall be numbered in accordance with a system of numbering established by the city's administrative procedures.
(Ord. of 11-13-89, § 2)
Sec. 2-258. - Display.
The number assigned by the city to each and every place as established in section 2-257 shall be promptly displayed by the attachment of the number assigned by the city to that particular place in plain lettering of sufficient size and placement as to be seen and identified from a vehicle moving along the city streets. Such size shall be no less than three inches in height. The colors of the numbers shall be in contrast to their background.
(Ord. of 11-13-89, § 3)
Sec. 2-259. - Assignment of correct numbers.
Prior to displaying a number or numbers, each resident or person in charge of such place or places shall apply to the director of planning of the city or his authorized representative to obtain the correct number for the dwelling or place in question, which number shall be assigned by the aforesaid agent of the city.
(Ord. of 11-13-89, § 4)
Sec. 2-260. - Penalty.
Any person who shall fail to comply with the terms and provisions of this article shall be charged with a violation hereof and upon trial and conviction in municipal court shall be fined and/or sentenced as for a misdemeanor in accordance with the Charter of the city.
(Ord. of 11-13-89, § 5)
Secs. 2-261—2-285. - Reserved.
