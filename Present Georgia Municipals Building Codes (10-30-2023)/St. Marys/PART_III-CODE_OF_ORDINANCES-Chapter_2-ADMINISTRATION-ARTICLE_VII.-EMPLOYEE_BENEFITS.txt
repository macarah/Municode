ARTICLE VII. - EMPLOYEE BENEFITS

Sec. 2-311. - Workers' compensation.
Volunteer firefighters, certified volunteer police officers, noncertified auxiliary police officers, and elected officials shall be covered under the G.M.A. Self-Insurance Workers' Compensation Fund while performing their duties on behalf of the city.
(Ord. of 5-10-93, § I)
Sec. 2-312. - Georgia Municipal Employees Benefit System.
(a)
Pursuant to O.C.G.A. § 47-5-40, the City of Saint Marys hereby subscribes to a plan of employee benefits consisting of group health offered through the GMEBS. Said benefits shall be provided to eligible employees and dependents as defined by the insurance carrier under Policy Number 1016955 (health) issued by Blue Cross Blue Shield of Georgia (or successor). The city agrees to include only qualified active employees. The city subscribes to the PPO 80/60 Plan.
(b)
The city agrees to remit to GMEBS the monthly contributions necessary to keep in force the coverage provided. Such contributions shall be due and payable on the first day of each month and late fees or charges, as established by the GMEBS Board of Trustees, shall be collected on any contributions not received by the 15th day of each month. The city agrees to provide 30 days advance written notice to GMEBS of its intent not to continue participation in the employee benefit program.
(c)
The plan year for the provision of employee benefits offered by the city to its employees shall begin on the first day of August 2007 with the next renewal plan year beginning on the first day of August, 2008. The renewal date in succeeding years to be established by GMEBS in accordance with its operating rules and regulations.
(Ord. of 6-11-07(3), §§ 1—3)
