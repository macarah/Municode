Sec. 42-101. - Definitions.
The following words, terms and phrases, when used in this article, shall have the meanings ascribed to them in this section, except where the context clearly indicates a different meaning:
Open container means any container which is immediately capable of being consumed from or the seal of which has been broken.
(Ord. No. 96-23, art. I(A), 5-21-1996)
Cross reference— Definitions generally, § 1-2.
Sec. 42-102. - Reserved.
Sec. 42-103. - Possession prohibited while operating motor vehicle.
No person shall possess an open container of any alcoholic beverage while operating a vehicle in this city. For the purposes of this article, an open container shall be considered to be in the possession of the operator of a vehicle if the container is not in the possession of a passenger and is not located in a locked glove compartment, locked trunk or other locked nonpassenger area of the vehicle.
(Ord. No. 96-23, art. I(B), 5-21-1996)
Sec. 42-104. - Penalty.
Any person who violates this article shall be cited for appearance before the municipal court and required to post bond. Upon the conviction, punishment shall be as set forth in section 1-11.
(Ord. No. 96-23, art. I(C), 5-21-1996)
