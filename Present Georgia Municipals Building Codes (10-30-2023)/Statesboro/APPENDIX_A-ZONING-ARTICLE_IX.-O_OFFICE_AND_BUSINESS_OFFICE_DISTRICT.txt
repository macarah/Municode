ARTICLE IX. - O OFFICE AND BUSINESS OFFICE DISTRICT

Section 900. - [Generally.]
In an O district, the following regulations shall apply.
Section 901. - Use regulations.
A building may be erected, altered or used, and a lot may be used or occupied for any of the following purposes and no other:
A.
Professional or business offices.
B.
Agencies, studios, schools.
C.
Social or fraternal lodge or club.
D.
Financial institutions, undertaking establishments.
E.
Municipal, county, state, or federal use.
F.
Public utility facilities.
G.
Retail establishments which are found to be complimentary to the surrounding uses permitted in this district when authorized as a special exception by the zoning board of appeals.
H.
Single-family detached dwelling units.
I.
Churches.
J.
Health care facilities, to include hospitals and nursing homes.
All uses shall meet the off-street parking requirements of article XVlI [XVI] and the sign requirements of article XVI [XV].
(Mo. of 10-18-88; Ord. of 4-2-96)
Section 902. - Height, area, width, and yard regulations.
Minimum regulations of building heights, lot area, yards, building line and building coverage shall be in conformity with the requirements of the adjoining residential zone, provided that in the case of two or more surrounding zones the lot area, yard size and setback distances of the least restrictive residential zone shall apply.
Buildings within an office or commercial development, if separated, shall not be less than 20 feet apart.
