Sec. 2-102. - Participation in social security system.
It is hereby declared to be the policy and purpose of the city to extend to the employees and officials thereof, and its instrumentalities, not excluded by law or by this division, the benefits of the system of old-age and survivors insurance as authorized by the federal Social Security Act and amendments thereto.
(Code 1987, § 17-1)
Sec. 2-103. - Agreements with state agency.
The mayor is hereby authorized and directed to execute all necessary agreements and amendments thereto with the employees' retirement system of the state to secure coverage of employees and officials.
(Code 1987, § 17-2)
Editor's note— The city's pension and retirement plan is compiled and published separately by the city. A copy of such plan is on file in the city clerk's office.
