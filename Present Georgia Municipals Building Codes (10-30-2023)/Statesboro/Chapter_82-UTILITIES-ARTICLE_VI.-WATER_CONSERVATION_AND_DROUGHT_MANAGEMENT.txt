ARTICLE VI. - WATER CONSERVATION AND DROUGHT MANAGEMENT

Sec. 82-250-1. - Restrictions on outdoor irrigation.
Persons may irrigate outdoors daily for the purposes of planting, growing, managing or maintaining ground cover, trees, shrubs or other plants between the hours of 4:00 p.m. and 10:00 a.m. Outdoor irrigation shall only be permitted at other times for the following outdoor water uses and applications:
(1)
Commercial agricultural operations as defined in O.C.G.A. § 1-3-3;
(2)
Irrigation of personal food gardens;
(3)
Irrigation of new and replanted plant, seed or turf in landscapes, golf courses or sports turn fields during installation and for a period of 30 days immediately following the date of installation;
(4)
Irrigation of athletic fields, golf courses or public turf grass recreational areas;
(5)
Handwatering with a hose with automatic cutoff or handheld container;
(6)
Installation, maintenance or calibration of irrigation systems;
(7)
Hydroseeding;
(8)
Capture and reuse of cooling system condensation or storm water in compliance with applicable local, state and federal guidelines and regulations;
(9)
Reuse of gray water in compliance with O.C.G.A. § 31-3-5.2 and applicable local, state and federal health regulations;
(10)
Use of drip irrigation or irrigation using soaker hoses;
(11)
Use of water withdrawn from private water wells or surface water by an owner or operator if such well or surface water is on said property; and
(12)
Irrigation of horticultural crops held for sale, resale or installation.
(Ord. No. 2010-23, § 3, 12-7-10)
Sec. 82-250-2. - Enforcement.
(a)
No person shall use or allow the use of water for the purposes of outdoor irrigation in violation of this article.
(b)
The code enforcement division shall be primarily responsible for the enforcement of this article. The city manager may delegate additional departments to aid in the enforcement of this article if deemed necessary.
(c)
Any person, firm or corporation violating any of the provisions of this article shall be guilty of a misdemeanor and shall be punished by a fine of not less than $50.00. Each and every day a violation continues shall be deemed a separate offense.
(Ord. No. 2010-23, § 4, 12-7-10)
