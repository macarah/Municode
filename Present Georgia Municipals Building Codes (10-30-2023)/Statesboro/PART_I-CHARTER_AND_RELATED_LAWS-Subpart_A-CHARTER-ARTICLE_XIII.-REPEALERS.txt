ARTICLE XIII. - REPEALERS

Section 13-1. - [Repealers.]
(a)
Specific repealer. An Act creating a new charter for the City of Statesboro, approved August 17, 1912 (1912 Ga. Laws, page 1331), as amended, is repealed in its entirety.
(b)
General repealer. All laws and parts of laws in conflict with this Act are repealed.
