DIVISION 1. - GENERALLY

Sec. 5-66. - Definitions.
As used in this article, the following terms shall be defined and mean as follows:
Journeyman plumber. A person who performs the manual work of installing plumbing or gas fitting under the direction of a master plumber.
Master plumber. A person who assumes responsible charge and direction of other persons in the installation of plumbing or gas fittings.
(Ord. No. 90-1, 1-11-90)
Secs. 5-67—5-72. - Reserved.
DIVISION 2. - PLUMBING CODE

Sec. 5-73. - Adopted; violation prohibited.
(a)
There is hereby adopted by reference that publication known as the Standard Plumbing Code, latest edition.
(b)
It shall be unlawful for any person to violate any of the provisions of the code adopted in subsection (a).
(Ord. No. 90-1, 1-11-90; Ord. No. 91-8, 9-26-91; Ord. No. 96-05, 3-28-96; Ord. No. 97-3, 2-13-97)
Sec. 5-74. - "Municipality" defined.
As used in the code adopted by the provisions of this division, the term "municipality" means the City of Port Wentworth.
(Ord. No. 90-1, 1-11-90)
Sec. 5-75. - Fees required.
On all buildings, structures or alterations requiring a plumbing permit, a fee for each plumbing permit shall be paid as required at the time of filing application, in accordance with the schedule outlined in section 5-78.
(Ord. No. 90-1, 1-11-90)
Sec. 5-76. - Who may be issued a permit.
A plumbing permit shall be issued only to a master plumber.
(Ord. No. 90-1, 1-11-90)
Sec. 5-77. - Inspection required.
All plumbing construction and repairing within the corporate limits of the town shall be inspected by the plumbing inspector or inspectors. Said inspector shall be a plumbing inspector working under the City of Port Wentworth Inspection Department. Inspectors shall be appointed by the mayor and council of the City of Port Wentworth.
(Ord. No. 90-1, 1-11-90)
Sec. 5-78. - Plumbing permit fees.
For the installation of plumbing only, the following schedule shall apply:
(a)
Permit fee. For issuing each permit the fee shall be fifteen dollars ($15.00), in addition to the following:
Each plumbing fixture .....$5.00
Each plugged or future opening .....5.00
Residential house sewer (new or replaced) .....5.00
Roof drain or area drain .....5.00
Water heater .....10.00
Any repairs, alterations or replacement of fixtures .....5.00
Commercial disposal unit (not to exceed 1½ hp) .....15.00
Residential disposal unit .....5.00
Commercial, industrial and multifamily sewers, per foot .....0.20
Residential, commercial, industrial and multifamily water service mains, per foot .....0.20
Residential water service lines in new houses .....5.00
Replacement of residential water service lines .....5.00
Lawn sprinkler systems, each .....20.00
Repair or alteration of drainage or vent piping .....5.00
Sprinkler systems or backflow preventors or protective devices .....20.00
Fire protection sprinkler systems, per head .....0.25
Oil or grease interceptors .....5.00
Icemakers .....5.00
Hose bibbs with or without vacuum breakers .....5.00
Water softeners .....10.00
Vacuum breakers .....5.00
(b)
Minimum fee. The minimum inspection fee shall be not less than fifteen dollars ($15.00).
(c)
Other fees. A fee for other plumbing inspections which is not established in the above schedule shall be determined by the plumbing inspector subject to the minimum inspection fee as provided above.
(Ord. No. 90-1, 1-11-90)
Secs. 5-79—5-83. - Reserved.
DIVISION 3. - GAS CODE

Sec. 5-84. - Adopted; violation prohibited.
(a)
There is hereby adopted by reference that publication known as the Standard Gas Code, latest edition, recommended by the Southern Building Code Congress International, Inc.
(b)
It shall be unlawful for any person to violate any of the provisions of the code adopted in subsection (a).
(Ord. No. 90-1, 1-11-90; Ord. No. 91-8, 9-26-91; Ord. No. 96-05, 3-28-96; Ord. No. 97-3, 2-13-97)
Sec. 5-85. - "Municipality" defined.
As used in the code adopted by the provisions of this division, the term "municipality" means the City of Port Wentworth.
Sec. 5-86. - Permit fees.
The fees required by the provisions of this division shall be as heretofore or hereafter established by the mayor and council or Port Wentworth.
(Ord. No. 90-1, 1-11-90)
Sec. 5-87. - Gas permit fee.
For gas installation only, the total fees for inspection of consumer's gas piping at one (1) location (including both rough and final piping inspection) shall be fifteen dollars ($15.00) for one (1) to three (3) outlets inclusive, and two dollars ($2.00) for each additional outlet. For L.P. gas tank installation inspection the fee shall be twenty dollars ($20.00).
(Ord. No. 90-1, 1-11-90)
Secs. 5-88—5-115. - Reserved.
