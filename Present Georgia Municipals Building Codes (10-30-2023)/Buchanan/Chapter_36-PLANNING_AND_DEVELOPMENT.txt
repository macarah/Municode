Sec. 36-1. - Adoption of joint comprehensive plan.
Pursuant to O.C.G.A. § 50-8-1 et seq., the document entitled Joint Comprehensive Plan for Haralson County and the Cities of Bremen, Buchanan, Tallapoosa, and Waco Update: 2007-2027 (February 2007) is approved and officially adopted.
(Res. of 2-20-2007)
