Sec. 2-26. - Meetings.
Regular meetings of the mayor and council shall be held at 5:30 p.m. on the first and third Monday in each month providing, however, such meetings may be rescheduled by the mayor and council for sufficient cause by resolution in advance of the regularly scheduled meeting.
(Code 1977, § 2-1005; Ord. No. 01-11, § 1, 1-3-11)
Sec. 2-27. - Rules of procedure adopted.
Robert's Rules of Order are hereby adopted to govern procedures in meetings of the mayor and council.
(Code 1977, § 2-1003)
Sec. 2-28. - Salary of mayor and councilmembers; car allowance.
(a)
The salary of the mayor shall be one hundred thousand dollars ($100,000.00) per annum, and the salary of each of the councilmembers shall be ten thousand dollars ($10,000.00).
(b)
The mayor shall receive a car allowance of two hundred fifty dollars ($250.00) per month unless provided with a city-owned vehicle, in which event the mayor shall not receive such monthly car allowance, and each councilmember shall receive an annual car allowance of three thousand four hundred dollars ($3,400.00).
(Code 1977, § 2-1001; Ord. No. 57-89, § 1, 12-5-89; Ord. No. 28-92, § 1, 5-18-92; Ord. No. 27-97, § 1, 4-7-97; Min. of 1-4-07; Ord. No. 23-09, § 1, 12-7-09; Ord. No. 13-19, § 1, 6-17-19)
Editor's note— Ord. No. 13-19, § 1, adopted June 17, 2019, changed the annual salary of councilmembers from $5,000.00 to $10,000.00. This change, though codified, if not effective until January 1, 2020.
Sec. 2-29. - Bond of mayor.
The bond to be given by the mayor is hereby set at twenty-five thousand dollars ($25,000.00).
(Code 1977, § 2-1006)
Sec. 2-30. - Per diem and travel expenses.
The mayor and council shall be allowed:
(1)
A per diem in an amount equal to the actual and necessary expenses;
(2)
The actual and necessary cost of lodging; and
(3)
That amount as specified under Internal Revenue Service regulations per mile for the use of personal vehicles incurred in carrying out their official duties.
(Code 1977, § 2-1002; Ord. No. 52-89, § 1, 10-16-89)
