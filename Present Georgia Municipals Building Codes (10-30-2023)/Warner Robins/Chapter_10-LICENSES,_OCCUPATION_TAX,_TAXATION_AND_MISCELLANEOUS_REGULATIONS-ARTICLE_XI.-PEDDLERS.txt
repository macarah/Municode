ARTICLE XI. - PEDDLERS

Sec. 10-195. - Compliance with article.
(a)
[Peddlers] meaning those engaged in selling [items such as] fruits, vegetables and Christmas trees.
One-day sale .....$30.00
Two- to three-day sale .....45.00
One-year license .....60.00
(b)
Peddlers selling items on public right-of-way during a parade or public assembly for which a permit has been issued by the police department will have to pay a license fee of forty dollars ($40.00) per day. A permit not requiring payment of a fee shall be required for peddlers selling on behalf of a non-profit entity.
(Ord. No. 48-94, 11-21-94; Ord. No. 56-17, § 1, 11-6-17)
