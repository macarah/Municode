ARTICLE VIII. - TAXICAB SERVICE[3]

Footnotes:
--- (3) ---
Editor's note— Ord. No. 30-15, § 1, adopted September 21, 2015, repealed the former article VIII, §§ 10-150—10-157, and enacted a new article VIII as set out herein. The former article VIII pertained to taxicabs/limousine service and derived from Ord. No. 48-94, adopted November 21, 1994 and Ord. No. 12-07, adopted February 5, 2007.

Sec. 10-150. - Definition.
The following words, terms and phrases, when used in this article, shall have the meanings ascribed to them in this section, except where the context clearly indicates a different meaning:
License means a business license issued under article IV of this chapter.
Taxicab means a motor-driven vehicle of not more than eight-passenger capacity operated by the owner thereof or an agent, lessee or employee of the owner and which is regularly available for hire to transport persons within or outside the city.
Taxicab business means any separately owned and operated taxicab; or a fleet of owned or leased taxicabs; or a fleet of taxicabs owned separately but operating under one (1) name.
(Ord. No. 30-15, § 1, 9-21-15)
Sec. 10-151. - License fee.
The license fee for operating a taxicab service shall be one hundred dollars ($100.00).
(Ord. No. 30-15, § 1, 9-21-15)
Sec. 10-152. - Schedule of fares to be posted.
The schedule of fares shall be posted in each taxicab and in the office of each taxicab company.
(Ord. No. 30-15, § 1, 9-21-15)
Sec. 10-153. - Application; license.
(a)
Every person desiring to operate a taxicab business in the city shall make application for license or privilege to so operate to the city clerk or his designee on blanks furnished by the city, and this application shall contain the name and address of the owner, the make and vehicle serial number, model, year, total mileage and passenger-carrying capacity for each vehicle operated, names of each operator are to be listed.
(b)
Each application shall have the following documents attached:
(1)
A list indicating the make, model, serial number, year, total mileage, and passenger capacity for each operating vehicle;
(2)
A list indicating each operator's name and address;
(3)
A copy of each operator's driver's license; and
(4)
Copies of each operator's for-hire license endorsement or private background check certification.
(Ord. No. 30-15, § 1, 9-21-15)
Sec. 10-154. - License issued; display of license.
If the application meets all requirements, a license shall be issued upon payment of the license fee. This license or a copy thereof shall be displayed on each taxicab operated in the city.
(Ord. No. 30-15, § 1, 9-21-15)
Sec. 10-155. - License amendment.
Each licensee under this article shall amend the license application originally filed for the license to include new taxicabs or exclude old taxicabs promptly upon adding or deleting taxicabs from the licensee's business so that an up-to-date list of operating taxicabs may be maintained.
(Ord. No. 30-15, § 1, 9-21-15)
Sec. 10-156. - Revocation of license or permit.
Any license under this article may be suspended or revoked by the municipal court upon a finding that any provision of this article has been violated or that the person or business holding the license or permit no longer meets the standards required for the issuance of the license or permit.
(Ord. No. 30-15, § 1, 9-21-15)
Secs. 10-157—10-174. - Reserved.
