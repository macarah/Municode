ARTICLE VII. - TATTOO PARLORS

Sec. 10-135. - Compliance with article.
All holders of licenses for the operation of tattoo parlors shall comply with the provisions in this article. Fee for tattoo parlor license is two hundred thirty-five dollars ($235.00).
(Ord. No. 48-94, 11-21-94)
Sec. 10-136. - Prohibitions.
No establishment shall tattoo any person who is:
(1)
Younger than eighteen (18) years of age; or
(2)
Under the influence of alcohol or drugs.
(Ord. No. 48-94, 11-21-94)
Sec. 10-137. - Zone restriction.
All businesses under this article must be operated in a district zoned C-2 or higher.
(Ord. No. 48-94, 11-21-94)
Sec. 10-138. - Inspection.
All parlors under this article shall have an annual inspection by the board of health.
(Ord. No. 48-94, 11-21-94)
