Chapter 15.08 - BUILDING CODE
Sections:

15.08.010 - Adopted.
There is hereby adopted for the purpose of establishing rules and regulations for the construction, alteration, removal, demolition, equipment, use and occupancy, location and maintenance of buildings and structures, including permits and penalties, that certain building code known as the Standard Building Code, recommended by the Southern Building Code Congress International, Inc., being particularly 1991 Edition thereof, including Chapter 1, and Appendix H as adopted by the Georgia Department of Community Affairs, and the whole thereof, of which a copy has been and now is filed in the office of the city clerk, and the same is hereby adopted and incorporated as fully as if set out at length herein, and the provisions of said code shall be controlling in the construction of all buildings and other structures contained within the corporate limits of the city.
