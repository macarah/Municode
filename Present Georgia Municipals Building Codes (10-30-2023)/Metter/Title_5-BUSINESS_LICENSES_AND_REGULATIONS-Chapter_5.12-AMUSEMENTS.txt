5.12.010 - License fees for carnivals, circuses, etc.
A.
A license fee of one hundred dollars ($100.00) per week, or fractional part thereof, is imposed upon each carnival, circus, tent show or other form of itinerant entertainment doing business within the corporate limits of the city; and the grant of any license for such itinerant form of entertainment, except when sponsored by the management of a county agricultural fair, shall be within the discretion of the mayor and council.
B.
The fact that any such form of itinerant entertainment may be sponsored by a civic, fraternal, educational, charitable or religious organization shall not authorize an exemption from the license fee imposed by this section.

5.12.020 - Bond, liability insurance required for amusement rides.
No person shall conduct, operate, manage or sponsor any Ferris wheel, merry-go-round or other amusement ride operated for hire or for the purpose of promoting or advertising any trade or business, without first filing with the city clerk a bond or certificate of liability insurance, in the amount of at least one million dollars ($1,000,000.00), or an amount equal to the limits of general liability insurance carried by the city, indemnifying the public against damages sustained by reason of the operation of such ride. Such bond or certificate of insurance shall apply to all persons, whether or not a license or permit is required by any other provision of this code, state law or city ordinance, rules or regulations.
