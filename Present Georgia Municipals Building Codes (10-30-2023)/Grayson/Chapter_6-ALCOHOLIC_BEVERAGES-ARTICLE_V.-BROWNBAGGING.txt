ARTICLE V. - BROWNBAGGING

Sec. 6-161. - Bring your own bottle "brown bagging" prohibited.
It is prohibited for any person to bring in his own alcoholic beverage (brown bag) in any establishment either licensed or unlicensed to serve alcoholic beverages.
(Ord. of 11-4-1996(3), § 7-2059)
