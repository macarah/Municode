Sec. 3.4.3.5. - Building standards.
A.
Minimum heated floor area. Minimum heated floor area shall be 5,000 square feet.
B.
Building height. Maximum building height shall be 40 feet.
C.
Building separation. No building shall be located less than 20 feet from another such building on the same lot.
D.
Exterior finish materials standards.
1.
The allowed exterior finishes include, but are not limited to, full-depth brick, stone, cast stone or other finished masonry, fiber cement panels, EIFS, glass, or similar material as approved by the community development director. Steel buildings as an exterior finish shall be prohibited.
2.
Restrictions on exterior insulations finish system (EIFS):
a.
EIFS siding shall be installed in accordance with the EIF manufacturer's specifications.
b.
Use of EIFS shall be consistent with the use of EIFS on surrounding development.
