ARTICLE I. - IN GENERAL

Sec. 13-1. - Preaching, lecturing, etc., in public streets restricted.
No preaching, lecturing, speaking or entertainment of any nature shall be permitted in the public streets or public places unless a written permit for same is obtained from the city manager, nor shall any preaching, lecturing, speaking, exhibition or entertainment, any street vending or pushcart stands be permitted except as provided in this chapter.
(Ord. No. 634, § 20.19, 7-5-1972)
