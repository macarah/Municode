ARTICLE IV. - AMUSEMENT SHOWS, CIRCUSES AND CARNIVALS

Sec. 13-116. - Definition.
For the purposes of this article, the word "amusement" shall mean and include all transient and itinerant circuses, carnivals, and amusement shows desiring to set up or operate in the city.
Cross reference— Definitions and rules of construction generally, § 1-2.
Sec. 13-117. - Notice of intent.
Before setting up or operating in the city, an amusement shall give written notice to the director of finance of the city by written application of intention to operate within the city 21 days prior to the date of commencement of the operation.
(Ord. No. 582, § 1(a), 5-21-1969)
Sec. 13-118. - Insurance coverage.
At the time of making application, an amusement shall procure and exhibit to the director of finance policies of insurance covering public liability for injuries to persons coming on or about the premises of operation for any purpose. Such policies of insurance shall be in the minimum amount of $50,000.00 and $100,000.00 and with a company approved by the director of finance.
(Ord. No. 582, § 1(b), 5-21-1969)
Sec. 13-119. - License required.
Before operating in the city, an amusement shall obtain a business license from the city in the amount of $200.00 per day or any fractional part of a day.
(Ord. No. 582, § 1(c), 5-21-1969; Ord. No. 678, § 1, 12-18-1974)
Sec. 13-120. - Compliance with safety codes.
Each amusement shall comply with all codes looking to the safety of the public as directed by fire chief and building inspector of the city.
(Ord. No. 582, § 1(d), 5-21-1969)
Sec. 13-121. - Time for set-up, removal.
Each amusement shall set up the operation in one day's time and remove the operation in one day's time.
(Ord. No. 582, § 1(e), 5-21-1969)
Sec. 13-122. - Clean up after operation.
Upon removal, the grounds used by an amusement shall be cleaned up and left in a neat and safe condition.
(Ord. No. 582, § 1(f), 5-21-1969)
Sec. 13-123. - Sunday operation prohibited.
Every amusement shall comply with the Sunday laws of the city and state and close the operation on Sunday.
(Ord. No. 582, § 1(g), 5-21-1969)
Sec. 13-124. - Location restricted.
The amusement operation shall be carried on at a fair grounds site or upon land zoned highway commercial as set out in the zoning ordinance of the city and on no other.
(Ord. No. 582, § 1(h), 5-21-1969)
Sec. 13-125. - Time for operation limited.
The duration of showing and operating an amusement in the city shall be for one seven-day period, Sundays excepted.
(Ord. No. 582, § 1(i), 5-21-1969)
Sec. 13-126. - Cancellation authorized.
All amusement operations shall comply with all laws of the city and the state. Any violation of such laws shall be cause for the cancellation of the business license issued, and upon notice by the city, its agents or employees, all operations shall cease.
(Ord. No. 582, § 1(j), 5-21-1969)
Secs. 13-127—13-150. - Reserved.
