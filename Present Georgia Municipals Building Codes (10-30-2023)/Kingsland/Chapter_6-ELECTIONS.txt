Sec. 6-1. - Use of county registration list.
(a)
The city hereby adopts and establishes the practice, policy and use of the county elector registration list, which is to say that the official county elector registration list shall be the official city elector registration list for all city elections and public determinations.
(b)
Any person who is a resident of the city and who is registered as an elector with the board of registrars of the county shall be eligible to vote in a municipal primary or election authorized and conducted by the city.
(Ord. No. 1985-3, §§ 1, 2, 4-8-85)
Sec. 6-2. - Limitation of partisan primaries.
No political party shall conduct primaries for the purpose of nominating candidates for municipal elections.
State Law reference— Municipalities may provide that no political party shall conduct primaries for the purpose of nominating candidates for municipal elections, O.C.G.A. § 21-3-95(b).
