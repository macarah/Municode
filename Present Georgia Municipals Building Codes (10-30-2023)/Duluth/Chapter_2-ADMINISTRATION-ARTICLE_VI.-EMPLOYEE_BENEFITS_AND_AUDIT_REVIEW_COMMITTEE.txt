Sec. 2-96. - Duties.
(a)
There is hereby established an employee benefits and audit review committee, composed of up to eight (8) voting members and two (2) staff members. Members shall be appointed by the mayor and council of the city to serve four-year staggered terms. Committee members with backgrounds in employee benefits and / or finance is desirable.
(b)
The committee shall consist of the following:
(1)
Two (2) members of the governing body.
(2)
Six (6) citizens, two (2) of which may be city business owners. One of whom shall be designated as an alternate position
The committee shall also consist of two (2) staff members who shall serve as non-voting members:
The city's finance director.
The city's human resources manager.
(d)
Members shall meet at least annually to analyze and evaluate employee benefits, compensation, and other related matters. Members are responsible for making recommendations before the mayor and council as well as reporting the results of the annual audit report. Members serve on a volunteer basis without compensation or reimbursement for personal expenses, unless specifically approved by the city council.
(e)
Any two (2) members of the committee may request a special meeting, and four (4) members shall constitute a quorum. The committee shall establish its own rules and procedure. The committee shall perform such other duties as may be assigned, or upon request of the governing body.
(Ord. No. O-2021-18 , § I, 3-8-21)
