ARTICLE XVII. - FOOD SALES ESTABLISHMENTS

Sec. 7-730. - Definitions.
Food sales establishment for the purpose of this section means retail and wholesale grocery stores; retail seafood stores and places of business; food processing plants, seafood processing plants, except those processing plants which are currently required to obtain a license from the commissioner of agriculture under any other provision of law; bakeries; confectionaries; fruit, nut, and vegetables stores or roadside stands; wholesale sandwich and salad manufacturers; including vending machines and operations connected therewith; and places of business and similar establishments, mobile or permanent, engaged in the sale of food primarily for consumption off premises.
(Ord. of 1-27-03)
Sec. 7-731. - Posted license required.
Each food sales establishment as defined herein licensed pursuant to this Code shall post such license on the premises in an open or conspicuous manner so as to be visible to the public.
(Ord. of 1-27-03)
Sec. 7-732. - Compliance required for issuance or renewal.
Pursuant to O.C.G.A. § 26-2-25, the city clerk shall not issue or renew any occupation tax for any food sales establishment as defined herein until such establishment complies with the requirements of the Codes of Georgia and of the city.
(Ord. of 1-27-03)
