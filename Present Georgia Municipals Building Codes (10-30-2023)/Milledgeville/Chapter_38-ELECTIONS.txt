Sec. 38-1. - Place of holding.
All municipal elections shall be held at polling places selected by resolution of the council as provided in O.C.G.A. § 21-2-265 et seq.
(Code 1986, § 2-56)
State Law reference— Selection of polling places, O.C.G.A. § 21-2-265 et seq.
Sec. 38-2. - Voting machines.
Voting machines shall be used for recording and computing votes in all municipal elections, and the finance committee of the city is authorized and directed to lease the necessary voting machines for such purpose, all as required by the Georgia Election Code (O.C.G.A. § 21-2-1 et seq.)
(Code 1986, § 2-57)
State Law reference— Voting machines, O.C.G.A. §§ 21-2-310 et seq. and 21-2-450 et seq.
