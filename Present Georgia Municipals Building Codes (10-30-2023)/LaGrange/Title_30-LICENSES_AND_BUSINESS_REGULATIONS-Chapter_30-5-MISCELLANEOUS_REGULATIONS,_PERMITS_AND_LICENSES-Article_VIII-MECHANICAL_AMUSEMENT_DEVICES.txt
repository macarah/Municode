Sec. 30-5-201. - Definition.
For the purposes of this article, the term "mechanical amusement device" shall mean any machine, device or contrivance which may be activated or which is permitted to function by the insertion of a coin, slug, token, plate or disc, and which is operated for amusement only and does not give or offer to give in any manner any form of payoff, prize or reward. The term "mechanical amusement device" does not include a jukebox or similar coin-activated musical devices and does not include a vending machine or similar coin-activated machines for the sale of merchandise.
(Code 1972, § 15-141)

Sec. 30-5-203. - Payment of fees and taxes.
No mechanical amusement device shall be operated or permitted to be operated within the limits of the city unless all fees and/or specified license taxes are paid as required by law.
(Code 1972, § 15-143)
