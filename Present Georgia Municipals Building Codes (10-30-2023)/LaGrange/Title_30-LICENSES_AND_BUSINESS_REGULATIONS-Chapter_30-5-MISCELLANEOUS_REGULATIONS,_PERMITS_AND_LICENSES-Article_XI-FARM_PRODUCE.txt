Article XI - FARM PRODUCE

Sec. 30-5-281. - Exemptions.
This article shall not apply to any farmer who raises and produces his own farm products which he offers for sale to the residents of the city, provided he does not sell his products from a permanent or semipermanent location in the city.
(Code 1972, § 13-44)
Sec. 30-5-282. - Stand or building construction.
(a)
Any person who shall offer for sale at either retail or wholesale any type of produce, fruits or vegetables from any permanent or semipermanent location in the city shall keep said fruits, produce and vegetables housed in a building containing a floor constructed of some material other than dirt or sawdust, and a watertight roof and four (4) walls constructed in such a manner that said structure can be locked when not in operation.
(b)
Any such person operating at a given location for a continuous period of one (1) hour or more shall be considered semipermanent or permanent.
(Code 1972, § 13-40)
State Law reference— Warehousemen, O.C.G.A. § 10-4-1 et seq.
Sec. 30-5-283. - Sale in nonconforming structure.
It shall be unlawful for any person to offer for sale any type of fruit, produce or vegetables from any permanent or semipermanent location in the city unless said fruit, produce and vegetables are housed in a structure as described in section 30-5-282.
(Code 1972, § 13-41)
Sec. 30-5-284. - Compliance prerequisite to license issuance.
All persons who apply for a license to sell any type of fruits, produce or vegetables in the city from a permanent or semipermanent location shall specify the location of the business and must have a structure as described in section 30-5-282 prior to the time that a license to do business in the city may be issued.
(Code 1972, § 13-42)
Sec. 30-5-285. - Compliance before sale by veterans, etc.
Any person who shall operate under a veteran's license or under any other form of license issued by some agency other than the city who shall offer for sale fruits, produce or vegetables of any type from any permanent or semipermanent location is required to comply with section 30-5-282 prior to the time that any of the above named merchandise may be offered for sale or sold from any permanent or semipermanent location in the city.
(Code 1972, § 13-43)
State Law reference— Disabled veterans, blind persons, etc., operating businesses, O.C.G.A. § 43-12-1 et seq.
