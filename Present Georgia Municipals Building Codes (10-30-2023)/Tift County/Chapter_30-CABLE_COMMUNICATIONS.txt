ARTICLE I. - IN GENERAL

Sec. 30-1. - Provisions saved from repeal.
The ordinance of November 29, 1979, authorizing the construction, maintenance and operation of a cable television system, together with the franchise agreement granted pursuant to such ordinance including all transfers and assignments, are not repealed and shall continue in full force and effect. Official copies of such ordinance and franchise are on file in the office of the clerk of the board of commissioners.
Secs. 30-2—30-35. - Reserved.
ARTICLE II. - BASIC SERVICE RATES AND CHARGES

Sec. 30-36. - County to follow FCC rate regulations.
The county will follow the FCC rate regulations in its regulation of the basic service rates and charges of the company and any other cable television system operating in the county, notwithstanding any different or inconsistent provisions in the franchise.
(Res. of 9-13-93, § 1)
Sec. 30-37. - Consideration of views of interested parties.
In connection with such regulation, the county will ensure a reasonable opportunity for consideration of the views of interested parties.
(Res. of 9-13-93, § 2)
Sec. 30-38. - Execution and filing of certification forms or other instruments.
The county administrator is authorized to execute on behalf of the county and file with the FCC such certification forms or other instruments as are now or may hereafter be required by the FCC rate regulations in order to enable the county to regulate basic service rates and charges.
(Res. of 9-13-93, § 3)
