Sec. 2-341. - Fiscal year designated.
The fiscal year of the county shall begin each year on July 1 and end on June 30.
(Mo. of 1-5-81)
State Law reference— Establishment of fiscal year required, O.C.G.A. § 36-81-3.
Sec. 2-342. - Budget officer appointed.
Pursuant to O.C.G.A. § 36-81-4, the county administrator is appointed as the budget officer for the county.
(Mo. of 7-1-91)
State Law reference— Appointment of budget officer, O.C.G.A. § 36-81-4.

Sec. 2-344. - Budget policies.
(1)
Transfers of appropriations within any fund or department shall require only the approval of the finance director so long as the amount of the budget of such fund or department is not increased.
(2)
Transfers of appropriations between or among departments shall be authorized upon the approval of the county manager so long as the total amount of the county budget then in force is not increased. Any interdepartmental transfers of appropriations authorized by the county manager shall be reported to the board of commissioners.
(3)
Any budget amendment which increases the amount of the county budget then in force shall require the approval of the board of commissioners as the governing authority of Tift County.
(Res. No. 2004-02, 1-12-04)
