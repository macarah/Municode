Chapter 47 - TREES

Sec. 47-1. - Tree board establishment.
(a)
There is hereby created the Fort Valley Tree Board. The board shall consist of five members appointed by majority vote of the mayor and council.
(b)
The terms of the members of the tree board shall be for five years; however, the initial board members' terms shall be one through five years as determined by lot. Members may be reappointed at the discretion of the mayor and council without unlimited terms.
(c)
The members shall serve without compensation.
(d)
At the first meeting of each new calendar year the board shall elect a chairperson who shall be a voting member of the board.
(e)
A meeting may be called by the chairperson or by three board members.
(f)
Three board members shall constitute a quorum.
(Ord. No. 06-0316-11, ยง 47-2, 3-16-2006)
Sec. 47-2. - Duties and authority of tree board.
(a)
The tree board is authorized to formulate provisions for a recommended tree ordinance for presentation to the mayor and council for consideration and adoption and, as appropriate, to recommend amendments to the tree ordinance adopted by the mayor and council.
(b)
The tree board is authorized to develop rules for conduct of the board and shall operate pursuant to such rules as approved by the mayor and council.
(c)
The tree board shall have such other duties and authority as conferred by the mayor and council.
