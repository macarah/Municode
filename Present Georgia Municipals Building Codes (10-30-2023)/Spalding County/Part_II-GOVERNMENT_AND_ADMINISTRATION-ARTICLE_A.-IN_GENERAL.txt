ARTICLE A. - IN GENERAL

Sec. 2-7001. - Terms.
Except as otherwise provided by ordinance, every appointed officer shall hold office at the will of the board of commissioners.
Sec. 2-7002. - Records.
All records kept by any officer of the county shall be open to inspection by any member of the board at all reasonable times.
Sec. 2-7003. - Moneys received.
Except as otherwise provided by law or by order of the board of commissioners, every officer or employee shall at least once each day turn over all moneys received by him in his official capacity to the county with a statement showing the source from which the same was received.
Sec. 2-7004. - Termination of office.
Every officer and employee, upon the expiration of his term for any cause whatsoever, shall deliver to his successor all county books and records.
