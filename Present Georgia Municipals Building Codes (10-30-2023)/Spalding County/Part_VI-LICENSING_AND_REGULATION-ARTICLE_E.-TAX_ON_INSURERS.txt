Sec. 6-1151. - Gross premiums tax imposed on insurance other than life insurance.
In accordance with the Official Code of Georgia Annotated, section 33-8-8.2, there is hereby levied, for the year 1984 and for each subsequent year thereafter, an annual tax upon each insurer doing insurance business within the unincorporated area of Spalding County, in an amount equal to two and one-half (2½) percent of the gross direct premiums received during the calendar year from all insurance, other than life insurance, as defined in Official Code of Georgia Annotated, section 33-3-5(2), (3), (4) and (5). "Gross direct premiums" shall mean gross direct premiums as used in Official Code of Georgia Annotated, section 33-8-8.2(a).
(Res. of 10-4-83, § 1)
