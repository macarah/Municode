Sec. 6-351. - Amendments.
The following sections are hereby revised:
Section 101.1 Insert "Douglas County, Georgia" as the name of jurisdiction.
Section 103.5. Insert "Said schedule shall be the actual cost to the County to perform the activities and services."
Section 302.4. Insert "8 inches" as the maximum permissible height that grass and weeds can reach.

Section 602.4. Insert "September" to "April" as the months during which heat must be available to indoor occupiable workspaces.
