Editor's note— An ordinance adopted Sept. 17, 1991, did not specifically amend the Code; hence, its inclusion herein as Art. XVI, § 6-280 was at the discretion of the editor.

Sec. 6-280. - Code adopted.
There is hereby adopted the Georgia State Minimum Energy Code, latest edition, as adopted by the State of Georgia, which code is adopted and incorporated as fully as if set out at length herein. At least one (1) copy of the energy code is on file in the office of the building official.
(Ord. of 9-17-91; Ord. of 8-19-97)
