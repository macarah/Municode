Editor's note— An ordinance adopted Sept. 17, 1991, did not specifically amend the Code; hence, its inclusion herein as Art. XVII, § 6-290 was at the discretion of the editor.

Sec. 6-290. - Code adopted as reference.
The Life Safety Code, latest edition, shall be referred to and shall be authoritative when it addresses matters not addressed in the Georgia State Minimum Codes in their latest editions as listed hereinabove.
(Ord. of 9-17-91)
