ARTICLE IX. - CARPORT SALES

Sec. 12-130. - Purpose.
The purpose of this article is to protect the already established surrounding residential development and insure orderly growth that will be in harmony with the county comprehensive development plan.
Sec. 12-131. - Application procedure.
(a)
No person may conduct a carport, garage or yard sale in the unincorporated limits of the county without first obtaining a permit from the business license office.
(b)
Applications shall be on forms provided by the business license office and permits, if issued, will be issued to the owner of the property on which the sale is to be conducted.
Sec. 12-132. - Number per year; duration.
Only two (2) carport sales may be permitted in any calendar year for any tract of land. No carport sale shall last more than three (3) days.
Sec. 12-133. - Signs.
No signs advertising carport sales may be placed in public rights-of-way. Signs may be placed on private property with permission of the owner thereof.
Sec. 12-134. - Remedies and penalties.
(a)
Any person or entity who violates any of the provisions of this article is subject to the penalties and remedies of section 1-8.
(b)
These regulations may be enforced by the zoning enforcement officer, planning and zoning director, sheriff's department or building official.
