Sec. 6-110. - Housing code adopted by reference.
There is hereby adopted by reference as fully and to the same extent as if set out at length herein, subject to all amendments and conflicting provisions contained in this Code of Ordinances, that certain published code known as the Standard Housing Code, latest edition as adopted and amended by the Georgia Department of Community Affairs (DCA) one (1) copy of which is on file in the office of clerk of the city which shall be the official copy of the said code and so designated by the city clerk.
(Code 1965, § 17-1; Ord. No. 159, § 2, 4-13-70; Ord. No. 286, § 3, 7-8-85; Ord. No. 334, § 6, 9-26-88; Ord. No. 440, § 12, 12-12-94; Ord. No. 501, § 4, 1-12-98)
State Law reference— Repair, etc., of unfit dwellings, OCGA, § 36-61-11.
Sec. 6-111. - Amendments to housing code.
The housing code hereinabove adopted by reference, is hereby amended in the following particulars:
Section 106.1 is repealed in its entirety and in lieu thereof a new section is hereby adopted, which shall read as follows:
"106.1 There is hereby established in the city a board to be called the housing board of adjustments and appeals, which shall consist of the governing body of the city."
Section 106.2 is repealed in its entirety and in lieu thereof a new section is hereby adopted, which shall read as follows:
"106.2 The governing body of the city shall serve as members of the housing board of adjustments and appeals for terms concurrent with their respective terms of office as mayor and council member. Vacancies, when they occur, shall be filled when vacancies in the offices of mayor and council member are filled in the manner provided by the charter of the city.
(Code 1965, § 17-2)
