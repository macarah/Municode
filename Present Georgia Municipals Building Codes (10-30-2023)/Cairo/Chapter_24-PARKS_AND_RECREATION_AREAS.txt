ARTICLE I. - IN GENERAL

Sec. 24-1. - Entering city parks and recreation areas after hours.
Various real property owned by the City of Cairo may be made available for public use as a recreational or social gathering area. Such areas shall be closed to the public from 11:00 p.m. to 7:00 a.m. Any such person who violates this provision by going upon the city owned property or recreation area following posting of the notice of hours of closing or following having been advised of the hours of closing by employees of the city shall be guilty of loitering as set forth within section 15-9 of this Code of Ordinances.
(Ord. No. 426, § 3, 11-8-93)
