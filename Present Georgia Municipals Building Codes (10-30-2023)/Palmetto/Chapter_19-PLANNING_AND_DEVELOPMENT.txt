Sec. 19-1. - City planner.
(a)
There is hereby created the position within the city known as city planner. The city planner shall be appointed by the mayor with the advice and consent of the council, and shall serve at their pleasure.
(b)
The position of city planner shall be a part-time employment until such time as the governing body decide otherwise. The salary to be paid to the city planner shall be fixed and approved by the governing body.
(c)
The city planner shall have the following duties:
(1)
To analyze land uses and study all zoning requests and appeals to the zoning board of appeals as may be required by the governing body.
(2)
To furnish the council with a written recommendation on all zonings and appeals to the zoning board of appeals as requested by the governing body.
(3)
To perform such other duties as may be prescribed by this Code, or required by ordinance, regulation or direction of the governing body.
(Ord. No. 88-03, § 2-4, 6-30-88)
