ARTICLE II. - TECHNICAL CODES

Sec. 14-21. - Regulations.
The city building inspection department shall follow regulations as outlined in the current edition of the following technical code books:
(1)
Standard Building Code.
(2)
Standard Excavation and Grading Code.
(3)
Standard Fire Prevention Code.
(4)
Standard Gas Code.
(5)
Standard Housing Code.
(6)
Standard Mechanical Code.
(7)
Standard Plumbing Code.
(8)
Standard Swimming Pool Code.
(9)
Standard Unsafe Building Abatement Code.
(10)
Standard for Flood Plain Management.
(11)
C.A.B.O. Codes for one- and two-family dwellings.
(12)
Georgia State Energy Code for Buildings.
(13)
Standard Amusement Device Code.
(14)
Standard Existing Building Code.
(Code 1988, § 5-1; Ord. of 12-10-1990; Ord. of 10-14-1991)
State Law reference— Constitutional authority to adopt building code, Ga. Const. art. IX, § II, ¶ III(a)(12); minimum required standards, O.C.G.A. §§ 8-2-20(9)(B)(i)(I), 8-2-25(a).
Sec. 14-22. - Amendments.
The following amendment is hereby adopted by reference for all technical and future technical codes adopted by the city:
Chapter 1, Administration, shall be deleted from such codes; and
Chapter 1, Administrative Provisions, shall be added to such codes.
The amended chapter is not set out at length in this section, but is on file and available for inspection in the offices of the city clerk and the building inspector as well as being set out in full in the Gwinnett County Development and Sewer Regulations, adopted by reference in this Code.
(Code 1988, § 5-2; Ord. of 5-9-1994)
