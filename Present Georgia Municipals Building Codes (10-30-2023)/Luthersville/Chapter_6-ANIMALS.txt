Sec. 6-1. - Provisions incorporated by reference.
The animal control ordinance of the county, currently codified as chapter 4, article III in the Meriwether County Code, is adopted by this reference and incorporated in this chapter and made a part of this Code in as full and complete a manner and with like effect as though set out in full in this section.
