State Law reference— Used Motor Vehicle Dealers' and Used Motor Vehicle Parts Dealers' Registration Act, O.C.G.A. § 43-47-1 et seq.

Sec. 18-225. - Exceptions.
Nothing in this article shall be deemed to apply to any individual making an isolated sale of his own vehicle.
(Code 1985, § 9-209(7))
Sec. 18-226. - License required.
(a)
Any used car or used motor vehicle dealer, as such terms are defined in the Used Motor Vehicle Dealers' and Used Motor Vehicle Parts Dealers' Registration Act (O.C.G.A. § 43-47-1 et seq.), who does business within the city shall be required to obtain a license from the city clerk in the manner specified in this chapter.
(b)
The annual business license fee for each used car or used motor vehicle dealer doing business in the city shall be $150.00.
(c)
No action on any application for a license under this article shall be taken by the city council until the city administrator or designee has reviewed such application and forwarded his recommendation thereon to the city clerk in the manner specified in this chapter.
(d)
No license under this article shall be issued to any applicant who has not been licensed by the state board of registration of used car dealers.
(Code 1985, § 9-209(1—4))
Sec. 18-227. - Records.
Each used car or used motor vehicle dealer licensed hereunder shall keep a record of all motor vehicles offered for sale, exchange, or disposal to the public, which record shall show the make of said motor vehicle, the year of its manufacture, its serial number, and its engine number, and which record shall always be kept available for the city administrator or designee and open to his inspection at any time. It shall be the further duty of every such person to immediately report to the city administrator or designee the presence in his place of business of any motor vehicle on which the serial or engine number has been defaced or altered.
(Code 1985, § 9-209(5))
Sec. 18-228. - Inspections.
It shall be the duty of the city administrator or designee to make inspections from time to time for the purpose of seeing that the records required herein are being kept.
(Code 1985, § 9-209(6))
