ARTICLE VIII. - COMMUNICATION ANTENNAS AND TOWERS

Sec. 119-485. - Chapter not affected by Code.
Nothing in this Code or the ordinance adopting this Code affects the ordinance adopted on December 12, 1996, relating to communication towers and antennas. Such ordinance is hereby adopted by reference as if set out in full in this article.
