Chapter 8 - ANIMALS[1]

State Law reference— Animals generally, O.C.G.A. § 4-1-1 et seq.

Sec. 8-1. - Provision of services; authority.
The city authorizes the Gwinnett County and Walton County animal control departments to provide animal control services for the residents of the city with respect to the county in which the resident lives within the city limits.
(Code 1994, § 17-101)
Sec. 8-2. - County policies adopted by reference.
The city adopts the animal control ordinances of Gwinnett County and Walton County including all rules, regulations and enforcement policies of the Gwinnett County and Walton County animal control departments in effect on the date of adopting of the ordinance codified in this chapter and any and all subsequent amendments thereto. All violations of such ordinances, rules and regulations shall be governed by the appropriate courts of Gwinnett County and Walton County, respectively. All license fees, fines, levies or any other revenue collected pursuant to such ordinances, rules, regulations and enforcement policies shall be retained by Gwinnett County and Walton County, respectively, to defray the cost of operating the respective animal control units and the enforcement of this chapter.
(Code 1994, § 17-102)
