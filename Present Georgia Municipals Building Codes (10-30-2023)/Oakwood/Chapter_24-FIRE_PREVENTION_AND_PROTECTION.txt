Chapter 24 - FIRE PREVENTION AND PROTECTION
ARTICLE I. - IN GENERAL

Secs. 24-1—24-18. - Reserved.
ARTICLE II. - FIRE PREVENTION CODE

Sec. 24-19. - Adopted.
The state minimum fire safety standards as set forth in chapter 2 of title 25 of the Official Code of Georgia Annotated (O.C.G.A. § 25-2-1 et seq.) are hereby adopted and thereby are enforceable within the corporate limits of the city.
(Ord. No. 284, § 1, 8-11-1997)
Sec. 24-20. - Enforcement.
Upon the effective date of the adoption of the state minimum fire safety standards as set forth in chapter 2 of title 25 of the Official Code of Georgia Annotated (O.C.G.A. § 25-2-1 et seq.), the county fire service will enforce all codes set forth therein pursuant to the ordinance from which this chapter is derived for the city.
(Ord. No. 284, § 2, 8-11-1997)
