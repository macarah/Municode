Chapter 22 - FEES

Sec. 22-1. - Available at city hall upon request.
Schedules for fees charged by the city for building permits, sewer service, signage, development services, certificates of occupancy; plumbing permits, electrical permits, mechanical permits, amusement and entertainment licenses; animal control licenses and pickup fees; filing fees and other applicable fees shall be reviewed for reasonableness annually during the city's budgetary process and adjusted to reflect actual costs of services provided by the city. Such schedules of fees and any updates and amendments thereto shall be adopted by resolution of the city council and shall be maintained at city hall by the city clerk and made available to the public upon request.
