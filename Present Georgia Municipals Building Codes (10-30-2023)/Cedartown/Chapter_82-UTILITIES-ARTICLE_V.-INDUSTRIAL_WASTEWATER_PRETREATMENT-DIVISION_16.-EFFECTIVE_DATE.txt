DIVISION 16. - EFFECTIVE DATE

Sec. 82-470. - Effective date.
All laws and parts of law in conflict herewith are specifically repealed. In the event any portion of this ordinance is found to be invalid or otherwise unenforceable, this shall not affect all other provisions hereof. This article shall be in full force and effect immediately following its passage, approval and publication, as provided by law.
(Ord. No. 18-2008, § 16(16.1), 10-13-08)
