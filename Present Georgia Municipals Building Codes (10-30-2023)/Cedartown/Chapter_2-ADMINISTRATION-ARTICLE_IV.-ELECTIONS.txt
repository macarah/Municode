Sec. 2-161. - Polling place.
All elections held according to law for federal, state or municipal offices, and to be held under the provisions of this article, shall be conducted at the Goodyear Civic Center on Prior Street in the City of Cedartown. This location shall be maintained and used as a polling place until changed as provided by law.
(Code 1969, § 8-2; Ord. No. 24-1998, § 1, 8-10-98; Ord. No. 8-1999, § 1, 8-9-99)
