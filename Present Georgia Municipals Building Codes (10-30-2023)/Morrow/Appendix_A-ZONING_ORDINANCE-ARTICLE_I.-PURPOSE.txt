ARTICLE I. - PURPOSE
This ordinance is part of a comprehensive plan designed for the purpose of reducing congestion in the roads and streets by regulating types of use and/or multiple use(s); protection of the development of both urban and non-urban areas; securing safety from fire, flood, erosion and other potential hazards, dangers or damages ensuing or being caused by the lack or absence of such regulations, rules as promulgated herein; providing adequate light and air; promoting the health and general welfare; encouraging a distribution of population and classification of land uses and distribution of land development and utilization as will tend to facilitate economic development and adequate provisions for transportation, communications, roads, airports, water supply, drainage, sanitation, education, recreation and other public requirements throughout the City of Morrow, Georgia. The mayor and city council do hereby ordain and enact into law the following articles and sections of the Zoning Ordinance of the City of Morrow, Georgia.
