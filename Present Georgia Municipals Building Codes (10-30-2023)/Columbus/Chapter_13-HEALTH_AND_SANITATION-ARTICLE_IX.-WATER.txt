Sec. 13-351. - Fluoridation of public water system.
The application of fluorides to the public water system of Columbus, Georgia, is hereby approved and the Columbus Water Board of Commissioners is hereby directed to adopt a system of applying fluorides to the public water system of Columbus, Georgia.
(Ord. No. 72-11, § 2, 1-25-72)
Sec. 13-352. - Open wells, cisterns; forbidden where city water system available.
It shall be unlawful for any person, owning or renting any residence or building, to have, keep, use or maintain on the premises, where same is located, any well or open cistern, which is used, or can be used for the purpose of supplying drinking water to the occupants of such residence or building, or to others, where such premises abut upon a street wherein is located pipes and mains for a public water supply which can, by proper connection, be made available to supply such residence or building with water for drinking purposes.
(Ord. of 11-6-18, § 1)
Sec. 13-353. - Injury to equipment of water works.
It shall be unlawful for any persons, without authority, to in any manner interfere with, injure or remove any of the fixtures or appurtenances of hydrants, pipes or appendages thereto, belonging to the system of water works in the city, or without authority, to use the water, or suffer others to use it, or wantonly or negligently waste the water, or permit others to waste the same, or use any of the hydrants or fixtures for hitching posts. The police shall be diligent and report every infraction of this section.
(Code 1914, § 773)
