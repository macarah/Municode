ARTICLE XV. - STOPPING FOR LOADING OR UNLOADING ONLY

Sec. 20-15.1. - Loading or unloading; trailers; hours on certain streets.
From the hour of nine-thirty o'clock a.m. (9:30 a.m.) to the hour of nine-thirty o'clock p.m. (9:30 p.m.), it shall be unlawful for the operator of any motor vehicle having a trailer or other vehicle attached thereto, to load or unload goods, wares or merchandise, or other articles or materials, or to stop such motor vehicle for the purpose of loading or unloading goods, wares or merchandise, or other articles or materials, on the following parts of streets in the city, to-wit:
(1)
Broadway, from the north line of Ninth Street to the south line of Fourteenth Street;
(2)
First Avenue, from the north line of Ninth Street to the north line of Fourteenth Street;
(3)
Tenth Street, from the west line of Second Avenue to the west line of Broadway;
(4)
Eleventh Street, from the west line of Second Avenue to the west line of Broadway;
(5)
Twelfth Street, from the west line of Second Avenue to the west line of Broadway;
(6)
Thirteenth Street, from the west line of Second Avenue to the west line of Broadway.
(Ord. No. 71-198, ยง 16, 7-20-71)
Sec. 20-15.2. - Backing against curb; time; direction.
If it should become necessary to back any vehicle against the curb to load or unload freight or materials, it shall be lawful, unless prohibited or restricted by other provisions of this chapter or other ordinances of the city, for the vehicle to be so backed and placed for such space of time only as may be necessary to load or unload. Such vehicle shall be headed as nearly as possible in the direction of the traffic.
(Ord. No. 71-198, ยง 16, 7-20-71)
Sec. 20-15.3. - Maximum time allowed in zone.
Vehicles are authorized to stop in and occupy loading zones only while actually engaged in loading or unloading materials, articles, equipment, furniture, or supplies, but no vehicle shall remain continuously in such zone for a period of more than 30 minutes.
(Ord. No. 71-198, ยง 16, 7-20-71)
Sec. 20-15.4. - Timed zones for loading and unloading.
A "15 Minute Parking" zone for loading and unloading shall be established along the west side of Citizens Way beginning 581 feet from the south curb line of Macon Road and running southerly for a distance of 73 feet.
