ARTICLE VII. - LOCAL SALES AND USE TAX

Sec. 19-100. - Levied; amount.
There is hereby levied a local sales and use tax in the amount of one percent in accordance with the terms of Section 26A of the Georgia Retailers and Consumers Sales and Use Tax Act approved February 2, 1951 (Ga. Laws 1951, p. 360), as now or hereafter amended, and specifically as amended by Ga. Laws 1975, p. 984, within the boundaries of each of the Urban Service Districts described in section 19-01 of the Code of Ordinances of Columbus, Georgia, (Ordinance No. 75-57 effective June 17, 1975) as such Urban Service Districts existed as of the date that said tax was approved in a referendum, to wit: September 11, 1975.
(Ord. No. 75-98, § 1, 10-14-75)
Sec. 19-101. - Administration and collection of tax.
The local sales and use tax levied herein above shall be administered and collected by the state revenue commissioner for the use and benefit of Columbus, Georgia.
(Ord. No. 75-98, § 1, 10-14-75)
