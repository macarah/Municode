ARTICLE II. - COUNTY PROPERTY

Sec. 6-19. - Definitions.
The following words, terms and phrases, when used in this article, shall have the meanings ascribed to them in this section, except where the context clearly indicates a different meaning:
Animal means any mammal, other than man, or any reptile, amphibian, or bird/avian species.
Control of an animal means that the animal is:
(1)
Being held by a person of sufficient size, age and maturity to restrain the animal; or
(2)
Under voice control of the presence of a competent person.
Service animal means a properly trained animal to provide an intended service required of the individual in control of the service animal.
(Res. of 1-9-2017, § 1(14.20))
Sec. 6-20. - Prohibition.
No animal shall be allowed in the county buildings or on county properties unless otherwise provided herein.
(Res. of 1-9-2017, § 1(14.21))
Sec. 6-21. - Exceptions.
Service animals shall not be prohibited. Other animals may be allowed upon approval by the county.
(Res. of 1-9-2017, § 1(14.22))
Sec. 6-22. - Notices.
Signs shall be placed clearly and conspicuously at the entrances of the county buildings and county properties indicating that such prohibition is effect.
(Res. of 1-9-2017, § 1(14.24))
Sec. 6-23. - Penalty for violation of the article.
Any person in violation of this article may be fined as provided in section 1-14.
(Res. of 1-9-2017, § 1(14.25))
Sec. 6-24. - Injunction.
Any person found to have an animal on county premises, not otherwise allowed hereby, may be enjoined from such conduct and shall immediately remove the animal from the county's property.
(Res. of 1-9-2017, § 1(14.26))
