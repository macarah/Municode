Sec. 62-1. - Sidewalk repairs.
If a property owner damages or destroys any portion of a sidewalk adjoining his property, the property owner must reimburse the city for its repair or reconstruction.
Sec. 62-2. - Parks.
No person shall throw paper or trash in the parks of the city or commit any act tending to injure said parks. No person shall allow or permit cattle, hens, pigs or other animals to graze, scratch, root, or otherwise injure the park.
(Code 1955, § 66)
