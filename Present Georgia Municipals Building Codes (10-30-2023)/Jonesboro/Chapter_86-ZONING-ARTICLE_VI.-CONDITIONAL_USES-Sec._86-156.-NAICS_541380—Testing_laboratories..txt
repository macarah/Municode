Sec. 86-156. - NAICS 541380—Testing laboratories.
The following conditions are assigned in the O&I, C-2 and M-1 districts:
(1)
No testing facility shall be permitted which introduces excessive noise, odor, vibration, electrical interference, threat of fire, explosion, or involves hazardous materials or other objectionable activities.
(Ord. No. 2019-003 , § 1, 2-11-2019; Ord. No. 2019-011 , § 1, 6-3-2019)
