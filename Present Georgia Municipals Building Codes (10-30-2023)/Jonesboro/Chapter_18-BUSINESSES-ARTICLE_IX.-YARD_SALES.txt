ARTICLE IX. - YARD SALES

Sec. 18-361. - Definitions.
The following words, terms and phrases, when used in this article, shall have the meanings ascribed to them in this section, except where the context clearly indicates a different meaning:
Yard sale means the sale or trading of clothing, furniture, household items, food, dishes, antiques or similar goods or merchandise, other than in the normal course of business, or the sale or trading of such goods, as outlined in this definition, by a person not regularly engaged in such business. Such goods or merchandise do not need to be attended for a sale to be deemed to be in existence. The term "yard sale" shall include garage sales, carport sales and similar types of sales or events.
(Code 1987, § 9-6-2(a))
Cross reference— Definitions generally, § 1-2.
Sec. 18-362. - Permit required.
At least 24 hours prior to conducting a yard sale, a permit for said sale shall be secured from the city clerk. Upon application for a permit, applicant shall be given opportunity to review the provisions of this article. Permit shall be placed in a conspicuous location in the area where yard sale is to occur, and in view of the general public. Failure to post permit will result in closure of yard sale. Future non compliance will result in the issuance of a citation, Municipal Court appearance and fines.
(Code 1987, § 9-6-2(b); Ord. No. 2011-005, § 2, 12-12-2011)
Sec. 18-363. - Number and duration.
Yard sales shall be allowed not more than 12 times in any calendar year for any premises. No sale shall run for more than three consecutive days.
(Code 1987, § 9-6-2(c); Ord. No. 2011-005, § 3, 12-12-2011)
Sec. 18-364. - Displays.
All displays of yard sale merchandise shall be additionally regulated by section 86-278 et seq. of this Code.
(Code 1987, § 9-6-2(d); Ord. No. 02-04, § 4, 6-10-2002)
Sec. 18-365. - Prohibited acts.
No individual, firm, corporation or partnership shall be permitted to engage in the business of promoting or conducting yard sales for others for a fee or other consideration. No merchandise shall be purchased in anticipation of resale at a yard sale.
(Code 1987, § 9-6-2(e))
