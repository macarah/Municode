Sec. 86-201. - NAICS 517—Telecommunications.
modified
The following conditions are assigned in the H-1, H-2, O&I, C-1, C-2 and M-1 districts:
(1)
No telecommunications structures greater than 20 feet in height, whether ground or building mounted, shall be permitted.
(Ord. No. 2019-003 , § 1, 2-11-2019; Ord. No. 2019-011 , § 1, 6-3-2019; Ord. No. 2021-014 , § 1(Exh. A), 6-14-2021)
