Sec. 86-192. - NAICS 71399—Neighborhood recreational centers, including tennis, pools and active primarily outdoor amenities, with or without food sales (private).
modified
The following conditions are assigned in the RM, O&I, MX, C-1 and C-2 districts:
(1)
Must be located on a street having a classification of collector or greater.
(2)
Must be established on a lot having a minimum area of one acre and a minimum frontage of 150 feet.
(3)
All other applicable standards of section 86-113 shall be met.
(Ord. No. 2019-003 , § 1, 2-11-2019; Ord. No. 2019-011 , § 1, 6-3-2019; Ord. No. 2020-006 , § 9, 3-9-2020; Ord. No. 2021-014 , § 1(Exh. A), 6-14-2021)
