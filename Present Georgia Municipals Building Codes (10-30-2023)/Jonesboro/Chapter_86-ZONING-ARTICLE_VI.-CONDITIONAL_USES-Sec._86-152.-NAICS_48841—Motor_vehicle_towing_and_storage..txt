Sec. 86-152. - NAICS 48841—Motor vehicle towing and storage.
The following conditions are assigned in the C-2 and M-1 districts:
(1)
Vehicle storage shall be limited to a 30-day period.
(2)
Storage shall be screened utilizing a ten-foot landscaped buffer and opaque fencing having a minimum height of six feet; landscaping as prescribed in section 86-15 shall be maintained outside the fencing.
(Ord. No. 2019-003 , § 1, 2-11-2019; Ord. No. 2019-011 , § 1, 6-3-2019)
