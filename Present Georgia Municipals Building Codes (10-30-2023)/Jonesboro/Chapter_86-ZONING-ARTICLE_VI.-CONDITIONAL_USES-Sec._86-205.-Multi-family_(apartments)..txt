Sec. 86-205. - Multi-family (apartments).
modified
The following conditions are assigned in the R-2, R-4, CCM, R-M, H-1, H-2 and M-X districts:
(1)
The standards of the R-M district shall control development of apartments.
(2)
Must be located off a street having a classification of collector or greater.
(3)
A minimum 25-foot wide buffer shall be maintained along all property lines adjacent to any single-family, detached residential property.
