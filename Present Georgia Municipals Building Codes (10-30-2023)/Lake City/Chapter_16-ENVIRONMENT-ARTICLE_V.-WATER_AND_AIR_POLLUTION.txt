ARTICLE V. - WATER AND AIR POLLUTION

Sec. 16-120. - Pollution of streams.
It shall be unlawful for any person to deposit or cause to be deposited in any lake or stream any petroleum product, detergent or other cleaning agent or product, garbage, refuse, waste material, whether solid or liquid, or any other substance which is harmful to plant or animal life or which is unsightly or which causes or tends to cause damming or which causes noxious odors.
(Code 1972, § 10-1; Code 1994, § 38-166)
Sec. 16-121. - Pollution of air.
It shall be unlawful for any person to cause to be placed in the atmosphere any fumes, dust, smoke, gas, ash, fly ash or similar substance which renders the air less safe for human, animal or plant life or which results in damage to property of others or which causes noxious odors.
(Code 1972, § 10-2; Code 1994, § 38-167)
