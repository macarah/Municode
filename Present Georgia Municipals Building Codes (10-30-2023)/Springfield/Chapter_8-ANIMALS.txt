Sec. 8-1. - Penalty.
Unless stated otherwise, violations of this chapter are punishable as provided in section 1-7.
Sec. 8-2. - County animal control ordinance.
The Effingham County/City Domestic Animal Control Ordinance as now or hereafter amended is hereby adopted and made a part of the Code of the City of Springfield, Georgia.
( Ord. No. 03-94, 7-12-1994 )
Sec. 8-3. - Bird sanctuary.
(a)
It shall be unlawful to trap, hunt, shoot or attempt to shoot or molest in any manner any bird or wild foul or to rob bird nests or wild foul nests. Provided, however, if starlings or similar birds are found to be congregating in such numbers in a particular locality that they constitute a nuisance or menace to health or property in the opinion of city council, then in such event said health authorities shall meet with representatives of the garden club or as many of said clubs as are found to exist in the city, after having given at least three days' actual notice of the time and place of said meeting to the representatives of said clubs.
(b)
If as a result of said meeting no satisfactory alternative is found to abate such nuisance, then said birds may be destroyed in such numbers and in such manner as is deemed advisable by the city council under the supervision of the chief of police of the city.
( Ord. No. 01-62, 10-1-1962 )
