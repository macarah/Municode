Sec. 2-234. - Fiscal year.
The fiscal year of the city shall commence on July 1 and end on June 30 of each year.
State Law reference— Fiscal year required to be established by ordinance, O.C.G.A. § 36-81-3.
Sec. 2-235. - Fee schedule; fees established by resolution.
The city maintains a schedule of fees for permits, licenses, and other municipal charges. The amount of fees or charges imposed by the city, including, but not limited to, those encompassed in the city's fee schedule, shall be established from time to time by resolution of the city council, as it deems necessary. A copy of the fee schedule is available in the office of the city clerk.
Sec. 2-236. - Identity theft prevention program.
For purposes of compliance with 16 CFR 681.1 et seq., the city has adopted an identity theft prevention (red flag) program, a copy of which is available in the office of the city clerk.
Sec. 2-237. - Ad valorem tax due date; delinquent payments; penalties.
All ad valorem taxes due to the City of Clayton shall be due and paid on January 20, and upon failure of any property owners to pay all taxes when due, the clerk and treasurer shall issue execution against such property owners, for taxes due as provided by the City Charter, this Code, and state law. The clerk and treasurer are further authorized to charge interest on unpaid taxes and penalties as are provided by state law.
(Code 1999, § 62-1; Ord. of 3-13-1990, § 1; Ord. of 11-19-2013(2))
State Law reference— Ad valorem taxation of property generally, O.C.G.A. § 48-5-1 et seq.
