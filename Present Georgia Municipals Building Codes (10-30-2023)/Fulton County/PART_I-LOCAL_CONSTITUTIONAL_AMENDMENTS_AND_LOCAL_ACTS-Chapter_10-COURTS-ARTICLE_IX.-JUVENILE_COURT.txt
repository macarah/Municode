ARTICLE IX. - JUVENILE COURT[12]

State Law reference— Juvenile courts, O.C.G.A. § 15-11-1 et seq.

Sec. 10-451. - Salary of judge.
Notwithstanding the provisions of any law to the contrary, the annual compensation of…each Judge of the Juvenile Court of Fulton County…shall be $30,000.00 per annum, payable in equal installments from the treasury of Fulton County.
(1972 Ga. Laws (Act No. 914), page 2395, § 1)
Editor's note— Provisions in the above section dealing with the compensation of the judges of other courts have been deleted and replaced with ellipses.
Sec. 10-452. - Budget oversight.
The chief administrative officer of the Fulton County Juvenile Court shall have oversight of the court's budget. After county funds have been appropriated for the operation of the court, the chief administrative officer, with the approval of the chief judge of the juvenile court, shall have the authority to make changes to line item appropriations.
(2014 Ga. Laws (Act No. 418), page 3989, § 1)
Sec. 10-453. - Payroll adjustments.
The Fulton County Finance Department shall make all payroll adjustments related to appointment, transfer, termination, or other personnel action requiring payroll adjustment upon written notification by the chief administrative officer.
(2014 Ga. Laws (Act No. 418), page 3989, § 2)
Sec. 10-454. - Unexpended appropriations.
Any unexpended county appropriated funds remaining with the court at the end of a fiscal year shall lapse to the general fund of Fulton County.
(2014 Ga. Laws (Act No. 418), page 3989, § 3)
