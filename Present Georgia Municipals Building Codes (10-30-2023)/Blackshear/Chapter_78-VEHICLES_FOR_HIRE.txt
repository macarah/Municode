Sec. 78-1. - Passenger vehicle license and permit—Required.
All persons operating or driving taxicabs or other vehicles for carrying passengers for hire in the city shall be licensed and have a permit for the operation of vehicles for transportation of passengers for hire within the city.
(Code 1987, § 18-1)
Sec. 78-2. - Same—Application; issuance or denial.
Every person seeking to operate a taxicab or other vehicle for transportation of passengers for hire within the city shall file with the city clerk an application for license and permit. The application must positively show that the applicant is 18 years of age or older; free of all contagious and infectious diseases, evidenced by a certificate from a reputable physician; and that the applicant has not been convicted of any offense or crime involving moral turpitude. The city shall have the right to grant or refuse to grant the license and permit sought by the applicant.
(Code 1987, § 18-2)
Sec. 78-3. - Same—Period.
All permits and licenses so granted and issued shall be for the same period of time as other licenses issued by the city, and a new application shall be required for each permit and license.
(Code 1987, § 18-3)
Sec. 78-4. - Registration of passenger vehicles for hire—Required.
All vehicles operating for hire for the transportation of passengers within the city shall be registered with the city clerk and the applicant for said registration must affirmatively show that the vehicle offered for registration is a safe and suitable vehicle for the transportation of passengers, as well as the make, model, body type and state license number of the vehicle.
(Code 1987, § 18-4)
Sec. 78-5. - Fees.
Each application under this chapter shall be accompanied by a registration fee as set forth in the schedule of fees and charges. Such fees shall be retained by the city.
(Code 1987, § 18-5)
Sec. 78-6. - Passenger vehicle insurance.
Each operator of a taxicab or other vehicle for hire for the transportation of passengers within the city shall have insurance as protection for the passengers from personal injury received while a fare-paying passenger. The minimum amount and kind of insurance shall be for bodily injury liability $10,000.00/$20,000.00 and for property damage liability $5,000.00, and the insurance carrier shall be registered with the insurance commission of the state. The name of the insurance company, policy number and premium paid shall be filed with the city clerk before the operation of any taxicab or vehicle for hire.
(Code 1987, § 18-6)
