Editor's note— The Pierce County Emergency Operations Plan was approved by city and county officials in 1995. It will be activated by the county emergency management director when directed by the chairman of the board of commissioners of the county or when an emergency is declared by the governor. An official copy of such plan is on file in the office of the county clerk.
