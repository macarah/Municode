27-3.16 DIVISION 16. - FAMA PINES COMMUNITY OVERLAY DISTRICT

Sec. 3.16.1. - Scope of the regulations, applicability and boundaries.
This division establishes standards and procedures that apply to any development that lies, in whole or in part, within the Fama Pines Community Overlay District. The boundaries of the Fama Pines Community Overlay District shall be established by a zoning map amendment adopted pursuant to this chapter which amendment is incorporated herein and made a part of this chapter 27. The provisions of this Code including but not limited to the provisions of division 8 of this article shall govern the demolition, new construction and residential development in whole or in part with any area of the Fama Pines Community Overlay District.
( Ord. No. 15-06 , 8-25-2015)
