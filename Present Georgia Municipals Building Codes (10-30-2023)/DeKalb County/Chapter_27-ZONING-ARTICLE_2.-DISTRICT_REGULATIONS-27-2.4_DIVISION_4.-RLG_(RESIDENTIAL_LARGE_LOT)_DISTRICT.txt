27-2.4 DIVISION 4. - RLG (RESIDENTIAL LARGE LOT) DISTRICT

Sec. 2.4.1. - Statement of purpose and intent.
The purpose and intent of the board of commissioners in establishing the RLG (Residential Large Lot) District is as follows:
A.
To provide for the protection of neighborhoods within DeKalb County where lots have a minimum area of twenty thousand (20,000) square feet, but may have narrow lot widths;
B.
To provide for compatible infill development in neighborhoods;
C.
To provide protections for existing development as new subdivisions are created;
D.
To respond to existing site development conditions and patterns;
E.
To assure that the uses and structures authorized in the RLG (Residential Large Lot) District are those uses and structures designed to serve the housing, recreational, educational, religious, and social needs of the neighborhood;
F.
To provide for appropriately sized accessible and useable open space in new developments for health, recreational and social opportunities for county residents;
G.
To implement the future development map of the county's most current comprehensive plan.
( Ord. No. 15-06 , 8-25-2015)
