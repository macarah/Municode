DIVISION 2. - DATA PROCESSING ADVISORY COMMITTEE

Sec. 2-166. - Created.
There is created a data processing advisory committee.
(Code 1976, § 2-2002(a))
Sec. 2-167. - Members.
The data processing advisory committee shall be composed of the following:
(1)
The executive assistant.
(2)
The data center director.
(3)
The finance director.
(4)
The chief of police.
(5)
The tax commissioner.
(6)
A representative from the superior court's administrator's office.
(7)
A representative from the state court's administrator's office.
(8)
Two (2) other representatives from user departments to be appointed by the other members of the committee to serve two-year terms.
(Code 1976, § 2-2002(b); Ord. No. 11-02, Pt. I, § 4, 11-27-01)
Sec. 2-168. - Officers.
The executive assistant shall be the chairperson of the data processing advisory committee. The data center director shall be the vice-chairperson of the committee.
(Code 1976, § 2-2002(b))
Sec. 2-169. - Duties.
The data processing advisory committee shall have the duty to:
(1)
Review all requests for new and expanded data processing services.
(2)
Recommend priorities for new and expanded data processing services to the data center director for presentation to the board of commissioners.
(3)
Assist the data center director on related issues which the data center director brings to the committee.
(4)
Carry out any reviews and investigations requested by the chief executive and/or board of commissioners on data processing issues.
(Code 1976, § 2-2002(a))
