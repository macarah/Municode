Sec. 32. - (Restatement) Conflict of interests.
No member of the city commission, the city manager, or any other officer or employee of the city shall directly or indirectly be interested in any contract job, work or service with or for the city, nor in the profits of [or] emoluments thereof, nor in the expenditure of any money on the part of the city other than the fixed compensation of such officer, and any contract with the city in which any such officer or employee is or becomes interested may be declared void by the commission.
