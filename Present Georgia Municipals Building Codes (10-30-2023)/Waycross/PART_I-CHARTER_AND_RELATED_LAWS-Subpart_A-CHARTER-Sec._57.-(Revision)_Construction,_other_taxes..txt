Sec. 57. - (Revision) Construction, other taxes.
The City of Waycross shall have the power to levy any other tax allowed, now or hereafter, by law, and the specific mention of any right, power, or authority in this Charter shall not be construed as limiting in any way the general powers of the city to govern its local affairs.
