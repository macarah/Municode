Sec. 60. - (Revision) Bonds.
The commission of the City of Waycross, by ordinance, shall have the power to issue bonds for the purpose of raising revenue to carry out any project, program, or venture authorized under this Charter or under the laws of this state. Such bonding authority shall be exercised in accordance with the laws governing bond issuance by municipalities in effect at the time said issue is undertaken.
