State Law reference— Regulation of bingo, O.C.G.A. § 16-12-50 et seq.

DIVISION 1. - GENERALLY

Sec. 22-321. - Times restricted.
Bingo games shall not be conducted on Sunday and bingo games shall not be conducted past midnight on any night.
(Code 1988, § 9-2-3)
Secs. 22-322—22-345. - Reserved.
DIVISION 2. - LICENSE

Sec. 22-346. - Required.
It shall be unlawful for any person to maintain, operate or conduct a bingo game in the city without first having a license issued by the city and also by complying with applicable state law.
(Code 1988, § 9-2-1)
Sec. 22-347. - Application for license.
Applications for a license to operate bingo in the city shall be made to the mayor and city council, and shall set forth the:
(1)
Name and address of the applicant and the nature of the business of the applicant, whether it is a church, parish, synagogue, or a nonprofit corporation;
(2)
Length of time the applicant has been organized and the objects for which the applicant was formed; and
(3)
Whether or not the applicant holds tax-exempt status from the Internal Revenue Service.
(Code 1988, § 9-2-2)
Sec. 22-348. - Qualification of applicant.
No license shall be issued under this division to an organization which is not a bona fide resident in the city.
(Code 1988, § 9-2-3)
Sec. 22-349. - Fee.
At the time of making application for a bingo license, the applicant must deposit a fee as set forth in the schedule of fees and charges on file in the office of the city clerk which shall be the annual license fee. No proration of licenses shall be allowed.
(Code 1988, § 9-2-4)
Secs. 22-350—22-379. - Reserved.
