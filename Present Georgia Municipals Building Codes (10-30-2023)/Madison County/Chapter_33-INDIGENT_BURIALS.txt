Chapter 33 - INDIGENT BURIALS

Sec. 33-1. - Establishment of uniform rules for indigent burials.
(a)
In accordance with O.C.G.A. ยง 36-12-5, whenever any person dies in Madison County and the decedent, his family, and his immediate kindred are indigent and unable to provide for his decent interment, the county finance director is authorized to make available from county funds a sum sufficient to provide a decent interment of the deceased indigent person or to reimburse such person as may have expended the cost thereof voluntarily, up to but not exceeding $500.00.
(b)
If the decedent was an inmate under the authority, jurisdiction, or control of the department of corrections, the finance director shall seek reimbursement from the department of corrections pursuant to O.C.G.A. ยง 36-12-5(b).
(c)
A person is "indigent" hereunder if his income is less than 100 percent of the most recent Federal Poverty Guidelines published by the United States Department of Health and Human Services, such guidelines currently being accessible at http://aspe.hhs.gov/poverty/index.shtml#latest.
(Ord. of 1-9-2012(2))
