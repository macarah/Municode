ARTICLE II. - FIREFIGHTING EQUIPMENT

Sec. 18-20. - Authorized use.
In order to comply with the regulations of the board of fire underwriters, the firefighting equipment belonging to the city shall not be used nor permitted to be taken beyond the limits of this city, except for the reasons set forth in section 18-21.
(Ord. No. 13, § 1, 3-13-1972)
Sec. 18-21. - Exceptions.
The fire department of this city is hereby granted permission and authority to use the firefighting equipment of this city beyond the limits of this city as needed in any location in Harris County.
(Ord. No. 13, § 2, 3-13-1972)
