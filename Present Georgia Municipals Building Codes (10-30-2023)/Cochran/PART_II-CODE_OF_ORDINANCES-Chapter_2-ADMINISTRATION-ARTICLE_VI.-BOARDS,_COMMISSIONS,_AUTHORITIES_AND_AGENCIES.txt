ARTICLE VI. - BOARDS, COMMISSIONS, AUTHORITIES AND AGENCIES

Sec. 2-314. - Establishment by council.
The city council shall establish such boards, commissions, and authorities as it deems necessary or desirable from time to time to fulfill any investigative, quasi-judicial or quasi-legislative function of the city council, upon such terms as are provided by the council by ordinance in accordance with the Charter.
(Code 2011, § 2-325)
Sec. 2-315. - Rules, regulations and bylaws.
Unless otherwise provided by the city council, each board, commission, and authority established by the city council may establish such bylaws, rules and regulations not inconsistent with the Charter, this Code, or general law, as it deems appropriate and necessary for the fulfillment of its duties or the conduct of its affairs; provided, however, that copies of such bylaws, rules and regulations shall be filed with the city clerk.
(Code 2011, § 2-326)
Sec. 2-316. - Heart of Georgia Regional Commission.
The city participates in the area planning commission known as the Heart of Georgia Regional Commission upon such terms as are incorporated in the agreements and city and county ordinances establishing same.
(Code 1999, § 3-202; Code 2011, § 2-327; Ord. of 7-13-1965)
Sec. 2-317. - Joint development authority.
(a)
By joint and concurrent resolutions of the county and the city, there has been created and activated the Cochran-Bleckley Industrial Development Authority. The authority described in this section is also referred to as the city's industrial development authority.
(b)
The board of directors of the authority shall consist of nine members, four of whom shall be recommended by the mayor and confirmed by the city council, and four of whom shall be appointed by the sole commissioner of the county and one of whom will be appointed at large by the authority.
(Code 2011, § 2-328; )
Editor's note— The Cochran-Bleckley Industrial Development Authority was established by 1966 Ga. Laws (Act No. 126), page 1042.
Sec. 2-318. - Municipal airport authority.
The Cochran Municipal Airport Authority has been created by the state legislature by the Act set forth in part I, subpart B of this Code.
(Code 2011, § 2-329)
Sec. 2-319. - Jointly-owned natural gas authority.
The city has entered into an agreement with certain other local government entities for the establishment of the jointly-owned natural gas authority. The mayor serves on the board of the authority, pursuant to the terms of the agreement among the governing authorities, as it may be amended from time to time. The mayor also serves as board chairperson every fourth year.
(Code 2011, § 2-330)
Sec. 2-320. - Joint recreation association.
The city has entered into an agreement with the county whereby there was established the Cochran/Bleckley County Recreation Association. The mayor has authority under the agreement for appointment of three members of the board of the association with the approval of the city council, pursuant to the terms of the agreement, as it may be amended from time to time by agreement between the city and county.
(Code 2011, § 2-331)
Secs. 2-321—2-343. - Reserved.
