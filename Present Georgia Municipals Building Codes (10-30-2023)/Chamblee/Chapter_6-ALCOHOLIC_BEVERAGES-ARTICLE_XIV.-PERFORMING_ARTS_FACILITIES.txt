ARTICLE XIV. - PERFORMING ARTS FACILITIES

Sec. 6-275. - Performing arts facility.
For the purposes of this article, a performing arts facility is defined in section 6-1 of this chapter. Performing arts facilities that allow consumption of alcohol on the premises are subject to the provisions of this article.
(Ord. No. 692 , pt. IV, 11-17-15; Ord. No. 754 , pt. XXVIII, 10-16-18)
Sec. 6-276. - License required; compliance with applicable regulations.
It shall be unlawful to operate an establishment governed under the provisions of this article without first obtaining a license from the finance director and paying such fees and taxes as may be required by the ordinances, rules and regulations of the city.
(Ord. No. 692 , pt. IV, 11-17-15; Ord. No. 801 , pt. XLI, 9-21-21)
Sec. 6-277. - Investigative and administrative fee.
Each application for a license under this article shall be accompanied by payment for the full amount of the license fee, together with payment in an amount as may be required by the ordinances, rules and regulations of the city to defray investigative and administrative costs. If the application is denied and the license refused, or if the applicant withdraws the application prior to its being issued, the license fee shall be refunded, but the investigative and administrative fees shall be retained. Notwithstanding the foregoing sentence, the license fee shall not be refunded to any applicant that sold, or offered to sell, alcoholic beverages during the license year at issue.
(Ord. No. 692 , pt. IV, 11-17-15; Ord. No. 801 , pt. XLII, 9-21-21)
Sec. 6-278. - Hours and location of sale generally.
(a)
Except as otherwise provided in this article, alcoholic beverages may not be sold for consumption on the premises at a performing arts facility except between the hours of 11:00 a.m. and 12:00 a.m.
(b)
Alcoholic beverages may not be dispensed earlier than one hour before the start of a performance or after the performance has ended.
(c)
Alcoholic beverages may only be sold at a bar located in the lobby area of the facility or by a waiter or waitress serving patrons within the facility.
(Ord. No. 692 , pt. IV, 11-17-15)
