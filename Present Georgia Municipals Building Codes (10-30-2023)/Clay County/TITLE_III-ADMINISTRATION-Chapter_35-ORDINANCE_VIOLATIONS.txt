Chapter 35 - ORDINANCE VIOLATIONS

Sec. 35.01 - Citation for violation of ordinances.
(A)
All ordinance violations may be tried upon citation with or without a prosecuting attorney as well as upon accusations.
(B)
Each citation shall state the time and place at which accused is to appear for trial, shall identify the offense with which accused is charged, shall have an identifying number by which it shall be filed with the court and shall identify the accused and be signed by authorized agent of the county.
(C)
The Clerk of the Board of Commissioners of Clay County is designated as the agent of the county to issue citations.
(D)
All citations shall be served by the Sheriff of Clay County or his or her lawful deputy.
(E)
The original of the citation shall be personally served upon the accused and a copy shall promptly be filed with the Magistrate Court of Clay County.
(Res. of 4-5-1988)
