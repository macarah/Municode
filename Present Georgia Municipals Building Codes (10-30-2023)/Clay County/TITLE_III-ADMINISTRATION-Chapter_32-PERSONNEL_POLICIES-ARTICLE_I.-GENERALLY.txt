Sec. 32.06 - Compensation.
The Board of Commissioners of Clay County shall be compensated at the rate as specified as the minimum rate of compensation for said service but not less than $40.00 per day, and no payments for service will be rendered for Board meetings until minutes of the meeting are provided to the governing authority documenting attendance and work progress of the meeting.
(Res. of 2-5-2002)
Editor's note— For local legislation regarding compensation of the Chairperson and members of the Board, and the selection of Chairperson and Vice Chairperson, see LC 9 0187 effective 1-1-2001.
Sec. 32.07 - Worker's compensation.
Elected officials shall be covered under the Association County Commissions of Georgia Group Self-Insured Worker's Compensation Fund while performing their duties as the Board of Commissioners, Sheriff, Probate Judge, Clerk of Court, Magistrate and Tax Commissioner shall be covered under the workers' compensation laws of the State of Georgia as stated in O.C.G.A. § 34-9-1.
(Res. of 2-1-2000)
Sec. 32.08 - Employee's retirement system of Georgia.
(A)
This county, by and through its lawfully constituted Board of Commissioners, does adopt the terms, conditions, requirements, reservation, benefits, privileges and other conditions thereunto appertaining of Title II of the Social Security Act, being 42 U.S.C. 401 et seq. as amended by Pub. L. 761, 83rd Congress, for and on behalf of all the officers and employees thereof and of its departments and instrumentalities, save and except any of such officers and employees who occupy any position, office or any employment not authorized to be covered by applicable state or federal laws or regulations.
(B)
There shall be withheld from any salary or compensation due any such officer or employee not excepted as aforesaid whose compensation qualifies him or her to participation therein the percentage of salary, wage or compensation required to be withheld by the Act, whether such compensation be computed in case, quarters, subsistence or in kind and reserved in the Treasury for remittance in accordance with the Federal Insurance Contributions Act (FICA) of the Internal Revenue Code.
(C)
There shall be appropriated from general funds such amounts at such times as may be required by applicable federal/state laws or regulations for employer's contributions, administrative expenses and such funds shall be paid in accordance with laws and regulations.
(D)
The rules and regulations of the state agency for the enforcement of the Act shall be adhered to and that reports relating to personnel, payrolls and otherwise required by the state agency shall be promptly made and that remittance of the tax on the employer and the employee shall be promptly paid in accordance with federal/state laws and regulations.
(E)
The County Administrator is designated the custodian of all sums withheld from the compensation of officers and employees and of the appropriated funds for the tax upon the county, and the County Administrator is made the withholding and reporting agent and charged with the duty of maintaining personnel records for the purpose of this section.
(F)
This county, accordingly and by the adoption of a resolution, in full and ample manner by its lawfully constituted Board of Commissioners, does authorize the Chairperson to make and enter into agreement with the State of Georgia, by and through its designated agency, the Employees' Retirement System of Georgia, in full accord with this section and the Social Security Act, as amended, and so afford its benefits to the officers and employees of this county and its instrumentalities, if any, to include any employees that are in positions covered by a public retirement system, in the manner by the law provided, namely, Probate Judges Retirement Fund.
(Res. of 6-15-1999)
