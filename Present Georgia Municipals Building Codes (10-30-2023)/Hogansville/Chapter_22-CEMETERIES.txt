Sec. 22-1. - Prices of lots and grave openings.
The prices of cemetery lots and grave openings shall be as fixed from time to time by the city council.
(Code 1988, § 5-3-1)
Sec. 22-2. - Driving off roadways.
No person shall drive a motor vehicle on cemetery property other than on the driveways designated for that purpose.
(Code 1988, § 5-3-2)
Sec. 22-3. - Removal of flowers.
(a)
It shall be unlawful to remove flowers placed in the Myrtle Hill Cemetery for a period of seven days after the flowers are so placed except by a member of the family of the deceased or by someone designated in writing by a member of the family and turned in to the city at city hall. The writing shall show the date the flowers are to be removed, the date signed, the name of the designated person, and the name of the grave, area or plot from which the flowers are to be removed.
(b)
After the first seven days that flowers are placed in Myrtle Hill Cemetery, the flowers and their frames shall become the property of the city and shall be removed by the cemetery custodian or his assistant. Frames will be sold by the city to the highest bidder.
(Code 1988, § 5-3-3)
