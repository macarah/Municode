ARTICLE V. - SPECIAL EVENTS

Sec. 18-151. - Definitions.
The following words, terms and phrases, when used in this article, shall have the meanings ascribed to them in this section, except where the context clearly indicates a different meaning:
Special event means, but shall not be limited to, fairs and concerts.
Cross reference— Definitions generally, § 1-2.
Sec. 18-152. - Permit required; issuance.
All persons or entities desiring to hold a special event, which shall include but not be limited to fairs, carnivals, concerts, demonstration events, parades, political events and sales events, shall be required to complete the application form and obtain approval for such event from the city manager. Upon payment and approval, the city manager will issue a permit enabling said person or entity to hold the special event.
(Ord. No. 87-007, 4-4-87; Ord. No. 2002-008, 12-23-02)
Sec. 18-153. - Insurance coverage.
The applicant for the special events permit shall attach to his application either an insurance policy or a certificate of insurance including the policy number, amount and the provision that the city is included as an additional insured. Each amount of required insurance shall be determined by the city manager and based upon the risk level of the event. No insurance requirements can be waived unless by prior approval of the mayor and council.
(Ord. No. 90-012, § 25, 8-27-90)
