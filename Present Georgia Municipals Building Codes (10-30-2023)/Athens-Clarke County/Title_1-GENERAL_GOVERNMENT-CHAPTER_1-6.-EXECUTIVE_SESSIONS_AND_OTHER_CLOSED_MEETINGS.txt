CHAPTER 1-6. - EXECUTIVE SESSIONS AND OTHER CLOSED MEETINGS

Sec. 1-6-1. - Procedures governing closed meetings.
Whenever the mayor and commission, any committee of the commission, or any other agency as defined by O.C.G.A. ยง 50-14-1 hold a closed meeting to discuss matters excluded from application of the provisions of the O.C.G.A. ยง 50-14-1, as provided for by O.C.G.A. ยง 50-14-3, then the following procedures shall apply:
(a)
At the conclusion of the meeting or closed portion thereof, every commissioner or agency member present at such meeting shall sign a notarized affidavit in substantially the form set forth in subsection (b) below, stating under oath that the subject matter of the meeting or the closed portion thereof was devoted to matters within the exceptions provided by law and identifying the specific relevant exception and stating that no other topics were presented or discussed other than those topics for which the closed meeting was called. If the mayor, any commissioner, or other agency member believes that another topic was presented or discussed, then the mayor, any commissioner, or other agency member shall make a notation of such belief beside his or her name on the form provided.
(b)
The form required by subsection (a) shall be substantially as follows:
