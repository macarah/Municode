Editor's note— This article is a compilation of various Special Acts of the Georgia General Assembly which do not justify treatment as an article or division of this Part I, Related Laws. History notes have been provided indicating the source of each section.

Sec. 1. - [Vehicle registration.]
Effective January 1, 1994, vehicles shall be registered and licensed to operate for the ensuing calendar year and thereafter in Emanuel County during designated registration periods as provided in Code Section 40-2-21 of the O.C.G.A.
(1993 Ga. Laws, page 4322, § 1)
