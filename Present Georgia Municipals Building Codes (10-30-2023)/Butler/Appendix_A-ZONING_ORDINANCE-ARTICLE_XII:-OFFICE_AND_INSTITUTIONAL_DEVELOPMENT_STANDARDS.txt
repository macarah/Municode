ARTICLE XII: - OFFICE AND INSTITUTIONAL DEVELOPMENT STANDARDS

Section 1: - Purpose and Intent
The Office and Institutional district is intended to establish and preserve areas for lower density office activities along 2-, 3-, or 4- lane state highways with a mix of certain compatible residential activities also permitted. In many cases this district may be appropriate to provide a transition from residential uses to commercial or industrial uses. Development standards for this district require adequate yard space for off-street parking and service facilities. Permitted uses are restricted and protected from encroachment by uses capable of adversely affecting the limited character of this district.
(Ord. of 3-12-2019)
