ARTICLE XV. - YARD SALES

Sec. 50-561. - Definitions.
The following words, terms and phrases, when used in this article, shall have the meanings ascribed to them in this section, except where the context clearly indicates a different meaning:
Yard sale means the sale of personal or household items of an individual who is a resident of the city including, but not limited to, personal clothing items, appliances, recreational items, lawn and garden items, and any and all personal items of individuals or family members that may have been acquired or accumulated for the personal use of the individual or its family members which may have been used or maintained in or about the family residence. The disposal or sale of motor vehicles or other items purchased for the specific intent of resale to the general public shall be excluded under the definition herein. It is the intent and design of this definition to allow for the sale and disposal of personal items of individuals which were accumulated during the normal course of providing for the family unit. Any person or individual desiring to sell items or merchandise for profit or business purposes are excluded under the terms of this definition.
(Code 1982, § 5-215(1))
Cross reference— Definitions generally, § 1-2.
Sec. 50-562. - Registration required.
Any person, persons or organizations who wish to conduct a yard sale in the city on behalf of themselves, or under the sponsorship of any charitable, religious, social, patriotic or civic club organization shall be required to register such yard sale with the city at least 48 hours in advance of such event and to obtain a permit thereof.
(Code 1982, § 5-215(2))
Sec. 50-563. - Limitation on number of yard sales to be conducted.
The number of yard sales to be conducted by any one individual is to be limited to two days per year. The number of yard sales to any charitable, religious, social, patriotic, civic club or organization shall be limited to two days per year. The yard sale conducted during any portion of one day shall be considered as one day and the total number of yard sales authorized may be allocated over any number of days during a one-year period so long as the amount of days do not exceed the amount allowable under this article.
(Code 1982, § 5-215(3))
Sec. 50-564. - Form of registration.
The registration required by this article shall be in writing, shall be signed by the individual or the chief officer of the club or organization, and shall contain the following information:
(1)
The name of the individual; the name and object of the charitable, religious, social, patriotic, civic club or organization conducting or sponsoring the yard sale to which the registration relates;
(2)
The day or days on which the yard sale will take place;
(3)
The location of the yard sale; and
(4)
The name and address of the chief officer of the club or organization conducting or sponsoring the yard sale.
(Code 1982, § 5-215(4))
Sec. 50-565. - Exemption.
This article shall not apply to any established business which is operating under a business permit issued by the city for the sale of related items.
(Code 1982, § 5-215(5))
Sec. 50-566. - Duty to exhibit.
Individuals or organizations conducting yard sales shall be required to exhibit their permit at the request of any citizen or authority of the city.
(Code 1982, § 5-215(6))
Sec. 50-567. - Unlawful acts.
It shall be unlawful for any person to execute a registration which contains any false statement, and it shall be unlawful for any person or organization to conduct a yard sale in the city without first obtaining said registration. Any person conducting a yard sale without first registering the same shall be guilty of a misdemeanor and subject to a fine of not more than $25.00.
(Code 1982, § 5-215(7))
Sec. 50-568. - Additional days.
Any individual, charitable, religious, social, patriotic, civic club organization desiring to hold a yard sale in excess of the days allowed under section 50-563 must obtain a permit from the city as required in section 50-562 with an additional fee of $10.00 per day.
(Code 1982, § 5-215(8))
Secs. 50-569, 50-570. - Reserved.
