DIVISION 20. - MU-2 (MIXED-USE LOW-MEDIUM DENSITY) DISTRICT

Sec. 46-597. - District requirements, standards and criteria.
All provisions found in the MU-1 (Mixed-Use Low Density) District shall apply to the MU-2 (Mixed-Use Low-Medium Density) District.
