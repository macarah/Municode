Sec. 34-1. - Health regulations adopted.
(a)
There is hereby adopted by reference the rules and regulations of the county board of health pertaining to:
(1)
Sewage disposal;
(2)
Water supplies;
(3)
Sanitary rules;
(4)
Rabies; and
(5)
Body art establishments.
(b)
There is hereby adopted by reference the rules and regulations promulgated by the state pertaining to:
(1)
Food services,
(2)
On-site sewage management systems,
(3)
Tourist accommodations, and
(4)
Department of human resources public health.
(c)
Copies of such rules and regulations adopted by this section shall be maintained on file in the office of the county clerk.
(Res. No. 93-13, 4-6-93; Res. No. 98-25, 9-1-98; Res. No. 2006-8, 1-31-06)
