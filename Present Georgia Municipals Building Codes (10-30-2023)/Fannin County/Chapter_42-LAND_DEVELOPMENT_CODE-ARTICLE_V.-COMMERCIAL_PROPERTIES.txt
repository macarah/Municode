ARTICLE V. - COMMERCIAL PROPERTIES

Sec. 42-383. - Compliance with federal, state and local laws.
Persons developing real estate for commercial purposes in the unincorporated areas of Fannin County must comply with all state and federal laws on setbacks from streams, the local ordinances on setbacks from property lines and road rights-of-way, and any other requirements in this chapter which may apply.
(Ord. of 3-12-2002, § 7.100)
