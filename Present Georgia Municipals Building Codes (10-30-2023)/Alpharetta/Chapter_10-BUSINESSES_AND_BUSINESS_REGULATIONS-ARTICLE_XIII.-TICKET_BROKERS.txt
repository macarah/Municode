State Law reference— Ticket brokers, O.C.G.A. § 43-4B-25 et seq.

Sec. 10-405. - Adoption of state law by reference.
(a)
The provisions of O.C.G.A. §§ 43-4B-25 through 43-4B-30 regarding ticket brokers are hereby adopted and incorporated herein by reference as the city's ticket broker regulations with like effect, as if fully recited herein.
(b)
Any person who violates this section is guilty of a misdemeanor and shall be subject to a fine according to the penalties described in section 1-8 and in the discretion of the municipal judge.
(Code 1989, § 12-206; Ord. No. 623, § 1, 5-19-2008)
