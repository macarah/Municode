Sec. 78-1. - Insurance.
All taxicabs and U-drive vehicles must have property damage and liability insurance in some respectable insurance company for $5,000.00 property damage, $10,000.00 personal injuries. Until such insurance is provided, no license shall be issued or continued in force.
(Code 1977, § 22-1)
Sec. 78-2. - Numbering of taxicab.
All taxicabs operating in the city shall have a numbered sticker on the taxi. The number shall be nontransferable.
(Code 1977, § 22-2)
Sec. 78-3. - Lettering on taxicab.
All taxicabs operated in the city shall carry a marking, with the word "taxi" and the company name, in letters not smaller than four inches.
(Code 1977, § 22-3)
