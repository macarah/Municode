Sec. 22-1. - Provisions saved from repeal.
The ordinance and agreements providing for cable television service in the county are compiled and published separately by the county. Such ordinances and agreements are not repealed and shall continue in full force and effect. Official copies of such documents are on file in the office of the clerk of the board of commissioners.
