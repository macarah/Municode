Chapter 14 - COMMUNITY DEVELOPMENT
ARTICLE I. - IN GENERAL

Secs. 14-1—14-18. - Reserved.
ARTICLE II. - DEVELOPMENT AUTHORITY

Sec. 14-19. - Established.
The board of commissioners does establish the county development authority pursuant to O.C.G.A. § 36-62-1 et seq.
(Code 1994, § 21-26; Res. of 7-9-1996)
Sec. 14-20. - Membership.
The county development authority consists of seven members to be hereinafter designated by the board of commissioners.
(Code 1994, § 21-27; Res. of 7-9-1996)
Sec. 14-21. - Powers.
The county development authority has those powers and authorities conferred upon such authorities as set forth in O.C.G.A. § 36-62-1 et seq.
(Code 1994, § 21-28; Res. of 7-9-1996)
