CHAPTER 1-16 - RECORDS RETENTION

1-16-010 - Adoption.
The City of Marietta does adopt a records retention schedule, attached as Exhibit A to the ordinance codified in this chapter.
(Code 1978, § 1-7001, Ord. No. 5416, 3/8/1995, § 1; Ord. No. 5575, 6/12/1996)
1-16-020 - Coordinator.
The city clerk is designated as the coordinator of the records management program and authorized to act for and on behalf of the City of Marietta in directing and coordinating all records management matters under the Georgia Record Act.
(Code 1978, § 1-7002, Ord. No. 5416, 3/8/1995, § 2; Ord. No. 5575, 6/12/1996)
1-16-030 - Records committee—Duties.
The City of Marietta does create a records committee consisting of the city clerk, court clerk, police chief, personnel director and MIS director (or their designees), and any other necessary staff members. It is the duty of the records committee to review and recommend approval, disapproval or modification of the records retention schedules to the city council. Review of the records retention schedules will be based upon legal, fiscal, administrative and historical retention requirements. The city clerk will serve as chairperson of this committee, and final approval and authority shall be the city council.
(Code 1978, § 1-7003, Ord. No. 5416, 3/8/1995, § 3; Ord. No. 5575, 6/12/1996)
1-16-040 - Archival depository.
An archival depository will be selected to store those records determined to have permanent historical value.
(Code 1978, § 1-7004, Ord. No. 5416, 3/8/1995, § 4; Ord. No. 5575, 6/12/1996)
1-16-050 - Centralized records center.
A centralized records center (and/or local holding area) shall be established, and all records not required for day-to-day operation shall be transferred to the records center or local holding area until destroyed in accordance with the approved records retention schedule.
(Code 1978, § 1-7005, Ord. No. 5416, 3/8/1995, § 5; Ord. No. 5575, 6/12/1996)
1-16-060 - Alteration or destruction of records.
No record shall be altered or destroyed except in accordance with provisions as set forth in the duly approved records retention schedules as referenced in Section 1-16-030 above.
(Code 1978, § 1-7006, Ord. No. 5416, 3/8/1995, § 6; Ord. No. 5575, 6/12/1996)
1-16-070 - Records retention schedule.
Records retention schedules approved by the City of Marietta shall be on file in the city clerk's office.
(Code 1978, § 1-7007, Ord. No. 5416, 3/8/1995, § 7; Ord. No. 5575, 6/12/1996)
