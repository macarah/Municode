ARTICLE III - ELECTIONS

Section 3.1 - Qualifications and registration of electors.
Electors in city elections shall be qualified and registered as provided in Code Title 34A of the Code of Georgia of 1933, as now or hereafter amended.
(Ga. L. 1977, p. 3541, Sec. 3.1)
Section 3.2 - Conduct of elections generally.
City elections shall be conducted as provided for by Code Title 34A of the Code of Georgia of 1933, as now or hereafter amended, and as provided for by ordinance or resolution of the council adopted pursuant to Code Title 34A of the Code of Georgia of 1933, as now or hereafter amended.
Challenges to electors shall be determined as provided in Code Title 34A of the Code of Georgia of 1993, as now or hereafter amended.
(Ga. L. 1977, p. 3541, Sec. 3.2)
Section 3.3 - Wards.
The wards of the city shall be as now or hereafter defined or described by law or ordinance. A map, or maps, of the city shall be kept on file or posted in the city hall on which maps shall appear the boundary lines of the wards.
(Ga. L. 1977, p. 3541, Sec. 3.3)
