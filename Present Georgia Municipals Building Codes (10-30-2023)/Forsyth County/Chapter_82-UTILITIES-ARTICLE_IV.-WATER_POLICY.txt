ARTICLE IV. - WATER POLICY

Sec. 82-111. - Time limit for tap-ons by individual obtaining rezoning.
Any individual or entity obtaining rezoning within the water and sewerage jurisdiction and service area of the board of commissioners, with a condition that adherence to the county water policy be required, shall tap-on to county water lines if water from the county is available and pay any fees due for such tap on within one year from the date the rezoning is approved. If water is not available from the county within one year after rezoning, such tap-on and payment of fees shall occur not later than one year after county water becomes available to the individual or entity receiving rezoning with the aforementioned condition.
(Ord. No. 58, § 1, 10-24-1988; Policy of 6-23-1986; Res. of 4-27-1987; Res. of 9-12-1988)
Sec. 82-112. - Dedication of lines.
Any water lines constructed by developers or other persons in accordance with the water policy concerning construction and installation of watermains and service lines adopted by the county on June 23, 1986, as amended, shall deed and/or dedicate such water lines upon completion of construction after acceptance of the quality of such lines by county officials. In addition, the developer shall warrant the quality and viability of such water lines for any and all dysfunctions for one year after dedication of such lines to the county.
(Ord. No. 58, § 2, 10-24-1988; Policy of 6-23-1986; Res. of 4-27-1987; Res. of 9-12-1988)
