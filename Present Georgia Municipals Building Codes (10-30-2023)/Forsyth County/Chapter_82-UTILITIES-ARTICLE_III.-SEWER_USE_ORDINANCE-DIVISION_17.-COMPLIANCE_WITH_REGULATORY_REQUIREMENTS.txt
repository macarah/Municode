DIVISION 17. - COMPLIANCE WITH REGULATORY REQUIREMENTS

Sec. 82-110.16. - State and federal.
The provisions of this article shall not be deemed as alleviating compliance with applicable state and federal laws and regulations. Specific user charge and industrial cost recovery requirements, promulgated pursuant to Public Law 92-500, shall be considered as a part of this article upon official adoption. All non-domestic users are required to comply with pretreatment standards as outlined in Title 40 of the Code of Federal Regulation, Part 403, The Act and the Forsyth County Sewer Use Ordinance. Each industrial user is required to develop a compliance schedule for the installation of technology required to meet applicable pretreatment standards and related requirements.
