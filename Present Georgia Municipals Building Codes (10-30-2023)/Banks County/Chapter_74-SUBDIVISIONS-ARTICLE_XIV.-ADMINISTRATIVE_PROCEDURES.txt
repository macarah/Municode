ARTICLE XIV. - ADMINISTRATIVE PROCEDURES

Sec. 74-421. - Supplemental regulations.
The county planning commission may supplement this chapter as necessary with administrative procedures so that the chapter may be more effectively and consistently applied. These administrative procedures must be explicitly recorded in the minutes of the county planning commission and entered into a booklet entitled "Administrative Procedures of the Banks County Planning Commission."
(Res. No. 2001-08, § 1, 3-13-2001)
