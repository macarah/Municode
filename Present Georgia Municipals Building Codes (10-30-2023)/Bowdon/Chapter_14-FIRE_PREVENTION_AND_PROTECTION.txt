Sec. 14-1. - Fire alarm apparatus; injuring, destroying or interfering with same.
It shall be unlawful for any person to injure, destroy, or in any manner interfere with the electric fire alarm of the city, or with the batteries, boxes, stations, wires, or other apparatus or appurtenances thereof, or used in connection therewith.
(Code 1974, § 8-14; Code 1991, § 4-2)
State Law reference— Similar provisions, O.C.G.A. § 16-7-24.
Sec. 14-2. - Inflammable weeds, grasses and debris; notice to remove; failure to comply, removal by city and assessment of costs therefor.
(a)
It shall be unlawful for any person to have and maintain within the city any vacant lot or premises whereon inflammable weeds, grasses and debris are permitted to remain after being notified by the city manager that the same constitutes a fire hazard.
(b)
After five days' notice as hereinabove provided to the owner, the city may, by its own forces, eliminate such fire hazard by the removal of the grass, weeds and other debris thereon and thereupon assess the costs thereof against the owner of the property.
(Code 1974, § 8-15; Code 1991, § 4-3)
Sec. 14-3. - Life Safety Code adopted.
(a)
There is hereby adopted the edition of the Life Safety Code adopted by the state.
(b)
At least three copies of such edition of the Life Safety Code shall be on file in the office of the building inspector of the city at all times.
(Code 1974, § 8-16; Code 1991, § 4-26)
Sec. 14-4. - International Fire Code adopted.
There is hereby adopted for the purpose of prescribing regulations governing conditions hazardous to life and property from fire or explosion that certain code known as the International Fire Code as adopted by the state. Copies of such code shall be kept on file in the office of the building inspector and shall be open to public inspection at all times. The provisions of such code shall be controlling within the corporate limits of the city.
(Code 1974, § 8-28; Code 1991, § 4-41)
State Law reference— International fire code as minimum standard, O.C.G.A. §§ 8-2-20(9)(B)(i)(VIII), 8-2-25(a).
