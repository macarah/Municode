State Law reference— Authority to provide for libraries, archives and arts and sciences programs and facilities, Ga. Const. art. IX, § II, ¶ III(a)(10).

Sec. 26-31. - Operation and maintenance.
The county enters into the operation and maintenance of a library to be known as M. E. Roden Memorial Library and its successors in name.
(Res. of 12-12-1973, § 1)
Sec. 26-32. - County acceptance of inventory, assets.
The county is authorized to accept the inventory and assets of the former library and acting through its former self-perpetuating board of trustees, to be held for the sole and exclusive use as a public library for the county.
(Res. of 12-12-1973, § 2)
Sec. 26-33. - Contract with city authorized.
The county is authorized, as provided by law, to contract with the City of Hawkinsville should the city elect to participate in the operation of the library.
(Res. of 12-12-1973, § 3)
Sec. 26-34. - Board of trustees.
(a)
A library board of trustees shall be appointed by the commissioner or such other successor governing body of the county, to consist of 11 members to serve staggered terms, or appoint such members to the board of trustees as may provide in a cooperative contract between the county and the City of Hawkinsville or such other political subdivision as may be contracted with.
(b)
The board of trustees shall be charged with the duty to maintain and operate the public library, appoint officers, and hire such personnel as the needs of the library shall require and shall submit a proposed budget for the consideration of the commissioner prior to each fiscal year of operation.
(c)
The commissioner shall be an ex officio member of the board of trustees and shall receive copies of all minutes of any meetings of the board of trustees.
(Res. of 12-12-1973, §§ 4, 5, 8)
Cross reference— Authorities, boards and commissions, § 2-121 et seq.
Sec. 26-35. - Bond.
Such officer as shall handle the funds of the public library shall be under bond for the sums handled by him, the cost of the bond to be borne by the library. Copies of such bond shall be filed with the appropriate agencies, including the commissioner.
(Res. of 12-12-1973, § 6)
Sec. 26-36. - Annual financial reports required.
Annual reports of the financial conditions of the public library shall be made to the commissioner along with such other information as the commissioner shall require.
(Res. of 12-12-1973, § 7)
