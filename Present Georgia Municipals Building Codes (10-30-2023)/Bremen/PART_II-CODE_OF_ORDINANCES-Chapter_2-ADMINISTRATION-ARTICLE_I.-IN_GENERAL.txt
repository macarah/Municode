ARTICLE I. - IN GENERAL

Sec. 2-1. - Exercise of powers.
The powers of the city may be exercised, as provided by the Charter, by the city, and by the parties with whom the city specifically contracts for municipal services.
(Ord. No. 2016-O-005, § 2.1.1, 7-18-2016)
Sec. 2-2. - Polling place.
The polling place for the city shall be located at the Multipurpose Center located at 417 River Circle, Bremen, GA 30110.
(Code 1996, § 38-1)
State Law reference— Duty of governing authority to fix location of polling place, O.C.G.A. § 21-3-163.
