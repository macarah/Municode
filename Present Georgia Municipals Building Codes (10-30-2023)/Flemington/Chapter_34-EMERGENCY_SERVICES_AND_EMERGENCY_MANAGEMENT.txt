State Law reference— Georgia Emergency Management Act of 1981, O.C.G.A. § 38-3-1 et seq.

Sec. 34-1. - National Incident Management System adopted.
The city council hereby adopts the National Incident Management System (NIMS) as established under HSPD 5 and the United Command System as established under O.C.G.A. § 38-3-57 as its system of preparing for and responding to disaster incidents and directs all incident managers and response organizations in the county to train and exercise and use these systems in their response operations.
(Ord. of 10-1-2005)
