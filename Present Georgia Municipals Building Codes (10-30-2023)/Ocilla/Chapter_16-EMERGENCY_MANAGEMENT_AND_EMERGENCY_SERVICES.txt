Sec. 16-1. - National Incident Management System (NIMS) adopted.
The City hereby adopts the National Incident Management System (NIMS) as established under the federal Homeland Security Presidential Directive (HSPD) 5 and the Unified Command System as established under O.C.G.A. § 38-3-57 as its system of preparing for and responding to disaster incidents and directs all incident managers and response organizations in the City train and exercise and use these systems in their response operations.
(Code 2003, § 3-3001; Ord. of 2-7-2006)
