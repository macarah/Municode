Sec. 42-1. - Issuance of mobile home location permit.
(a)
Each year every owner of a mobile home subject to taxation under O.C.G.A. tit. 48, ch. 5, art. 10, Ad Valorem Taxation of Motor Vehicles and Mobile Homes (O.C.G.A. § 48-5-440 et seq.) shall obtain on or before May 1 from the county tax collector a mobile home location permit.
(1)
The issuance of the permit shall be evidenced by the issuance of a decal. Each decal shall reflect the county as the county of issuance. and the calendar year for which the permit is issued.
(2)
The decal shall be prominently attached and displayed on the mobile home by the owner.
(b)
Except as provided for mobile homes owned by a dealer, no mobile home location permit shall be issued until all ad valorem taxes due on the mobile home have been paid.
(1)
Each year every owner of a mobile home situated in this county on January 1 which is not subject to taxation under O.C.G.A. tit. 48, ch. 5, art. 10, Ad Valorem Taxation of Motor Vehicles and Mobile Homes (O.C.G.A. § 48-5-440 et seq.) shall obtain on or before May 1 a mobile home location permit.
(2)
The issuance of the permit shall be evidenced by the issuance of a decal which shall reflect the county of issuance and the calendar year for which the permit is issued.
(3)
The decal shall be prominently attached and displayed on the mobile home by the owner.
State law reference— Issuance of mobile home location permits and display requirements, O.C.G.A. § 48-5-492.
Sec. 42-2. - Display of decal; penalty for violation.
(a)
It shall be unlawful to fail to attach and display on a mobile home the decal as required by O.C.G.A. § 48-5-492.
(b)
Any person who violates subsection (a) of this section shall be guilty of a misdemeanor and, upon conviction thereof, shall be punished by a fine of not less than $25.00 nor more than $200.00, except that upon the receipt of proof of purchase of a decal prior to the date of the issuance of a summons, the fine shall be $25.00.
(Res. No. 88-004, 5-3-1988)
