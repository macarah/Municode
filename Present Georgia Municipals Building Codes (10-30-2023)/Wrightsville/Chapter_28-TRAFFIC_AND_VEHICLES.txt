Chapter 28 - TRAFFIC AND VEHICLES
ARTICLE I. - IN GENERAL

Sec. 28-1. - Uniform Rules of the Road.
Pursuant to O.C.G.A. § 40-6-372, the Uniform Rules of the Road, O.C.G.A. § 40-6-1 et seq., are adopted by reference thereto.
Secs. 28-2—28-20. - Reserved.
ARTICLE II. - PERSONAL TRANSPORTATION VEHICLES AND GOLF CARTS

Sec. 28-21. - Definitions.
(a)
The term "PTV" or "personal transportation vehicle" is defined as a golf cart or motorized cart under state law; a PTV is any motor vehicle having no fewer than three wheels, and a gross weight of 1,300 pounds or less, which cannot operate at more than 20 miles per hour.
(b)
A golf cart, by definition, cannot operate at more than 15 miles per hour.
(Ord. No. 9-119, § 1, 12-18-2017)
Sec. 28-22. - Prohibition.
(a)
All operators of a PTV and/or golf cart must have a valid driver's license.
(b)
All operators of a PTV and/or golf cart must be at least 18 years of age.
(c)
All operators of PTVs or golf carts must have liability insurance on the PTVs or golf carts, and proof of such insurance must be kept in the PTVs or golf carts at all times.
(d)
Any operator of a PTV or golf cart who does not have proof of current liability insurance on the PTV or golf cart that he is operating will be in violation of this Code, as defined in chapter 1, and subject to the provisions thereof.
(e)
Operation of any PTV or golf cart on any city street must be between dawn and dusk. No PTV or golf cart shall be operated at night at any time or for any reason.
(f)
All PTVs and/or golf carts are governed by the Uniform Rules of the Road as set forth in O.C.G.A. § 40-6-1 et seq., and adopted by reference herein.
(Ord. No. 9-119, § 2, 12-18-2017)
