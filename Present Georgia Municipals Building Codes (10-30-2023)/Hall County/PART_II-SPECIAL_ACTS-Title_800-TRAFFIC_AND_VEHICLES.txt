800.20.001. Registration, licensing required.
 
Sec. 800.20.001. - Registration, licensing required.
Effective January 1, 1987, vehicles shall be registered and licensed to operate for the ensuing calendar year and thereafter in Hall County during designated registration periods as provided in Code section 40-2-20.1 of the O.C.G.A.
(1986 Ga. Laws (Act No. 1572), p. 5616, § 1)
