2.30.010. - Name created.
The position of marshal, a/k/a the Hall County Marshals was originally created by the Hall County Board of Commissioners on January 26, 1981. The name was changed to the Enforcement Division of Hall County Public Safety Department on October 28, 1991 and to the Enforcement Division of Hall County on December 14, 1995. The Enforcement Division of Hall County shall now hereafter be known as the Hall County Marshals, and any reference to the enforcement division within this Code shall be deemed to refer to the Hall County Marshals.
(Ord. of 1-14-10, § 1(Exh. A))
2.30.020. - Powers and duties.
The Hall County Marshals shall have the powers and duties as well as other statutory authority as set out in O.C.G.A. §§ 36-8-1 through 36-8-7 as well as any other powers and duties that may have been heretofore or hereafter will be conferred upon positions of this nature.
(Ord. of 1-14-10, § 1(Exh. A))
2.30.030. - Chain of command.
The chain of command for the Hall County Marshals enforcing the regulations of the Official Code of Hall County, Georgia, shall be as follows:
A.
The Hall County Board of Commissioners.
B.
The Hall County Administrator.
(Ord. of 1-14-10, § 1(Exh. A))
