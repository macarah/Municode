Sec. 70-1. - Compliance with personnel manual required; penalty.
(a)
All employees of the city who are regulated by the City of Kennesaw Employee Personnel Manual shall comply with such rules and regulations.
(b)
Any employee violating any of the provisions of the city's employee personnel manual shall be subject to suspension and/or dismissal in addition to any penalty which may be imposed for the violation of such employee personnel manual.
(Code 1986, § 2-4-11; Ord. No. 2014-12, 9-2-14)
