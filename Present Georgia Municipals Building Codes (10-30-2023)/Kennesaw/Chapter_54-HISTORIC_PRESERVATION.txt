ARTICLE II. - HISTORIC DESIGN REVIEW GUIDELINES

Sec. 54-31. - Design standards.
The design standards for the city are hereby adopted by reference and made a part of the Kennesaw City Code of Ordinances. These provisions are on file and available for inspection in the office of the city clerk and the zoning administrator.
(Ord. No. 95-19, 8-21-95; Ord. No. 2007-04, §§ 2, 3, 2-5-07)
Secs. 54-32—54-37. - Reserved.
