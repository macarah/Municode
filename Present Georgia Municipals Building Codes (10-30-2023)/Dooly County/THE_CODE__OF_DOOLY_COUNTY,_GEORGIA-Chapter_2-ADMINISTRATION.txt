ARTICLE V. - BOARDS, COMMISSIONS AND AUTHORITIES
DIVISION 1. - GENERALLY

Sec. 2-121. - Board of commissioners to serve absent appointments.
Except as otherwise provided by law, whenever an ordinance establishes or references a board or commission and such board or commission has not been established or the membership of such board or commission has not been appointed, the board of commissioners shall serve as such board.

DIVISION 2. - MIDDLE GEORGIA SOLID WASTE MANAGEMENT AUTHORITY

FOOTNOTE(S):
(4) State Law reference— Regional Solid Waste Management Authorities Act, O.C.G.A. § 12-8-50 et seq.
Sec. 2-141. - Activation of Authority.
The County, after diligent study and review, hereby joins with the other Participants in finding and declaring that there is a need for an authority to function in the area of solid waste management. In conjunction with the other Participants, the County hereby activates the Middle Georgia Regional Solid Waste Management Authority, a public corporation created as an institution of purely public charity, to perform an essential governmental function in the exercise of the power conferred upon it by this division and the Regional Solid Waste Management Authorities Act (O.C.G.A. § 12-8-50 et seq.).
(Ord. of 8-30-1993, § 1)
State law reference— Resolution of need required to activate authority, O.C.G.A. § 12-8-52.
