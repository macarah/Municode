ARTICLE II. - HEALTH CODE

Sec. 32-19. - Adoption of county health rules and regulations.
(a)
There is hereby adopted by reference and made a part of this Code the rules and regulations of the county health department, it being the intent hereof that said rules and regulations and the provisions of this article shall be liberally construed so that the people of the city shall be protected to the fullest extent.
(b)
The enforcing officer of the county health department is authorized to enforce the provisions of this article within the jurisdictional limits of the city.
(Code 1966, § 13-1; Ord. No. 89-04, § 1, 8-28-1989)
