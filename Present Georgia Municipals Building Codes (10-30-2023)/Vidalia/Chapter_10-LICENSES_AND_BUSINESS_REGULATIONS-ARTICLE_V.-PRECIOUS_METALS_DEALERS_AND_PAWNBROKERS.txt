ARTICLE V. - PRECIOUS METALS DEALERS AND PAWNBROKERS

Sec. 10-60. - Statutes adopted.
The provisions of O.C.G.A. tit. 43, ch. 37 (O.C.G.A. § 43-37-1 et seq.), pertaining to precious metals and gems, and the provisions of O.C.G.A. tit. 44, ch. 12, pt. 5 (O.C.G.A. § 44-12-130 et seq.) are adopted by reference.
(Mo. of 3-11-91(2))
