ARTICLE II. - RECREATION DEPARTMENT

Sec. 50-31. - Enactment and short title.
The board of commissioners of Screven County, Georgia, (herein, the "board") hereby enacts and adopts the following ordinance which shall be known and cited as the "Screven County Recreation Department Ordinance."
(Ord. No. 2003-02, § 1, 2-25-2003)
Sec. 50-32. - Creation of recreation department.
There is hereby created, as a department of the government of Screven County, Georgia, the county recreation department (herein, the "department"). The county manager shall, with the approval of the board, employ a director of recreation (herein, the "director") at a salary to be established by the board of commissioners who shall have such duties as may be provided herein and as may be delegated by the board through the county manager. The director of the department shall, with the approval of the county manager, employ such personnel as may be required to carry out the functions of the department. The director and all employees of the department shall be subject to the county personnel policy.
(Ord. No. 2003-02, § 2, 2-25-2003)
Sec. 50-33. - Duties of the director of recreation.
The director shall plan, promote, organize and supervise a comprehensive county recreation program and administer the same in the interest of all of the citizens of the county, and shall have, in addition to other responsibilities as may be assigned to it from time to time, primary responsibility for the following:
(1)
Design of all parks, parkways and public grounds;
(2)
The care, maintenance and landscaping of all public lands, including parks, parkways, recreation complexes, and playgrounds;
(3)
Organization and programming of all recreational activities involving general public participation in a comprehensive and diversified program, including athletics, senior citizen programs, arts and crafts, and other such programs; and,
(4)
Preparation and presentation of an annual departmental budget to be considered by the board of commissioners in connection with the development of the county's annual budget and the management of department's finances within the budget so established.
(Ord. No. 2003-02, § 3, 2-25-2003)
Sec. 50-34. - Creation of advisory committee on recreation.
The board may, by resolution, create a citizens advisory committee to be known as "The Screven County Recreation Advisory Committee." The committee shall be composed of a cross-section of county citizens who have an interest in the recreation activities of the county and its primary function shall be to advise the board of commissioners in all matters related to the county recreation program.
(Ord. No. 2003-02, § 4, 2-25-2003)
