DIVISION 13. - NON-DISCIPLINARY DEMOTIONS

Sec. 2-401. - Employee request to fill open positions; salary reduction in pay grade.
Employees may request that they be demoted to open positions at lower pay grades if they are qualified for the position. If such a request is granted, the employee's salary will be reduced to an appropriate step within the pay grade of the new job.
(Mo. of 12-4-2007, art. XIII)
