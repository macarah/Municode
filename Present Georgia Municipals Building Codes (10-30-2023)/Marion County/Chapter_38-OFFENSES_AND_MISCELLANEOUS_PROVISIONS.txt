Chapter 38 - OFFENSES AND MISCELLANEOUS PROVISIONS

Sec. 38-1. - Penalty assessment for jail construction and staffing.
Pursuant to the provisions of an act of the legislature called the Jail Construction and Staffing Act and identified as 1989 Ga. Laws, page 1753 (O.C.G.A. ยง 15-21-90 et seq.), the county hereby levies a ten percent penalty on every fine levied in the county. The provisions of said act are hereby put into legal effect. It shall be the responsibility of every person, agency or legal entity collecting fines to add an additional ten percent onto the fine imposed by the court or fine imposing entity or tribunal and collect the same from the person being required to pay the fine and penalty. Such person or legal entity collecting the ten percent penalty shall, by the tenth day of the month following the month in which such penalties are collected, pay to the county governing authorities said sums collected. Such funds shall be deposited in a county jail fund for the sole purpose of being used for constructing, operating, and staffing of jails, correctional institutions, and detention facilities by the county. The ten percent surcharge shall begin being imposed as of January 1, 1990.
(Res. of 7-11-1989)
