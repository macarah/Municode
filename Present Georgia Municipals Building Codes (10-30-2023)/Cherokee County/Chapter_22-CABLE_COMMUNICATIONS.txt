Sec. 22-1. - Provisions saved from repeal.
The provisions of the ordinance adopted December 29, 1988, granting to Prestige Cable TV, Inc., a franchise to operate a cable system within the county is not repealed and shall continue in full force and effect pending a revision of the subject matter by the county.
