ARTICLE II. - 9-1-1 CHARGE ON PREPAID WIRELESS SERVICES

Sec. 30-19. - Charge on transactions.
In accordance with O.C.G.A. § 46-5-134.2, there is hereby imposed a prepaid wireless 9-1-1 charge as defined by O.C.G.A. § 46-5-134.2(a)(4) upon every prepaid wireless retail transaction occurring within the jurisdiction of the public service answering point in the amount of $0.75.
(Ord. of 8-11-2011, § 1)
Sec. 30-20. - Collection of charge on transactions.
Prepaid wireless 9-1-1 charges collected by sellers shall be remitted to the commissioner of the department of revenue at the times and in the manner provided by O.C.G.A. title 48, ch. 8 with respect to the sales and use tax imposed on prepaid wireless calling service.
(Ord. of 8-11-2011, § 2)
Sec. 30-21. - Administrative provisions.
The county administrator/clerk of the county is hereby directed to file with the state revenue commissioner a certified copy of this article and amendments thereto, in accordance with O.C.G.A. § 46-5-134.2(j)(1), within ten days of the enactment of the ordinance from which this article is derived.
(Ord. of 8-11-2011, § 3)
Sec. 30-22. - Depositing of funds; use of funds.
In accordance with O.C.G.A. § 46-5-134.2(j)(5), funds received by the county from charges imposed by this article shall be deposited in the emergency telephone system fund maintained by this county pursuant to O.C.G.A. § 46-5-134 and kept separate from general revenue of the jurisdiction. All such funds shall be used exclusively for the purposes authorized by O.C.G.A. § 46-5-134(f).
(Ord. of 8-11-2011, § 4)
