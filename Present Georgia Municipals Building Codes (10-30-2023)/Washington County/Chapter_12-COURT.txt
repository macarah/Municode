Chapter 12 - COURT
ARTICLE I. - IN GENERAL

Secs. 12-1—12-18. - Reserved.
ARTICLE II. - SUPPLEMENTAL PENALTIES

Sec. 12-19. - Additional penalty.
In every case in which any court is this county shall impose a fine, which shall be construed to include cost, for any offense against a criminal or traffic law of the state or any offense against a court ordinance, there shall be imposed as an additional penalty a sum equal to ten percent of the original fine.
(Res. No. WC-52, 8-9-1990)
Sec. 12-20. - Additional percentage of bond or bail required.
At the time of posting bail or bond in any case involving a violation of a criminal or traffic law of the state or a violation of a county ordinance, an additional sum equal to ten percent of the original amount of bail or bond shall be posted. In every case in which any court in this county shall order the forfeiture of bail or bond, the additional sum equal to ten percent of the original bail or bond shall be collected.
(Res. No. WC-52, 8-9-1990)
Sec. 12-21. - Additional penalties are supplemental.
The additional penalties set forth in section 12-19 above shall be in addition to that amount required by O.C.G.A. § 47-17-60 to be paid into the Peace Officers' Annuity and Benefit Fund, by O.C.G.A. § 47-11-51 to be paid into the Judges of the Probate Courts Retirement Fund of Georgia, or by any other provision of law.
(Res. No. WC-52, 8-9-1990)
Sec. 12-22. - Penalties to be collected by Clerk of the Court.
The additional penalties as herein prescribed in section 12-19 shall be assessed and collected by the Clerk of the Court or court officer charged with the duty of collecting monies arising from fines and forfeited bonds. Such additional penalties shall be paid over to the board of commissioners by the tenth day of the month following the month in which these sums are collected.
(Res. No. WC-52, 8-9-1990)
Sec. 12-23. - Special account.
All such sums collected and paid over to the board of commissioners shall be deposited into a special account which shall be known as the "county jail fund."
(Res. No. WC-52, 8-9-1990)
Sec. 12-24. - Jail fund expenditures restricted.
All moneys collected pursuant to this article and placed in the county jail fund shall be expended solely and exclusively for construction, operating, and staffing the county correctional facilities, or for such other purposes as specified in O.C.G.A. § 15-21-95.
(Res. No. WC-52, 8-9-1990)
Sec. 12-25. - Limited applicability of additional penalties.
The additional penalties provided for by this article shall not be imposed or collected in any court operated by any municipality located in the county.
(Res. No. WC-52, 8-9-1990)
