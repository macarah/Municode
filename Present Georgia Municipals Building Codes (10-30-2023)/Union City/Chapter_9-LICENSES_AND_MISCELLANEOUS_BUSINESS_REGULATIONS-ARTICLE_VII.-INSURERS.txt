ARTICLE VII. - INSURERS

Sec. 9-181. - License fees generally.
There is hereby levied for the year 2022 and for each year thereafter an annual license fee upon each insurer doing business within the City of Union City, Georgia, in the amount of one hundred dollars ($100.00). For each separate business location in excess of one (1) not covered by section 9-182, which is operating on behalf of such insurers within the City of Union City, Georgia, there is hereby levied a license fee in the amount of one hundred dollars ($100.00). For the purposes of this article, the term "insurer" means a company which is authorized to transact business in any of the classes of insurance designated in O.C.G.A. § 33-3-5.
(Ord. No. 84-12, 12-3-84; Ord. No. 92-10, § 1, 9-15-92; Ord. No. 2001-10, § 1, 10-16-01; Ord. No. 2021-05 , § 1, 12-21-21
State Law reference— Municipal license fee authorized, O.C.G.A. § 33-8-8.
Sec. 9-182. - License fees for insurers insuring certain risks at additional business locations.
For each separate business location, not otherwise subject to a license fee hereunder, operated and maintained by a business organization which is engaged in the business of lending money or transacting sales involving term financing and in connection with such loans or sales offers, solicits or takes application for insurance through a licensed agent of an insurer for insurance said insurer shall pay an additional license fee of thirty-five dollars ($35.00) per location for the year 2022 and for each year thereafter.
(Ord. No. 84-12, 12-3-84; Ord. No. 2001-10, § 2, 10-16-01; Ord. No. 2021-05 , § 2, 12-21-21)
State Law reference— License fee authorized, O.C.G.A. § 33-8-8.
Sec. 9-183. - Insurance agency license fees; independent insurance agencies, brokers, etc., not otherwise licensed.
There is hereby levied upon independent agencies and brokers an annual license fee of fifty dollars ($50.00) for each separate business location within the city from which an insurance business is conducted, and which is not subject to the company license fee imposed by section 9-181.
(Ord. No. 84-12, 12-3-84; Ord. No. 92-14, § 2, 9-15-92)
Sec. 9-184. - Gross premiums tax.
(a)
Life insurers. There is hereby levied for the year 2002 and for each year thereafter an annual tax based solely upon gross direct premiums upon each insurer writing life, accident and sickness insurance within the State of Georgia in an amount equal to one (1) percent of the gross direct premiums received during the preceding calendar year in accordance with O.C.G.A. section 33-8-8.1. Gross direct premiums as used in this section shall mean gross direct premiums as used in O.C.G.A. section 33-8-4. The premium tax levied by this section is in addition to the license fees imposed by section 9-181 of this article.
(b)
All other insurers. There is hereby levied for the year 2002 and for each year thereafter an annual tax based solely upon gross direct premiums upon each insurer, other than an insurer transacting business in the class of insurance designated in O.C.G.A. section 33-3-5(1), doing business within the State of Georgia in an amount equal to two and one-half (2.5) percent of the gross direct premiums received during the preceding calendar year in accordance with O.C.G.A. section 33-8-8.2. Gross direct premiums as used in this section shall mean gross direct premiums as used in O.C.G.A. section 33-8-4. The premium tax levied by this section is in addition to the license fees imposed by section 9-181 of this article.
(Ord. No. 84-12, 12-3-84; Ord. No. 2001-10, §§ 3, 4, 10-16-01)
Cross reference— Taxation and finance, Ch. 13.
Sec. 9-185. - Due date for license fees.
License fees imposed in sections 9-181 and 9-182 of this article shall be due and payable on the first day of [January] 2002 and on the first date of each subsequent year.
(Ord. No. 84-12, 12-3-84; Ord. No. 2001-10, § 5, 10-16-01)
Sec. 9-186. - Administrative provisions.
The city clerk is hereby directed to forward a duly certified copy of this ordinance to the state insurance commissioner within forty-five (45) days of its enactment.
(Ord. No. 84-12, 12-3-84; Ord. No. 2001-10, § 6, 10-16-01)
Sec. 9-187. - Enforcement.
The fees and taxes levied by this article may be enforced by execution in the same manner as other taxes of the city. A violation of this article shall be grounds for refusing or revoking a license, and the person responsible may be punished as for violations of other city ordinances.
(Ord. No. 84-12, 12-3-84)
