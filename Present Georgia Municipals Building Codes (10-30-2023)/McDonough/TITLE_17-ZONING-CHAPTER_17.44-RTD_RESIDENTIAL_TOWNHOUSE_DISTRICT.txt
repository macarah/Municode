CHAPTER 17.44 - RTD RESIDENTIAL TOWNHOUSE DISTRICT

Sections:
17.44.010 - Purpose.
This district is composed of certain lands in the City intended to be used for medium density residential development. The district requirements are also designed to provide quality development, variety in housing concepts, and to promote individual home ownership. The residential townhouse district shall permit fee simple unit construction of townhouse type single-family attached dwellings.
(Ord. of 9-16-1985, (part))
17.44.020 - Density and spacing requirements.
A.
A maximum of eight (8) dwelling units per acre is permitted in the RTD district with a maximum of eight (8) dwelling units in any one (1) contiguous building.
B.
There shall be a minimum of twenty (20) feet between any two (2) buildings in all directions provided that a two (2) hour fire wall is provided on both buildings constructed of brick and mortar, otherwise the buildings shall be thirty (30) feet between any two (2) buildings in all directions.
(Ord. of 9-16-1985, (part); Ord. No. 04-12-06-B, § XVI, 12-6-2004; Ord. No. 08-01-22(C), § XVIII, 1-22-2008)
17.44.030 - Parking.
An enclosed two (2) car garage shall be required for each dwelling unit. The exterior of all rear loaded garages must be constructed of the same materials as the dwelling unit.
(Ord. of 9-16-1985, (part); Ord. No. 04-12-06-B, § XVII, 12-6-2004; Ord. No. 08-01-22(C), § XIX, 1-22-2008)
17.44.040 - Buffer strip.
A 25-foot buffer strip shall be required whenever the project site abuts any property which is zoned for commercial or industrial use. A ten-foot landscaped buffer shall be provided along all other lots abutting the project site. As used in this Chapter, a project site is the entire parcel of land for which townhouse units are proposed prior to the creation of any townhouse lots. The required buffer shall not lie within any individual townhouse lot.
(Ord. of 9-16-1985, (part); Ord. No. 05-02-07(D), § 1, 2-7-2005)
17.44.050 - Streets.
All streets within the RTD district shall be public and constructed according to the subdivision regulations of the City.
(Ord. of 9-16-1985, (part))
17.44.060 - Plan review.
Before a building permit can be issued in an RTD district for any development, a preliminary site plan shall be submitted to, and approved by, the City Council in accordance with the provisions of Chapter 16.12 of the City of McDonough Code of Ordinances.
(Ord. of 9-16-1985, (part); Ord. No. 04-12-06-B, § XVIII, 12-6-2004; Ord. No. 08-01-22(C), § XX, 1-22-2008)
17.44.070 - Permitted uses.
Within any RTD district, the following uses shall be permitted:
A.
Single-family attached fee simple townhomes.
(Ord. of 9-16-1985, (part); Ord. No. 04-12-06-B, § XIX, 12-6-2004; Ord. No. 08-01-22(C), § XXI, 1-22-2008)
17.44.080 - Landscaping.
A.
Individual lots. Each individual townhouse lot shall be at least 20 feet wide at the street right-of-way, have a minimum front yard of 20 feet from the right-of-way, and have a minimum rear yard of 20 feet.
B.
Individual units:
1.
Each individual unit shall have the following minimum heated living area:
a.
One-bedroom unit: 1,200 square feet;
b.
Two-bedroom unit, 1,300 square feet; and
c.
Three-bedroom unit, 1,400 square feet.
2.
No part of any individual unit shall be closer than 30 feet from any local residential street right-of-way or 30 feet from any other street right-of-way.
3.
Maximum height: No building may exceed 35 feet in height.
(Ord. of 9-16-1985, (part) ; Ord. No. 05-02-07(D), § 2, 2-7-2005; Ord. No. 08-01-22(C), § XXII, 1-22-2008)
17.44.090 - Lot Coverage.
The project site shall not exceed fifty (50%) percent impervious surface.
(Ord. No. 04-12-06-B, § XX, 12-6-2004; Ord. No. 05-02-07(D), § 3, 2-7-2005; Ord. No. 08-01-22(C), § XXIII, 1-22-2008; Ord. No. 08-12-29, § VIII, 12-29-2008)
17.44.100 - Landscaping.
Each fee simple lot and all common areas shall be provided with automatic underground outdoor lawn sprinklers for all landscaped areas.
(Ord. No. 08-01-22(C), § XXIV, 1-22-2008)
