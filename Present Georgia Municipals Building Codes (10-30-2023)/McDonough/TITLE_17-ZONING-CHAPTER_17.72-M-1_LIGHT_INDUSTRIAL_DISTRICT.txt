17.72.010 - Purpose and permitted uses.
A.
The M-1 light industrial district is intended to protect and promote areas for industrial and related uses, which do not create excessive noise, odor, smoke, dust, vibration and other emissions which might be detrimental to surrounding neighborhoods or other uses.
B.
Within any M-1 Light Industrial District, the following uses shall be permitted unless one or more of these uses are otherwise prohibited, not authorized, limited, or restricted by the City Council when acting upon a zoning application and/or rezoning of a parcel(s) of property:
1.
Any industrial use which involves manufacturing, processing or assembly operations or the storage and sale of heavy materials, products or equipment; but not including junk or salvage yards or uses which may cause injurious or obnoxious noise, vibrations, smoke, gas, fumes, odor, dust, fire hazard or other objectionable conditions to nearby areas.
2.
Animal hospitals or veterinary clinics; but excluding open kennels on the premises.
3.
Apparel manufacturing plants.
4.
Agricultural implement sales and service provided outside storage is located within the rear yard.
5.
Automobile service, sales and repair shops, provided all operations are conducted indoors and provided there be no outdoor storage or maintenance of my parts or waste materials.
6.
Automobile and truck car washing.
7.
Bottling plants and/or brewery.
8.
Cabinet shops.
9.
Cold storage, ice plants and freezer lockers.
10.
Dairy bars and ice cream manufacturing.
11.
Distribution of products and merchandise.
12.
Dry cleaning and laundering establishments with no retail sales.
13.
Electrical appliances and equipment sales and repairs, but excluding open storage.
14.
Electronic manufacturing and assembly plants.
15.
Fabricating shops such as upholstery and sheet metal shops.
16.
Garages, repair shops and machine shops provided there is no outside storage.
17.
Plumbing shops, but excluding open storage.
18.
Printing, publishing and reproducing establishments.
19.
Processing and compounding of non-explosive materials.
20.
Public works and public utility facilities such as distribution lines, transformer stations, transmission lines and towers, water tanks and towers, pumping stations, telephone exchanges, and storage yards.
21.
Scientific or research laboratories.
22.
Service stations, provided that:
a.
All pumps and canopies shall be located at least twenty (20) feet from any public right-of-way;
b.
All buildings and appurtenances shall be located at least one hundred (100) feet from any residential district line; and
c.
All fuel is stored underground outside any public right-of-way.
23.
Sign painting and fabricating shops.
24.
Storage, inside.
25.
Textile manufacturing.
26.
Tire recapping and retreading shops provided there is no outside storage.
27.
Truck and bus terminals.
28.
Wholesaling and warehousing.
29.
Customary accessory uses and structures when located on the same lot as the main structure, excluding, however, open storage.
30.
Adult entertainment, as defined in Chapter 5.08 of this Code.
( Ord. No. 15-04-20(A) , § 1, 4-20-2015)
17.72.020 - Special uses.
Within any M-1 Light Industrial District, the following uses may be allowed upon obtaining a Special Use Permit from the Mayor and Council:
A.
Churches and associated buildings.
B.
Rooming/boarding house.
C.
Halfway house.
D.
Truck sales and service.
( Ord. No. 15-04-20(A) , § 1, 4-20-2015)
