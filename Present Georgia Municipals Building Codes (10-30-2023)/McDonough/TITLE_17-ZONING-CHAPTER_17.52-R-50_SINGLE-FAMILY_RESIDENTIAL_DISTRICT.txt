CHAPTER 17.52 - R-50 SINGLE-FAMILY RESIDENTIAL DISTRICT

Sections:
17.52.010 - Purpose.
The R-50 single-family residential district is intended to provide suitable areas for single-family, detached dwellings at medium densities, with access to both public water and sewage. The district requirements are designed to insure high-quality and secure development with maximum utilization of existing land.
(Ord. No. 89-001, (part))
17.52.020 - Purpose and permitted uses.
A.
Within any R-50 Single-Family Residential District, the following uses shall be permitted:
1.
Single-family detached dwellings.
2.
Customary residential accessory buildings or uses, provided such shall be permitted only in a rear yard and shall be situated not less than three (3) feet from any property line; and further provided that in the case of corner lots, such buildings or structures shall be set back at least ten (10) feet from any side street right-of-way line.
3.
Parks, playgrounds, community centers, swimming pools, golf courses, and other recreational facilities, operated on a nonprofit basis, provided no building or is located less than fifty (50) feet from any property line, and further provided that provisions are made for a buffer strip that is twenty-five (25) feet wide along all side and rear lot lines.
4.
Residential subdivision amenity centers provided by developers or homeowner's associations.
(Ord. No. 89-001, (part); Ord. No. 04-12-06-B, § XXII, 12-6-2004; Ord. No. 08-01-22(C), § XXVI, 1-22-2008)
17.52.030 - Development standards.
Within any R-50 residential district, the following development standards shall apply:
A.
Development Requirements.
1.
Minimum lot size: eight thousand (8,000) square feet;
2.
Minimum lot width: sixty (60) feet;
3.
Minimum floor area: one thousand six hundred (1,600) square feet.
B.
Minimum Setback Requirements.
1.
From major street right-of-way: ten feet;
2.
From minor street right-of-way: ten feet;
3.
Side yard: seven and one-half (7.5) feet;
4.
Rear yard: thirty (30) feet.
C.
Maximum height: thirty-five (35) feet.
D.
Maximum lot coverage: thirty-five (35) percent.
E.
An enclosed two (2) car garage shall be required for each dwelling.
(Ord. No. 89-001, (part); Ord. of 3-19-1990; Ord. No. 04-12-06-B, § XXIII, 12-6-2004; Ord. No. 08-01-22(C), § XXVII, 1-22-2008)
17.52.040 - Special Uses.
Within any R-50 Single-Family Residential District, the following uses may be allowed upon obtaining a Special Use Permit from the City Council:
A.
Senior condominium Center.
(Ord. No. 04-12-06-B, § XXIV, 12-6-2004; Ord. No. 08-01-22(C), § XXVIII, 1-22-2008)
