Sec. 9-1. - Qualifications of electors; registration of electors for municipal elections.
Any person who is a resident of the city who is registered with the board of registrars of the county shall be eligible to vote in municipal elections.
(Code 1966, § 6-1)
State Law reference— Registration of voters generally, O.C.G.A. § 21-2-210 et seq.
Sec. 9-2. - Conduct of elections by Houston County.
(a)
All municipal elections shall be conducted by Houston County.
(b)
The designee of the board of commissioners of Houston County shall perform all duties as superintendent of any municipal election.
(c)
The city shall enter into a contract with Houston County for the conduct of each municipal election. The city will pay the county all costs incurred in conducting the election. Each contract shall be approved by the mayor and council.
(Ord. No. 97-5, § 1, 5-6-97)
Sec. 9-3. - Type of voting equipment.
The type of voting equipment used for voting at each polling location shall be direct recording electronic (DRE) units.
(Ord. No. 2003-22, § 1, 4-15-03)
Sec. 9-4. - Counting of absentee ballots.
Absentee ballots will be counted using optical scan (AccuVote-OS).
(Ord. No. 2003-22, § 2, 4-15-03)
