ARTICLE VII. - WELLHEAD PROTECTION

Sec. 25-241. - Purpose.
The purpose of this article is to insure the provision of a safe and sanitary drinking water supply for the City of Perry by the establishment of wellhead protection zones surrounding the wellheads for all wells and springs which are the supply sources for the city water system, and by the designation and regulation of property uses and conditions which may be maintained within such zones.
(Ord. No. 2008-29, 11-4-08)
Sec. 25-242. - Establishment of a wellhead protection zone.
There is hereby established a use district to be known as a wellhead protection zone as identified and described within the State of Georgia approved wellhead protection plan.
(Ord. No. 2008-29, 11-4-08)
Sec. 25-243. - Prohibited uses.
Land use and activities in the wellhead protection zones as identified and described as potential pollution sources in the State of Georgia approved wellhead protection plan will be prohibited as stated in the plan.
(Ord. No. 2008-29, 11-4-08)
Sec. 25-244. - Administration.
The policies and procedures for administration of any wellhead protection zone established under this article shall be the same as provided in the existing zoning ordinance for the city, as the same is presently enacted or may from time to time amended.
(Ord. No. 2008-29, 11-4-08)
