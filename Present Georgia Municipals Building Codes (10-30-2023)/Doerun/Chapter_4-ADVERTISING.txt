Sec. 4-1. - Posting or displaying without consent.
It shall be unlawful for any person to post or display in or upon any bridge or upon any telegraph, telephone or electric company's pole, or upon any public property or the private property of any person, any bills, signs or advertisements, without the consent, in writing, of the owner.
(Code 1982, § 9-1-5; Code 2000, § 6-1)
