Editor's note— Ord. No. 2001-57, § 1, adopted July 24, 2001, repealed §§ 14-131—14-135 and 14-146—14-148 in their entirety. Formerly said sections pertained to definitions, right of entry for purpose of inspection, duty of operator or manager to enforce article, frequenting by intoxicated persons, dances operated exclusively for minors, required license fee, license application, license issuance, license rejection and revocation as related to dancehalls. The user of this Code is directed to ch. 14, art. IX, divs. 1 and 2 for similar provisions. See the Code Comparative Table.
