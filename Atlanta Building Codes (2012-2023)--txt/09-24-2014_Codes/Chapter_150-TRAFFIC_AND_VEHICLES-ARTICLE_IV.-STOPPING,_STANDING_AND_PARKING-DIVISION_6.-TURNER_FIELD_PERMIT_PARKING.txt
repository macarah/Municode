Sec. 150-181. - Definitions.
The following words, terms and phrases, when used in this division, shall have the meanings ascribed to them in this section, except where the context clearly indicates a different meaning:
Commissioner means the commissioner of the department of public works.
Curb parking space means a 24-foot-long section of street, adjacent to the curb or edge or roadway, where a motor vehicle may lawfully park.
Turner Field Permit Parking Area means:
(a)
That area on the East Side of Interstate 75-85 bounded on the North by Interstate 20, bounded on the East by Connally Street, S.E. and bounded on the South by Little Street, S.E.; and
(b)
That area on the West side of Interstate 75-85 comprised of 1) Formwalt Street, S. W. between Glenn Street, S.W. and Bass Street, S.W.; 2) Glenn Street, S.W. between Formwalt Street S.W. and Pryor Street, S.W., and 3) Pryor Street, S.W. between Glenn Street, S.W. and Ralph David Abernathy Boulevard, S.W.
(Code 1977, § 13-2602; Ord. No. 2012-09(12-O-0353), § 1, 3-28-12)
Cross reference— Definitions generally, § 1-2.
Sec. 150-182. - Program established; permit required.
There is established a permit parking program that will be in effect only during Atlanta Braves baseball games whereby motor vehicles bearing a special parking permit issued pursuant to requirements stated in this division may be parked in the Turner Field area, as defined in section 150-181, beginning two hours before and ending two hours after all Atlanta Braves baseball games played at Turner Field. Parking a motor vehicle on a street within the Turner Field permit parking area two hours before, during, and two hours after any Atlanta Braves baseball game at Turner Field shall be restricted unless the motor vehicle displays a valid Turner Field area parking permit.
(Code 1977, § 13-2601; Ord. No. 2012-09(12-O-0353), § 2, 3-28-12)
Sec. 150-183. - Application for permit.
The application for a Turner Field area parking permit shall provide the name of the owner or operator of the motor vehicle to be permitted; the residential address; the motor vehicle make, model and license number; and other information requested on the application form.
(Code 1977, § 13-2605; Ord. No. 2012-09(12-O-0353), § 3, 3-28-12)
Sec. 150-184. - Issuance of permits; expiration; renewal.
(a)
The commissioner shall issue Turner Field area parking permits as provided in this division.
(b)
Two resident permits shall be issued for each valid street address within the Turner Field permit parking area upon application. The permit shall be issued only to a motor vehicle owner or operator who resides in the Turner Field permit parking area. Each Turner Field area parking permit shall have a unique permit expiration date, and permits shall be renewed biennially.
(Code 1977, § 13-2603; Ord. No. 2012-09(12-O-0353), § 4, 3-28-12)
Sec. 150-185. - Permit form and display.
The Turner Field area parking permit shall be a decal and shall be affixed only to the motor vehicle for which it was issued. The decal shall be permanently attached to the inside lower corner of the rear window, driver's side, and must be clearly visible from outside the vehicle. Information shown on the permit decal shall include, but not be limited to, the Turner Field permit parking area identification letter and the expiration date.
(Code 1977, § 13-2604; Ord. No. 2012-09(12-O-0353), § 5, 3-28-12)
Sec. 150-186. - Uniformity of parking regulations.
Within the Turner Field permit parking area there shall be uniform parking regulations applied to all locations where parking is allowed. Upon approval of the Turner Field permit parking area, the commissioner shall have parking regulation signs installed within the area indicating the time, location and conditions under which parking shall be by permit only.
(Code 1977, § 13-2606; Ord. No. 2012-09(12-O-0353), § 6, 3-28-12)
Sec. 150-187. - Limitation on permit use.
A motor vehicle which displays a valid Turner Field area parking permit shall be allowed to stand or park in the Turner Field permit parking area without being limited by the parking restrictions established in this division. Such permits shall not authorize a motor vehicle to stand or park in any place where, or during any time when, the stopping, standing or parking of a motor vehicle is prohibited or the area is designated for other uses.
(Code 1977, § 13-2607; Ord. No. 2012-09(12-O-0353), § 7, 3-28-12)
Sec. 150-188. - Penalties for violations.
(a)
It shall be unlawful and a violation of this division for a person to furnish false information in an application for any permit authorized by this division, or for such person to represent themselves falsely as eligible for such permit.
(b)
A person holding a valid parking permit issued pursuant to this division shall not allow the use or display of such permit on a vehicle other than that for which the permit was issued. Such conduct shall be unlawful and a violation of this division both by the person holding the valid permit and the person who so uses or displays the permit improperly.
(c)
It shall be unlawful and a violation of this division for a person to produce or display a facsimile or counterfeit parking permit in order to evade time limitations on parking in the Turner Field permit parking area.
(d)
Violation of the provisions of this section shall constitute an offense and shall be punishable as provided under section 1-8 of the City Code.
(Code 1977, § 13-2608; Ord. No. 2012-09(12-O-0353), § 8, 3-28-12)
Secs. 150-189, 150-190. - Reserved.
