328.8.1
Whenever a review for impact on the fire prevention, natural resources, and/or fire resource allocation responsibilities of the fire department is required, as part of the environmental review process, the applicant shall pay a minimum deposit fee of $1,000 at the time of submittal to the fire department, and such supplemental fees and deposits as specified in subsections 328.8.2 through 328.8.7 of this section to complete the required review.
