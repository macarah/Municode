901.6.3.1 - Above-Ground Water Control Valve Signs.
Above-ground water control valves used for water based fire protection systems, including private fire hydrant systems, shall have a permanent sign identifying areas or systems controlled. Signs shall be metal, painted white with red letters a minimum of one-inch high with 3/16 inch stroke, and shall be permanently banded to the valve or permanently affixed to a wall.
