5601.2.1 - Fees.
As required by California Health and Safety Code section 12105, a permit for the storage of explosives shall not be issued until after the payment of a fee of $10, unless the quantity of explosives is 100 pounds or less, in which case the fee shall be $2. The permit fee shall be equally divided and deposited into the treasury of the County of Los Angeles and into the State Treasury.
