5704.2.9.1.1 - Required Foam Fire Protection Systems.
All above-ground tanks exceeding 1,500 square feet of liquid surface area used for the storage of Class I or Class II flammable liquids shall be provided with foam fire protection.
Exceptions:
1.
Tanks with floating roofs for storage of crude oil exceeding 1,500 square feet of liquid surface area and less than 12,300 square feet of liquid surface area shall have foam fire protection only for the seal area.
2.
Floating roof tanks or pressure tanks operating at or above 1-pound-per square inch gauge.
