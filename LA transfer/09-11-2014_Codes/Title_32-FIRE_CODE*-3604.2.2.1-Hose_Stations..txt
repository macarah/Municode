3604.2.2.1 - Hose Stations.
Hose stations shall be spaced to provide protection to all portions of floats or floating vessels. Hoses shall be mounted on a reel or rack and enclosed within an approved cabinet. Hose stations shall be labeled FIRE HOSE - EMERGENCY USE ONLY. Listed equipment shall be used. Each hose station shall be provided with a 2 ½-inch fire hose valve with a 2 ½ x 1 ½ reducer and a maximum 100 feet of lined hose and an approved fog nozzle.
The pipe sizing shall be a minimum of 2 ½ inches and shall be based on providing 65 psi at 100 gpm at the most remote hose station valve outlet, using a maximum 150 psi at the fire department inlet connection.
(Ord. 2014-0014 § 190, 2014.)
