328.8.1
Whenever a review for impact on the fire prevention, natural resources, and/or fire resource allocation responsibilities of the Fire Department is required as part of the environmental review process, the applicant shall pay a minimum deposit fee of $1,000.00 at the time of submittal to the Fire Department and such supplemental fees and deposits as specified in Subsections 328.8.2 through 328.8.7 of this section to complete the required review.
