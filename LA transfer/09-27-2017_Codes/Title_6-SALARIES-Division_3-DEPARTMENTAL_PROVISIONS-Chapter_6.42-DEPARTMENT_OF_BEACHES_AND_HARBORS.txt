6.42.030 - Small craft harbor commission.
Each member of the Los Angeles County small craft harbor commission (Item No. 9430) shall be entitled to receive as compensation the sum of $25.00 for each meeting of the commission attended by him, not to exceed 52 meetings in any one calendar year.
(Ord. 2012-0017 § 42, 2012; Ord. 2006-0053 § 2 (part), 2006.)
6.42.040 - Small craft harbor design control board.
Each member of the small craft harbor design control board (Item No. 9431) shall be entitled to receive as compensation the sum of $25.00 for each meeting of said board attended by him, not to exceed 20 meetings in any one calendar year.
(Ord. 2012-0017 § 43, 2012; Ord. 2006-0053 § 2 (part), 2006.)
